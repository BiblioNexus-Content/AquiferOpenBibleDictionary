## Jaasiel (id: 6139)

JAASIEL
=======

1\. Warrior among David’s mighty men. He is called “the Mezobaite” ([1 Chr 11:47](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:47)).

2\. Abner’s son and the leader of Benjamin’s tribe during David’s reign ([1 Chr 27:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 11:47; 1CH 27:21
