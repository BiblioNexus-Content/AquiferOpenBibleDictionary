## Daberath (id: 4734)

DABERATH
========

Town in Issachar’s territory given to the Levite family of Gershon ([Jos 21:28](https://ref.ly/Josh21:28); [1 Chr 6:72](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:72)). It was located west of Mt Tabor on the Issachar\-Zebulun border, and has been identified with the modern Deburiyeh.
