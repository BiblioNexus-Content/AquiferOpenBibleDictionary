## Chuza (id: 4562)

CHUZA
=====

The steward of Herod Antipas and a powerful and influential man. Chuza was either a manager of Herod's property or a political appointee. He was married to Joanna, whom Jesus healed. She then traveled with Jesus and his disciples ([Luke 8:3](https://ref.ly/Luke8:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 8:3
