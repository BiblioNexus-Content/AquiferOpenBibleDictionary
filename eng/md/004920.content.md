## Sea of the Arabah (id: 3686)

SEA OF THE ARABAH
=================

An alternate name for the Dead Sea. This body of water is called "the Sea of Arabah" because it is located within the area of the land of Israel called Arabah ([Deuteronomy 3:17](https://ref.ly/Deut3:17); [4:49](https://ref.ly/Deut4:49); [Joshua 3:16](https://ref.ly/Josh3:16); [12:3](https://ref.ly/Josh12:3); [2 Kings 14:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:25)). 

*See* Dead Sea.
