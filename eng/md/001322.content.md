## City of David (id: 4573)

CITY OF DAVID
=============

1. A southeastern hill (also called Ophel) in the present\-day city of Jerusalem. It was the site occupied by King David as his royal city. It is also called Zion (for example, in [1 Kings 8:1](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:1)). David captured the Jebusite fortress of Jerusalem and transferred his capital to it from Hebron ([2 Samuel 5:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:1-2Sam5:10)[–](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:1-2Sam5:10)[10](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:1-2Sam5:10)).

    *See* Jerusalem.

2. An alternate name for Bethlehem, David’s hometown, in the New Testament ([Luke 2:11](https://ref.ly/Luke2:11)).

    *See* Bethlehem.
