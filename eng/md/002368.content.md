## Hammelech (id: 5621)

HAMMELECH\*
===========

Hebrew word meaning “the king,” taken to be a personal name by the kjv, but more correctly translated as “the king” by other versions ([Jer 36:26](https://ref.ly/Jer36:26); [38:6](https://ref.ly/Jer38:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 36:26; JER 38:6
