## Adaiah (id: 3359)

ADAIAH
======

1. Josiah’s grandfather on his mother's side. Josiah’s mother, Jedidah, was Adaiah’s daughter ([2 Kings 22:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:1)).
2. Ethan’s son, a Levite of the Gershon clan and an ancestor of Asaph the psalmist ([1 Chronicles 6:41](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:41)). He is sometimes identified with the Iddo of [1 Chronicles 6:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:21).

    *See* Iddo \#2.

3. Shimei’s son, a minor member of the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:21)).
4. Jeroham’s son, a priest who returned to Jerusalem after the exile in Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:12); [Nehemiah 11:12](https://ref.ly/Neh11:12)).
5. Maaseiah’s father. Maaseiah was a captain under Jehoiada the priest ([2 Chronicles 23:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:1)).
6. Bani’s son, who obeyed Ezra’s advice to divorce his non\-Jewish wife after the exile in Babylon ([Ezra 10:29](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:29)).
7. The son of a different Bani, who also obeyed Ezra's advice to divorce his non\-Jewish wife ([Ezra 10:39](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:39)).
8. Joiarib’s son. He was descended from Perez and was an ancestor of Maaseiah ([Nehemiah 11:5](https://ref.ly/Neh11:5)).
