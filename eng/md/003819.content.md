## Mesha (Place) (id: 7079)

MESHA (Place)
=============

Place in southern Arabia defining the western boundary of the territory in which the descendants of Joktan settled ([Gn 10:30](https://ref.ly/Gen10:30)). Its location is unknown. Some suggest that Mesha was a seaport town situated along the eastern shores of the Red Sea in the vicinity of what is modern Yemen; others place it along the Persian Gulf’s northwestern banks near the region of Mesene.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:30
