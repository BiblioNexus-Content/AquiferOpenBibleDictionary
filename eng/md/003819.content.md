## Meshach (id: 7077)

MESHACH
=======

One of the three friends of the prophet Daniel. Meshach was thrown into the fiery furnace along with Shadrach and Abednego ([Daniel 1:7](https://ref.ly/Dan1:7); [2:49](https://ref.ly/Dan2:49); [3:12–30](https://ref.ly/Dan3:12-Dan3:30)). *See* Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego.

* **Associated Passages:** DAN 1:7; DAN 2:49; DAN 3:12–DAN 3:30
* **Associated Articles:** Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego (ID: `8259@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
