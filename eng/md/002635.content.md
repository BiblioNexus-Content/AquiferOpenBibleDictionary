## Holy Spirit (id: 5885)

HOLY SPIRIT
===========

The Holy Spirit is God's Spirit and the third person of the Trinity. This divine person is also called the Spirit of God, and in older Bible translations, the Holy Ghost. The Holy Spirit lives in believers, guides them, and helps them understand God's truth.

*See* Spirit of God.
