## Farthing (id: 5238)

FARTHING
========

1\. The King James Version translation for a small coin. A copper coin which was equal to one\-sixteenth of the silver denarius ([Matthew 10:29](https://ref.ly/Matt10:29); [Luke 12:6](https://ref.ly/Luke12:6)). A denarius was one day's wage.

2\. The King James Version translation for another word translated “penny." A coin worth one\-fourth of \#1 above, or one\-sixty\-fourth of the denarius ([Matthew 5:26](https://ref.ly/Matt5:26); [Mark 12:42](https://ref.ly/Mark12:42)).

*See also* Coins; Money.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 5:26; MAT 10:29; MRK 12:42; LUK 12:6
