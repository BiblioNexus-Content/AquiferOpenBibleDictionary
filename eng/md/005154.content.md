## Shimron (Place) (id: 8420)

SHIMRON (Place)
===============

Canaanite town whose king joined the confederacy of King Jabin of Hazor in unsuccessful resistance to the Israelites under Joshua ([Jos 11:1](https://ref.ly/Josh11:1)). Its site is uncertain, though it was in Zebulun’s territory ([19:15–16](https://ref.ly/Josh19:15-Josh19:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 11:1; JOS 19:15–JOS 19:16
