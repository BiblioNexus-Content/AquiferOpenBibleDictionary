## Archi, Archite (id: 3718)

ARCHI, ARCHITE
==============

A tribe descended from Canaan or a member of this tribe ([Joshua 16:2](https://ref.ly/Josh16:2)). This is sometimes spelled "Arkite." Hushai, David’s loyal adviser, was an Archite ([2 Samuel 15:32](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:32)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 16:2; 2SA 15:32
