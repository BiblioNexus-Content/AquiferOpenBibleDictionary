## Mahlon (id: 6878)

MAHLON
======

Son of Elimelech and Naomi, and Chilion’s brother. While with his family in Moab, he married Ruth, the Moabitess. He died in Moab, however, and Ruth later married Boaz ([Ru 1:2, 5](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:2,Ruth1:5); [4:9–10](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:9-Ruth4:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** RUT 1:2; RUT 1:5; RUT 4:9–RUT 4:10
