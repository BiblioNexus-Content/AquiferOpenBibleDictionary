## Zabbai (id: 9113)

ZABBAI
======

1\. Bebai’s son and one of the priests who divorced his foreign wife at Ezra’s command ([Ezr 10:28](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:28)).

2\. Father of Baruch. Baruch repaired a section of the Jerusalem wall during Nehemiah’s day ([Neh 3:20](https://ref.ly/Neh3:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:28; NEH 3:20
