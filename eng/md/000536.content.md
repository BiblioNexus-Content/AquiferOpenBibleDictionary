## Ashima (id: 3803)

ASHIMA
======

A god who the people of Hamanth worshiped. We do not know where they came from. They were moved to Samaria after the fall of Israel in 722 BC ([2 Kings 17:30](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:30)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 17:30
