## Hazar-Shual (id: 5731)

HAZAR\-SHUAL
============

Simeonite city located in the southern section of Judah ([Jos 15:28](https://ref.ly/Josh15:28); [19:3](https://ref.ly/Josh19:3); [1 Chr 4:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:28)). It is also listed among those cities occupied by the Jews who returned from captivity ([Neh 11:27](https://ref.ly/Neh11:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:28; JOS 19:3; 1CH 4:28; NEH 11:27
