## Hazarmaveth (id: 5730)

HAZARMAVETH
===========

Descendant of Shem through Joktan ([Gn 10:26](https://ref.ly/Gen10:26); [1 Chr 1:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:20)) whose progeny lived in southern Arabia ([Gn 10:30](https://ref.ly/Gen10:30)) in the Wadi Hadhramaut. Excavations there revealed a flourishing economy in the fifth century BC, based on frankincense trade. This trade, revived in the second century BC, made the area prosperous and influential.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:26; GEN 10:30; 1CH 1:20
