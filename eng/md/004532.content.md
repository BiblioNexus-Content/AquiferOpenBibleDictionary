## Potsherd (id: 7792)

POTSHERD\*
==========

Piece of broken pottery used in OT times to carry hot coals or to dip water. Potsherds were also used as lids for storage containers or cooking kettles, as a medium for written communication, or to add grit to waterproofing compounds. The symbolic importance of sherds is clear in [Psalm 22:15](https://ref.ly/Ps22:15), [Isaiah 30:14](https://ref.ly/Isa30:14), [45:9](https://ref.ly/Isa45:9), and [Ezekiel 23:34](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:34).
