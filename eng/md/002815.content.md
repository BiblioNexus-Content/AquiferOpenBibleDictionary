## Ishbosheth (id: 6066)

ISHBOSHETH
==========

Another name for Eshbaal, son of King Saul. He became king after his father died ([2 Samuel 2–4](https://ref.ly/2Sam2:1-2Sam4:12)). Ishbosheth ruled for a short time before his death.

*See* Eshbaal.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 2:1–2SA 4:12
