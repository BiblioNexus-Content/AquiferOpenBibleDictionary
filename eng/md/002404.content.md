## Haradah (id: 5657)

HARADAH
=======

The twentieth wilderness encampment of the Israelites in their journey from Egypt to the promised land. It is the ninth place they camped after leaving Sinai. It is listed between Mount Shepher and Makheloth. Its location is uncertain ([Numbers 33:24–25](https://ref.ly/Num33:24-Num33:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:24–NUM 33:25
