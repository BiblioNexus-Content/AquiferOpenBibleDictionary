## Shaaph (id: 8253)

SHAAPH
======

1\. Jahdai’s sixth son, included in the genealogy of Caleb, Jerahmeel’s brother ([1 Chr 2:47](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:47)).

2\. Son of Caleb by his concubine; the brother of Jerahmeel; the father of Madmannah ([1 Chr 2:49](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:49)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:47; 1CH 2:49
