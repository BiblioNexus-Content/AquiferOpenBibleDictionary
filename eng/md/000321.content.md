## Anab (id: 3583)

ANAB
====

A town in the hill country of Hebron where giant warriors lived. After Joshua eliminated the giants, Anab was allotted to Judah’s tribe ([Joshua 11:21](https://ref.ly/Josh11:21); [15:50](https://ref.ly/Josh15:50)). Today, Anab is known as Khirbet ‘Anab el\-Kebireh.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 11:21; JOS 15:50
