## Jegar-Sahadutha (id: 6263)

JEGAR\-SAHADUTHA
================

Aramaic name given by Laban to the heap of stones that he and Jacob piled up as a memorial to their covenant; Jacob called it “Galeed” ([Gn 31:47](https://ref.ly/Gen31:47)). The name means “heap of witness.”
