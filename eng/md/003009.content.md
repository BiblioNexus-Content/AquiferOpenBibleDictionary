## Jehallelel (id: 6264)

JEHALLELEL
==========

1\. Descendant of Judah who had four sons ([1 Chr 4:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:16)).

2\. Levite of the family of Merari whose son Azariah participated in the cleansing of the temple in Hezekiah’s time ([2 Chr 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:16; 2CH 29:12
