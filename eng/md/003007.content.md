## Jeezer, Jeezerite (id: 6262)

JEEZER, JEEZERITE
=================

Jeezer and Jeezerite are names used in the King James Version of the Bible. These names are shortened forms of Abiezer and Abiezerite. Abiezer was a son of Gilead, and the Abiezerites were his descendants ([Numbers 26:30](https://ref.ly/Num26:30)). 

*See* Abiezer \#1.
