## Mibsam (id: 7103)

MIBSAM
======

1\. One of Ishmael’s sons and the founder of a tribe named after him ([Gn 25:13](https://ref.ly/Gen25:13); [1 Chr 1:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:29)).

2\. Shallum’s son and the father of Mishma ([1 Chr 4:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:13; 1CH 1:29; 1CH 4:25
