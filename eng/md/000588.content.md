## Ataroth-Addar (id: 3855)

ATAROTH\-ADDAR
==============

A town on the boundary between Ephraim’s and Benjamin's tribal land ([Joshua 16:5](https://ref.ly/Josh16:5); [18:13](https://ref.ly/Josh18:13)). It was about seven miles (or 11 kilometers) north of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 16:5; JOS 18:13
