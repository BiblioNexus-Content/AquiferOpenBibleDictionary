## Eubulus (id: 5178)

EUBULUS
=======

A Roman Christian who sent greetings to Timothy while the apostle Paul was imprisoned for a second time in Rome ([2 Timothy 4:21](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:21)). Since Eubulus is a Greek name it could indicate his gentile (non\-Jewish) origin.

* **Associated Passages:** 2TI 4:21
