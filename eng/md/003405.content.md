## Lahmam (id: 6663)

LAHMAM
======

Judahite town in the Shephelah district of Lachish ([Jos 15:40](https://ref.ly/Josh15:40)), usually identified with modern Khirbet el\-Lahm; alternately spelled Lahmas in some versions (niv, nasb).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:40
