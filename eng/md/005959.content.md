## Zeruiah (id: 9203)

ZERUIAH
=======

A daughter of Nahash and the sister of Abigail ([2 Samuel 17:25](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:25)). Zeruiah later gave birth to three sons: Joab, Abishai, and Asahel. All of them were friends of David when he was king of Israel ([2 Samuel 2:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam2:18); [3:39](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:39); [8:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:16); [18:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 2:18; 2SA 3:39; 2SA 8:16; 2SA 17:25; 2SA 18:2
