## Sheerah (id: 8329)

SHEERAH
=======

Sheerah was the daughter or granddaughter of Ephraim. Her children built Lower and Upper Beth\-horon and Uzzen\-sheerah ([1 Chronicles 7:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:24)). Uzzen\-sheerah is named after Sheerah.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:24
