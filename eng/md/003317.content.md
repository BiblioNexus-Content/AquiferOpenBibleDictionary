## Kibzaim (id: 6574)

KIBZAIM
=======

One of several cities in Ephraim given to the Levitical family of Kohath after the conquest of Canaan ([Jos 21:22](https://ref.ly/Josh21:22)). It is probably the same as the Jokmeam of [1 Chronicles 6:68](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:68).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 21:22; 1CH 6:68
