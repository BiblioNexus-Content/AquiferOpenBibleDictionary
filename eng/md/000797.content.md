## Beer-Elim (id: 4066)

BEER\-ELIM
==========

One of the cities of Moab that Isaiah predicted would hear crying when the Moabite kingdom fell ([Isaiah 15:8](https://ref.ly/Isa15:8)). It might be the same as Beer ([Numbers 21:16](https://ref.ly/Num21:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 21:16; ISA 15:8
