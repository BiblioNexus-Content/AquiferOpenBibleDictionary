## Gomorrah (id: 5510)

GOMORRAH
========

One of the “cities of the valley” destroyed by God because of its wickedness ([Gn 19](https://ref.ly/Gen19:1-Gen19:38)). *See* Cities of the Plain; Sodom and Gomorrah.
