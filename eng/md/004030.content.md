## Naharai (id: 7286)

NAHARAI
=======

One of the mighty men of King David. He was also the armor bearer for Joab. Naharai was from the city of Beeroth ([2 Samuel 23:37](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:37); [1 Chronicles 11:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:39)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:37; 1CH 11:39
