## Ephraim, Gate of (id: 5113)

EPHRAIM, Gate of
================

Gate in the Jerusalem wall positioned about 600 feet (182\.9 meters) east of the Corner Gate ([2 Kgs 14:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:13); [2 Chr 25:23](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:23)). It was rebuilt in Nehemiah’s day ([Neh 12:39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:39)) and was situated close to the Water Gate and courts of the temple.
