## Gabael (id: 5328)

GABAEL
======

1\. Ancestor of Tobit and a member of the tribe of Naphtali ([Tb 1:1](https://ref.ly/Tob1:1)).

2\. Brother or son of Gabrias and resident of Rages, a city in Media, with whom Tobit left in trust 10 talents of silver ([Tb 1:14](https://ref.ly/Tob1:14)). Tobit later told his son Tobias about the money, and Tobias was led by the angel Raphael to Gabael ([4:1, 20](https://ref.ly/Tob4:1,Tob4:20); [5:6](https://ref.ly/Tob5:6); [6:9](https://ref.ly/Tob6:9); [10:2](https://ref.ly/Tob10:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** TOB 1:1; TOB 1:14; TOB 4:1; TOB 4:20; TOB 5:6; TOB 6:9; TOB 10:2
