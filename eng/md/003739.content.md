## Mearah (id: 6999)

MEARAH
======

Region of Palestine that the Israelites had not possessed ([Jos 13:4](https://ref.ly/Josh13:4), niv “Arah”). Its location is uncertain. Suggestions include the district of caves (Mearah means “cave”) near Sidon called Mughar Jezzin, and the towns Khirbet ‘Arah and Mogheiriyeh.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 13:4
