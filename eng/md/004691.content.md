## Razis (id: 7954)

RAZIS
=====

A fiercely patriotic Jewish elder sought by the Syrian general Nicanor for opposition to Hellenism ([2 Macc 14:37–46](https://ref.ly/2Macc14:37-2Macc14:46)). He committed suicide, throwing his own innards at spectators, rather than face arrest by Syrian soldiers.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 14:37–2MA 14:46
