## Abednego (id: 3275)

ABEDNEGO
========

One of Daniel’s three friends who was sentenced to death by Nebuchadnezzar but was protected in the fiery furnace by an angel ([Daniel 1:7](https://ref.ly/Dan1:7); [3:12](https://ref.ly/Dan3:12-Dan3:30)–[30](https://ref.ly/Dan3:12-Dan3:30)). 

*See* Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego; Daniel, Additions to (Prayer of Azariah).

* **Associated Passages:** DAN 1:7; DAN 3:12–DAN 3:30
* **Associated Articles:** Daniel, Additions to (ID: `4745@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego (ID: `8259@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
