## Ephai (id: 5102)

EPHAI
=====

Netophathite (resident of the town Netophah, near Bethlehem) whose sons fought against the Babylonian army ([Jer 40:8](https://ref.ly/Jer40:8)). They, with others, approached Gedaliah, the governor of Judah appointed by Babylon, and requested his protection. They died, along with Gedaliah, in an uprising led by Ishmael, the son of Nethaniah ([41:3](https://ref.ly/Jer41:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 40:8; JER 41:3
