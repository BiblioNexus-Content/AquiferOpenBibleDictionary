## Father-in-Law (id: 5242)

FATHER\-IN\-LAW
===============

A father\-in\-law is the father of a person's husband or wife. For example, if a woman marries a man, her father\-in\-law is her husband's father. Similarly, a man’s father\-in\-law is the father of his wife.

*See* Family Life and Relations.
