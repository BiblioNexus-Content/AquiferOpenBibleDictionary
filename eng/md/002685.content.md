## Hozai (id: 5934)

HOZAI\*
=======

Author of annals describing the life of Manasseh, king of Judah, and included in the “Chronicles of the Seers” ([2 Chr 33:18–19](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:18-2Chr33:19)). The Septuagint renders Hozai as “seers,” which is preferred by many commentators and the nlt (see [2 Chr 33:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr33:19) mg).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 33:19; 2CH 33:18–2CH 33:19
