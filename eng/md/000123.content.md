## Adlai (id: 3383)

ADLAI
=====

The father of Shaphat, who took care of the king’s cattle in the valleys when David was king of Israel ([1 Chronicles 27:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 27:29
