## Palti (id: 7566)

PALTI
=====

1. One of the 12 spies Moses sent to explore the land of Canaan before the Israelite conquest. Palti represented the tribe of Benjamin ([Numbers 13:9](https://ref.ly/Num13:9)).
2. A son of Laish, to whom King Saul gave Michal, his daughter and wife of David. This was after the break between Saul and David ([1 Samuel 25:44](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:44)). Ish\-bosheth recovered Michal from Palti and returned her to David ([2 Samuel 3:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:15); here called “Paltiel”).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:9; 1SA 25:44; 2SA 3:15
