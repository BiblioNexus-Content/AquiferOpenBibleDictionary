## Bosor (Place) (id: 4313)

BOSOR (Place)
=============

Bosor was a city in the region of Gilead. Judas Maccabeus captured the city to rescue the Jewish people living there ([1 Maccabees 5:26, 36](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:26,1Macc5:36)). Today, this place is called Busr El\-Hariri.

*See* Bozrah \#3.
