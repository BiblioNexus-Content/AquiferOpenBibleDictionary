## Kolaiah (id: 6640)

KOLAIAH
=======

1\. Benjamite; forefather of a family who lived in Jerusalem after the exile ([Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)).

2\. Father of Ahab, the false prophet who, along with Zedekiah, prophesied falsely in the name of God during Jeremiah’s day ([Jer 29:21](https://ref.ly/Jer29:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:7; JER 29:21
