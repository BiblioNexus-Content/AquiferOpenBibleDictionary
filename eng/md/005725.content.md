## Uri (id: 8978)

URI
===

1. The father of Bezalel from Judah’s tribe, and a builder of the tabernacle ([Exodus 31:2](https://ref.ly/Exod31:2); [35:30](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30); [38:22](https://ref.ly/Exod38:22); [1 Chronicles 2:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:20); [2 Chronicles 1:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr1:5)).
2. The father of Geber, one of Solomon’s officers in Gilead ([1 Kings 4:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:19)).
3. One of the temple gatekeepers who divorced his non\-Jewish wife at Ezra’s request ([Ezra 10:24](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 31:2; EXO 35:30; EXO 38:22; 1KI 4:19; 1CH 2:20; 2CH 1:5; EZR 10:24
