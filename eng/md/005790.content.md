## Water Gate (id: 9036)

WATER GATE
==========

One of the principal gates on the east side of Jerusalem. It was rebuilt during Nehemiah’s day and served as the location for Ezra’s reading of the law ([Neh 3:26](https://ref.ly/Neh3:26); [8:3, 16](https://ref.ly/Neh8:3,Neh8:16); [12:37](https://ref.ly/Neh12:37)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:26; NEH 8:3; NEH 8:16; NEH 12:37
