## Ishvi (id: 6087)

ISHVI
=====

1. The third son of Asher ([Genesis 46:17](https://ref.ly/Gen46:17); [1 Chronicles 7:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:30)). He was the founder of the Ishvite clan ([Numbers 26:44](https://ref.ly/Num26:44)).
2. A variant form of the name "Ishbosheth." He was one of the sons of King Saul ([1 Samuel 14:49](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:49)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:17; NUM 26:44; 1SA 14:49; 1CH 7:30
