## Paltiel (id: 7564)

PALTIEL
=======

1. A son of Azzan and a leader of the tribe of Issachar ([Numbers 34:26](https://ref.ly/Num34:26)). Eleazar and Joshua chose him to assist in the distribution of the land west of the Jordan River among the ten tribes to whom it was given.
2. Another name for Palti, a son of Laish ([2 Samuel 3:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:15)).

    *See* Palti \#2.
