## Pethahiah (id: 7681)

PETHAHIAH
=========

1\. Levite and ancestor of one of the postexilic priestly families ([1 Chr 24:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:16)).

2\. Levite who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his pagan wife after the exile ([Ezr 10:23](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:23)).

3\. Levite who assisted Ezra at the Feast of Tabernacles ([Neh 9:5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:5)).

4\. Meshezabel’s son from Judah’s tribe, who served as an adviser to the Persian king ([Neh 11:24](https://ref.ly/Neh11:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:16; EZR 10:23; NEH 9:5; NEH 11:24
