## Jashub (id: 6234)

JASHUB
======

1\. Issachar’s third son ([1 Chr 7:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:1); alternately called Iob in [Gn 46:13](https://ref.ly/Gen46:13)), and founder of the Jashubite family ([Nm 26:24](https://ref.ly/Num26:24)).

2\. Bani’s descendant, who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his pagan wife after the exile ([Ezr 10:29](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:13; NUM 26:24; 1CH 7:1; EZR 10:29
