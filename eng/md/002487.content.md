## Hazo (id: 5743)

HAZO
====

Nahor’s fifth son ([Gn 22:22](https://ref.ly/Gen22:22)); probably used as the name for a Nahorite clan. It has been identified with the name Hazu, which designated a mountainous region in northern Arabia mentioned in an inscription telling of Esarhaddon’s Arabian campaign.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 22:22
