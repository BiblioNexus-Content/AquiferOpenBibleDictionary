## Amasiah (id: 3545)

AMASIAH
=======

A military leader in the time of Jehoshaphat. He was in charge of 200,000 men. Amasiah was Zichri’s son and a man of unusual piety ([2 Chronicles 17:16](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 17:16
