## Nehushtan (id: 7347)

NEHUSHTAN
=========

Name given to the bronze serpent that Moses made during the wilderness wanderings. At the time of King Hezekiah’s reforms, it was destroyed ([2 Kgs 18:4](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:4)). *See* Bronze Serpent, Bronze Snake.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 18:4
* **Associated Articles:** Bronze Snake, Bronze Serpent (ID: `4344@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
