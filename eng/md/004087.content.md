## Nehushta (id: 7348)

NEHUSHTA
========

Nehushta was the mother of Jehoiachin, who was a king of Judah. She was taken to Babylon with her son when they were forced to leave their homeland ([2 Kings 24:8–15](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:8-2Kgs24:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 24:8–2KI 24:15
