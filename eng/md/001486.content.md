## Dagon (id: 4736)

DAGON
=====

Deity worshiped throughout the Mesopotamian world. In the OT, Dagon is the principal god of the Philistines ([Jgs 16:23](https://ref.ly/Judg16:23); [1 Sm 5:2–7](https://ref.ly/1Sam5:2-1Sam5:7); [1 Chr 10:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:10)). Shrines to Dagon were found in Israel’s territories (Beth\-dagon, [Jos 15:41](https://ref.ly/Josh15:41); [19:27](https://ref.ly/Josh19:27)). *See* Canaanite Deities and Religion.
