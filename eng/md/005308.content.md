## Song of Ascents, Song of Degrees (id: 8566)

SONG OF ASCENTS, SONG OF DEGREES
================================

A title that is given to each of the Psalms from 120 to 134\. It is possible that these psalms were sung by pilgrims travelling up to Jerusalem for the major feasts. *See* Music; Musical Instruments; Psalms, Book of.
