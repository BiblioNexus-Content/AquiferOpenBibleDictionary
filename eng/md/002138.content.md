## Gebim (id: 5393)

GEBIM
=====

Small town just north of Jerusalem. [Isaiah 10:31](https://ref.ly/Isa10:31) prophesied that its inhabitants would flee when the Assyrian army came to invade. Its exact location is unknown.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 10:31
