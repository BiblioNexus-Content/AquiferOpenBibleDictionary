## Tirshatha (id: 8864)

TIRSHATHA\*
===========

KJV translation of a Hebrew word designating a title of authority with the connotation of “governor.” It is appended to Zerubbabel’s name ([Ezr 2:63](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:63)) and Nehemiah’s name ([Neh 8:9](https://ref.ly/Neh8:9); [10:1](https://ref.ly/Neh10:1)), both of whom held the office in Jerusalem during the postexilic period.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:63; NEH 8:9; NEH 10:1
