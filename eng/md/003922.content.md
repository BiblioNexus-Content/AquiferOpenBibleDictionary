## Mnason (id: 7182)

MNASON
======

A Christian man who lived in Jerusalem ([Acts 21:16](https://ref.ly/Acts21:16)). He was born on the island of Cyprus and had been a follower of Jesus for many years. When Paul and the people traveling with him arrived in Jerusalem, Mnason welcomed them and provided a place for them to stay as his guests.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 21:16
