## Bozkath (id: 4322)

BOZKATH
=======

Town near Lachish and Eglon in Judah’s territory ([Jos 15:39](https://ref.ly/Josh15:39)), home of King Josiah’s mother ([2 Kgs 22:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:39; 2KI 22:1
