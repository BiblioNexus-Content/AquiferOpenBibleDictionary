## Arba (id: 3710)

ARBA
====

An ancestor of the giant Anakim and a great hero among his people ([Joshua 15:13](https://ref.ly/Josh15:13); [21:11](https://ref.ly/Josh21:11)). Arba was the founder of Kiriath\-arba (city of Arba), later known as Hebron ([Joshua 14:15](https://ref.ly/Josh14:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 14:15; JOS 15:13; JOS 21:11
