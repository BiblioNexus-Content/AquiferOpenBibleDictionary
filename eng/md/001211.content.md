## Chaereas (id: 4464)

CHAEREAS
========

Chaereas was the brother of Timothy. He was the governor (ruler) of the city of Gazara ([2 Maccabees 10:32](https://ref.ly/2Macc10:32)). The Maccabees attacked Gazara and defeated its army. Many people died in the battle, including Chaereas and Timothy (verses [36–37](https://ref.ly/2Macc10:36-2Macc10:37)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 10:32; 2MA 10:36–2MA 10:37
