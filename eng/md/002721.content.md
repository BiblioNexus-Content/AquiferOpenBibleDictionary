## Hyrcanus (id: 5969)

HYRCANUS
========

1\. John Hyrcanus, Hasmonean ruler. *See* Hasmoneans.

2\. Tobias’s son who had a large amount of money deposited in the temple during the time of Heliodorus ([2 Macc 3:11](https://ref.ly/2Macc3:11)).
