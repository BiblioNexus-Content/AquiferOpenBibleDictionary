## Antipater (id: 3642)

ANTIPATER
=========

1. A goodwill ambassador sent out with Numenius to the Spartans and Romans by Jonathan, the high priest ([1 Maccabees 12:16](https://ref.ly/1Macc12:16); [14:22](https://ref.ly/1Macc14:22)).
2. The father of Herod the Great. *See* Herod, Herodian Family.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 12:16; 1MA 14:22
* **Associated Articles:** Herod, Herodian Family (ID: `5821@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
