## Uzza (id: 8988)

UZZA
====

1\. Owner or initial planter of a garden that served as the burial place for kings Manasseh and Amon of Judah ([2 Kgs 21:18, 26](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:18,2Kgs21:26)). The “garden of Uzza” was apparently adjacent to Manasseh’s royal residence.

2\. KJV spelling for Uzzah, Shimei’s son, in [1 Chronicles 6:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:29). *See* Uzzah \#2.

3\. Son or descendant of Ehud from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 8:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:7)), listed as an ancestor of Mordecai in extrabiblical texts.

4\. KJV spelling for Uzzah, Abinadab’s son, in [1 Chronicles 13:7–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:7-1Chr13:11). *See* Uzzah \#1.

5\. Forefather of a family of temple servants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel following the exile ([Ezr 2:49](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:49); [Neh 7:51](https://ref.ly/Neh7:51)).
