## Igal (id: 5989)

IGAL
====

1. A son of Joseph from the tribe of Issachar. He was one of the 12 spies sent by Moses to search out Canaan ([Numbers 13:7](https://ref.ly/Num13:7)).
2. A son of Nathan and one of the mighty men of King David ([2 Samuel 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:36)). In [1 Chronicles 11:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:38) he is called Joel, the brother of Nathan. In Hebrew, this names is only one letter different from Igal.
3. A son of Shemaiah and a descendant of King David through King Jehoiachin ([1 Chronicles 3:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:7; 2SA 23:36; 1CH 3:22; 1CH 11:38
