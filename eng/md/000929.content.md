## Beth-Rehob (id: 4200)

BETH\-REHOB
===========

A city or district mentioned in [Judges 18:28](https://ref.ly/Judg18:28) and [2 Samuel 10:6\.](https://ref.ly/2Sam10:6) It was probably the northernmost point the 12 Israelite spies reached when they searched the land of Canaan. It is probably the same as Rehob ([Numbers 13:21](https://ref.ly/Num13:21); [2 Samuel 10:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam10:8)).

*See* Rehob (Place).
