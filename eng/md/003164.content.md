## Jokmeam (id: 6422)

JOKMEAM
=======

1\. City mentioned in [1 Kings 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:12) (kjv “Jokneam”); seemingly close in proximity and perhaps the same as Jokneam. *See* Jokneam.

2\. City given to the Kohathite Levites out of Ephraim’s inheritance ([1 Chr 6:68](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:68)). A parallel passage in [Joshua 21:22](https://ref.ly/Josh21:22) lists the city as Kibzaim. *See* Kibzaim.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 21:22; 1KI 4:12; 1CH 6:68
* **Associated Articles:** Jokneam (ID: `6423@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Kibzaim (ID: `6574@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
