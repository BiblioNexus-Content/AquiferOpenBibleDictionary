## Sia, Siaha (id: 8472)

SIA\*, SIAHA
============

Ancestor of a group of temple assistants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel following the exile ([Neh 7:47](https://ref.ly/Neh7:47), nlt mg); spelled Siaha in [Ezra 2:44](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:44).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:44; NEH 7:47
