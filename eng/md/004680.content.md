## Rapha (id: 7946)

RAPHA
=====

1\. Benjamin’s fifth son ([1 Chr 8:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:2)). His name is omitted in the earlier list of [Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21).

2\. KJV spelling of Raphah, an alternate name for Rephaiah, Binea’s son, in [1 Chronicles 8:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:37). *See* Rephaiah \#4.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; 1CH 8:2; 1CH 8:37
* **Associated Articles:** Rephaiah (ID: `8001@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
