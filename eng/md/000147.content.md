## Adversary (id: 3407)

ADVERSARY
=========

Any foe, opponent, or enemy of God and his people. The apostle Peter's description of the devil as "your adversary" ([1 Peter 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Pet5:8)) has led to the use of "the adversary" as a reference to Satan in literature and popular speech. 

*See* Satan.
