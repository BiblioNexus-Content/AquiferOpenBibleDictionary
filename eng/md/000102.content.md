## Adam (Place) (id: 3364)

ADAM (Place)
============

A city on the Jordan River. When Joshua led the Israelites across the river, God made the river dry up, from Adam to the Dead Sea, so the people could cross on dry land ([Joshua 3:16](https://ref.ly/Josh3:16)). Adam is modern\-day Tell ed\-Damiyeh. 

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 3:16
