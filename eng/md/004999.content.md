## Shahazumah (id: 8262)

SHAHAZUMAH
==========

Town situated between Tabor and Beth\-shemesh on the border of the land allotted to Issachar’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 19:22](https://ref.ly/Josh19:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:22
