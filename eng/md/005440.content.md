## Tabor (Place) (id: 8703)

TABOR (Place)
=============

City mentioned in [1 Chronicles 6:77](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:77) in a list of the towns of Zebulun. In a similar list in [Joshua 19:12](https://ref.ly/Josh19:12) the city is named Kisloth\-Tabor; if the same city is meant, the Chronicler may have abbreviated the name here.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:12; 1CH 6:77
