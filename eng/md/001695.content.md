## Eduth (id: 4947)

EDUTH
=====

A Hebrew word usually translated as “testimony,” “witness,” or “commandment.” 

It can refer to: 

* The tabernacle ([Numbers 17:7–8](https://ref.ly/Num17:7-Num17:8); [18:2](https://ref.ly/Num18:2); [2 Chronicles 24:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:6))
* The ark ([Exodus 25:16](https://ref.ly/Exod25:16))
* The Ten Commandments ([Exodus 31:18](https://ref.ly/Exod31:18))
* The law of God in general ([Psalm 19:8](https://ref.ly/Ps19:8))

The title of [Psalm 60](https://ref.ly/Ps60:1-Ps60:12) in Hebrew is *Shushan Eduth*, which means “Lily of the Covenant.”
