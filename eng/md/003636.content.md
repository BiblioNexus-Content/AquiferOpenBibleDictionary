## Malchiah (id: 6894)

MALCHIAH\*
==========

1\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Gershon’s descendant, in [1 Chronicles 6:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:40). *See* Malchijah \#1.

2\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Parosh’s son, in [Ezra 10:25](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:25). *See* Malchijah \#4.

3\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Harim’s son, in [Ezra 10:31](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:31). *See* Malchijah \#6.

4\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Recab’s son, in [Nehemiah 3:14](https://ref.ly/Neh3:14). *See* Malchijah \#7.

5\. KJV spelling of Malchijah the goldsmith in [Nehemiah 3:31](https://ref.ly/Neh3:31). *See* Malchijah \#8.

6\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Ezra’s assistant, in [Nehemiah 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4). *See* Malchijah \#9.

7\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, Adaiah’s forefather, in [Nehemiah 11:12](https://ref.ly/Neh11:12). *See* Malchijah \#2.

8\. KJV spelling of Malchijah, the royal prince in whose cistern Jeremiah was imprisoned ([Jer 38:6](https://ref.ly/Jer38:6)). Malchiah is also spelled Melchiah ([21:1](https://ref.ly/Jer21:1)). *See* Malchijah \#12.
