## Makaz (id: 6883)

MAKAZ
=====

One of the 12 cities that provided food one month out of the year for King Solomon and his household ([1 Kgs 4:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:9)). Situated in northwest Judah, it may be identified with Khirbet el\-Mukheizin, south of Ekron.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:9
