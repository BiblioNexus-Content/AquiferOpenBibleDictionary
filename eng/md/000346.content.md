## Aner (Person) (id: 3608)

ANER (Person)
=============

An Amorite ally of Abram and brother of Mamre and Eshcol ([Genesis 14:13](https://ref.ly/Gen14:13)). With his brothers, Aner helped Abram defeat a group of four kings who had robbed Sodom and Gomorrah and had captured Abram’s nephew Lot ([Genesis 14:14–16, 21–24](https://ref.ly/Gen14:14-Gen14:16,Gen14:21-Gen14:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:13; GEN 14:14–GEN 14:16; GEN 14:21–GEN 14:24
