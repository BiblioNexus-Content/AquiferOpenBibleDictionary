## Zippor (id: 9243)

ZIPPOR
======

The father of Balak, who was the king of Moab. Balak asked a man named Balaam to curse the Israelites ([Numbers 22:2, 10, 16](https://ref.ly/Num22:2,Num22:10,Num22:16); [23:18](https://ref.ly/Num23:18); [Joshua 24:9](https://ref.ly/Josh24:9); [Judges 11:25](https://ref.ly/Judg11:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 22:2; NUM 22:10; NUM 22:16; NUM 23:18; JOS 24:9; JDG 11:25
