## Shaul (id: 8301)

SHAUL
=====

1. An alternate name for Saul, an Edomite king, in [Genesis 36:37–38](https://ref.ly/Gen36:37-Gen36:38) and [1 Chronicles 1:48–49](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:48-1Chr1:49). *See* Saul \#1.
2. The son of Simeon by a Canaanite woman ([Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10); [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15); [1 Chronicles 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24)). He was the leader of the Shaulite family ([Numbers 26:13](https://ref.ly/Num26:13)).
3. Uzziah’s son ([1 Chronicles 6:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:24)). He was a Kohathite from the tribe of Levi.
