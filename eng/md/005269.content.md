## Sistrum (id: 8533)

SISTRUM
=======

An ancient percussion instrument. A sistrum had a thin metal frame with many metal rods or loops that made jingling sounds when shaken. In [2 Samuel 6:5](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:5), this instrument is translated as "castanets" in the New Living Translation.

*See* Musical Instruments (Mena anim; Shalishim).
