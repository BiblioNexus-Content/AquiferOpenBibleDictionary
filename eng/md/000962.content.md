## Bichri, Bichrite, Bicri (id: 4232)

BICHRI, BICHRITE, BICRI
=======================

The father of Sheba from the tribe of Benjamin. Sheba led a revolt against King David ([2 Samuel 20:1–22](https://ref.ly/2Sam20:1-2Sam20:22)). The descendants of Bichri were the Bicrites ([2 Samuel 20:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam20:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 20:14; 2SA 20:1–2SA 20:22
