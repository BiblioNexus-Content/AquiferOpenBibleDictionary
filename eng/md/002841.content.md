## Israel (Person) (id: 6094)

ISRAEL (Person)
===============

Name meaning “one who struggles with God” or “God struggles” ([Gn 32:28](https://ref.ly/Gen32:28), nlt mg). It was given to Isaac’s son Jacob and to his descendants ([35:9–12](https://ref.ly/Gen35:9-Gen35:12); cf. [Dt 6:1–4](https://ref.ly/Deut6:1-Deut6:4)). *See* Jacob \#1; Israel, History of.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 32:28; GEN 35:9–GEN 35:12; DEU 6:1–DEU 6:4
