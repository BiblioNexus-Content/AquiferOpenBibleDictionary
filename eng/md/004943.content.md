## Secu (id: 8207)

SECU
====

Town or topographical landmark where Saul stopped to ask the whereabouts of Samuel and David, located between Gibeah and Ramah. It was especially noted for its large well—a natural place to go for information ([1 Sm 19:22](https://ref.ly/1Sam19:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 19:22
