## Nobah (Person) (id: 7415)

NOBAH (Person)
==============

A man from the tribe of Manasseh. He conquered the town of Kenath, which was located east of the Jordan River. After capturing the town, he renamed it after himself, calling it Nobah ([Numbers 32:42](https://ref.ly/Num32:42)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 32:42
