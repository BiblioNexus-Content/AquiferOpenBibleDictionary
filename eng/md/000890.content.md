## Beth-Barah (id: 4161)

BETH\-BARAH
===========

A place where warriors from the tribe of Ephraim, under Gideon's leadership, tried to block the retreat of the Midianites over the Jordan River ([Judges 7:24](https://ref.ly/Judg7:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 7:24
