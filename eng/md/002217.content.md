## Ginnethon (id: 5474)

GINNETHON
=========

1\. Priest who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant during the postexilic period ([Neh 10:6](https://ref.ly/Neh10:6)).

2\. Priest and head of Meshullam’s household during the postexilic days of Joiakim the high priest ([Neh 12:16](https://ref.ly/Neh12:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 10:6; NEH 12:16
