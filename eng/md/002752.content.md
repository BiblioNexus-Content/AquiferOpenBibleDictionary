## Imlah (id: 6002)

IMLAH
=====

Father of Michaiah, a prophet during King Ahab’s reign, whom the king despised for speaking the truth ([1 Kgs 22:8–9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:8-1Kgs22:9); [2 Chr 18:7–8](https://ref.ly/2Chr18:7-2Chr18:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 22:8–1KI 22:9; 2CH 18:7–2CH 18:8
