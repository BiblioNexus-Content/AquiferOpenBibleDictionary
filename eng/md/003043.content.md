## Jehudijah (id: 6294)

JEHUDIJAH\*
===========

Not a proper name; kjv mistranslation for “Jewish,” a descriptive term distinguishing Mered’s Jewish wife from his other wife, who was an Egyptian princess ([1 Chr 4:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:18
