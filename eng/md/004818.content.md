## Sabeans (id: 8083)

SABEANS
=======

Inhabitants of Sheba (Saba), a country in southwest Arabia. The Sabeans were noted as men of stature ([Is 45:14](https://ref.ly/Isa45:14)) and for their murder and theft of Job’s servants and property ([Jb 1:15](https://ref.ly/Job1:15)).

*See also* Sheba (Place) \#2.
