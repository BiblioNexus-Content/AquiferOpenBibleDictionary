## Upper Gate (id: 8970)

UPPER GATE
==========

One of the gates leading to the temple mount in Jerusalem. It was built by Jotham ([2 Kgs 15:35](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:35); [2 Chr 27:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr27:3)) and served as the principal access between the royal palace and the temple area ([2 Chr 23:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:20); [Ez 9:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek9:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 15:35; 2CH 23:20; 2CH 27:3; EZK 9:2
