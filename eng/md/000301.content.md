## Amminadab (id: 3561)

AMMINADAB
=========

1. The father of Elisheba, who was Aaron’s wife ([Exodus 6:23](https://ref.ly/Exod6:23)). Amminadab was also the father of Nahshon, Judah’s tribal leader in the wilderness ([Numbers 1:7](https://ref.ly/Num1:7); [2:3](https://ref.ly/Num2:3); [7:12, 17](https://ref.ly/Num7:12,Num7:17); [10:14](https://ref.ly/Num10:14); [1 Chronicles 2:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:10)). Amminadab is listed in the list of David's ancestors ([Ruth 4:18–22](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:18-Ruth4:22)). Later, he is listed in the list of Jesus Christ's ancestors ([Matthew 1:4](https://ref.ly/Matt1:4); [Luke 3:33](https://ref.ly/Luke3:33)). *See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.
2. An alternate name for Izhar, one of Kohath’s sons ([1 Chronicles 6:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:22)). *See* Izhar \#1.
3. A Levite contemporary of King David who helped bring the ark of the Lord to Jerusalem ([1 Chronicles 15:1–4, 10–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:1-1Chr15:4,1Chr15:10-1Chr15:11)).
