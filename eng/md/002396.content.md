## Hanniel (id: 5650)

HANNIEL
=======

1. A son of Ephod and leader of the tribe of Manasseh. He represented his tribe when Moses portioned land to Israel ([Numbers 34:23](https://ref.ly/Num34:23)).
2. A son of Ulla and warrior in the tribe of Asher ([1 Chronicles 7:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:39)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:23; 1CH 7:39
