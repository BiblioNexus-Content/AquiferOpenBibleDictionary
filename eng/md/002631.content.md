## Holon (id: 5880)

HOLON
=====

1\. Town in the uplands of Judah’s inheritance ([Jos 15:51](https://ref.ly/Josh15:51)) given to the Levites ([21:15](https://ref.ly/Josh21:15)). In [1 Chronicles 6:58](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:58), the town is called Hilen. Holon may be Khirbet ‘Alin, northwest of Hebron. *See* Levitical Cities.

2\. City near Heshbon, located in the plain of Moab ([Jer 48:21](https://ref.ly/Jer48:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:51; JOS 21:15; 1CH 6:58; JER 48:21
