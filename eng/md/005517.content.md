## Teman (Person) (id: 8776)

TEMAN (Person)
==============

One of the chiefs of the Edomites and Eliphaz’s firstborn son ([Gn 36:11, 15, 42](https://ref.ly/Gen36:11,Gen36:15,Gen36:42); [1 Chr 1:36, 53](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:36,1Chr1:53)). He was likely either the founder or a chief of the Edomite city of Teman.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:11; GEN 36:15; GEN 36:42; 1CH 1:36; 1CH 1:53
