## Bezai (id: 4219)

BEZAI
=====

1. An ancestor of a group of people who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile in Babylon ([Ezra 2:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:17); [Nehemiah 7:23](https://ref.ly/Neh7:23)).
2. A political leader who signed Ezra’s promise of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 10:18](https://ref.ly/Neh10:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:17; NEH 7:23; NEH 10:18
