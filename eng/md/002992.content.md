## Jearim, Mount (id: 6247)

JEARIM, Mount
=============

Mountain on the northwest border of Judah’s territory between Beth\-shemesh and Kiriath\-jearim. Kesalon was located on its northern slope ([Jos 15:10](https://ref.ly/Josh15:10)). It is associated with Mt Seir and Mt Ephron.

*See also* Kesalon.
