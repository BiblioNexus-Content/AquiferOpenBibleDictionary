## Methuselah (id: 7095)

METHUSELAH
==========

The son of Enoch, Lamech’s father, and the grandfather of Noah through Seth’s line ([Genesis 5:21](https://ref.ly/Gen5:21-Gen5:27)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen5:21-Gen5:27)[27](https://ref.ly/Gen5:21-Gen5:27); [1 Chronicles 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:3)). Methuselah lived 969 years. He is the oldest recorded person in the Bible. His ancestry is included in Luke’s record of Jesus's ancestors ([Luke 3:37](https://ref.ly/Luke3:37)).

*See also* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:3; LUK 3:37; GEN 5:21–GEN 5:27
