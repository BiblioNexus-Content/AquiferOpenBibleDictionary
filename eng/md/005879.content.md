## Zacharias (id: 9124)

ZACHARIAS
=========

1. The King James Version spelling of Zechariah, Jehoiada’s son, in [Matthew 23:35](https://ref.ly/Matt23:35) and [Luke 11:51](https://ref.ly/Luke11:51).

    *See* Zechariah (Person) \#14.

2. The King James Version spelling of Zechariah, John the Baptist’s father, in [Luke 1:5](https://ref.ly/Luke1:5-Luke1:67)[–](https://ref.ly/Luke1:5-Luke1:67)[67](https://ref.ly/Luke1:5-Luke1:67).

    *See* Zechariah (Person) \#31.

3. The King James Version spelling of Zechariah, a proposed name for John the Baptist, in [Luke 1:59](https://ref.ly/Luke1:59).

    *See* John the Baptist; Zechariah (Person) \#32.
