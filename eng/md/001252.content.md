## Chephar-Ammoni, Chephar-Haammonai (id: 4505)

CHEPHAR\-AMMONI, CHEPHAR\-HAAMMONAI
===================================

Alternate spellings of the name Kephar\-ammoni, the city given to Benjamin's tribe for an inheritance after Israel conquered Canaan ([Joshua 18:24](https://ref.ly/Josh18:24)).
