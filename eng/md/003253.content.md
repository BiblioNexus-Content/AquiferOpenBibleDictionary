## Kadmiel (id: 6511)

KADMIEL
=======

Head of a Levite family who returned from the exile with Zerubbabel ([Ezr 2:40](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:40); [Neh 7:43](https://ref.ly/Neh7:43); [12:8](https://ref.ly/Neh12:8)). His name appears in the list of those who supervised the temple rebuilding project ([Ezr 3:9](https://ref.ly/Ezra3:9)), participated in sealing the covenant ([Neh 10:9](https://ref.ly/Neh10:9)), and were prominent in the praise service ([9:4–5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:4-Neh9:5); [12:24](https://ref.ly/Neh12:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:40; EZR 3:9; NEH 7:43; NEH 10:9; NEH 12:8; NEH 12:24; NEH 9:4–NEH 9:5
