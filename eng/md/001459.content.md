## Cuneiform (id: 4708)

CUNEIFORM
=========

An ancient writing script that used nail or wedge\-shaped marks to cut into a writing material, usually clay, in specific patterns. Each pattern, or “sign,” represents a sound or word. The system was used by the Akkadians, Elamites, Hittites, Hurrians, and Sumerians. Ugaritic is also a cuneiform language script.
