## Cab (id: 4376)

CAB
===

A *cab* was a dry measure used in Bible times. The Jewish historian Josephus said it was about the size of a quart (about one liter). Some experts think it may have been larger (see [2 Kings 6:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs6:25)).
