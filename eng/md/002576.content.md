## Hethlon (id: 5829)

HETHLON
=======

Site mentioned by Ezekiel ([Ez 47:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:15); [48:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek48:1)) describing part of the northern boundary of the restored kingdom of Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 47:15; EZK 48:1
