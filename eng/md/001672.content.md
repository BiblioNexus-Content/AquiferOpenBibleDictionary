## Ebed (id: 4925)

EBED
====

1\. Gaal’s father ([Jgs 9:26–35](https://ref.ly/Judg9:26-Judg9:35)). Gaal led the men of Shechem in an unsuccessful revolt against Abimelech, judge of Israel.

2\. Adin’s descendant and son of Jonathan. Ebed was the head of a family that returned to Judah with Ezra after the exile ([Ezr 8:6](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 8:6; JDG 9:26–JDG 9:35
