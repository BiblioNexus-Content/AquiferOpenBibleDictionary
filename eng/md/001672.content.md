## Ebal (id: 4923)

EBAL
====

1\. Shobal’s son and descendant of Seir the Horite ([Gn 36:23](https://ref.ly/Gen36:23); [1 Chr 1:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:40)).

2\. Joktan’s son and descendant of Shem ([1 Chr 1:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:22)). He is called Obal in [Genesis 10:28](https://ref.ly/Gen10:28).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:28; GEN 36:23; 1CH 1:22; 1CH 1:40
