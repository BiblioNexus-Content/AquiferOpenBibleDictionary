## Gallio Inscription (id: 5356)

GALLIO INSCRIPTION
==================

A dated Greek inscription found in Delphi, Greece. It identifies Gallio as proconsul (governor) and establishes the time of Paul’s first visit to Corinth (compare [Acts 18:12–17](https://ref.ly/Acts18:12-Acts18:17)). 

*See* Timeline of the Bible (New Testament); Inscriptions.
