## Beth-Haggan (id: 4178)

BETH\-HAGGAN
============

A town where King Ahaziah of Judah fled for his life from Jehu of Israel ([2 Kings 9:27](https://ref.ly/2Kgs9:27)). Beth\-haggan was probably the same as En\-gannim. It has been identified as modern Jenin.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 9:27
