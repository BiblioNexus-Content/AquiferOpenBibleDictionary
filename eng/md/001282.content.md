## Chitlish (id: 4535)

CHITLISH
========

A city given to the tribe of Judah ([Joshua 15:40](https://ref.ly/Josh15:40)). Chitlish was located in the lowland area of Judah, also known as the Shephelah. We do not know the exact location of Chitlish.
