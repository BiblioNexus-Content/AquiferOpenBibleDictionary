## Baaseiah (id: 3953)

BAASEIAH
========

Malchijah’s son and an ancestor of the temple musician Asaph ([1 Chronicles 6:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:40)). Baaseiah may be a mistake, where a copyist intended to write the common name Maaseiah ([1 Chronicles 15:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:40; 1CH 15:18
