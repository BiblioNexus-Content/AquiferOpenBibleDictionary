## Joshbekashah (id: 6455)

JOSHBEKASHAH
============

Heman’s son and head of the 17th of 24 divisions of priestly musicians for ministry in the sanctuary during David’s reign ([1 Chr 25:4, 24](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4,1Chr25:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:24
