## Jephunneh (id: 6307)

JEPHUNNEH
=========

1. The father of Caleb. Caleb was one of the 12 spies sent by Moses to explore the land of Canaan ([Numbers 13:6](https://ref.ly/Num13:6); [14:6](https://ref.ly/Num14:6); [26:65](https://ref.ly/Num26:65); [1 Chronicles 4:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:15); [6:56](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:56)). The Bible refers to Jephunneh as a Judahite and a Kenizzite ([Joshua 14:6](https://ref.ly/Josh14:6)).
2. A son of Jether from the tribe of Asher ([1 Chronicles 7:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:38)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:6; NUM 14:6; NUM 26:65; JOS 14:6; 1CH 4:15; 1CH 6:56; 1CH 7:38
