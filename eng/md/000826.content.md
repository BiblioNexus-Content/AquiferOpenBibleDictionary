## Ben (Noun) (id: 4115)

BEN (Noun)
==========

A Hebrew word used at the beginning of names to describe a relationship. It literally means “son,” and it is used 4,850 times in the Old Testament. In Aramaic, it is *bar* (see [Matthew 16:17](https://ref.ly/Matt16:17)).
