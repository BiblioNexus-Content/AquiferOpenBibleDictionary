## Bakbakkar (id: 3970)

BAKBAKKAR
=========

A Levite who returned to Jerusalem from exile in Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:15)). His name is missing in another list in [Nehemiah 11:17\.](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17) It might be the same as Bakbukiah.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:15; NEH 11:17
