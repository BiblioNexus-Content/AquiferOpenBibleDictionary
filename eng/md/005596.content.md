## Timna (Person) (id: 8848)

TIMNA (Person)
==============

1\. Daughter of Seir, sister of Lotan, and a native Horite inhabitant of Edom ([Gn 36:22](https://ref.ly/Gen36:22); [1 Chr 1:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:39)). She was a concubine to Eliphaz, Esau’s son, and the mother of Amalek ([Gn 36:12](https://ref.ly/Gen36:12)).

2\. Edomite chief ([Gn 36:40](https://ref.ly/Gen36:40); [1 Chr 1:36, 51](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:36,1Chr1:51)). This name may refer either to the name of the ancestor of the Edomite clan or to the geographical area occupied by the clan.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:12; GEN 36:22; GEN 36:40; 1CH 1:36; 1CH 1:39; 1CH 1:51
