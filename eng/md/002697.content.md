## Hupham, Huphamite (id: 5946)

HUPHAM, HUPHAMITE
=================

Benjamite and the founder of the Huphamite family ([Nm 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39)); he is perhaps identifiable with Huppim ([Gn 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21); [1 Chr 7:12, 15](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12,1Chr7:15)) and Huram ([1 Chr 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:5)).

*See also* Huppim; Huram \#1.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:39; 1CH 7:12; 1CH 7:15; 1CH 8:5
