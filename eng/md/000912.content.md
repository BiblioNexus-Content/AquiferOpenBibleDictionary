## Beth-Jeshimoth (id: 4183)

BETH\-JESHIMOTH
===============

A city given to the tribe of Reuben ([Joshua 13:20](https://ref.ly/Josh13:20)), which is later described as a Moabite town ([Ezekiel 25:9](https://ref.ly/Ezek25:9)). Before Israel’s conquest of the promised land, they made camp along the Jordan from Beth\-jeshimoth to Abel\-shittim ([Numbers 33:49](https://ref.ly/Num33:49)). The city is usually identified with Tell el\-Azeimeh.

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
