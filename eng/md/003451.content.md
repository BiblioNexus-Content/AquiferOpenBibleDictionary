## Lebo-Hamath (id: 6709)

LEBO\-HAMATH
============

Town on the Orontes River below Riblah; perhaps the correct reading for the phrase “entrance of Hamath” in numerous OT passages ([Nm 34:8](https://ref.ly/Num34:8); [1 Kgs 8:65](https://ref.ly/1Kgs8:65); [2 Kgs 14:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:25); [Ez 47:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:15)). *See* Hamath \#1.
