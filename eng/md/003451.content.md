## Lebonah (id: 6710)

LEBONAH
=======

Town located between Shiloh and Shechem ([Jgs 21:19](https://ref.ly/Judg21:19)). It is usually identified with modern Lubban, about three miles (4\.8 kilometers) northwest of Shiloh.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 21:19
