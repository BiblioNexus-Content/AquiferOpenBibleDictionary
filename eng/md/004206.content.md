## Ohad (id: 7465)

OHAD
====

Simeon’s son ([Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10); [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15)). His name does not appear in the list of [Numbers 26:12–14](https://ref.ly/Num26:12-Num26:14).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:10; EXO 6:15; NUM 26:12–NUM 26:14
