## Jehdeiah (id: 6265)

JEHDEIAH
========

1\. Shubael’s son, a Levite in David’s time ([1 Chr 24:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:20)).

2\. Royal steward from Meronoth who was in charge of David’s donkeys ([1 Chr 27:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:30)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:20; 1CH 27:30
