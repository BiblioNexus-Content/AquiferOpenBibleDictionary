## Ramoth (Place) (id: 7935)

RAMOTH (Place)
==============

1\. Abbreviated form of Ramoth\-gilead. *See* Ramoth\-gilead.

2\. Alternate name for Baalath\-beer, a city in the Negev, in [1 Samuel 30:27](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:27). *See* Baalath\-beer.

3\. Alternate name (or textual alteration) of Jarmuth, a Levitical city, in [1 Chronicles 6:73](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:73). *See* Jarmuth \#2.
