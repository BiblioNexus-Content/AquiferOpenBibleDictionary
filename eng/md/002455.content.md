## Hasupha (id: 5709)

HASUPHA
=======

Ancestor of a group of temple assistants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Ezr 2:43](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:43); [Neh 7:46](https://ref.ly/Neh7:46)). He is perhaps the same person as Gishpa in [Nehemiah 11:21](https://ref.ly/Neh11:21). *See* Gishpa, Gispa.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:43; NEH 7:46; NEH 11:21
* **Associated Articles:** Gishpa, Gispa (ID: `5478@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
