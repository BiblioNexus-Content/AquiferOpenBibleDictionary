## Ezel (id: 5217)

EZEL\*
======

Hebrew word designating a stone where Jonathan and David met prior to David’s departure from the court of Saul ([1 Sm 20:19](https://ref.ly/1Sam20:19), nlt mg).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 20:19
