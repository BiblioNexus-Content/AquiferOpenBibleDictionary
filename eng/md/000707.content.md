## Balah (id: 3977)

BALAH
=====

A city in southern Canaan ([Joshua 19:3](https://ref.ly/Josh19:3)), probably identical with Baalah ([Joshua 15:29](https://ref.ly/Josh15:29)) and Bilhah ([1 Chronicles 4:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:29; JOS 19:3; 1CH 4:29
