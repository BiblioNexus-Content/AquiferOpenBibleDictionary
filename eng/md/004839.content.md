## Salem (id: 8104)

SALEM
=====

City from which the priest\-king Melchizedek came ([Gn 14:18](https://ref.ly/Gen14:18); [Ps 76:2](https://ref.ly/Ps76:2); [Heb 7:1–2](https://ref.ly/Heb7:1-Heb7:2)). Salem is believed to be an ancient name of Jerusalem. *See* Jerusalem.
