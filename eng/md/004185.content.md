## Oak of the Pillar (id: 7443)

OAK OF THE PILLAR
=================

Sacred meeting place in Shechem where the citizens of that city and the inhabitants of Beth\-millo made Abimelech king ([Jgs 9:6](https://ref.ly/Judg9:6), asv, nas). It is perhaps identifiable with the Oak of Moreh. *See* Moreh, Oak of; Plain of the Pillar.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 9:6
* **Associated Articles:** Moreh, Oak of (ID: `7203@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Plain of the Pillar (ID: `7759@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
