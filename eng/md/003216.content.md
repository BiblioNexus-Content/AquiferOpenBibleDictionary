## Juda (Person) (id: 6480)

JUDA (Person)
=============

1. An alternate spelling of Judas in the King James Version. Judas is Jesus’s brother in [Mark 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3). Judas is also called Jude in [Jude 1](https://ref.ly/Jude1:1-Jude1:25). *See* Jude (Person).
2. An alternate King James Version spelling of Joda, Joanan’s son, in [Luke 3:26](https://ref.ly/Luke3:26). *See* Joda.
3. An alternate King James Version spelling of Judah, Joseph’s son, in [Luke 3:30](https://ref.ly/Luke3:30). *See* Judah (Person) \#8.
4. An alternate King James Version spelling of Judah, Jacob’s son, in [Luke 3:33](https://ref.ly/Luke3:33). *See* Judah (Person) \#1.
5. An alternate King James Version spelling for Judah’s tribe ([Hebrews 7:14](https://ref.ly/Heb7:14); [Revelation 5:5](https://ref.ly/Rev5:5); [7:5](https://ref.ly/Rev7:5)). *See* Judah, Tribe of.
