## Baal-Hamon (id: 3933)

BAAL\-HAMON
===========

The site of a vineyard owned by Solomon and rented by local farmers ([Song of Solomon 8:11](https://ref.ly/Song8:11)). The surrounding text suggests that the vineyard produced excellent grapes.

* **Associated Passages:** SNG 8:11
