## Tambourine (id: 8729)

TAMBOURINE
==========

Percussion instrument consisting of a shallow one\-headed drum with small metal discs attached on the side, which jingle when the instrument is tapped or shaken ([1 Sm 10:5](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:5)). *See* Musical Instruments (Toph).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 10:5
