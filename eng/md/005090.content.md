## Shemer (id: 8352)

SHEMER
======

1\. Owner of the hill of Samaria, which Omri, king of Israel, bought as the site of his new capital city and named after Shemer ([1 Kgs 16:24](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:24)).

2\. Merarite Levite, Mahli’s son and the father of Bani; he was a temple singer during David’s reign ([1 Chr 6:46](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:46)).

3\. Asherite, Heber’s son and a leader among his people ([1 Chr 7:34](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:34)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 16:24; 1CH 6:46; 1CH 7:34
