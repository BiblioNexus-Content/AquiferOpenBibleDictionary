## Ahio (id: 3453)

AHIO
====

1. Abinadab’s son. Ahio and his brother Uzzah drove the ox cart carrying the ark of the covenant to its new home in Jerusalem ([2 Samuel 6:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:3-2Sam6:4)–[4](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:3-2Sam6:4); [1 Chronicles 13:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:7)).
2. Elpaal’s son from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:14)).
3. The son of Jeiel and his wife Maacah. Ahio was either the brother or uncle of Kish, Saul’s father ([1 Chronicles 8:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:31); [9:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:36-1Chr9:37)–[37](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:36-1Chr9:37)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 8:14; 1CH 8:31; 1CH 13:7; 2SA 6:3–2SA 6:4; 1CH 9:36–1CH 9:37
