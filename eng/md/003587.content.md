## Madmenah (id: 6844)

MADMENAH
========

Benjamite town positioned north of Jerusalem along the route taken by the Assyrian army during Sennacherib’s military incursion into Judah (c. 701 BC) against King Hezekiah (715–686 BC) and the Holy City ([Is 10:31](https://ref.ly/Isa10:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 10:31
