## Jaazer (id: 6142)

JAAZER
======

The King James Version alternate form of Jazer. Jazer was an Amorite city in Gilead ([Numbers 21:32](https://ref.ly/Num21:32) and [32:35](https://ref.ly/Num32:35)).
