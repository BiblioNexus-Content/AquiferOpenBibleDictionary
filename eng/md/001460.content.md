## Cup (id: 4712)

CUP
===

The word may refer to the container or to what it holds. It can be used literally or figuratively.

1\. A small drinking container made of different materials (leather, metal, or pottery) and in different sizes and designs.

2\. A figure of speech to represent someone’s share or involvement in something. It is used in the Bible to discuss:

* Consolation ([Jeremiah 16:7](https://ref.ly/Jer16:7))
* Demons ([1 Corinthians 10:21](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:21))
* Divination ([Genesis 44:2, 5](https://ref.ly/Gen44:2,Gen44:5))
* Drunkenness ([Proverbs 23:31](https://ref.ly/Prov23:31))
* Immorality ([Revelation 17:4](https://ref.ly/Rev17:4); [18:6](https://ref.ly/Rev18:6))
* Inheritance ([Psalm 16:5](https://ref.ly/Ps16:5))
* Judgment ([Psalms 11:6](https://ref.ly/Ps11:6); [75:8](https://ref.ly/Ps75:8); [Isaiah 51:17, 22](https://ref.ly/Isa51:17,Isa51:22); [Jeremiah 49:12](https://ref.ly/Jer49:12); [Ezekiel 23:33](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:33); [Zechariah 12:2](https://ref.ly/Zech12:2); [Revelation 14:10](https://ref.ly/Rev14:10); [16:19](https://ref.ly/Rev16:19); [18:6](https://ref.ly/Rev18:6))
* The Lord ([1 Corinthians 10:21](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:21))
* Prosperity or blessing ([Psalm 23:5](https://ref.ly/Ps23:5))
* Salvation ([Psalm 116:13](https://ref.ly/Ps116:13))
* Suffering ([Matthew 20:22](https://ref.ly/Matt20:22); [26:39](https://ref.ly/Matt26:39); [Mark 10:39](https://ref.ly/Mark10:39); [14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:36); [Luke 22:42](https://ref.ly/Luke22:42); [John 18:11](https://ref.ly/John18:11))
* Thanksgiving ([1 Corinthians 10:16](https://ref.ly/1Cor10:16))
