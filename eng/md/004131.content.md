## Nezib (id: 7391)

NEZIB
=====

One of the cities in the lowland allotted to Judah for an inheritance ([Jos 15:43](https://ref.ly/Josh15:43)). Its site is identified with modern Khirbet Beit Nesib, east of Lachish.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:43
