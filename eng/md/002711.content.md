## Hushim (id: 5960)

HUSHIM
======

1\. Dan’s son ([Gn 46:23](https://ref.ly/Gen46:23)), alternately called Shuham in [Numbers 26:42](https://ref.ly/Num26:42), where he is mentioned as the founder of the Shuhamite family.

2\. Benjamite descendant of Aher ([1 Chr 7:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12)).

3\. One of the Benjamite Shaharaim’s three wives ([1 Chr 8:8–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:8-1Chr8:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:23; NUM 26:42; 1CH 7:12; 1CH 8:8–1CH 8:11
