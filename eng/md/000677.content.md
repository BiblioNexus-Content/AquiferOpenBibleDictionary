## Baal-Tamar (id: 3947)

BAAL\-TAMAR
===========

A place between Gibeah and Bethel in the tribal land of Benjamin, north of Jerusalem. The 11 other Israelite tribes rallied their forces there in a final victorious battle against Benjamin over crimes committed in the city of Gibeah ([Judges 20:33](https://ref.ly/Judg20:33)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 20:33
