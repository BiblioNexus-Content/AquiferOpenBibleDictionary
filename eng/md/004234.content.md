## On (Person) (id: 7501)

ON (Person)
===========

The son of Peleth. He was from the tribe of Reuben. On joined the rebellion of Korah against Moses and Aaron in the wilderness ([Numbers 16:1](https://ref.ly/Num16:1)). This rebellion challenged the leadership of Moses and Aaron.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 16:1
