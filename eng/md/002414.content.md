## Harhas (id: 5668)

HARHAS
======

Shallum’s grandfather. Shallum’s wife was Huldah the prophetess ([2 Kgs 22:14](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:14); spelled “Hasrah” in [2 Chr 34:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:22)), who delivered an oracle for Josiah after the discovery of the Book of the Law by the high priest Hilkiah.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 22:14; 2CH 34:22
