## Padon (id: 7548)

PADON
=====

Forefather of a family of temple servants who returned with Zerubbabel to Palestine following the Babylonian captivity ([Ezr 2:44](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:44); [Neh 7:47](https://ref.ly/Neh7:47)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:44; NEH 7:47
