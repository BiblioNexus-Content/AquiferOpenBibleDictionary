## Ashtoreth (id: 3811)

ASHTORETH
=========

A pagan mother\-goddess who was widely worshiped throughout the ancient Near East ([1 Kings 11:5, 33](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:5,1Kgs11:33); [2 Kings 23:13](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:13)). She was also called Astarte. 

*See* Canaanite Deities and Religion.
