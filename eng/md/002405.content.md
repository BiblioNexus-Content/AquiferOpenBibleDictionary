## Haran (Person) (id: 5658)

HARAN (Person)
==============

1\. Terah’s son, brother of Abraham, and the father of Lot ([Gn 11:26–31](https://ref.ly/Gen11:26-Gen11:31)).

2\. Caleb’s son by his concubine Ephah, a member of Judah’s tribe and the father of Gazez ([1 Chr 2:46](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:46)).

3\. Shimei’s son, a member of the Gershonite division of Levi’s tribe ([1 Chr 23:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:46; 1CH 23:9; GEN 11:26–GEN 11:31
