## Publius (id: 7850)

PUBLIUS
=======

Publius was an important official who lived on the island of Malta. The Bible mentions him in [Acts 28:7–8](https://ref.ly/Acts28:7-Acts28:8). Paul and his companions were shipwrecked on Malta during their journey to Rome. Publius welcomed them and gave them a place to stay for a short time. Publius had a father who was very sick. During Paul's visit, he healed Publius's father.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 28:7–ACT 28:8
