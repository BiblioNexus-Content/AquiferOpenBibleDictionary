## Hosah (Person) (id: 5914)

HOSAH (Person)
==============

Merarite Levite who guarded the gate of the tent where the sacred ark was kept ([1 Chr 16:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:38)) when David brought it to Jerusalem. His gatekeeping responsibilities were shared by his sons ([26:10–16](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:10-1Chr26:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 16:38; 1CH 26:10–1CH 26:16
