## Rabboni (id: 7896)

RABBONI
=======

A variation on the title of respect, *rabbi, which means* “my great one” or “my superior one.” This title was used to address teachers. Rabboni is used only in [Mark 10:51](https://ref.ly/Mark10:51) and [John 20:16](https://ref.ly/John20:16). 
  
*See* Rabbi.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 10:51; JHN 20:16
