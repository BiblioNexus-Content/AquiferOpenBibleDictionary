## Jediael (id: 6258)

JEDIAEL
=======

1\. Benjamin’s son ([1 Chr 7:6, 10–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:6,1Chr7:10-1Chr7:11)), whose descendants were warriors, numbering 17,200 by David’s time. Some suggest that he is identifiable with Ashbel, also Benjamin’s son ([Gn 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)).

*See also* Ashbel, Ashbelite.

2\. Shimri’s son, listed among David’s mighty men ([1 Chr 11:45](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:45)).

3\. One who deserted Saul to join David at Ziklag ([1 Chr 12:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:20)). He may be the same as \#2 above.

4\. Member of the Levitical family of Korah, appointed a doorkeeper of the temple during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; 1CH 7:6; 1CH 11:45; 1CH 12:20; 1CH 26:2; 1CH 7:10–1CH 7:11
* **Associated Articles:** Ashbel, Ashbelite (ID: `3793@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
