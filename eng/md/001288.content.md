## Choinix (id: 4540)

CHOINIX\*
=========

Dry commodity measure equivalent to a little more than a quart (one liter) ([Rv 6:6](https://ref.ly/Rev6:6); see nlt mg). The rsv and niv read “quart.” *See* Weights and Measures.
