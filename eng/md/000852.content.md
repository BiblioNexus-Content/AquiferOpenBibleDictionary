## Bera (id: 4127)

BERA
====

The ruler of Sodom during the days of Abraham and Lot. Bera was one of five Canaanite city kings who failed to rebel against King Chedorlaomer of Elam and his three allies ([Genesis 14:2](https://ref.ly/Gen14:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:2
