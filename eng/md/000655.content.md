## Azzan (id: 3925)

AZZAN
=====

Paltiel’s father and a member of the tribe of Issachar. Paltiel was chosen to help Eleazar and Joshua in dividing the promised land ([Numbers 34:26](https://ref.ly/Num34:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:26
