## Zior (id: 9235)

ZIOR
====

One of the cities of the hill country allotted to Judah’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 15:54](https://ref.ly/Josh15:54)). Since it is associated with Hebron in the text, Zior is likely identified with modern Sa’ir, the traditional location for Esau’s tomb.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:54
