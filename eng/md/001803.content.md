## Eltolad (id: 5056)

ELTOLAD
=======

Town assigned to Simeon’s tribe in the southern portion of Judah’s inheritance ([Jos 15:30](https://ref.ly/Josh15:30); [19:4](https://ref.ly/Josh19:4)). It is alternately called Tolad in [1 Chronicles 4:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:29).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:30; JOS 19:4; 1CH 4:29
