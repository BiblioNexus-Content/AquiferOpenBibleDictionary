## Berothah, Berothai (id: 4140)

BEROTHAH, BEROTHAI
==================

A city between Damascus and Hamath that the prophet Ezekiel said was on the northern border of the restored Israel ([Ezekiel 47:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:16)). Berothah is probably the same as Berothai, a city captured by David ([2 Samuel 8:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:8); called Cun in [1 Chronicles 18:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:8; 1CH 18:8; EZK 47:16
