## Arcturus (id: 3723)

ARCTURUS
========

The constellation Ursa Major (the Great Bear), referred to in [Job 9:9](https://ref.ly/Job9:9) and [38:32](https://ref.ly/Job38:32) (in the King James Version). It is mentioned in connection with the constellation Orion and the Pleiades. *See* Astronomy.  
  
\[Editor's note: Arcturus is actually a star in the constellation Boötes, which is near the constellation Ursa Major.]

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 9:9; JOB 38:32
* **Associated Articles:** Astronomy (ID: `3846@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
