## Flour (id: 5281)

FLOUR
=====

A fine, powdery substance made by grinding the inner kernels of wheat. In the Bible, flour was used in baking and also for cereal offerings ([Leviticus 2](https://ref.ly/Lev2:1-Lev2:16)). 

*See* Food and Food Preparation.
