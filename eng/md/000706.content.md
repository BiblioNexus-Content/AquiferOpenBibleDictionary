## Baladan (id: 3976)

BALADAN
=======

The king of Babylon and father of Merodach\-baladan. Baladan’s son sent letters and a gift to King Hezekiah of Judah after Hezekiah’s recovery from a serious illness ([2 Kings 20:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:12); [Isaiah 39:1](https://ref.ly/Isa39:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 20:12; ISA 39:1
