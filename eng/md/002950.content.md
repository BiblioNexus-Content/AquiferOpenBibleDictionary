## Jamin (id: 6204)

JAMIN
=====

1. Simeon’s son ([Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10); [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15); [1 Chronicles 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24)) and founder of the Jaminite family ([Numbers 26:12](https://ref.ly/Num26:12)).
2. Ram’s son from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 2:27](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:27)).
3. One of the men (perhaps a Levite) who taught and explained the law to the people following Ezra’s public reading ([Nehemiah 8:7](https://ref.ly/Neh8:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:10; EXO 6:15; NUM 26:12; 1CH 2:27; 1CH 4:24; NEH 8:7
