## Armoni (id: 3749)

ARMONI
======

One of King Saul’s two sons by his concubine Rizpah. David handed Armoni, along with six more of Saul's sons, over to the Gibeonites. Armoni and his brothers were killed to avenge Saul’s slaughter of the Gibeonites ([2 Samuel 21:1, 8–9](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:1,2Sam21:8-2Sam21:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 21:1; 2SA 21:8–2SA 21:9
