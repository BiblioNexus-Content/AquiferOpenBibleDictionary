## Nepheg (id: 7355)

NEPHEG
======

1. A Levite of the family of Kohath and the second of Izhar’s three sons ([Exodus 6:21](https://ref.ly/Exod6:21)).
2. David’s son born to him during his reign in Jerusalem ([2 Samuel 5:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:15); [1 Chronicles 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:7); [14:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:21; 2SA 5:15; 1CH 3:7; 1CH 14:6
