## Cephas (id: 4459)

CEPHAS
======

The Aramaic translation for the name of the apostle Simon Peter in [John 1:42](https://ref.ly/John1:42); [1 Corinthians 1:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:12); and [Galatians 1:18](https://ref.ly/Gal1:18). The name means "rock" or "stone." *See* Simon Peter.

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 1:42; 1CO 1:12; GAL 1:18
