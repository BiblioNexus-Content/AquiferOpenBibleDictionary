## Most High (id: 7216)

MOST HIGH
=========

An ancient name for God used in both the Old and New Testaments ([Psalm 21:7](https://ref.ly/Ps21:7); [Acts 7:48](https://ref.ly/Acts7:48)). This title emphasizes God's supreme authority and position above all other powers.

*See* God, Names of.
