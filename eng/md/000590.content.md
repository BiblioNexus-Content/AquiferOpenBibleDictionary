## Athach (id: 3858)

ATHACH
======

A city to which David sent part of his loot after a victory over the Amalekites ([1 Samuel 30:30](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:30)). It was probably near Ziklag in southern Judah

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 30:30
