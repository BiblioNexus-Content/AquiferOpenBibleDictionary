## Pochereth-hazzebaim, Pochereth of Zebaim (id: 7766)

POCHERETH\-HAZZEBAIM, POCHERETH OF ZEBAIM
=========================================

The head of a family of Solomon’s servants who returned from the exile in Babylon with Zerubbabel ([Ezra 2:57](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:57); [Nehemiah 7:59](https://ref.ly/Neh7:59)). Some translations, like the King James Version, write the name as “Pochereth of Zebaim,” treating Zebaim as a place name.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:57; NEH 7:59
