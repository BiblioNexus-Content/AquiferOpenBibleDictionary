## Judith (Person) (id: 6497)

JUDITH (Person)
===============

1. A daughter of Beeri the Hittite and one of the wives of Esau ([Genesis 26:34](https://ref.ly/Gen26:34)). [Genesis 36:2](https://ref.ly/Gen36:2)calls her “Oholibamah.“

    *See* Oholibamah.

2. The main character in the book of Judith. She was a brave, beautiful Judean widow from Bethulia. She beheaded the Assyrian general Holofernes and saved her people from destruction.

    *See* Judith, Book of.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 26:34; GEN 36:2
