## Terah (Place) (id: 8789)

TERAH (Place)
=============

One of the places where the Israelites stopped during their wilderness wanderings. It was located between Tahath and Mithcah ([Numbers 33:27–28](https://ref.ly/Num33:27-Num33:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:27–NUM 33:28
