## Jorkeam, Jorkoam (id: 6441)

JORKEAM, JORKOAM\*
==================

Identified with Raham, a descendant of Judah through Caleb’s line ([1 Chr 2:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:44); kjv “Jorkoam”), the name should perhaps be understood as a place\-name and be identified with Jokdeam ([Jos 15:56](https://ref.ly/Josh15:56)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:56; 1CH 2:44
