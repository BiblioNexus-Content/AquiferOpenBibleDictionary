## Hadoram (id: 5578)

HADORAM
=======

1\. Joktan’s fifth son; Hadoram and his brothers were the sixth generation from Noah ([Gn 10:27](https://ref.ly/Gen10:27); [1 Chr 1:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:21)).

2\. Alternate spelling of Joram in [1 Chronicles 18:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:10) (kjv). *See* Joram \#1.

3\. Alternate spelling of Adoniram in [2 Chronicles 10:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr10:18) (kjv). *See* Adoniram.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:27; 1CH 1:21; 1CH 18:10; 2CH 10:18
* **Associated Articles:** Adoniram (ID: `3393@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Joram (ID: `6438@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
