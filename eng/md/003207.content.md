## Jotbah (id: 6464)

JOTBAH
======

Hometown of Haruz, the father of Meshullemeth, who was the mother of King Amon of Judah ([2 Kgs 21:19](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:19)). Its location is uncertain; however, some identify it with the town later named Jotapata by the Romans, situated six miles (9\.7 kilometers) north of Sepphoris (modern Khirbet Jefat).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 21:19
