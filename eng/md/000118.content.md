## Adiel (id: 3378)

ADIEL
=====

1. A prince from the tribe of Simeon who led a group of Simeonites to the entrance of Gedor to find land for their flocks ([1 Chronicles 4:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:36-1Chr4:39)–[39](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:36-1Chr4:39)).
2. An ancestor of Maasai, a priest of Israel who was one of the first to return to Palestine after the exile ([1 Chronicles 9:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:12)).
3. An ancestor of Azmaveth. Azmaveth was in charge of King David’s treasuries ([1 Chronicles 27:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:12; 1CH 27:25; 1CH 4:36–1CH 4:39
