## Shuhite (id: 8459)

SHUHITE
=======

Arab tribe, apparently descended from Shuah, the son of Abraham by Keturah. They were located near the land of Uz. Bildad, one of the three friends of Job, is identified as a Shuhite ([Jb 2:11](https://ref.ly/Job2:11); [8:1](https://ref.ly/Job8:1); [18:1](https://ref.ly/Job18:1); [25:1](https://ref.ly/Job25:1); [42:9](https://ref.ly/Job42:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 2:11; JOB 8:1; JOB 18:1; JOB 25:1; JOB 42:9
