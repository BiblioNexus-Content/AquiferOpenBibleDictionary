## Callisthenes (id: 4396)

CALLISTHENES
============

Callisthenes was a Syrian in the army of General Nicanor. He set fire to the temple gates during the persecution of the Jews by Antiochus Epiphanes ([2 Maccabees 8:33](https://ref.ly/2Macc8:33)). In 165 BC, Nicanor’s army was defeated. After the battle, the Jews punished Callisthenes and others who had burned the temple gates. They were burned to death.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 8:33
