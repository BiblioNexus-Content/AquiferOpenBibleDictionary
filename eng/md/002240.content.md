## Gob (id: 5496)

GOB
===

A place where David and his men fought against the Philistines in two battles ([2 Samuel 21:18–19](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:18-2Sam21:19)). In the similar account found in [1 Chronicles 20:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr20:4), the location is called Gezer instead of Gob.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 20:4; 2SA 21:18–2SA 21:19
