## Rithmah (id: 8047)

RITHMAH
=======

A place where the Israelites stopped during their journey through the wilderness. It was located between Hazeroth and Rimmon\-perez ([Numbers 33:18–19](https://ref.ly/Num33:18-Num33:19)). 

*See* Wilderness Wanderings.
