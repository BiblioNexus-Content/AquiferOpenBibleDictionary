## Dedan (Person) (id: 4793)

DEDAN (Person)
==============

1\. Grandson of Cush in the list of Noah’s descendants. His father was Raamah, and his brother’s name was Sheba ([Gn 10:7](https://ref.ly/Gen10:7); [1 Chr 1:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:9)).

2\. Grandson of Abraham through Keturah ([Gn 25:3](https://ref.ly/Gen25:3)). His father was Jokshan, his brother was Sheba, and his descendants were the Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:7; GEN 25:3; 1CH 1:9
