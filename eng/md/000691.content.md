## Bacenor (id: 3961)

BACENOR
=======

One of Judas Maccabeus’s officers, according to [2 Maccabees 12:35](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:35). The name, might be a corrupt form of “Toubiani.” The verse might read, “Dositheus, a Toubian, who was on horseback” (see [2 Maccabees 12:17](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 12:17; 2MA 12:35
