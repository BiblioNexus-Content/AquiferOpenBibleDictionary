## Ahinoam (id: 3452)

AHINOAM
=======

1. The daughter of Ahimaaz and wife of King Saul ([1 Samuel 14:50](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:50)).
2. A Jezreelite woman who became David’s wife after Saul took back his daughter Michal and married her to Palti ([1 Samuel 25:43](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:43-1Sam25:44)–[44](https://ref.ly/1Sam25:43-1Sam25:44)). In Hebron, Ahinoam became the mother of David’s oldest son, Amnon ([2 Samuel 3:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:2); [1 Chronicles 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 14:50; 2SA 3:2; 1CH 3:1; 1SA 25:43–1SA 25:44
