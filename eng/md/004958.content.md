## Senaah (id: 8221)

SENAAH
======

Father of a family of Israelites who returned with Zerubbabel to Palestine following the exile ([Ezr 2:35](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:35); [Neh 7:38](https://ref.ly/Neh7:38)). They helped Nehemiah rebuild part of the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 3:3](https://ref.ly/Neh3:3); Hassenaah is an alternate spelling for Senaah). Hassenuah is a possible variant for Senaah ([1 Chr 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:7); [Neh 11:9](https://ref.ly/Neh11:9), where kjv reads “Senuah”).

*See also* Hassenuah.
