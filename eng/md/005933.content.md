## Zemarites (id: 9178)

ZEMARITES
=========

One of the families of the Canaanites in the ethnological lists of [Genesis 10](https://ref.ly/Gen10:1-Gen10:32) (v [18](https://ref.ly/Gen10:18)) and [1 Chronicles 1](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:1-1Chr1:54) (v [16](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:16)). The Zemarites were a Hamitic tribe mentioned in connection with the Arvadites and the Hamathites. They were probably located near the Mediterranean in the vicinity of Tripoli.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:18; 1CH 1:16; GEN 10:1–GEN 10:32; 1CH 1:1–1CH 1:54
