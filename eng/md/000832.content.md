## Ben-Deker (id: 4100)

BEN\-DEKER
==========

One of 12 officers chosen to collect food for King Solomon’s household ([1 Kings 4:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:9), “son of Dekar” in the King James Version). Ben\-deker’s area was an area along the southern border of Dan’s tribe near Beth\-shemesh.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:9
