## Ham (Place) (id: 5631)

HAM (Place)
===========

Place where Chedorlaomer and his cohorts defeated the Zuzim ([Gn 14:5](https://ref.ly/Gen14:5)). The name is probably preserved by Tell Ham, near the modern village on the Wadi er\-Rejeilah. Bronze and Iron Age settlements have been unearthed there.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:5
