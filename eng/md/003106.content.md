## Jeush (id: 6363)

JEUSH
=====

1. The eldest of three sons born to Esau by Oholibamah. She was the daughter of the Canaanite Anah. Jesus was a chief among the descendants of Esau in Edom ([Genesis 36:5–18](https://ref.ly/Gen36:5-Gen36:18); [1 Chronicles 1:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:35)).
2. A son of Bilhan from the family of Jediael. He was also a leader in the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 7:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:10)).
3. A Benjamite, son of Eshek and a descendant of King Saul ([1 Chronicles 8:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:39)).
4. A Levite from the family of Gershon and the third of the four sons of Shimei. He and his youngest brother, Beriah, had few sons. So the records during the rule of King David count them as one family ([1 Chronicles 23:10–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:10-1Chr23:11)).
5. The eldest of three sons born to King Rehoboam by Mahalath. She was the granddaughter of Eliab ([2 Chronicles 11:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:35; 1CH 7:10; 1CH 8:39; 2CH 11:19; 1CH 23:10–1CH 23:11; GEN 36:5–GEN 36:18
