## Claudius, Edict of (id: 4582)

CLAUDIUS, Edict of
==================

A message carved on a marble slab (a flat piece of stone) found in Nazareth. The message warns people not to steal from graves. It was likely written when Claudius was the Roman emperor, between the years 41 and 54 AD. *See* Inscriptions.
