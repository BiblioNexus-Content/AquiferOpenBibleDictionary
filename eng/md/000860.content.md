## Bered (Place) (id: 4131)

BERED (Place)
=============

A place in the southern part of Israel called the Negev Desert. We do not know exactly where Bered was. God spoke to Sarai’s maid, Hagar, at a well between Kadesh and Bered ([Genesis 16:14](https://ref.ly/Gen16:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 16:14
