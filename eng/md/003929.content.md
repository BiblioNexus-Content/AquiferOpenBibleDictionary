## Molech (id: 7188)

MOLECH
======

Molech was a god worshiped by the Ammonite people. People who worshiped Molech would sacrifice humans to him ([Leviticus 18:21](https://ref.ly/Lev18:21); [Jeremiah 32:35](https://ref.ly/Jer32:35)). Molech is also known as Milcom or Malcham.

*See* Milcom.
