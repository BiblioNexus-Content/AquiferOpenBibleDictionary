## Jehud (id: 6296)

JEHUD
=====

One of the towns given to Dan’s tribe after the Conquest ([Jos 19:45](https://ref.ly/Josh19:45)). It has been variously identified with the village of el\-Yehudiyeh, about seven miles (11\.3 kilometers) southeast of Joppa, and with Yazur, about five miles (8 kilometers) southeast of Joppa.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:45
