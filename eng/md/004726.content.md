## Rekem (Person) (id: 7988)

REKEM (Person)
==============

1. The prince or king of Midian who was killed with his four partners in a battle led by Moses at the command of the Lord ([Numbers 31:8](https://ref.ly/Num31:8), [Joshua 13:21](https://ref.ly/Josh13:21)). The Israelites who lived near the area ruled by Rekem had been led away to worship the false god called Baal\-peor.
2. The son of Hebron, who was a descendant of Caleb. Rekem was the father of Shammai ([1 Chronicles 2:43–44](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:43-1Chr2:44)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 31:8; JOS 13:21; 1CH 2:43–1CH 2:44
