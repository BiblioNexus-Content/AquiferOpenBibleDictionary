## Beor (id: 4122)

BEOR
====

1. Bela’s father ([Genesis 36:32](https://ref.ly/Gen36:32)). Bela was a king of Edom.
2. Balaam’s father ([Numbers 22:5](https://ref.ly/Num22:5); [2 Peter 2:15](https://ref.ly/2Pet2:15), sometimes called “Bosor”). Balaam was asked by Balak, king of Moab, to curse Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:32; NUM 22:5; 2PE 2:15
