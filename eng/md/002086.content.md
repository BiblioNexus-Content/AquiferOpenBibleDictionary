## Gadfly (id: 5338)

GADFLY
======

Any of a number of large flies, including the horsefly and botfly, that irritate livestock. King Nebuchadnezzar is called a gadfly in the only biblical reference to this insect ([Jer 46:20](https://ref.ly/Jer46:20)). *See* Animals (Fly).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 46:20
