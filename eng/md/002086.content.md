## Gaddiel (id: 5336)

GADDIEL
=======

The son of Sodi from the tribe of Zebulun. Moses sent Gaddiel to search out the land of Canaan ([Numbers 13:10](https://ref.ly/Num13:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:10
