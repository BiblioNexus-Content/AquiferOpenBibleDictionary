## Jobab (id: 6393)

JOBAB
=====

1. A son of Joktan in the family line of Eber ([Genesis 10:29](https://ref.ly/Gen10:29); [1 Chronicles 1:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:23)).
2. An early Edomite king. He was the son of Zerah of Bozrah ([Genesis 36:33–34](https://ref.ly/Gen36:33-Gen36:34); [1 Chronicles 1:44–45](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:44-1Chr1:45)).
3. A king of Madon. Along with other Canaanite kings, he joined Jabin of Hazor in a northern alliance. This was to stop the Israelites from taking over the northern section of Canaan. Jobab died in battle at the waters of Merom ([Joshua 11:1](https://ref.ly/Josh11:1); [12:19](https://ref.ly/Josh12:19)).
4. A son of Shaharaim by his wife Hodesh, a member of the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:9)).
5. A son of Elpaal from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:29; JOS 11:1; JOS 12:19; 1CH 1:23; 1CH 8:9; 1CH 8:18; GEN 36:33–GEN 36:34; 1CH 1:44–1CH 1:45
