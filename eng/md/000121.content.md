## Adino (id: 3380)

ADINO
=====

Possibly another name for Josheb\-basshebeth, one of David’s top three military heroes ([2 Samuel 23:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:8)). He was also called Jashobeam ([1 Chronicles 11:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:11)). 

*See* Jashobeam \#1.
