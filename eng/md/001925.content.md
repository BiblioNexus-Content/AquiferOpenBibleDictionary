## Eunice (id: 5182)

EUNICE
======

The mother of the apostle Timothy and daughter of Lois ([2 Timothy 1:5](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:5)). She was the wife of a pagan Greek man. Eunice was a Jewish Christian ([Acts 16:1](https://ref.ly/Acts16:1)). She taught Timothy the Old Testament Scriptures “from infancy” ([2 Timothy 3:15](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:15)). Eunice converted to Christianity during the first trip of the apostle Paul to her home in Lystra. This happened sometime before his visit mentioned in [Acts 16:1\.](https://ref.ly/Acts16:1)
