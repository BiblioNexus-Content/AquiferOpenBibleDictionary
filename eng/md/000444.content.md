## Arbathite (id: 3708)

ARBATHITE
=========

A resident of the city of Beth\-arabah. Beth\-arabah was the hometown of Abi\-albon (Abiel), one of David’s thirty "mighty men" ([2 Samuel 23:31](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:31); [1 Chronicles 11:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:32)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:31; 1CH 11:32
