## Belteshazzar (id: 4094)

BELTESHAZZAR
============

Daniel’s Babylonian name ([Daniel 1:7](https://ref.ly/Dan1:7)). Daniel was one of the young men taken captive to Babylon to be trained as servants for King Nebuchadnezzar ([Daniel 1](https://ref.ly/Dan1:1-Dan1:21)). 

*See* Daniel (Person) \#3.
