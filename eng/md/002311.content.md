## Hacmoni (id: 5566)

HACMONI
=======

Name of Jehiel’s family. Jehiel was David’s servant ([1 Chr 27:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:32)), apparently a companion or tutor of David’s sons.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 27:32
