## Asur (id: 3849)

ASUR
====

One of the temple servants whose descendants were among those who returned with Zerubbabel from exile in Babylon ([1 Esdras 5:31](https://ref.ly/1Esd5:31)). The name Harhur is also listed in [Ezra 2:51](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:51) and [Nehemiah 7:53](https://ref.ly/Neh7:53).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:51; NEH 7:53; 1ES 5:31
