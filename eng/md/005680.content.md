## Triumphal Entry (id: 8929)

TRIUMPHAL ENTRY\*
=================

A term that denotes Jesus Christ’s entry into Jerusalem, where the multitudes welcomed him and hailed him as Son of David, King of the Jews. Ironically, this event occurred just a few days prior to his betrayal, arrest, trial, and crucifixion.

*See also* Jesus Christ (The Final Days in Jerusalem).
