## Chemosh (id: 4501)

CHEMOSH
=======

Chemosh was the name of the main god that the Moabite people worshipped ([Numbers 21:29](https://ref.ly/Num21:29)). The Bible also mentions that the Ammonite people were connected to this false god ([Judges 11:24](https://ref.ly/Judg11:24)). 

*See* Canaanite Deities and Religion; Moab, Moabites.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 21:29; JDG 11:24
