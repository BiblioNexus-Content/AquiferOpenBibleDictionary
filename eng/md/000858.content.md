## Berechiah (id: 4129)

BERECHIAH
=========

1. Son of Zerubbabel and descendant of King David ([1 Chronicles 3:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:20)).
2. A Levite from the family of Gershon. He was the father of Asaph ([1 Chronicles 6:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:39); [15:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:17)). Asaph was a famous musician of Israel.
3. The son of Asa. He was the head of a family of Levites who returned to Judah after the exile in Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:16)).
4. A Levite who King David assigned as a gatekeeper for the ark of the covenant ([1 Chronicles 15:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:23)).
5. The son of Meshillemoth, a leader of the tribe of Ephraim. He was one of three men in Samaria who supported the prophet Obed in sending prisoners of war back to their homes in Judah ([2 Chronicles 28:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:12)).
6. The father of Meshullam. Meshullam told Nehemiah, the governor, to rebuild the wall of Jerusalem ([Nehemiah 3:4, 30](https://ref.ly/Neh3:4,Neh3:30); [6:18](https://ref.ly/Neh6:18)).
7. The son of Iddo and father of Zechariah the prophet ([Zechariah 1:1, 7](https://ref.ly/Zech1:1,Zech1:7)).
