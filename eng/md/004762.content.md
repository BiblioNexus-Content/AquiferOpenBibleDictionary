## Rezon (id: 8026)

REZON
=====

Son of Eliada, who set himself up as ruler of Damascus and Syria following David’s killing of Hadadezer, king of Zobah. Rezon was a God\-appointed adversary who despised Israel and was a constant problem to Solomon during his reign ([1 Kgs 11:23–25](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:23-1Kgs11:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 11:23–1KI 11:25
