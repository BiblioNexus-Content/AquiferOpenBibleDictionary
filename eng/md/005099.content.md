## Shepham (id: 8362)

SHEPHAM
=======

One of the places used by Moses to establish the eastern border of the promised land. It was located between Hazar\-enan, which marked the northeastern corner of the land, and Riblah ([Numbers 34:10–11\)](https://ref.ly/Num34:10-Num34:11).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:10–NUM 34:11
