## Joses (id: 6450)

JOSES
=====

1. An alternate spelling for Joseph, Mary’s son, in [Mark 6:3](https://ref.ly/Mark6:3). *See* Joseph \#7.
2. The King James Version spelling for Joseph. He was also called Barnabas in [Acts 4:36](https://ref.ly/Acts4:36). *See* Barnabas.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 6:3; ACT 4:36
