## Abda (id: 3269)

ABDA
====

1\. Adoniram’s father. Adoniram was in charge of forced labour under King Solomon ([1 Kings 4:6](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:6)).

2\. Shammua’s son. He was a Levite leader in Jerusalem after the exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17)). In 1 Chronicles, their names are given as Shemaiah and Obadiah ([1 Chronicles 9:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:6; 1CH 9:16; NEH 11:17
