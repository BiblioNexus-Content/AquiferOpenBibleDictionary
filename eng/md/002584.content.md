## Hezron (Person) (id: 5838)

HEZRON (Person)
===============

1. Reuben’s son ([Genesis 46:9](https://ref.ly/Gen46:9); [Exodus 6:14](https://ref.ly/Exod6:14); [1 Chronicles 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:3)) and founder of the Hezronite family in the tribe of Reuben ([Numbers 26:6](https://ref.ly/Num26:6)).
2. Perez’s son ([Genesis 46:12](https://ref.ly/Gen46:12); [Ruth 4:18](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:18-Ruth4:19)[–](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:18-Ruth4:19)[19](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:18-Ruth4:19); [1 Chronicles 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:5-1Chr2:25)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:5-1Chr2:25)[25](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:5-1Chr2:25); [4:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:1)) and founder of the Hezronite family in the tribe of Judah ([Numbers 26:21](https://ref.ly/Num26:21)), An ancestor of Jesus Christ ([Matthew 1:3](https://ref.ly/Matt1:3); [Luke 3:33](https://ref.ly/Luke3:33)).
