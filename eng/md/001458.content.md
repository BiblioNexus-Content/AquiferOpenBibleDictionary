## Cun (id: 4709)

CUN
===

A Syrian city belonging to Hadadezer, King of Zobah. King David attacked Cun and took large amounts of bronze from the city ([1 Chronicles 18:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:8)). In another account of this same event, Berothai may refer to the same place ([2 Samuel 8:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:8)).

*See also* Berothah, Berothai.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:8; 1CH 18:8
