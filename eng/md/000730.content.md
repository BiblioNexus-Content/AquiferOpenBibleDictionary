## Barachel (id: 3999)

BARACHEL
========

Barachel was the father of Elihu. He is described as a Buzite ([Job 32:2, 6](https://ref.ly/Job32:2,Job32:6); compare [Genesis 22:21](https://ref.ly/Gen22:21); [Jeremiah 25:23](https://ref.ly/Jer25:23)). Elihu tried to counsel Job after the failed attempts by Job’s three older friends.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 22:21; JOB 32:2; JOB 32:6; JER 25:23
