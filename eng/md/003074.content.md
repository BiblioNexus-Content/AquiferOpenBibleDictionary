## Jeruel (id: 6330)

JERUEL
======

Wilderness lying southeast of Tekoa near En\-gedi, just above and west of the cliff of Ziz ([2 Chr 20:16](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:16)). Some identify it with el\-Hasasah.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 20:16
