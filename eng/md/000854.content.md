## Beracah, Valley of (id: 4123)

BERACAH, Valley of
==================

The place where King Jehoshaphat brought the people of Judah to praise God ([2 Chronicles 20:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:26)). The people were grateful for God’s help in defeating the attacking armies of Moab, Ammon, and Mount Seir ([2 Chronicles 20:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:1-2Chr20:25)[–](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:1-2Chr20:25)[25](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:1-2Chr20:25)). The valley is probably the area Wadi el ’Arrub, not far from Tekoa, near a ruin called Bereikut.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 20:26; 2CH 20:1–2CH 20:25
