## Kidon (id: 6576)

KIDON
=====

Another spelling of Chidon, the threshing floor where God struck down Huzzah as he attempted to steady the ark of the covenant ([1 Chronicles 13:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr13:9)).

*See* Chidon.
