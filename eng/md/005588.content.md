## Tikvah (id: 8839)

TIKVAH
======

1\. Harhas’s son, father of Shallum, and the father\-in\-law of Huldah the prophetess ([2 Kgs 22:14](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:14)); alternately called Tokhath (kjv “Tikvath”) in [2 Chronicles 34:22](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:22).

2\. Father of Jahzeiah, who was one of the four individuals on record who opposed Ezra’s command to divorce foreign wives ([Ezr 10:15](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 22:14; 2CH 34:22; EZR 10:15
