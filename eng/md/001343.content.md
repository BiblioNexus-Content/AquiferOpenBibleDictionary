## Cleopas (id: 4592)

CLEOPAS
=======

A follower of Jesus who talked with him on the way to Emmaus ([Luke 24:18](https://ref.ly/Luke24:18)). Some identify this Cleopas with the Clopas of [John 19:25](https://ref.ly/John19:25), but this is unlikely. 

*See* Clopas.
