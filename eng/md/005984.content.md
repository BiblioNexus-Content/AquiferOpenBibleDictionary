## Zimri (Place) (id: 9229)

ZIMRI (Place)
=============

Place and peoples of the East, listed along with Elam and Media, against whom God’s wrath would fall ([Jer 25:25](https://ref.ly/Jer25:25)). Zimri’s location and history are unknown; some identify its progenitor with Zimran, Abraham and Keturah’s son ([Gn 25:2](https://ref.ly/Gen25:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:2; JER 25:25
