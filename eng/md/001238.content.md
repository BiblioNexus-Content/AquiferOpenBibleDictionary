CHEDORLAOMER

Alternate spelling of Kedorlaomer, a king of Elam mentioned in Genesis who led a coalition of kings in a war against Sodom and Gomorrah ([Genesis 14](https://ref.ly/Gen14:1-Gen14:24)).
