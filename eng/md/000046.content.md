## Abimael (id: 3304)

ABIMAEL
=======

One of the many sons or descendants of Joktan, and thus a descendant of Shem ([Genesis 10:28](https://ref.ly/Gen10:28); [1 Chronicles 1:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:28; 1CH 1:22
