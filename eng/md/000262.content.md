## Almon-Diblathaim (id: 3520)

ALMON\-DIBLATHAIM
=================

An area in Moab where the Israelites camped during their 40 years of wandering ([Numbers 33:46–47](https://ref.ly/Num33:46-Num33:47)). Some identify it with Beth\-diblathaim ([Jeremiah 48:22](https://ref.ly/Jer48:22)).

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.

* **Associated Passages:** JER 48:22; NUM 33:46–NUM 33:47
