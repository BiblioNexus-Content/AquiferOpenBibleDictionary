## Esdris (id: 5143)

ESDRIS
======

Mentioned in [2 Maccabees 12:36](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:36) as an army officer under Judas Maccabeus. Some Greek texts and the kjv read Gorgias, which may be either a place or a person.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 12:36
