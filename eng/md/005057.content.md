## Shebuel (id: 8320)

SHEBUEL
=======

1\. Gershon’s son and Moses’ grandson from Levi’s tribe ([1 Chr 23:15–16](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:15-1Chr23:16)); father of Jehdeiah ([24:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:20), “Shubael”). He was the chief officer in charge of the treasuries ([26:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:24)).

2\. Levite, Heman’s son and a musician in the tabernacle ([1 Chr 25:4, 20](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4,1Chr25:20), “Shubael”).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:20; 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:20; 1CH 26:24; 1CH 23:15–1CH 23:16
