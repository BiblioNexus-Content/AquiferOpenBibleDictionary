## Ocina (id: 7459)

OCINA
=====

A town on the coast south of Tyre ([Jdt 2:28](https://ref.ly/Jdt2:28)). Its location is uncertain, and it has been identified with both Sandaliam and Acco.

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 2:28
