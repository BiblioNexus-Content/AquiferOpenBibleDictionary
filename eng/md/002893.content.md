## Jabal (id: 6145)

JABAL
=====

Descendant of Cain and the first son of Lamech and Adah. He was the father of a nomadic people who dwelt in tents ([Gn 4:20](https://ref.ly/Gen4:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:20
