## Ira (id: 6046)

IRA
===

1. A priest of King David or chief official in his service at the time of the revolt of Sheba ([2 Samuel 20:26](https://ref.ly/2Sam20:26)).
2. A warrior among the mighty men of King David, known as “the thirty” ([2 Samuel 23:26](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:26)). He was the son of Ikkesh of Tekoa ([1 Chronicles 11:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:28); [27:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:9)). He became a commander of the sixth division of the militia of David.
3. A warrior among the mighty men of King David, known as “the thirty.” He is an Ithrite ([2 Samuel 23:38](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:38); [1 Chronicles 11:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:40)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 20:26; 2SA 23:26; 2SA 23:38; 1CH 11:28; 1CH 11:40; 1CH 27:9
