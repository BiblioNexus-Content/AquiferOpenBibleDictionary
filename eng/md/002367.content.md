## Hammedatha (id: 5620)

HAMMEDATHA
==========

Hammedatha was the father of Haman. Haman was a chief adviser to King Ahasuerus of Persia (also called Xerxes). In the book of Esther, Haman hated the Jewish people and planned to destroy them ([Esther 3:1, 10](https://ref.ly/Esth3:1,Esth3:10); [8:5](https://ref.ly/Esth8:5); [9:10, 24](https://ref.ly/Esth9:10,Esth9:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** EST 3:1; EST 3:10; EST 8:5; EST 9:10; EST 9:24
