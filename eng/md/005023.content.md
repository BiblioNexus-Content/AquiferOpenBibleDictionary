## Shapham (id: 8286)

SHAPHAM
=======

Leader in Gad’s tribe ([1 Chr 5:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:12)). He is believed to have lived in Bashan and served during the days of Jotham, king of Judah (v [17](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 5:12; 1CH 5:17
