## Bartimaeus (id: 4017)

BARTIMAEUS
==========

Timaeus’s son, a blind beggar who called out to Jesus as he left Jericho on his final journey to Jerusalem ([Mark 10:46–52](https://ref.ly/Mark10:46-Mark10:52)). Seeing Bartimaeus’s faith, Jesus healed his blindness.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 10:46–MRK 10:52
