## Ironsmith (id: 6051)

IRONSMITH\*
===========

An artisan who normally worked in iron ([Is 44:12](https://ref.ly/Isa44:12)); a blacksmith. The first worker in iron recorded in the Bible is Tubal\-cain ([Gn 4:22](https://ref.ly/Gen4:22)). In Israel iron became widely known and used about the 11th century BC ([Dt 3:11](https://ref.ly/Deut3:11); [Jos 6:19, 24](https://ref.ly/Josh6:19,Josh6:24); [17:16](https://ref.ly/Josh17:16); [Jgs 1:19](https://ref.ly/Judg1:19); [4:3, 13](https://ref.ly/Judg4:3,Judg4:13)). *See* Minerals and Metals.
