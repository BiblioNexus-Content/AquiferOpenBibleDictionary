## Betah (id: 4148)

BETAH
=====

Another name for Tibhath ([1 Chronicles 18:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:8)). This was a town in the city\-kingdom of Aram\-zobah that King David brought under his control ([2 Samuel 8:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:8)).
