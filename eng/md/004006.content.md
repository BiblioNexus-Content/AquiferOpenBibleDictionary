## Naamathite (id: 7261)

NAAMATHITE
==========

Any resident of Naamah in northwest Arabia. Zophar, one of the friends of Job, was a Naamathite ([Job 2:11](https://ref.ly/Job2:11); [11:1](https://ref.ly/Job11:1); [20:1](https://ref.ly/Job20:1); [42:9](https://ref.ly/Job42:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 2:11; JOB 11:1; JOB 20:1; JOB 42:9
