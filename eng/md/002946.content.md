## Jambres (id: 6199)

JAMBRES
=======

An enemy of Moses. Paul uses him, along with Jannes, as an example of those who "oppose the truth" and are "disqualified from the faith" ([2 Timothy 3:8–9](https://ref.ly/2Tim3:8-2Tim3:9)). 

*See* Jannes and Jambres.
