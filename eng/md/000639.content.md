## Azel (id: 3909)

AZEL
====

A descendant of Benjamin, Saul, and Jonathan. Azel was the son of Eleasah and the father of six sons ([1 Chronicles 8:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:37-1Chr8:38)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:37-1Chr8:38)[38](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:37-1Chr8:38); [9:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:43-1Chr9:44)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:43-1Chr9:44)[44](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:43-1Chr9:44)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 8:37–1CH 8:38; 1CH 9:43–1CH 9:44
