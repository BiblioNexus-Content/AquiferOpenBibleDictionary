## Hassophereth (id: 5708)

HASSOPHERETH
============

The ancestor of a family of temple assistants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile in Babylon ([Ezra 2:55](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:55)). Hassophereth may be the same person called Sophereth in [Nehemiah 7:57](https://ref.ly/Neh7:57).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:55; NEH 7:57
