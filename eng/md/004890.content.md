## Saton (id: 8156)

SATON
=====

The Greek word in [Matthew 13:33](https://ref.ly/Matt13:33) for a dry measure. It is equal to the Old Testament *seah*, about one peck (8\.8 liters). The plural is *sata*. 

*See* Weights and Measures.
