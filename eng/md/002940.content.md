## Jakim (id: 6193)

JAKIM
=====

1\. Shimei’s descendant from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 8:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:19)).

2\. Family leader of the 12th group of Aaron’s descendants assigned to temple duty in David’s time ([1 Chr 24:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 8:19; 1CH 24:12
