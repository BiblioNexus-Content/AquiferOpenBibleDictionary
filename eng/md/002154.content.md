## Gemariah (id: 5410)

GEMARIAH
========

1\. Hilkiah’s son and emissary to Nebuchadnezzar from King Zedekiah. He carried Jeremiah’s letter to the exiles in Babylon ([Jer 29:3](https://ref.ly/Jer29:3)).

2\. Son of Shaphan the scribe. In the temple chamber of Gemariah, Baruch read Jeremiah’s scroll ([Jer 36:10–12, 25](https://ref.ly/Jer36:10-Jer36:12,Jer36:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 29:3; JER 36:25; JER 36:10–JER 36:12
