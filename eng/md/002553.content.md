## Hepher (Place) (id: 5807)

HEPHER (Place)
==============

Canaanite city located northwest of Jerusalem. It was captured by Joshua ([Jos 12:17](https://ref.ly/Josh12:17)) and later used as an administrative district under Solomon ([1 Kgs 4:10](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 12:17; 1KI 4:10
