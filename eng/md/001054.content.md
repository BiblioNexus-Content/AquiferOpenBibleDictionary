## Borrow, Borrowing (id: 4310)

BORROW, BORROWING
=================

Receiving money or goods that a person promises to return. The law of Moses regulated borrowing and lending ([Deuteronomy 23:19–20](https://ref.ly/Deut23:19-Deut23:20)). 

*See* Banker, Banking.
