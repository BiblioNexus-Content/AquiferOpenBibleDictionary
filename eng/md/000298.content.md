## Ammidians (id: 3558)

AMMIDIANS
=========

A name used in a list of towns identifying groups of exiles that returned from Babylon with Zerubbabel around 538 BC ([1 Esdras 5:20](https://ref.ly/1Esd5:20)). The lists in [Ezra 2:25–26](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:25-Ezra2:26) and [Nehemiah 7:29–30](https://ref.ly/Neh7:29-Neh7:30) do not mention a group from such a place.

* **Associated Passages:** 1ES 5:20; EZR 2:25–EZR 2:26; NEH 7:29–NEH 7:30
