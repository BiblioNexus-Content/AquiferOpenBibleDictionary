## Ginath (id: 5473)

GINATH
======

Tibni’s father. Tibni unsuccessfully attempted to gain the throne of Israel; Omri became king instead ([1 Kgs 16:21–22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:21-1Kgs16:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 16:21–1KI 16:22
