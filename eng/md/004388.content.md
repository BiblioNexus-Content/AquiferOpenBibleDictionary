## Penny (id: 7645)

PENNY
=====

1\. The King James Version's translation of *denarius* ([Matthew 20:2, 9–10, 13](https://ref.ly/Matt20:2,Matt20:9-Matt20:10,Matt20:13); [22:19](https://ref.ly/Matt22:19); [Mark 12:15](https://ref.ly/Mark12:15); [Luke 20:24](https://ref.ly/Luke20:24); [Revelation 6:6](https://ref.ly/Rev6:6)).

2\. The Revised Standard Version and New Living Translation word for the Roman *assarion*, equivalent to one\-sixteenth of the denarius ([Matthew 10:29](https://ref.ly/Matt10:29); [Luke 12:6](https://ref.ly/Luke12:6)).

3\. The Revised Standard Version and New Living Translation word for the Roman *quadrans*, equivalent to one\-fourth of the *assarion* or one sixty\-fourth of the *denarius* ([Matthew 5:26](https://ref.ly/Matt5:26); [Mark 12:42](https://ref.ly/Mark12:42)).
