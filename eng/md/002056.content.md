## Fringe (id: 5310)

FRINGE
======

The border of a garment, or a “tassel.” Jewish men wore four fringes on their upper garments because God commanded them to do so ([Deuteronomy 22:12](https://ref.ly/Deut22:12)). Those tassels were reminders of God's laws.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 22:12
