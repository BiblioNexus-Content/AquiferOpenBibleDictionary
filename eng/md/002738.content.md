## Iezer, Iezerites (id: 5988)

IEZER, IEZERITES
================

Iezer and Iezerites are shortened forms of the names Abiezer and Abiezrite. Abiezer was a son of Gilead, and the Abiezrites were Abiezer's descendants ([Numbers 26:30](https://ref.ly/Num26:30)). 

*See* Abiezer \#1.
