## Hundred-Weight (id: 5944)

HUNDRED\-WEIGHT\*
=================

Measure equal to about 1 talent or 75 pounds (34 kilograms), mentioned only in [Revelation 16:21](https://ref.ly/Rev16:21). *See* Weights and Measures.

* **Associated Passages:** REV 16:21
