## Pelet (id: 7635)

PELET
=====

1\. Jahdai’s son from Judah’s tribe ([1 Chr 2:47](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:47)).

2\. Warrior from Benjamin’s tribe who joined David at Ziklag in his struggle against King Saul. Pelet was one of David’s ambidextrous archers and slingers ([1 Chr 12:2–3](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:2-1Chr12:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:47; 1CH 12:2–1CH 12:3
