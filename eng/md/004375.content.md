## Peleth (id: 7634)

PELETH
======

1. A man from the tribe of Reuben who was the father of On ([Numbers 16:1](https://ref.ly/Num16:1)).
2. A man from the tribe of Judah who was the son of Jonathan. He belonged to the Jerahmeelite family group ([1 Chronicles 2:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:33)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 16:1; 1CH 2:33
