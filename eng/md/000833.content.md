## Bene-Berak (id: 4101)

BENE\-BERAK
===========

One of the cities of Dan ([Joshua 19:45](https://ref.ly/Josh19:45)). The modern name is Ibn Ibrak, a residential area northwest of Tel Aviv.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:45
