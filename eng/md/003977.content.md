## Mount Shepher (id: 8368)

MOUNT SHEPHER
=============

A temporary camping place for the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings. Mount Shepher was located between Kehelathah and Haradah ([Numbers 33:23–24](https://ref.ly/Num33:23-Num33:24)).

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
