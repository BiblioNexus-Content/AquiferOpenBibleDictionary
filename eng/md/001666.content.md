## East Gate (id: 4919)

EAST GATE
=========

Gate in the walled city of Jerusalem ([Neh 3:29](https://ref.ly/Neh3:29)). “East gate” also refers to the gate of the temple mentioned in [Ezekiel 10:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek10:19); [11:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:1); and [43:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek43:1). *See* Jerusalem.
