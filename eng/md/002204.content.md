## Gier Eagle (id: 5460)

GIER EAGLE
==========

An older English name for the Egyptian vulture (*Neophron percnopterus*). The King James Version uses “gier eagle” in [Leviticus 11:18](https://ref.ly/Lev11:18) and [Deuteronomy 14:17](https://ref.ly/Deut14:17) for the bird now often called the carrion vulture.

*See* Birds; Vulture. 

* **Associated Passages:** LEV 11:18; DEU 14:17
