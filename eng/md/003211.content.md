## Jozacar, Jozachar (id: 6470)

JOZACAR, JOZACHAR
=================

An alternate name for Jozabad, son of Shimeath the Ammonitess. He was one of the royal servants who plotted against the murdered King Joash of Judah ([2 Kings 12:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:21)). He is also called Zabad in [2 Chronicles 24:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:26). 

*See* Zabad.
