## Onesiphorus (id: 7496)

ONESIPHORUS
===========

A Christian who took care of Paul while imprisoned in Ephesus. Then Paul was moved to Rome. Onesiphorus searched for him and "refreshed" him there ([2 Timothy 1:16](https://ref.ly/2Tim1:16)). Paul mentions Onesiphorus in the greetings of his Second Letter to Timothy. Paul greeted Onesiphorus and his household ([4:19](https://ref.ly/2Tim4:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2TI 1:16; 2TI 4:19
