## Cozbi (id: 4678)

COZBI
=====

A woman from Midian who had an improper relationship with an Israelite man named Zimri. This relationship broke God's laws. During this time, a deadly disease was spreading among the Israelites as punishment. Phinehas was the grandson of Aaron the high priest. He stopped the disease by killing both Zimri and Cozbi ([Numbers 25:15–18](https://ref.ly/Num25:15-Num25:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 25:15–NUM 25:18
