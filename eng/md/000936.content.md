## Beth-Togarmah (id: 4207)

BETH\-TOGARMAH
==============

A Hebrew phrase meaning “house of Togarmah.” It refers to the nation that was descended from Gomer’s son Togarmah. They traded with Tyre ([Ezekiel 27:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:14); [38:6](https://ref.ly/Ezek38:6)). 

*See* Togarmah.
