## Dositheus (id: 4888)

DOSITHEUS
=========

1\. A Jew representing himself as a priest and Levite. He delivered a letter from Mordecai concerning the Feast of Purim (possibly containing the book of Esther) to Ptolemy and Cleopatra in the fourth year of their reign ([Add Est 11:1](https://ref.ly/EsthGr11:1)).

2\. One of Judas Maccabeus’s captains. With Sosipater he captured and destroyed a stronghold of 10,000 soldiers left behind by Timothy, one of Antiochus IV Epiphanes’ governors ([2 Macc 12:19](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:19)).

3\. One of Bacenor’s men. This Dositheus was a horseman with great strength. His attempt to capture Gorgias (one of Ptolemy’s generals) was unsuccessful ([2 Macc 12:35](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:35)).

4\. Drimylus’s son and an apostate. A general of the Seleucids, he prevented the assassination of Ptolemy (ruler of the Seleucid Empire) by Theodotus ([3 Macc 1:3](https://ref.ly/3Macc1:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** ESG 11:1; 2MA 12:19; 2MA 12:35; 3MA 1:3
