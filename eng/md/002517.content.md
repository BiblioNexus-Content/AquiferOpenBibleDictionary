## Helam (id: 5770)

HELAM
=====

A place east of the Jordan River where King David defeated the armies of Hadadezer, King of Syria ([2 Samuel 10:16–17](https://ref.ly/2Sam10:16-2Sam10:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 10:16–2SA 10:17
