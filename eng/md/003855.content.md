## Michmethath (id: 7115)

MICHMETHATH
===========

A location that describes part of the border between the territories of Ephraim and Manasseh ([Joshua 16:6](https://ref.ly/Josh16:6); [17:7](https://ref.ly/Josh17:7)). It was in the mountains west of the Jordan, midway between the Dead Sea and the Sea of Galilee.
