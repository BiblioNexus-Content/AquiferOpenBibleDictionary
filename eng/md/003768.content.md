## Melchi (id: 7032)

MELCHI
======

Melchi is a name found in the genealogy (family line) of Jesus Christ in the Gospel of Luke.

1. Melchi was the son of Jannai according to [Luke 3:24](https://ref.ly/Luke3:24).
2. Melchi was the son of Addi according to [Luke 3:28](https://ref.ly/Luke3:28).

*See also* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 3:24; LUK 3:28
