## Cankerworm (id: 4410)

CANKERWORM
==========

Cankerworm is a word used in the King James Version to translate a Hebrew word that means "locust." Locusts are insects that eat plants and crops. Large groups of locusts can cause great damage to farmland.

*See* Locusts.
