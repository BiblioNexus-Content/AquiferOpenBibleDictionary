## Grape (id: 5526)

GRAPE
=====

Smooth\-skinned, juicy berry that grows in clusters on woody vines. Grapes are eaten fresh or dried, and are fermented for wine. *See* Agriculture; Plants (Vine); Vines, Vineyard; Wine.
