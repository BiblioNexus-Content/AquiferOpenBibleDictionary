## Shimshai (id: 8421)

SHIMSHAI
========

Persian government official whose territory included Palestine. With another official (Rehum), he wrote a letter to Artaxerxes opposing the rebuilding of the temple by the Jews returned from exile ([Ezr 4:8–9](https://ref.ly/Ezra4:8-Ezra4:9)). He succeeded in halting the rebuilding project.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 4:8–EZR 4:9
