## Kittim (id: 6632)

KITTIM
======

The ancient Hebrew name for the island of Cyprus in the Mediterranean Sea. The name comes from descendants of Javan (the Kittites) mentioned in the Table of Nations ([Genesis 10:4\)](https://ref.ly/Gen10:4). The prophet Daniel mentions "ships from Kittim" in a vision ([Daniel 11:30](https://ref.ly/Dan11:30))
