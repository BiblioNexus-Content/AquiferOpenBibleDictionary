## Bagpipe (id: 3967)

BAGPIPE
=======

The instrument used in King Nebuchadnezzar’s court ([Daniel 3:5, 7, 10, 15](https://ref.ly/Dan3:5,Dan3:7,Dan3:10,Dan3:15)). It may actually have been a string instrument, but is sometimes translated as "bagpipe." Bagpipes are wind instruments. Instruments like bagpipes were known in the ancient world. 

*See* Musical Instruments (Psantrin; Sumponia).

* **Associated Passages:** DAN 3:5; DAN 3:7; DAN 3:10; DAN 3:15
