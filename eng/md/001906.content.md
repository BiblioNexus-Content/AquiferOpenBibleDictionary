## Etam (id: 5164)

ETAM
====

1\. Rocky area in western Judah where Samson hid from his pursuers ([Jgs 15:8, 11](https://ref.ly/Judg15:8,Judg15:11)).

2\. Unknown site located in Simeonite territory ([1 Chr 4:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:32)).

3\. Town in the Judean uplands near Bethlehem fortified by Rehoboam of Judah after the division of the kingdom ([2 Chr 11:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:6)). It is identified with Khirbet el\-Khokh, just southwest of Bethlehem. The spring at Etam supplied Jerusalem with additional water in the Greek and Roman periods.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 15:8; JDG 15:11; 1CH 4:32; 2CH 11:6
