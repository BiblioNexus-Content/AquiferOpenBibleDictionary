## Aiah (id: 3473)

AIAH
====

1. Zibeon’s son, who was a Horite descended from Seir. Aiah is listed in Esau’s list of ancestors ([Genesis 36:24](https://ref.ly/Gen36:24); [1 Chronicles 1:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:35-1Chr1:40)–[40](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:35-1Chr1:40)).
2. The father (or possibly mother) of Saul’s concubine Rizpah ([2 Samuel 3:7](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:7); [21:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:8-2Sam21:11)–[11](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:8-2Sam21:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:24; 2SA 3:7; 1CH 1:35–1CH 1:40; 2SA 21:8–2SA 21:11
