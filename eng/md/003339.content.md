## Kinnereth (id: 6596)

KINNERETH
=========

Kinnereth is way of spelling Chinnereth, which is another name for the Sea of Galilee. The name can also describe the land around the lake.

*See* Chinnereth, Chinneroth.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:11; DEU 3:17; JOS 11:2; JOS 12:3; JOS 13:27; JOS 19:35; 1KI 15:20; LUK 5:1
* **Associated Articles:** Chinnereth, Chinneroth (ID: `4530@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
