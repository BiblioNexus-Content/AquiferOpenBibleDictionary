## Iye-abarim, Iyim (id: 6121)

IYE\-ABARIM, IYIM
=================

An Israelite camping place on the southeast border of Moab during their wilderness wanderings ([Numbers 33:44](https://ref.ly/Num33:44)). In verse [45](https://ref.ly/Num33:45) the town is called Iyim, which is a shortened form of Iye\-abarim.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:44; NUM 33:45
