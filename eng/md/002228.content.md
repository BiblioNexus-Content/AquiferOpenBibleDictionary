## Glass (id: 5484)

GLASS
=====

Translation for “mirror” in [Isaiah 3:23](https://ref.ly/Isa3:23), [1 Corinthians 13:12](https://ref.ly/1Cor13:12), and [James 1:23](https://ref.ly/Jas1:23) (kjv). Since mirrors of Bible times were polished metal sheets, “glass” is incorrect. *See* Mirror.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 3:23; 1CO 13:12; JAS 1:23
