## Tarshish (Person) (id: 8744)

TARSHISH (Person)
=================

1. One of Javan’s four sons and a descendant of Noah through Japheth’s family line ([1 Chronicles 1:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:7)).
2. The sixth of Bilhan’s seven sons. He was a capable leader in the tribe of Benjamin and was among those able to go to war ([1 Chronicles 7:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:10)).
3. One of the seven princes of Persia and Media. These men had personal access to King Ahasuerus (also called Xerxes). Their positions of honor were second only to the king's ([Esther 1:14](https://ref.ly/Esth1:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:7; 1CH 7:10; EST 1:14
