## Onam (id: 7493)

ONAM
====

1\. Grandson of Seir and Shobal’s fifth son ([Gn 36:23](https://ref.ly/Gen36:23); [1 Chr 1:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:40)).

2\. Son of Jerahmeel and Atarah, the father of a clan in Judah ([1 Chr 2:26–28](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:26-1Chr2:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:23; 1CH 1:40; 1CH 2:26–1CH 2:28
