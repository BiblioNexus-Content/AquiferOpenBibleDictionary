## Helbah (id: 5771)

HELBAH
======

One of the Canaanite strongholds that was not conquered by the tribe of Asher after they took possession of the land ([Jgs 1:31](https://ref.ly/Judg1:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 1:31
