## Helbon (id: 5772)

HELBON
======

District north of Damascus, which produced choice wine ([Ez 27:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:18)); perhaps identifiable with modern Halbun, where the vine is still cultivated.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 27:18
