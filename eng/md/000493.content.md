## Aroerite (id: 3758)

AROERITE
========

A resident of Aroer. The Aroerite Hotham, father of two of David’s "mighty men," was a native of Aroer ([1 Chronicles 11:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:44)). *See* Aroer \#3.
