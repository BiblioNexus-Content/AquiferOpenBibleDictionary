## Aznoth-Tabor (id: 3917)

AZNOTH\-TABOR
=============

A location on the southwest border of the tribal land of Naphtali ([Joshua 19:34](https://ref.ly/Josh19:34)). It is translated as the “peaks (or slopes) of Tabor.” 

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:34
