## Reba (id: 7959)

REBA
====

Reba was one of five kings of Midian. Moses had these kings killed at God's command because they had led the Israelites into worshipping false gods ([Numbers 31:8](https://ref.ly/Num31:8); [Joshua 13:21](https://ref.ly/Josh13:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 31:8; JOS 13:21
