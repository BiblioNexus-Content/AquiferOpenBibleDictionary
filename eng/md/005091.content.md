## Shemida, Shemidah, Shemidaite (id: 8353)

SHEMIDA, SHEMIDAH, SHEMIDAITE
=============================

Shemida was the father of the family group called the Shemidaites ([Numbers 26:32](https://ref.ly/Num26:32)). This clan was part of the tribe of Manasseh ([Joshua 17:2](https://ref.ly/Josh17:2); [1 Chronicles 7:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:19)). Shemidah is an alternate spelling of Shemida.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:32; JOS 17:2; 1CH 7:19
