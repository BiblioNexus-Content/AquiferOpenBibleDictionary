## Zimran (id: 9227)

ZIMRAN
======

One of the sons of Abraham by Keturah ([Gn 25:2](https://ref.ly/Gen25:2); [1 Chr 1:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:32)). Unlike the other sons of Abraham by Keturah, there is little evidence that Zimran is associated with a later tribal group.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:2; 1CH 1:32
