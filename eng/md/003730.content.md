## Mattithiah (id: 6991)

MATTITHIAH
==========

1\. Levite and Shallum’s firstborn son, who was in charge of making the baked cakes that accompanied the offerings in the temple ([1 Chr 9:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:31)).

2\. Musician appointed by the Levites to play the lyre, along with five others, when the ark was brought to Jerusalem in David’s time ([1 Chr 15:18, 21](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:18,1Chr15:21); [16:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:5)).

3\. One of Jeduthun’s six sons, who was a musician in David’s time ([1 Chr 25:3, 21](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:3,1Chr25:21)); perhaps identifiable with \#2 above.

4\. Nebo’s son, who divorced his foreign wife as commanded by Ezra ([Ezr 10:43](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:43)).

5\. One who stood to Ezra’s right when Ezra read the law to the people after the exile ([Neh 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:31; 1CH 15:18; 1CH 15:21; 1CH 16:5; 1CH 25:3; 1CH 25:21; EZR 10:43; NEH 8:4
