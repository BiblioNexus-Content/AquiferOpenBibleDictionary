## Reuel (id: 8016)

REUEL
=====

1. The son of Esau by his wife Basemath, and the father of four sons: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah ([Genesis 36:4, 10–17](https://ref.ly/Gen36:4,Gen36:10-Gen36:17)).
2. A priest of Midian who gave his daughter to Moses for a wife. He is perhaps the same person as \#1 above, and identical to Jethro ([Exodus 2:18](https://ref.ly/Exod2:18); compare [Exodus 3:1](https://ref.ly/Exod3:1)). He is also called Raguel in [Numbers 10:29](https://ref.ly/Num10:29) in the King James Version. *See* Jethro.
3. An alternate spelling of Deuel, Eliasaph’s father, in [Numbers 2:14](https://ref.ly/Num2:14). *See* Deuel.
4. An ancestor of Meshullam in Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 9:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:8)).
