## Dessau (id: 4818)

DESSAU
======

A village in Judea where Judas Maccabeus led the Jews in an attack against the forces of Nicanor ([2 Maccabees 14:16](https://ref.ly/2Macc14:16)). Nicanor was a Syrian officer under the command of Lysias.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 14:16
