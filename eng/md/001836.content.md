## Enosh (id: 5090)

ENOSH
=====

Seth’s son and the grandson of Adam ([Genesis 4:26](https://ref.ly/Gen4:26); [1 Chronicles 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:1)). He became the father of Kenan at 90 years of age, after which he had other sons and daughters, dying at the age of 905 ([Genesis 5:6](https://ref.ly/Gen5:6-Gen5:11)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen5:6-Gen5:11)[11](https://ref.ly/Gen5:6-Gen5:11)). He is mentioned as Jesus’s ancestor in [Luke 3:38](https://ref.ly/Luke3:38). Enosh is spelled “Enos” in the King James Version and Revised Standard Version. 

*See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.
