## Cheran (id: 4507)

CHERAN
======

Cheran was the son of Dishon. He was was a member of the Horite tribe during the time of Esau ([Genesis 36:26](https://ref.ly/Gen36:26); [1 Chronicles 1:41](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:41)).
