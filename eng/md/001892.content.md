## Eshcol (Person) (id: 5148)

ESHCOL (Person)
===============

Amorite who, with his brothers Mamre and Aner, helped the patriarch Abraham defeat the forces of Kedorlaomer and rescue Lot and his family ([Gn 14:13, 24](https://ref.ly/Gen14:13,Gen14:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:13; GEN 14:24
