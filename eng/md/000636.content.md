## Azaziah (id: 3905)

AZAZIAH
=======

1. A temple musician who was a Levite. He played the lyre when King David brought the ark of the covenant into Jerusalem ([1 Chronicles 15:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:21)).
2. Hoshea’s father. Hoshea was leader of the tribe of Ephraim during King David’s rule ([1 Chronicles 27:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:20)).
3. A temple administrator who was a Levite. He was appointed by King Hezekiah of Judah to help manage the offerings stored in the temple ([2 Chronicles 31:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 15:21; 1CH 27:20; 2CH 31:13
