## Japhia (Place) (id: 6216)

JAPHIA (Place)
==============

Town described as part of the southern border of Zebulun’s territory ([Jos 19:12](https://ref.ly/Josh19:12)). It has been identified with modern Yafa, about two miles (3\.2 kilometers) southwest of Nazareth.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:12
