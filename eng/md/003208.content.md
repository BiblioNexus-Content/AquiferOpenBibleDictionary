## Jotbathah (id: 6465)

JOTBATHAH
=========

Temporary camping place of the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings, located between Hor\-haggidgad and Abronah ([Nm 33:33–34](https://ref.ly/Num33:33-Num33:34)). Later, following the death of Aaron, Israel journeyed from Gudgodah to this place, noted for its streams of water ([Dt 10:7](https://ref.ly/Deut10:7)).

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
