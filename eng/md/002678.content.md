## Hothir (id: 5927)

HOTHIR
======

Levite and the head of the 21st of 24 divisions of priests for sanctuary service, formed during David’s reign ([1 Chr 25:4, 28](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4,1Chr25:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:28
