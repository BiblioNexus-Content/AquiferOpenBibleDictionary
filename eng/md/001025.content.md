## Bondage, House of (id: 4295)

HOUSE OF BONDAGE
================

A phrase used in the Old Testament to describe Egypt during the time when the Israelite people were forced to work as slaves there. This period lasted about 400 years until God helped the Israelites escape from Egypt ([Exodus 13:3](https://ref.ly/Exod13:3); [Joshua 24:17](https://ref.ly/Josh24:17)). 

*See* Exodus, Book of.
