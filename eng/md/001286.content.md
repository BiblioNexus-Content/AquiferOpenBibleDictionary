## Chloe (id: 4538)

CHLOE
=====

Woman whose household members (possibly slaves) informed Paul in Ephesus of arguments in the Corinthian church ([1 Cor 1:11](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:11)). It is not known whether Chloe lived in Corinth or Ephesus, or even whether she herself was a believer.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CO 1:11
