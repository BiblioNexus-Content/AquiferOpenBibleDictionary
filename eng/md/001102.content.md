## Bukki (id: 4357)

BUKKI
=====

1. A leader of the tribe of Dan who helped Joshua divide the land of Canaan among the Israelites ([Numbers 34:22](https://ref.ly/Num34:22)).
2. An ancestor of Ezra ([1 Chronicles 6:5, 51](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:5,1Chr6:51); [Ezra 7:1, 4–5](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:1,Ezra7:4-Ezra7:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:22; 1CH 6:5; 1CH 6:51; EZR 7:1; EZR 7:4–EZR 7:5
