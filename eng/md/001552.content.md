## Delaiah (id: 4802)

DELAIAH
=======

1\. Son of Elioenai who traced his line of descent through Zerubbabel to David ([1 Chr 3:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:24), kjv “Dalaiah”).

2\. Priest in the time of David ([1 Chr 24:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:18)).

3\. Head of a postexilic family that returned with Zerubbabel to Judea. The group was unable to prove true Israelite descent ([Ezr 2:60](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:60); [Neh 7:62](https://ref.ly/Neh7:62)).

4\. Father of a fifth\-century BC man named Shemaiah. Shemaiah opposed Nehemiah ([Neh 6:10](https://ref.ly/Neh6:10)).

5\. Counselor in the reign of Jehoiakim (609–598 BC) who urged the king not to destroy Jeremiah’s scroll, which Baruch had just read ([Jer 36:12, 25](https://ref.ly/Jer36:12,Jer36:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:24; 1CH 24:18; EZR 2:60; NEH 6:10; NEH 7:62; JER 36:12; JER 36:25
