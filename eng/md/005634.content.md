## Toi (id: 8885)

TOI
===

A king who ruled over Hamath during the time when David defeated the armies of Hadadezer ([2 Samuel 8:9–10](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:9-2Sam8:10)). He is also called Tou in [1 Chronicles 18:9–10](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:9-1Chr18:10). Toi sent his son Joram to congratulate David and to give him gifts.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:9–2SA 8:10; 1CH 18:9–1CH 18:10
