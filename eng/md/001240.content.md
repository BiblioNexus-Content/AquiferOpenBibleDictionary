## Chelal (id: 4494)

CHELAL
======

The son of Pahath\-moab. Chelal obeyed Ezra's instruction to divorce his non\-Jewish wife after the people of Israel returned from exile in Babylon ([Ezra 10:30](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:30)). It is also spelled Kelal.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:30
