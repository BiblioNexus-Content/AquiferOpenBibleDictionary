## Tahath (Person) (id: 8713)

TAHATH (Person)
===============

1\. Son of Assir and a descendant of Levi through Kohath’s line. He was an ancestor of Heman, one of David’s musicians, and the father of Uriel and Zephaniah ([1 Chr 6:24, 37](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:24,1Chr6:37)).

2\. Ephraimite, the son of Bered and the father of Eleadah ([1 Chr 7:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:20)).

3\. Ephraimite, the son of Eleadah and the father of Zabad ([1 Chr 7:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:24; 1CH 6:37; 1CH 7:20
