## Mote (id: 7218)

MOTE
====

A term used in the King James Version to describe a small piece of material stuck in the eye of a “brother” ([Matthew 7:3](https://ref.ly/Matt7:3-Matt7:5)[–](https://ref.ly/Matt7:3-Matt7:5)[5](https://ref.ly/Matt7:3-Matt7:5); [Luke 6:41](https://ref.ly/Luke6:41-Luke6:42)[–](https://ref.ly/Luke6:41-Luke6:42)[42](https://ref.ly/Luke6:41-Luke6:42)). More recent translations use the term “speck.”

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 7:3–MAT 7:5; LUK 6:41–LUK 6:42
