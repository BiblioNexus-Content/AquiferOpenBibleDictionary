## Rimmon (Person) (id: 8041)

RIMMON (Person)
===============

1. A Benjaminite from the town of Beeroth. His two sons, Baanah and Recab, killed Ishbosheth ([2 Samuel 4:2, 5, 9](https://ref.ly/2Sam4:2,2Sam4:5,2Sam4:9)). Ishbosheth was the son of King Saul and became king of Israel after his father died.
2. A god worshiped by the Syrians of Damascus. Naaman was captain of the Syrian army. He and his king often visited the temple of Rimmon ([2 Kings 5:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:18)).

    *See* Syria, Syrians.
