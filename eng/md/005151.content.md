## Shimri (id: 8416)

SHIMRI
======

1\. Simeonite, Shemaiah’s son and the father of Jedaiah ([1 Chr 4:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:37)).

2\. Father of Jediael (and perhaps Joha), two of David’s mighty men ([1 Chr 11:45](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:45)).

3\. Merarite Levite, Hosah’s son and a temple gatekeeper during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:10)).

4\. Levite, of the family of Elizaphan, who assisted in Hezekiah’s temple reforms ([2 Chr 29:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:37; 1CH 11:45; 1CH 26:10; 2CH 29:13
