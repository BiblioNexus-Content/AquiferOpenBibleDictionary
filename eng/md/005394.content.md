## Suph (id: 8656)

SUPH
====

Region mentioned in [Deuteronomy 1:1](https://ref.ly/Deut1:1), which was “beyond the Jordan in the wilderness, in the Arabah over against Suph” (rsv; kjv “the Red Sea”). The exact location of Suph is uncertain. It may refer to the region of Suphah (cf. [Nm 21:14](https://ref.ly/Num21:14)), east of the Jordan River, or perhaps to the Gulf of Aqaba, the northeastern branch of the Red Sea.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 21:14; DEU 1:1
