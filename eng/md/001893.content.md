## Eshcol (Place) (id: 5149)

ESHCOL (Place)
==============

Valley near Hebron from which the spies sent by Moses brought back pomegranates, figs, and a large cluster of grapes ([Nm 13:23–24](https://ref.ly/Num13:23-Num13:24); [32:9](https://ref.ly/Num32:9); [Dt 1:24](https://ref.ly/Deut1:24)). This site may be identifiable with ’Ain Eshkali, just north of Hebron.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 32:9; DEU 1:24; NUM 13:23–NUM 13:24
