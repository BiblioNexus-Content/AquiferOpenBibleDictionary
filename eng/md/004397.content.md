## Peor (Deity) (id: 7655)

PEOR (deity)
============

A shortened form of the name of the Canaanite god called Baal\-peor. It may also refer to the place where this false god was worshipped ([Numbers 23:28](https://ref.ly/Num23:28); [25:3, 5](https://ref.ly/Num25:3,Num25:5)). 

*See* Baal\-peor; Canaanite Deities and Religion.
