## Jerusha, Jerushah (id: 6334)

JERUSHA, JERUSHAH\*
===================

Zadok’s daughter, wife of King Uzziah of Judah and mother of King Jotham ([2 Kgs 15:33](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:33); [2 Chr 27:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr27:1), alternately spelled “Jerushah”).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 15:33; 2CH 27:1
