## Solomon’s Porch (id: 8565)

SOLOMON’S PORCH
===============

A covered walkway or colonnade that was part of the outer court of Herod's temple in Jerusalem ([John 10:23](https://ref.ly/John10:23); [Acts 3:11](https://ref.ly/Acts3:11); [5:12](https://ref.ly/Acts5:12)).
