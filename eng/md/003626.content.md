## Makheloth (id: 6884)

MAKHELOTH
=========

A place where the Israelites camped during their journey from Egypt to Canaan. They stopped here after leaving Haradah and before going to Tahath ([Numbers 33:25–26](https://ref.ly/Num33:25-Num33:26)). 

*See* Wilderness Wanderings.
