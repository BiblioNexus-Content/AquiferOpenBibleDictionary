## Adamah (id: 3361)

ADAMAH
======

One of the 19 strong cities belonging to the tribe of Naphtali ([Joshua 19:36](https://ref.ly/Josh19:36)). It might be modern Qarn Hattin.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:36
