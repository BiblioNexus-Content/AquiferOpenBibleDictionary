## Zemaraim (id: 9177)

ZEMARAIM
========

1\. Town near the northern border of the territory of Benjamin ([Jos 18:22](https://ref.ly/Josh18:22)). The most likely location is Ras ez\-Zeimara, about five miles (8 kilometers) northeast of Bethel in the hill country between et\-Taiyibeh and Rammum.

2\. Mountain in the hill country of Ephraim ([2 Chr 13:4](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:4)) and the scene of Abijah’s speech of rebuke against Jeroboam and the Israelites.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 18:22; 2CH 13:4
