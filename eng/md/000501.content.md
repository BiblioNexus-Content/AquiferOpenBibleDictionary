## Artemas (id: 3767)

ARTEMAS
=======

Christian coworker with the apostle Paul. Paul considered Artemas as a replacement for Titus on the island of Crete ([Titus 3:12](https://ref.ly/Titus3:12)). Later tradition describes Artemas as bishop of Lystra.

* **Associated Passages:** TIT 3:12
