## Sabbath, Covert for the (id: 8079)

SABBATH\*, Covert for the
=========================

Covered place in the court of the temple reserved for the king who stood there with his attendants on a Sabbath or feast day ([2 Kgs 16:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs16:18), kjv; nlt “canopy”).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 16:18
