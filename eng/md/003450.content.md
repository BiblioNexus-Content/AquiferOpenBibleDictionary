## Lebbaeus (id: 6708)

LEBBAEUS
========

An alternate name in the King James Version given to Thaddaeus, one of the 12 disciples, in [Matthew 10:3](https://ref.ly/Matt10:3). Most versions do not include the name, which comes from a textual variant followed by the King James Version's translators. 

*See* Thaddaeus, The Apostle.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 10:3
* **Associated Articles:** Thaddaeus, the Apostle (ID: `8801@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
