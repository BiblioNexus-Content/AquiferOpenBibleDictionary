## Joel (Person) (id: 6401)

JOEL (Person)
=============

1\. Levite from the family of Kohath. He was Azariah’s son and an ancestor of Elkanah, the father of Samuel the prophet ([1 Samuel 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:1); [1 Chronicles 6:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:36)).

2\. Oldest son of Samuel the prophet. He and his brother Abijah so corrupted the office of judge that the elders increased their demands for a king ([1 Samuel 8:2–5](https://ref.ly/1Sam8:2-1Sam8:5)). He was the father of Heman the singer ([1 Chronicles 6:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:33); [15:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:7)). His name has been mistakenly translated “Vashni” in the KJV in [1 Chronicles 6:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:33).

3\. Prince from one of the Simeonite families that emigrated to the valley of Gedor ([1 Chronicles 4:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:35)).

4\. Member of Reuben’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 5:4, 8](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:4,1Chr5:8)).

5\. Chief of Gad’s tribe residing in Bashan ([1 Chronicles 5:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:12)).

6\. Third of Izrahiah’s four named sons and a chief of Issachar’s tribe in David’s time ([1 Chronicles 7:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:3)).

7\. Nathan’s brother and one of David’s mighty men ([1 Chronicles 11:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:38)). He is alternately called Igal the son of Nathan in [2 Samuel 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:36). *See* Igal \#2.

8\. Levite from the family of Gershon who took part in the royal procession that brought the ark of God to Jerusalem during David’s reign ([1 Chronicles 15:7–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:7-1Chr15:11)). He may have managed the treasuries of the temple in Jerusalem ([1 Chronicles 26:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:22)).

9\. Pedaiah’s son, who served as the leader of the west half of Manasseh’s tribe during David’s reign ([1 Chronicles 27:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:20))

10\. Levite from the family of Kohath who helped with King Hezekiah’s reform of the temple in Jerusalem ([2 Chronicles 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)).

11\. Nebo’s son, who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife during the postexilic period ([Ezra 10:43](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:43)).

12\. Zicri’s son and the supervisor of 128 Benjaminites who relocated to Jerusalem after the exile ([Nehemiah 11:9](https://ref.ly/Neh11:9)).

13\. Prophet who wrote the second book of the Minor Prophets. Little is known about him except that he was Pethuel’s son ([Joel 1:1](https://ref.ly/Joel1:1); [Acts 2:16](https://ref.ly/Acts2:16)). *See* Joel, Book of.
