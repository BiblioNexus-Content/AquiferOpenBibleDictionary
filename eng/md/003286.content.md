## Kenan (id: 6546)

KENAN
=====

A descendant of Adam. He lived four generations after Adam, as the son of Enosh ([Genesis 5:9–14](https://ref.ly/Gen5:9-Gen5:14); [1 Chronicles 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:2)). He is also called Cainan in [Luke 3:37](https://ref.ly/Luke3:37). 

*See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:2; LUK 3:37; GEN 5:9–GEN 5:14
* **Associated Articles:** Genealogy of Jesus Christ (ID: `5412@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
