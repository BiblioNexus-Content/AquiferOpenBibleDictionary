## Kenaanah (id: 6543)

KENAANAH
========

1\. Father of Zedekiah, the false prophet who incorrectly prophesied victory for kings Ahab and Jehoshaphat over the Syrians ([1 Kgs 22:11, 24](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:11,1Kgs22:24); [2 Chr 18:10, 23](https://ref.ly/2Chr18:10,2Chr18:23)).

2\. Bilhan’s son, who was chief of the subclan of Jediael in Benjamin’s tribe in the time of King David ([1 Chr 7:10–11](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:10-1Chr7:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 22:11; 1KI 22:24; 2CH 18:10; 2CH 18:23; 1CH 7:10–1CH 7:11
