## Alush (id: 3531)

ALUSH
=====

A place where Israel set up camp during their journey through the wilderness. [Numbers 33:13–14](https://ref.ly/Num33:13-Num33:14) mentions it as being between Dophkah and Rephidim on the way to Mount Sinai.

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
