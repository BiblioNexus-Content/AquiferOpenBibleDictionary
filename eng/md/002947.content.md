## Jambri (id: 6200)

JAMBRI
======

Ancestor of an Arab tribe. During the Hasmonean (Maccabean) wars when Jonathan was ruler, the Jambrites captured a baggage train of the Jews when it was being sent to the Nabateans for safekeeping ([1 Macc 9:36](https://ref.ly/1Macc9:36)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 9:36
