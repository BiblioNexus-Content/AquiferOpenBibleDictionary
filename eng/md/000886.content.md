## Beth-Ashbea (id: 4157)

BETH\-ASHBEA
============

The place where some families from Judah lived. These families were known for their production of linen ([1 Chronicles 4:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:21)). The King James Version calls “Ashbea” a family name rather than a place of residence.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:21
