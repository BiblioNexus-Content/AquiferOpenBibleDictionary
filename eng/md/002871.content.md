## Izhar (id: 6124)

IZHAR
=====

1. One of Kohath’s sons from the tribe of Levi ([Exodus 6:18, 21](https://ref.ly/Exod6:18,Exod6:21); [Numbers 3:19](https://ref.ly/Num3:19); [16:1](https://ref.ly/Num16:1); [1 Chronicles 6:2, 18, 38](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:2,1Chr6:18,1Chr6:38); [23:12, 18](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:12,1Chr23:18)). He was the father of the Izharite family ([Numbers 3:27](https://ref.ly/Num3:27); [1 Chronicles 24:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:22); [26:23, 29](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:23,1Chr26:29)). He is also called Amminadab in [1 Chronicles 6:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:22). One of Izhar’s sons was Korah, who led the rebellion against Moses and Aaron ([Numbers 16:1–11](https://ref.ly/Num16:1-Num16:11)).
2. Helah’s son from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 4:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:7)).
