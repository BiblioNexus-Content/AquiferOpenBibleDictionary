## Thunder, Sons of (id: 8827)

THUNDER, Sons of
================

"Sons of Thunder" is the literal meaning of the word "Boanerges." Jesus gave this nickname to James and John ([Mark 3:17](https://ref.ly/Mark3:17)). *See* Boanerges.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 3:17
