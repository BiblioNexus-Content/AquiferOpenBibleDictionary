## Raphanah (id: 7945)

RAPHANAH\*
==========

One of the original 10 Greek cities rebuilt by Rome after Pompey’s conquest of Palestine and Syria around 63 BC. Raphanah (also spelled Raphana) was situated in the Decapolis region. *See* Decapolis.
