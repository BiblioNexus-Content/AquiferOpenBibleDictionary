## Imri (id: 6014)

IMRI
====

1\. Ancestor of Uthai, one of the postexilic Jews of Judah’s tribe ([1 Chr 9:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:4)). In the genealogy of [Nehemiah 11:4](https://ref.ly/Neh11:4), Imri and Amariah are probably the same person.

2\. Father of Zaccur, a rebuilder of the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 3:2](https://ref.ly/Neh3:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:4; NEH 3:2; NEH 11:4
