## Pildash (id: 7735)

PILDASH
=======

The sixth son of Nahor and Milcah. He was a nephew of the patriarch Abraham ([Genesis 22:22](https://ref.ly/Gen22:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 22:22
