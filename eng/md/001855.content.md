## Ephod (Person) (id: 5109)

EPHOD (Person)
==============

The father of Hanniel. Hanniel was a leader from the tribe of Manasseh, one of the two tribes that came from Joseph ([Numbers 34:23](https://ref.ly/Num34:23)). He helped divide the land of Canaan among the tribes of Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:23
