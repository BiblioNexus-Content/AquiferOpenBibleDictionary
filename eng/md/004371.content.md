## Pelaiah (id: 7629)

PELAIAH
=======

1\. Elioenai’s son and a remote descendant of David ([1 Chr 3:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:24)).

2\. Levite who helped Ezra explain (or translate) the Law to the people after it was read to them ([Neh 8:7](https://ref.ly/Neh8:7); [10:10](https://ref.ly/Neh10:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:24; NEH 8:7; NEH 10:10
