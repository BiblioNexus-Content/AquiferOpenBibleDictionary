## Sarid (id: 8151)

SARID
=====

Town located in the region of Zebulun near its southern border, situated between Maralah to the west and Kisloth\-tabor to the east ([Jos 19:10, 12](https://ref.ly/Josh19:10,Josh19:12)). Some suggest that this town is the same as Tell Shadud, a town near the valley of Jezreel.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:10; JOS 19:12
