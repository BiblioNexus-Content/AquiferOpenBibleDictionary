## Jezreelite (id: 6376)

JEZREELITE\*
============

Inhabitant of one of two cities named Jezreel. Two such persons are specifically named:

1\. Naboth, who lived in the Jezreel in Issachar’s territory ([1 Kgs 21:1–16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs21:1-1Kgs21:16); [2 Kgs 9:21, 25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs9:21,2Kgs9:25)). *See* Jezreel (Place) \#1.

2\. David’s wife Ahinoam, who was a native of the Jezreel in Judah’s territory ([1 Chr 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:1)). *See* Jezreel (Place) \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 9:21; 2KI 9:25; 1CH 3:1; 1KI 21:1–1KI 21:16
