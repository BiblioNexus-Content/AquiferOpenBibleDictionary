## Aram (Person) (id: 3700)

ARAM (Person)
=============

1. Shem’s son and Noah’s grandson ([Genesis 10:22–23](https://ref.ly/Gen10:22-Gen10:23); [1 Chronicles 1:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:17)). The ancestor of the Arameans. *See* Syria, Syrians.
2. Kemuel’s son, the grandson of Abraham’s brother Nahor ([Genesis 22:21](https://ref.ly/Gen22:21)).
3. Shemer’s son from Asher’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 7:34](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:34)).
4. The Aram used in the list of the ancestors of Jesus Christ ([Matthew 1:3–4](https://ref.ly/Matt1:3-Matt1:4), King James Version) is a mistranslation of the Greek word *Aram* which means “Ram." It is an entirely different name ([Ruth 4:19](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:19)). *See* Ram (Person) \#1.
