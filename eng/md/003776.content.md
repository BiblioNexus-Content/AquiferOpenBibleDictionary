## Melita (id: 7036)

MELITA
======

The King James Version form of Malta, an island south of Sicily in the Mediterranean Sea ([Acts 28:1](https://ref.ly/Acts28:1)). This is where Paul's ship was wrecked during a storm while he was being transported to Rome as a prisoner.
