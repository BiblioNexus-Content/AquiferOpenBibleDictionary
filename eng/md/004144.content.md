## Night Monster (id: 7402)

NIGHT MONSTER
=============

A term used in some Bible translations for a bird that lives in desert areas ([Isaiah 34:14](https://ref.ly/Isa34:14)). Modern translations usually identify this creature as an owl. In other translations, this same creature is called a "night creature," "night hag," or "screech owl."

*See* Birds (Owl, Scops).
