## Adami, Adami-Nekeb (id: 3362)

ADAMI, ADAMI\-NEKEB
===================

The names of a city located near the southern border of Naphtali ([Joshua 19:33](https://ref.ly/Josh19:33)). It is usually identified as modern Khirbet ed\-Damiyeh.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:33
