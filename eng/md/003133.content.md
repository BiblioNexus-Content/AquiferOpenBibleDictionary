## Joarib (id: 6390)

JOARIB
======

Ancestor of Mattathias ([1 Macc 2:1](https://ref.ly/1Macc2:1); [14:29](https://ref.ly/1Macc14:29)), and according to [1 Chronicles 24:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:7) (there spelled Jehoiarib), head of the first of the 24 courses of priests in David’s time.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:7; 1MA 2:1; 1MA 14:29
