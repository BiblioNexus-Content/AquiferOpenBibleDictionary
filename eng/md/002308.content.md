## Hachilah (id: 5563)

HACHILAH
========

An unidentified site in Horesh, near the city of Hebron. David fled here when Saul tried to kill him ([1 Samuel 23:19](https://ref.ly/1Sam23:19); [26:1, 3](https://ref.ly/1Sam26:1,1Sam26:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 23:19; 1SA 26:1; 1SA 26:3
