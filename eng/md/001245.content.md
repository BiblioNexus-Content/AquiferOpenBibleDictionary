## Chelub (id: 4499)

CHELUB
======

1. The brother of Shuhah and the father of Mehir from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 4:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:11)).
2. The father of Ezri. Ezri was in charge of the workers who farmed King David's fields ([1 Chronicles 27:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:11; 1CH 27:26
