## Chelubai (id: 4498)

CHELUBAI
========

A different spelling of the name Caleb. He was the son of Hezron and the brother of Jerahmeel ([1 Chronicles 2:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:9)). In Hebrew, "Chelubai" is a variant form of the name "Caleb," as shown in [1 Chronicles 2:18, 42](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:18,1Chr2:42). 

*See* Caleb \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:9; 1CH 2:18; 1CH 2:42
