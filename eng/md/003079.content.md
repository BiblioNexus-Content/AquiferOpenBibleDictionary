## Jeshaiah (id: 6335)

JESHAIAH
========

1\. Hananiah’s son; the father of Rephaiah and a descendant of David through Zerubbabel’s line, who lived in postexilic Palestine ([1 Chr 3:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:21)).

2\. Jeduthun’s son and the leader of the eighth of 24 divisions of musicians trained for service in the sanctuary during David’s reign ([1 Chr 25:3, 15](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:3,1Chr25:15)).

3\. Rehabiah’s son and one of the Levites in charge of the temple treasury during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:25)).

4\. Son of Athaliah from the house of Elam, who returned with Ezra to Judah following the Babylonian captivity ([Ezr 8:7](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:7)).

5\. Levite of the family of Merari, who returned with Ezra to Jerusalem after the exile ([Ezr 8:19](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:19)).

6\. Benjamite, Ithiel’s father, and an ancestor of Sallu. Sallu resettled in Jerusalem during the postexilic era ([Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:21; 1CH 25:3; 1CH 25:15; 1CH 26:25; EZR 8:7; EZR 8:19; NEH 11:7
