## Remeth (id: 7992)

REMETH
======

Border town in Issachar’s territory ([Jos 19:21](https://ref.ly/Josh19:21)), and probably the same as Ramoth ([1 Chr 6:73](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:73)), also called Jarmuth. *See* Jarmuth \#2.
