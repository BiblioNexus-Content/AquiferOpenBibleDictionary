## Samlah (id: 8123)

SAMLAH
======

King of the Edomites from the town of Masrekah. Samlah came to power before any king ruled in Israel ([Gn 36:36–37](https://ref.ly/Gen36:36-Gen36:37); [1 Chr 1:47–48](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:47-1Chr1:48)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:36–GEN 36:37; 1CH 1:47–1CH 1:48
