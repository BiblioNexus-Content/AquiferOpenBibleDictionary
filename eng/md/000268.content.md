## Alphaeus (id: 3528)

ALPHAEUS
========

1\. Father of James, one of the 12 apostles ([Matthew 10:3](https://ref.ly/Matt10:3); [Mark 3:18](https://ref.ly/Mark3:18); [Luke 6:15](https://ref.ly/Luke6:15); [Acts 1:13](https://ref.ly/Acts1:13)). He is thought by some to be the same as Clopas of [John 19:25](https://ref.ly/John19:25).

2\. Father of Levi, the tax collector ([Mark 2:14](https://ref.ly/Mark2:14)). He is also known in the gospels as Matthew ([Matthew 9:9](https://ref.ly/Matt9:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 9:9; MAT 10:3; MRK 2:14; MRK 3:18; LUK 6:15; JHN 19:25; ACT 1:13
