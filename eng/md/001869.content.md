## Ephron (Place) (id: 5124)

EPHRON (Place)
==============

1\. Mountainous district on the northern edge of Judah ([Jos 15:9](https://ref.ly/Josh15:9)).

2\. City near Bethel, captured by Abijah ([2 Chr 13:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:19)); perhaps the Ophrah of [Joshua 18:23](https://ref.ly/Josh18:23).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:9; JOS 18:23; 2CH 13:19
