## Scurvy (id: 8176)

SCURVY
======

Term used three times in the Bible ([Lv 21:20](https://ref.ly/Lev21:20) and [22:22](https://ref.ly/Lev22:22), kjv; [Dt 28:27](https://ref.ly/Deut28:27), nlt). In none of the instances does it refer to the modern disease by the same name, which results from severe vitamin C deficiency. In the Leviticus passages, “scurvy” is found in the sequence “scurvy or scabbed.” This phrase is translated “oozing sores or scabs on his skin” in the nlt. *See* Disease; Medicine and Medical Practice.

* **Associated Passages:** LEV 21:20; LEV 22:22; DEU 28:27
