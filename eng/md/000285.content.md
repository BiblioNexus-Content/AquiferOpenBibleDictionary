## Amaw (id: 3546)

AMAW
====

A region near the Euphrates River. It included the city of Pethor, to which King Balak of Moab sent messengers in search of the prophet Balaam ([Numbers 22:5](https://ref.ly/Num22:5)). Amaw's name appears in the Idrimi Inscription from 1450 BC. It also appears on the tomb of Qen\-amun, who served under Amenophis II of Egypt near the end of the 15th century BC.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 22:5
