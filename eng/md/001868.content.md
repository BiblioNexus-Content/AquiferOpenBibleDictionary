## Ephron (Person) (id: 5123)

EPHRON (Person)
===============

Hittite from whom Abraham purchased the cave of Machpelah with its adjoining field for 400 shekels of silver ([Gn 23:8–17](https://ref.ly/Gen23:8-Gen23:17)). Sarah was buried there, as was Abraham ([25:9](https://ref.ly/Gen25:9)) and Jacob ([50:13](https://ref.ly/Gen50:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:9; GEN 50:13; GEN 23:8–GEN 23:17
