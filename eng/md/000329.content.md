## Anan (id: 3596)

ANAN
====

One of the chiefs of the people. Anan agreed to follow God's law by putting his seal on a covenant organized by Ezra. The covenant was a special agreement meant to help the people obey God's teachings ([Nehemiah 10:26](https://ref.ly/Neh10:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 10:26
