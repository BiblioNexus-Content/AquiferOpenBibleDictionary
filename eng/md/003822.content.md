## Meshelemiah (id: 7081)

MESHELEMIAH
===========

Korahite Levite, Kore’s son from the house of Asaph, and a gatekeeper of the sanctuary with his sons in the time of David ([1 Chr 9:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:21); [26:1–2, 9](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:1-1Chr26:2,1Chr26:9)); alternately called Shelemiah in [1 Chronicles 26:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:14).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:21; 1CH 26:9; 1CH 26:14; 1CH 26:1–1CH 26:2
