## Meshezabeel, Meshezabel (id: 7082)

MESHEZABEEL\*, MESHEZABEL
=========================

1\. Ancestor of Meshullam who helped repair the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 3:4](https://ref.ly/Neh3:4)).

2\. Political leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God during the postexilic period ([Neh 10:21](https://ref.ly/Neh10:21)).

3\. Father of Pethahiah, an adviser to King Artaxerxes regarding the people in Judah ([Neh 11:24](https://ref.ly/Neh11:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:4; NEH 10:21; NEH 11:24
