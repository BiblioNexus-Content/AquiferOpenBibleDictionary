## Hezion (id: 5835)

HEZION
======

Tabrimmon’s father and the grandfather of Ben\-hadad, king of Syria. Ben\-hadad formed an alliance with King Asa of Judah (910–869 BC) and opposed Israel’s King Baasha (908–886 BC; [1 Kgs 15:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 15:18
