## Rakkon (id: 7919)

RAKKON
======

One of the cities assigned to Dan’s tribe ([Jos 19:46](https://ref.ly/Josh19:46)). It is identified today with Tell er\-Ragguat, one and a half miles (2\.4 kilometers) north of the mouth of the Yorkon River.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:46
