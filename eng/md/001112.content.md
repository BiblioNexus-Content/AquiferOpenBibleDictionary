## Bushel (id: 4366)

BUSHEL
======

1\. A small vessel (“basket,” New Living Translation) that could cover a light ([Matthew 5:15](https://ref.ly/Matt5:15); [Mark 4:21](https://ref.ly/Mark4:21); [Luke 11:33](https://ref.ly/Luke11:33)). *See* Weights and Measures.

2\. A unit of measure roughly equal to 19 quarts or 18 liters. In Hebrew, this is called an "ephah." 

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 5:15; MRK 4:21; LUK 11:33
* **Associated Articles:** Weights and Measures (ID: `9051@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
