## Buttercup (id: 715987)

Buttercup
=========

The Persian buttercup (*Ranunculus asiaticus*) is one of the flowers or grasses of the field ([Matthew 6:28–30](https://ref.ly/Matt6:28-Matt6:30)). It is a showy plant that blooms in all brilliant colors except blue. Its double flowers sometimes measure 5\.1 centimeters (two inches) across.
