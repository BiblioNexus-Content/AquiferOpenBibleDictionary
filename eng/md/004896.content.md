## Scall (id: 8162)

SCALL
=====

A scall is a term used in the King James Version of the Bible for a certain type of skin condition. It refers to a scaly or crusty skin eruption or rash mentioned in [Leviticus 13:30–37](https://ref.ly/Lev13:30-Lev13:37) and [14:54](https://ref.ly/Lev14:54). 

*See* Sore.
