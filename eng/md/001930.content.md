## Eupolemus (id: 5187)

EUPOLEMUS
=========

A Jewish ambassador sent by Judas Maccabeus to Rome along with Jason, the son of Eleazar, to establish an alliance. He is identified in [1 Maccabees 8:17](https://ref.ly/1Macc8:17) as the son of John, the son of Accos.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 8:17
