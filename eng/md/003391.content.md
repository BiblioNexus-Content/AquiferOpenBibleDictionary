## Koz (id: 6649)

KOZ
===

1\. Descendant of Judah and possibly an ancestor of the priestly house of Hakkoz ([1 Chr 4:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:8)).

2\. KJV rendering of the priestly family of Hakkoz ([Ezr 2:61](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:61); [Neh 3:4, 21](https://ref.ly/Neh3:4,Neh3:21); [7:63](https://ref.ly/Neh7:63)); perhaps identifiable with \#1 above. *See* Hakkoz.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:8; EZR 2:61; NEH 3:4; NEH 3:21; NEH 7:63
* **Associated Articles:** Hakkoz (ID: `5601@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
