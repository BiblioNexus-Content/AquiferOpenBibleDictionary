## Geder (id: 5401)

GEDER
=====

One of the 31 royal cities in Canaan, whose kings were defeated by Joshua ([Jos 12:13](https://ref.ly/Josh12:13)). Geder is perhaps identifiable with Gedor in the mountains of Judah ([15:58](https://ref.ly/Josh15:58)) or with Beth\-gader ([1 Chr 2:51](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:51)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 12:13; JOS 15:58; 1CH 2:51
