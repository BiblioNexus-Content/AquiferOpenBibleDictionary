## Hephzibah (id: 5808)

HEPHZIBAH
=========

1\. Mother of Manasseh, king of Judah ([2 Kgs 21:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:1)).

2\. Symbolic name (kjv) for the restored city of Jerusalem, meaning “my delight is in her” ([Is 62:4](https://ref.ly/Isa62:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 21:1; ISA 62:4
