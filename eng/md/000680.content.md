## Baana (id: 3951)

BAANA
=====

1. Ahilud’s son. He was one of 12 officers assigned to retrieve food for King Solomon’s household. He served in the district of Taanach and Megiddo ([1 Kings 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:12)).
2. Hushai’s son, and another of King Solomon’s supply officers; his district was Asher and Aloth ([1 Kings 4:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:16)).
3. Zadok’s father. Zadok helped Nehemiah rebuild the Jerusalem wall ([Nehemiah 3:4](https://ref.ly/Neh3:4)). He might be the same as Baanah ([Nehemiah 10:27](https://ref.ly/Neh10:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:12; 1KI 4:16; NEH 3:4; NEH 10:27
