## Chileab (id: 4526)

CHILEAB
=======

The second son of King David. Chileab was the first child born to him by Abigail ([2 Samuel 3:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:3)). Another name for Kileab is Daniel in [1 Chronicles 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:1).
