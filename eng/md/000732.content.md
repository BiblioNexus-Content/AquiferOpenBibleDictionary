## Barak (id: 4002)

BARAK
=====

The son of Abinoam of Kedesh in Naphtali ([Judges 4:6](https://ref.ly/Judg4:6); [5:1](https://ref.ly/Judg5:1)). He was a partner of the prophetess Deborah. Barak led an army of Israel that defeated the forces of Jabin, king of the Canaanites ([Judges 4](https://ref.ly/Judg4:1-Judg4:24)). Barak is one of the heroes of faith listed in the New Testament ([Hebrews 11:32](https://ref.ly/Heb11:32)). 

*See* Deborah \#2; Judges, Book of.
