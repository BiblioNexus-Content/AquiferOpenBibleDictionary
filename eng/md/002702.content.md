## Huram (id: 5951)

HURAM
=====

1. A son of Bela from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:5)). He may be the same person as Hupham ([Numbers 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39)).
2. Another spelling of Hiram, the Phoenician king of Tyre who was an ally of David and Solomon. Hiram supplied materials for the building of the temple ([2 Chronicles 2:3, 11–12](https://ref.ly/2Chr2:3,2Chr2:11-2Chr2:12); [8:2, 18](https://ref.ly/2Chr8:2,2Chr8:18); [9:10, 21](https://ref.ly/2Chr9:10,2Chr9:21)).

    *See* Hiram \#1.

3. Another spelling of Hiram, a craftsman from Tyre who worked on the temple of Solomon ([2 Chronicles 4:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr4:11)).

    *See* Hiram \#2.
