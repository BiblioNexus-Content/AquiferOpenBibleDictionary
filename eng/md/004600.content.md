## Purim (id: 7863)

PURIM
=====

The Hebrew name for the Jewish festival celebrating the Jewish people's rescue from Haman ([Esther 9:26–32](https://ref.ly/Esth9:26-Esth9:32)). Purim means "lots," small objects used for making decisions.

*See* Feasts and Festivals of Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 9:26–EST 9:32
