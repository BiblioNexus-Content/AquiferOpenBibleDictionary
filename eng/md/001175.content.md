## Carmi (id: 4432)

CARMI
=====

1. One of Reuben’s sons; he joined his grandfather Jacob in moving to Egypt ([Genesis 46:9](https://ref.ly/Gen46:9); [Exodus 6:14](https://ref.ly/Exod6:14); [1 Chronicles 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:3)). He founded the family of Carmites ([Numbers 26:5–7](https://ref.ly/Num26:5-Num26:7)).
2. Achan’s father and a member of the tribe of Judah ([Joshua 7:1, 18](https://ref.ly/Josh7:1,Josh7:18); [1 Chronicles 2:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:7); [4:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:9; EXO 6:14; JOS 7:1; JOS 7:18; 1CH 2:7; 1CH 4:1; 1CH 5:3; NUM 26:5–NUM 26:7
