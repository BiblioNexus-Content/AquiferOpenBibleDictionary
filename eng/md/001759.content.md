## Elihu (id: 5010)

ELIHU
=====

1\. Ephraimite, Tohu’s son and an ancestor of Samuel the prophet ([1 Sm 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:1)); perhaps also called Eliab and Eliel in [1 Chronicles 6:27, 34](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:27,1Chr6:34), respectively.

2\. One of the soldiers of Manasseh’s tribe who joined up with David’s army at Ziklag ([1 Chr 12:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:20)).

3\. Korahite Levite and a gatekeeper of the tabernacle during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:7)).

4\. Alternate name for Eliab, David’s eldest brother, in [1 Chronicles 27:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:18). *See* Eliab \#3.

5\. One of Job’s friends, a Buzite, the son of Barachel ([Jb 32:2](https://ref.ly/Job32:2)). He spoke about suffering as a form of discipline after three of Job’s friends failed to answer Job’s arguments (chs [32–37](https://ref.ly/Job32:1-Job37:24)).
