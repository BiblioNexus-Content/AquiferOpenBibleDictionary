## Gedor (Place) (id: 5403)

GEDOR (Place)
=============

1\. City in the Shephelah (hill country) allotted to Judah’s tribe ([Jos 15:58](https://ref.ly/Josh15:58)) named with Halhul, Beth\-zur, Maarath, Beth\-anoth, and Eltekon. It has been identified with Khirbet Gedur north of Hebron near Bethlehem.

2\. Place founded by Penuel, one of the families of Judah ([1 Chr 4:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:4)).

3\. Settlement established by Jered of Judah ([1 Chr 4:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:18)).

4\. City and its valley settled by the Simeonites ([1 Chr 4:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:39)).

5\. Town in the territory of Benjamin and the home of Joelah and Zebadiah, the sons of Jeroham ([1 Chr 12:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:7)); perhaps the same as \#1 above.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:58; 1CH 4:4; 1CH 4:18; 1CH 4:39; 1CH 12:7
