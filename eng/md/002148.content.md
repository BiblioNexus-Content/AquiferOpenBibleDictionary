## Ge-Harashim (id: 5404)

GE\-HARASHIM\*
==============

Name for a richly wooded valley near Lod and Ono, settled by Joab from Judah’s tribe, whose posterity called the valley Ge\-harashim, meaning “valley of craftsmen,” after their own trade ([1 Chr 4:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:14), nlt mg). In the fifth century BC the area was resettled by people from Benjamin’s tribe ([Neh 11:35](https://ref.ly/Neh11:35), “valley of craftsmen“).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:14; NEH 11:35
