## Elasa (id: 4967)

ELASA
=====

Mentioned in [1 Maccabees 9:5](https://ref.ly/1Macc9:5) as the place where Judas Maccabeus pitched his tents when Bacchides engaged him in battle and Judas was killed ([9:18](https://ref.ly/1Macc9:18)). It is usually identified with Il’asa near Beth\-horon.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 9:5; 1MA 9:18
