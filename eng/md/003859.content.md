## Micmash (id: 7118)

MICMASH
=======

Micmash is another way to spell Michmash. Michmash was a town in the southern part of the hill country of Ephraim. It was near the edge of the wilderness that slopes down to the Jordan Valley in the east.

*See* Michmash.
