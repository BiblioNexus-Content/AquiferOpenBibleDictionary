## Meshullemeth (id: 7087)

MESHULLEMETH
============

Mother of Amon, king of Judah (642–640 BC) and the daughter of Haruz of Jotbah ([2 Kgs 21:19](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 21:19
