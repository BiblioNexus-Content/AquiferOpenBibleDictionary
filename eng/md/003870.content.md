## Mikneiah (id: 7130)

MIKNEIAH
========

Levite of the second order who was a gatekeeper and musician during David’s reign ([1 Chr 15:18, 21](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:18,1Chr15:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 15:18; 1CH 15:21
