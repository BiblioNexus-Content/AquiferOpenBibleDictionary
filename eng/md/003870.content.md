## Mikloth (id: 7129)

MIKLOTH
=======

1\. Resident of Gibeon, son of the Benjamite Jeiel, and father of Shimeah ([1 Chr 8:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:32); [9:37–38](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:37-1Chr9:38)).

2\. Officer in David’s army who served under Dodai ([1 Chr 27:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:4)), according to some manuscripts.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 8:32; 1CH 27:4; 1CH 9:37–1CH 9:38
