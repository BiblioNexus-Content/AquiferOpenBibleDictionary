## Asarelah (id: 3782)

ASARELAH
========

One of Asaph’s four sons. King David appointed him to help with prophecy and music in the sanctuary ([1 Chronicles 25:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:2)). He is also called Jesharelah in [1 Chronicles 25:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:14).
