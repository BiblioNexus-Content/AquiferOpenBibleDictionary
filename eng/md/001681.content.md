## Ebronah (id: 4932)

EBRONAH
=======

The King James Version form of Abronah. Abronah was one of the places where the Israelites camped during their journey through the wilderness after leaving Egypt ([Numbers 33:34–35](https://ref.ly/Num33:34-Num33:35)).
