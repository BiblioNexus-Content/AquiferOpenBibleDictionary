## Beth-Zaith (id: 4211)

BETH\-ZAITH
===========

A city where Bacchides, a Syrian general of the Maccabean period, camped after his massacre of 60 Hasidian Jews in Jerusalem ([1 Maccabees 7:19](https://ref.ly/1Macc7:19)). At Beth\-zaith, more Jews were killed and thrown into a pit. Beth\-zaith is identified with modern Beit Zeita, near Bethlehem.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 7:19
