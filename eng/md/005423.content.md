## Syntyche (id: 8683)

SYNTYCHE
========

Woman encouraged by Paul to reconcile her differences with Euodia. Syntyche worked with Paul in proclaiming the gospel and evidently held a position of leadership in the Philippian church ([Phil 4:2](https://ref.ly/Phil4:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** PHP 4:2
