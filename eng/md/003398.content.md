## Laban (Place) (id: 6656)

LABAN (Place)
=============

Israelite camping place in Sinai ([Dt 1:1](https://ref.ly/Deut1:1)). Some equate it with the Libnah of [Numbers 33:20–21](https://ref.ly/Num33:20-Num33:21). Proposals for its location have ranged from just south of Rabbath\-ammon to the Arabian coast south of Elath. Its site is still unknown.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 1:1; NUM 33:20–NUM 33:21
