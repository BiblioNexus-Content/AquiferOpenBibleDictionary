## Jubal (id: 6472)

JUBAL
=====

A son of Adah, wife of Lamech, and descendant of Cain. The Bible says he was the first person to play music and make musical instruments like harps and flutes ([Genesis 4:19–21](https://ref.ly/Gen4:19-Gen4:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:19–GEN 4:21
