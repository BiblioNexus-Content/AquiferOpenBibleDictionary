## Eglath-Shelishiyah (id: 4950)

EGLATH\-SHELISHIYAH
===================

Place in Moab mentioned in [Isaiah 15:5](https://ref.ly/Isa15:5) and [Jeremiah 48:34](https://ref.ly/Jer48:34) in pronouncements of judgment. The name means literally “the third Eglath.” It was probably near Zoar at the southern end of the Dead Sea, but its exact location is uncertain.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 15:5; JER 48:34
