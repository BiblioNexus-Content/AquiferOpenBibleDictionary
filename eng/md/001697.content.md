## Eglaim (id: 4949)

EGLAIM
======

Town mentioned in [Isaiah 15:8](https://ref.ly/Isa15:8). It cannot be located with certainty, but it was probably in southern Moab. A village called Aigaleim was mentioned by Eusebius and another called Agalla by Josephus (*Antiquities* 14\.1\.4\). However, their identification with Eglaim is uncertain.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 15:8
