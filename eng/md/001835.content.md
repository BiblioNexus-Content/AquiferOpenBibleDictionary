## Enoch (Person) (id: 5088)

ENOCH (Person)
==============

1\. Cain’s son and grandson of Adam ([Gn 4:17, 19](https://ref.ly/Gen4:17,Gen4:19)).

2\. Jared’s son among the descendants of Seth; Methuselah’s father ([Gn 5:18–24](https://ref.ly/Gen5:18-Gen5:24); [1 Chr 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:3)). He lived in such close relationship to God that he was taken to heaven without having died.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:17; GEN 4:19; 1CH 1:3; GEN 5:18–GEN 5:24
