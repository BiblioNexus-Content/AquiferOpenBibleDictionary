## Elzabad (id: 5062)

ELZABAD
=======

1\. Military leader from Gad’s tribe who joined David at Ziklag ([1 Chr 12:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:12)).

2\. Korahite Levite from Obed\-edom’s family, and a gatekeeper of the sanctuary ([1 Chr 26:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:12; 1CH 26:7
