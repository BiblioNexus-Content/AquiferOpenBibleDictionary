## Shebaniah (id: 8312)

SHEBANIAH
=========

1\. One of the seven priests assigned to blow a trumpet before the ark of God in the procession led by David, when the ark was moved to Jerusalem ([1 Chr 15:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:24)).

2\. One of the Levites who led the people in worship when Ezra read the law ([Neh 9:4–5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:4-Neh9:5)).

3\. Head of a priestly family who set his seal on the covenant of Ezra ([Neh 10:4](https://ref.ly/Neh10:4); [12:14](https://ref.ly/Neh12:14)) and perhaps the same person as Shecaniah in [Nehemiah 12:3](https://ref.ly/Neh12:3). *See* Shecaniah, Shechaniah \#9.

4\. Another Levite who set his seal on the covenant of Ezra ([Neh 10:12](https://ref.ly/Neh10:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 15:24; NEH 10:4; NEH 10:12; NEH 12:3; NEH 12:14; NEH 9:4–NEH 9:5
