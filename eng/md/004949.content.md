## Seirah (id: 8210)

SEIRAH
======

Place where Ehud sought refuge after murdering Eglon, the king of Moab, and from where he summoned Israel to war against the Moabites ([Jgs 3:26](https://ref.ly/Judg3:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 3:26
