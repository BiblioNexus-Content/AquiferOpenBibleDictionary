## Xanthicus (id: 9098)

XANTHICUS\*
===========

Name of the month in the Macedonian calendar corresponding to the Hebrew month Nisan (March–April); mentioned in [2 Maccabees 11:27–38](https://ref.ly/2Macc11:27-2Macc11:38). *See* Calendars, Ancient and Modern.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 11:27–2MA 11:38
