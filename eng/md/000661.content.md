## Baalath (id: 3929)

BAALATH
=======

1. A town in Dan. It may be the same city as \#2 below, although some scholars don't agree ([Joshua 19:44](https://ref.ly/Josh19:44)).
2. A store city built by Solomon, probably west of Gezer in the original tribal land given to Dan ([1 Kings 9:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs9:18); [2 Chronicles 8:6](https://ref.ly/2Chr8:6)).
