## Maroth (id: 6953)

MAROTH
======

City in Palestine, mentioned only by Micah ([Mi 1:12](https://ref.ly/Mic1:12)). It may be the same as the Maarath in [Joshua 15:59](https://ref.ly/Josh15:59); if so, it would be an ancient Canaanite city, part of the inheritance of Judah’s tribe.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:59; MIC 1:12
