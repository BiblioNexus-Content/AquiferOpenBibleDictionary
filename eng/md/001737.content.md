## Eleven, the (id: 4989)

ELEVEN, The
===========

A name used for Jesus's disciples after Jesus was raised from the dead ([Mark 16:14](https://ref.ly/Mark16:14); [Luke 24:9, 33](https://ref.ly/Luke24:9,Luke24:33)) and at the Jewish festival Pentecost ([Acts 2:14](https://ref.ly/Acts2:14)). This name was used after Judas Iscariot killed himself. 

*See* Apostle, Apostleship.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 16:14; LUK 24:9; LUK 24:33; ACT 2:14
