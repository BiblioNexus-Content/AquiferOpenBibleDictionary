## Nehiloth (id: 7345)

NEHILOTH\*
==========

Hebrew term in the superscription of [Psalm 5](https://ref.ly/Ps5:1-Ps5:12) (kjv); musical cue, meaning “flutes,” describing the kind of musical accompaniment for the performance of the psalm. *See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** PSA 5:1–PSA 5:12
* **Associated Articles:** Music (ID: `7248@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
