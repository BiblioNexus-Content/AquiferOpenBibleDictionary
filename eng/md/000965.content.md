## Bigtha (id: 4236)

BIGTHA
======

A eunuch who served King Ahasuerus of Persia. He and six others were in charge of the royal household ([Esther 1:10](https://ref.ly/Esth1:10)). He may be Bigthana from [Esther 2:21](https://ref.ly/Esth2:21); [6:2](https://ref.ly/Esth6:2).

*See also* Bigthan, Bigthana.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:10; EST 2:21; EST 6:2
