## Hezron (Place) (id: 5839)

HEZRON (Place)
==============

A town on the border of the territory of Judah ([Joshua 15:3](https://ref.ly/Josh15:3)). In [Numbers 34:4](https://ref.ly/Num34:4), it is probable that it forms part of the name Hazar\-addar.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:4; JOS 15:3
