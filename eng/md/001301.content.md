## Chrysolite (id: 4553)

CHRYSOLITE
==========

Magnesium, iron silicate, usually olive green; mentioned in Ezekiel’s vision of the four wheels ([Ez 1:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek1:16)) and as one of the gems in the foundation wall of the new Jerusalem ([Rv 21:20](https://ref.ly/Rev21:20)). *See* Minerals and Metals; Stones, Precious.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 1:16; REV 21:20
* **Associated Articles:** Minerals and Metals (ID: `7145@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Stones, Precious (ID: `8628@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
