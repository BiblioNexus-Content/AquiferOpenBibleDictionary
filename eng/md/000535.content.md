## Ashhur (id: 3802)

ASHHUR
======

Asshur was the son of Caleb. He was the father of Tekoa ([1 Chronicles 2:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:24); [4:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:5)), or maybe the founder of a village named Tekoa ([2 Samuel 14:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:1-2Sam14:3)–[3](https://ref.ly/2Sam14:1-2Sam14:3); [Amos 1:1](https://ref.ly/Amos1:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:24; 1CH 4:5; AMO 1:1; 2SA 14:1–2SA 14:3
