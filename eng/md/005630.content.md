## Tobijah (id: 8879)

TOBIJAH
=======

1. A Levite sent by King Jehoshaphat to teach the law in the cities of Judah ([2 Chronicles 17:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:8)).
2. One of four men who returned from Babylon to Jerusalem with gold and silver used to make a crown for the high priest Joshua (spelled “Jeshua" in the New Living Translation, [Zechariah 6:10, 14](https://ref.ly/Zech6:10,Zech6:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 17:8; ZEC 6:10; ZEC 6:14
