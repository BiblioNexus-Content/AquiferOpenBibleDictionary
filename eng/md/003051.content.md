## Jemuel (id: 6305)

JEMUEL
======

Simeon’s first son ([Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10); [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15)). He is called Nemuel in [1 Chronicles 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24) and is the founder of the Nemuelite family ([Numbers 26:12](https://ref.ly/Num26:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:10; EXO 6:15; NUM 26:12; 1CH 4:24
