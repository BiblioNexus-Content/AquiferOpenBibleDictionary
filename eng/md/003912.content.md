## Mithna (id: 7171)

MITHNA
======

Town of Joshaphat, one of David’s mighty warriors ([1 Chr 11:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:43), nlt). Other translations describe him as “the Mithnite.”

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 11:43
