## Joktheel (id: 6426)

JOKTHEEL
========

1\. Town in the Shephelah of Judah near Lachish ([Jos 15:38](https://ref.ly/Josh15:38)).

2\. Ancient Edomite stronghold originally named Sela. Amaziah changed its name to Joktheel after defeating the Edomites ([2 Kgs 14:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:38; 2KI 14:7
