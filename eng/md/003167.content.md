## Joktan (id: 6425)

JOKTAN
======

A son of Eber and a younger brother of Peleg. A number of Arabian groups descended from him ([Genesis 10:25–29](https://ref.ly/Gen10:25-Gen10:29); [1 Chronicles 1:19–23](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:19-1Chr1:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:25–GEN 10:29; 1CH 1:19–1CH 1:23
