## Libni (id: 6740)

LIBNI
=====

1. Gershon’s son, the grandson of Levi, and Shimei’s brother ([Exodus 6:17](https://ref.ly/Exod6:17); [Numbers 3:18](https://ref.ly/Num3:18); [1 Chronicles 6:17, 20](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:17,1Chr6:20)). He was the father of three sons and the founder of the Libnite family ([Numbers 3:21](https://ref.ly/Num3:21)). Libni is also called Ladan in [1 Chronicles 23:7–9](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:7-1Chr23:9) and [26:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:21).
2. Mahli’s son, the father of Shimei, and a descendant of Levi through Merari’s line ([1 Chronicles 6:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:17; NUM 3:18; NUM 3:21; 1CH 6:17; 1CH 6:20; 1CH 6:29; 1CH 26:21; 1CH 23:7–1CH 23:9
