## Ithiel (id: 6107)

ITHIEL
======

1\. Ancestor of Sallu, a Benjamite who lived in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile ([Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)).

2\. One of the two persons to whom Agur spoke his proverbs ([Prv 30:1](https://ref.ly/Prov30:1), nlt mg).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:7; PRO 30:1
