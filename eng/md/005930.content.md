## Zelotes (id: 9175)

ZELOTES
=======

The King James Version spelling of “Zealot” in [Luke 6:15](https://ref.ly/Luke6:15) and [Acts 1:13](https://ref.ly/Acts1:13). Zealot was the additional name of Simon, one of Jesus's 12 disciples.
