## Salmone (id: 8109)

SALMONE
=======

A promontory (a high point of land extending into the sea) on the east side of Crete. The apostle Paul's ship sailed near this location during his journey to Rome ([Acts 27:7](https://ref.ly/Acts27:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 27:7
