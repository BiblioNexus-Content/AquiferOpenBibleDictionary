## Aholah (id: 3465)

AHOLAH
======

The King James Version form of Oholah. Aholah was the symbolic name for Samaria, the capital of the northern kingdom of Israel ([Ezekiel 23](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:1-Ezek23:49)).

*See* Oholah and Oholibah.
