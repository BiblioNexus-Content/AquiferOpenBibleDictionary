## Avaran (id: 3882)

AVARAN
======

The nickname of Eleazar, brother of Judas Maccabeus ([1 Maccabees 2:5](https://ref.ly/1Macc2:5); [6:43](https://ref.ly/1Macc6:43)). The name might mean “Awake,” “Paleface,” or “Piercer.”

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 2:5; 1MA 6:43
