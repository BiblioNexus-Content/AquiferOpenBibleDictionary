## Salmon (Person) (id: 8110)

SALMON (Person)
===============

Nahshon’s son and an ancestor of David from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 2:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:11)). Salmon fathered Boaz by Rahab ([Ruth 4:20](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:20-Ruth4:21)[–](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:20-Ruth4:21)[21](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:20-Ruth4:21)) and is listed in Matthew’s family list as a family member of Jesus Christ ([Matthew 1:4](https://ref.ly/Matt1:4-Matt1:5)[–](https://ref.ly/Matt1:4-Matt1:5)[5](https://ref.ly/Matt1:4-Matt1:5)). In the Greek, his name is spelled Sala in Luke’s genealogy ([Luke 3:32](https://ref.ly/Luke3:32)).

*See also* Genealogy of Jesus Christ.
