## Gath-Hepher (id: 5377)

GATH\-HEPHER
============

Town in Galilee, in Zebulun’s territory, which was the birthplace of Jonah ([Jos 19:13](https://ref.ly/Josh19:13); [2 Kgs 14:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:25)). Modern el\-Meshad occupies the site of Gath\-hepher.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:13; 2KI 14:25
