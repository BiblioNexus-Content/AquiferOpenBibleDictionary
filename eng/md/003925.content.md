## Moadiah (id: 7185)

MOADIAH
=======

Head of a family of postexilic priests, whose house was headed by Piltai during the days of Joiakim the high priest ([Neh 12:17](https://ref.ly/Neh12:17)); alternately called Maadiah in verse [5](https://ref.ly/Neh12:5). *See* Maadiah.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:5; NEH 12:17
