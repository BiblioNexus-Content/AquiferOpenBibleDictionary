## Taanath-Shiloh (id: 8689)

TAANATH\-SHILOH
===============

City on the northeast border of the territory allotted to Ephraim’s tribe for an inheritance, positioned between Micmethath and Janoah ([Jos 16:6](https://ref.ly/Josh16:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 16:6
