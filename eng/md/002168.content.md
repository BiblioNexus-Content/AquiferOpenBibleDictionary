## Gera (id: 5426)

GERA
====

1. One of the sons of Benjamin ([Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)). But the name does not appear in a similar list in [Numbers 26:38–41](https://ref.ly/Num26:38-Num26:41).
2. The father of the judge Ehud ([Judges 3:15](https://ref.ly/Judg3:15)).
3. The father of Shimei. Shimei cursed and threw stones at King David during the rebellion of Absalom. Later, he begged David to pardon him ([2 Samuel 16:5](https://ref.ly/2Sam16:5); [19:16–18](https://ref.ly/2Sam19:16-2Sam19:18); [1 Kings 2:8](https://ref.ly/1Kgs2:8)).
4. A son of Bela from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:3, 5](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:3,1Chr8:5)). In verse [7](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:7), another name for Gera is "Heglam."

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; JDG 3:15; 2SA 16:5; 1KI 2:8; 1CH 8:3; 1CH 8:5; 1CH 8:7; NUM 26:38–NUM 26:41; 2SA 19:16–2SA 19:18
