## Pallu, Palluite (id: 7559)

PALLU, PALLUITE
===============

Reuben’s son, the father of Eliab ([Genesis 46:9](https://ref.ly/Gen46:9); [Exodus 6:14](https://ref.ly/Exod6:14); [Numbers 26:8](https://ref.ly/Num26:8); [1 Chronicles 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:3)). The founder of the Palluite family ([Numbers 26:5](https://ref.ly/Num26:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:9; EXO 6:14; NUM 26:5; NUM 26:8; 1CH 5:3
