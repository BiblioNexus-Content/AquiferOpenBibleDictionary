## Abba (id: 3268)

ABBA
====

Aramaic word for “father,” which is applied to God in [Mark 14:36](https://ref.ly/Mark14:36); [Romans 8:15](https://ref.ly/Rom8:15); and [Galatians 4:6](https://ref.ly/Gal4:6). The name expresses a very intimate and inseparable relationship between Christ and the Father, and between believers (as children) and God (as Father).

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 14:36; ROM 8:15; GAL 4:6
