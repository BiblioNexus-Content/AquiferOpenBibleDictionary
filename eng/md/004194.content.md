## Obed (id: 7454)

OBED
====

1. Ruth and Boaz’s first child, listed among the family members of Jesus ([Ruth 4:17, 21](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:17,Ruth4:21-Ruth4:22)[–](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:17)[22](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:17,Ruth4:21-Ruth4:22); [1 Chronicles 2:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:12); [Matthew 1:5](https://ref.ly/Matt1:5); [Luke 3:32](https://ref.ly/Luke3:32)).

    *See* Genealogy of Jesus Christ.

2. Jerahmeelite and Ephlal’s son ([1 Chronicles 2:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:37-1Chr2:38)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:37-1Chr2:38)[38](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:37-1Chr2:38)).
3. One of David’s mighty men ([1 Chronicles 11:47](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:47)).
4. Shemaiah’s son and an able leader who ruled his father’s house ([1 Chronicles 26:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:6-1Chr26:7)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:6-1Chr26:7)[7](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:6-1Chr26:7)).
5. Father of Azariah, a captain of Jehoiada ([2 Chronicles 23:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:1)).
