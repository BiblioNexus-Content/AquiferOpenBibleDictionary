## Philetus (id: 7709)

PHILETUS
========

A false teacher who worked with Hymenaeus. They taught people wrong ideas about the resurrection of believers. They believed this rising from death had already happened ([2 Timothy 2:17](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:17); compare verse [11](https://ref.ly/2Tim2:11)).

*See also* Hymenaeus.

* **Associated Passages:** 2TI 2:11; 2TI 2:17
