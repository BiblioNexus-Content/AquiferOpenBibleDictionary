## Ladan (id: 6659)

LADAN
=====

1\. Member of Ephraim’s tribe who was Joshua’s ancestor ([1 Chr 7:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:26)).

2\. Gershonite Levite, named as the head of several families ([1 Chr 23:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:7); [26:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:21)). He is also called Libni. *See* Libni \#1.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:26; 1CH 23:7; 1CH 26:21
