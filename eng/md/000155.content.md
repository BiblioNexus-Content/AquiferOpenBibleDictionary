## Agabus (id: 3415)

AGABUS
======

A prophet in New Testament times. He made two predictions recorded in Acts. He correctly predicted a severe famine, which happened when Claudius was the emperor of Rome ([Acts 11:27](https://ref.ly/Acts11:27-Acts11:28)–[28](https://ref.ly/Acts11:27-Acts11:28)). He also predicted that Paul would be handed over to the gentiles by the Jews in Jerusalem if he went there ([Acts 21:10](https://ref.ly/Acts21:10-Acts21:11)–[11](https://ref.ly/Acts21:10-Acts21:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 11:27–ACT 11:28; ACT 21:10–ACT 21:11
