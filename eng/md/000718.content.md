## Ban (id: 3992)

BAN
===

A religious practice involving dedicating those who are hostile to God to destruction. This practice was used in Israel during wartime. They would completely destroy the Canaanites because of their wickedness and evil practices

*See also* Conquest and Allotment of the Land; Joshua, Book of; Warfare; War, Holy.
