## Sirah (id: 8527)

SIRAH
=====

A well or cistern where Joab's messengers stopped Abner as he was returning from pledging his loyalty to David in Hebron ([2 Samuel 3:26](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:26)). This place is probably the same as the modern ‘Ain Sarah. It is located about 2\.4 kilometers (one and a half miles) northwest of Hebron.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 3:26
