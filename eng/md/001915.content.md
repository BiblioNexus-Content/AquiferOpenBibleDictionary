## Ether (id: 5172)

ETHER
=====

1\. A town in Judah in the Shephelah region ([Jos 15:42](https://ref.ly/Josh15:42)). The modern town of Khirbet el\-Ater is the probable site.

2\. A village in Simeon ([Jos 19:7](https://ref.ly/Josh19:7)), paralleled in [1 Chronicles 4:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:32) by “Token.”

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:42; JOS 19:7; 1CH 4:32
