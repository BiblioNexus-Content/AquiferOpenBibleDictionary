## Hegemonides (id: 5765)

HEGEMONIDES
===========

Principal Syrian officer appointed by Antiochus, king of Syria, to govern the territory from Ptolemais to Gerar ([2 Macc 13:24](https://ref.ly/2Macc13:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 13:24
