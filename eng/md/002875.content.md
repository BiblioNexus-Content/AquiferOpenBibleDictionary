## Izri (id: 6128)

IZRI\*
======

Temple musician and head of the 4th of the 24 divisions of priests for service as musicians in the sanctuary ([1 Chr 25:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:11), nlt mg). He is called Zeri in [1 Chronicles 25:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:3).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:3; 1CH 25:11
