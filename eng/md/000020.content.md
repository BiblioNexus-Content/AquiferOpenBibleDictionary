## Abel-keramim (id: 3277)

ABEL\-KERAMIM
=============

A city taken by Jephthah, the Israelite judge, when he conquered the Ammonites ([Judges 11:33](https://ref.ly/Judg11:33)). It was located south of the Jabbok River.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 11:33
