## Almodad (id: 3519)

ALMODAD
=======

A son or descendant of Joktan. He is a member of Noah’s family through Noah's son Shem ([Genesis 10:26](https://ref.ly/Gen10:26); [1 Chronicles 1:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:26; 1CH 1:20
