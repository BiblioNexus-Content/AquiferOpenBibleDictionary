## Prison Gate (id: 7822)

PRISON GATE
===========

The King James Version translation of “Gate of the Guard.” This was a gate in Jerusalem. It is possible that it was on the palace grounds ([Nehemiah 12:39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:39)).
