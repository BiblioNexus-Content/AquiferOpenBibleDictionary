## Eliehoenai (id: 5003)

ELIEHOENAI
==========

1\. Korahite Levite who, with his six brothers and his father, Meshelemiah, served as a temple doorkeeper during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:3)).

2\. Zerahiah’s son, who came to Jerusalem with Ezra, bringing his family and others from Babylon ([Ezr 8:4](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 26:3; EZR 8:4
