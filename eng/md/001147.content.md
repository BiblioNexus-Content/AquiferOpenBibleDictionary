## Camphire (id: 4403)

CAMPHIRE
========

The King James Version name for henna. Henna is a fragrant shrub with pale\-green leaves and white, pink, or yellow blossoms ([Song of Solomon 1:14](https://ref.ly/Song1:14); [4:13](https://ref.ly/Song4:13)). 

*See* Plants (Henna).
