## Bilgai (id: 4241)

BILGAI
======

A priest who signed the covenant of Ezra to be faithful to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 10:8](https://ref.ly/Neh10:8)). It is possible that this is the same person as Bilgah in [Nehemiah 12:18](https://ref.ly/Neh12:18). 

*See* Bilgah \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 10:8; NEH 12:18
