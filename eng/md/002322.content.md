## Hadlai (id: 5577)

HADLAI
======

Amasa’s father from Ephraim’s tribe ([2 Chr 28:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:12)). Amasa opposed the taking of prisoners from Judah’s tribe after a battle.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 28:12
