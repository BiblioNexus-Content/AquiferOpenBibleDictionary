## Charax (id: 4482)

CHARAX
======

Charax was a place that may have been in the land of Tob. Judas Maccabeus and his army passed through Charax while chasing their Syrian enemies ([2 Maccabees 12:17–19](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:17-2Macc12:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 12:17–2MA 12:19
