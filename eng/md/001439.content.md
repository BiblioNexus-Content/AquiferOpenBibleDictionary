## Credit, Creditor (id: 4688)

CREDIT, CREDITOR
================

Acknowledgment of payment on a debt incurred through goods or services sold on trust, and the one who operates the business of selling on credit. The Mosaic law regulated credit and creditors ([Dt 23:19–20](https://ref.ly/Deut23:19-Deut23:20)).

*See also* Banker, Banking; Debt.
