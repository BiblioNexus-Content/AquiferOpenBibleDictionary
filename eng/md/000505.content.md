## Arubboth (id: 3771)

ARUBBOTH
========

A town that served as headquarters for one of King Solomon’s 12 administrative districts ([1 Kings 4:10](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:10)). Arubboth was probably in Manasseh’s tribal territory, about 14\.5 kilometers (nine miles) north of Samaria, at the site of modern ’Aarabeh.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:10
