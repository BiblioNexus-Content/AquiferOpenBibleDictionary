## Weeks, Feast of (id: 9049)

FEAST OF WEEKS
==============

A celebration of the beginning of the wheat harvest ([Exodus 23:14–17](https://ref.ly/Exod23:14-Exod23:17); [Deuteronomy 16:16](https://ref.ly/Deut16:16)). It occurs seven weeks after Passover on the sixth day of Sivan (June). It is also known as the Feast of Pentecost. 

*See* Feasts and Festivals of Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 16:16; EXO 23:14–EXO 23:17
