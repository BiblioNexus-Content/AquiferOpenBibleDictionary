## Half-Shekel Tax (id: 5606)

HALF\-SHEKEL TAX
================

The tax on all adult Jews throughout the world. It began during the time between Testaments to support the temple. It was continued by Vespasian for the Roman replacement; the temple tax of [Matthew 17:24](https://ref.ly/Matt17:24-Matt17:25)[–](https://ref.ly/Matt17:24-Matt17:25)[25](https://ref.ly/Matt17:24-Matt17:25).

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 17:24–MAT 17:25
