## Not Loved, Not Pitied (id: 7429)

NOT LOVED, NOT PITIED
=====================

A symbolic name the prophet Hosea gave to his daughter ([Hosea 1:6–8](https://ref.ly/Hos1:6-Hos1:8)). It was a warning of the coming judgment of God upon Israel. 

*See*Ruhamah.
