## Mahazioth (id: 6873)

MAHAZIOTH
=========

One of the 14 sons of Heman the Kohathite, and head of the 23d course of tabernacle musicians who ministered with cymbals, harps, and lyres ([1 Chr 25:4, 30](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4,1Chr25:30)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:30
