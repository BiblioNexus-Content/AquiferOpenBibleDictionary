## Gammad (id: 5362)

GAMMAD
======

Home of mercenaries who served in the army of Tyre, according to Ezekiel’s prophecy ([Ez 27:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:11)). Gammad may have been located in Syria and is identified as Kumidi in the Tell el\-Amarna letters.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 27:11
