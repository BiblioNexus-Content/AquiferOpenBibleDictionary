## Arod, Arodi, Arodite (id: 3757)

AROD, ARODI, ARODITE
====================

Arod was Gad’s sixth son and the founder of the Arodite family ([Numbers 26:17](https://ref.ly/Num26:17)). He is called Arodi in the list of those who went to Egypt with Jacob ([Genesis 46:16](https://ref.ly/Gen46:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:16; NUM 26:17
