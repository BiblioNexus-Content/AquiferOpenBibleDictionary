## Zephathah (id: 9184)

ZEPHATHAH\*
===========

Valley where Asa defeated Zerah the Ethiopian ([2 Chr 14:10](https://ref.ly/2Chr14:10)). The valley of Zephathah is located in the vicinity of Mareshah and thus in southwestern Judah.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 14:10
