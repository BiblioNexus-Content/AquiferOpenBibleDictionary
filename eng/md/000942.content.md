## Beth-Zechariah (id: 4213)

BETH\-ZECHARIAH
===============

A place 16\.1 kilometers (10 miles) southwest of Jerusalem where Judas Maccabeus was defeated by a Seleucid ruler, Antiochus V Eupator, son of Antiochus Epiphanes ([1 Maccabees 6:32](https://ref.ly/1Macc6:32-1Macc6:47)[–](https://ref.ly/1Macc6:32-1Macc6:47)[47](https://ref.ly/1Macc6:32-1Macc6:47)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 6:32–1MA 6:47
