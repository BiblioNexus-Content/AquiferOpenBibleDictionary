## Ahoah, Ahohi, Ahohite (id: 3464)

AHOAH, AHOHI, AHOHITE
=====================

One of Bela’s nine sons, a member of the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:4)). 

Ahoah’s descendants were called Ahohites, and two of them were among King David’s most effective warriors: 

* Dodo (“son of… the Ahohite,” [2 Samuel 23:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:9); it is spelled “Dodai” in [1 Chronicles 27:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:4))
* Zalmon the Ahohite ([2 Samuel 23:28;](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:28) he is called “Ilai” in [1 Chronicles 11:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:29))

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:9; 2SA 23:28; 1CH 8:4; 1CH 11:29; 1CH 27:4
