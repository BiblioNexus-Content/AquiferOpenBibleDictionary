## Neballat (id: 7326)

NEBALLAT
========

Town situated in the hills overlooking the southeastern region of the plain of Sharon, settled by Benjamites after the exile ([Neh 11:34](https://ref.ly/Neh11:34)). It is identified with modern Beit Nebala, four miles (6\.4 kilometers) east of Lod and two miles (3\.2 kilometers) north of Hadid.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:34
