## Rabmag (id: 7897)

RABMAG\*
========

Title given to a certain Babylonian officer, Nergal\-sharezer, who was in charge of Jeremiah’s safety during the fall of Jerusalem ([Jer 39:3, 13](https://ref.ly/Jer39:3,Jer39:13)). The meaning of the title in uncertain.

* **Associated Passages:** JER 39:3; JER 39:13
