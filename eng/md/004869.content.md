## Sansannah (id: 8135)

SANSANNAH
=========

One of 29 cities at the southern extremity of the land inherited by the sons of Judah ([Jos 15:31](https://ref.ly/Josh15:31)). It is possibly the same city as Hazar\-susah mentioned in a parallel description of the territory allotted to Simeon within Judah’s inheritance ([19:5](https://ref.ly/Josh19:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:31; JOS 19:5
