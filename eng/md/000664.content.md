## Baale-Judah, Baale of Judah (id: 3931)

BAALE\-JUDAH, BAALE OF JUDAH
============================

An alternate name for Kiriath\-jearim. It was a village on the road from Jerusalem to Tel Aviv ([2 Samuel 6:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:2)). 

*See* Kiriath\-jearim.
