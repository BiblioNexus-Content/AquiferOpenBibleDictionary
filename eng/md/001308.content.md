## Chusi (id: 4561)

CHUSI
=====

Place mentioned near Acraba and beside the brook Mochmur ([Jdt 7:18](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:18)). Chusi is probably south of modern Nablus in Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 7:18
