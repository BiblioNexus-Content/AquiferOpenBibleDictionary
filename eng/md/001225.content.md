## Chaos, Waters of (id: 4478)

CHAOS\*, Waters of
==================

In ancient thought, the primeval seas that were divided. The world was then situated between the “waters above” and “waters below,” or “the Deep” ([Gn 1:1–2, 6–7](https://ref.ly/Gen1:1-Gen1:2,Gen1:6-Gen1:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 1:1–GEN 1:2; GEN 1:6–GEN 1:7
