## Marsena (id: 6956)

MARSENA
=======

One of the seven princes of Persia and Media who served King Ahasuerus. These officials were the most powerful people in the kingdom after the king ([Esther 1:14](https://ref.ly/Esth1:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:14
