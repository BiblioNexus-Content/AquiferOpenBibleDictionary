## Acraba (id: 3347)

ACRABA
======

The site where some of the Edomite\-Ammonites in Holofernes’s army were located during the siege of Bethulia ([Judith 7:18](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:18)). Acraba was probably located about 10 miles (16 kilometers) southeast of Shechem. It is the modern\-day Nablus.

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 7:18
