## Siphmoth (id: 8525)

SIPHMOTH
========

Town in southern Judah to which David gave part of the spoils of his victory over the Amalekites because its residents had aided him in his flight from Saul ([1 Sm 30:28](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 30:28
