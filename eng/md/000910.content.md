## Beth-Hoglah (id: 4181)

BETH\-HOGLAH
============

A city given to the tribe of Benjamin ([Joshua 18:21](https://ref.ly/Josh18:21)). It was southeast of Jericho near the mouth of the Jordan River on the border of the territories of Judah and Benjamin ([Joshua 15:6](https://ref.ly/Josh15:6); [18:19](https://ref.ly/Josh18:19)). It is identified with modern Ain Hajlah.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:6; JOS 18:19; JOS 18:21
