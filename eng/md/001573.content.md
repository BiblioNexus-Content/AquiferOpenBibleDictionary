## Devoted Things (id: 4826)

DEVOTED THINGS
==============

Persons, animals, or objects that God did not allow the Israelites to keep or use for themselves. These items were set apart as belonging completely to God ([Leviticus 27:28–29](https://ref.ly/Lev27:28-Lev27:29); [Numbers 18:14](https://ref.ly/Num18:14)). 

*See* Curse, Cursed.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 18:14; LEV 27:28–LEV 27:29
