## Bilgah (id: 4240)

BILGAH
======

1\. Head of the 15th of 24 divisions of priests whom King David assigned to official duties in the temple ([1 Chr 24:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:14)).

2\. Priest who returned to Jerusalem under Zerubbabel’s leadership after the exile ([Neh 12:18](https://ref.ly/Neh12:18)). He is perhaps identifiable with Bilgai in [Nehemiah 10:8](https://ref.ly/Neh10:8). *See* Bilgai.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:14; NEH 10:8; NEH 12:18
