## Heshmon (id: 5828)

HESHMON
=======

Town mentioned only in [Joshua 15:27](https://ref.ly/Josh15:27). It was located near Beth\-pelet in southern Judah. The notion that the Hasmoneans originated there is unsubstantiated.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:27
