## Nain (id: 7295)

NAIN
====

A village in southern Galilee near the border of Samaria. It is the location of the miracle in which Jesus brought a dead man back to life ([Luke 7:11](https://ref.ly/Luke7:11)). The man was the son of a widow who lived in this village.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 7:11
