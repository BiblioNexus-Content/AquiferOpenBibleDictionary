## Shobab (id: 8438)

SHOBAB
======

1. The second of the four sons of King David by his wife Bathsheba ([2 Samuel 5:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:14); [1 Chronicles 3:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:5); [14:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:4)).
2. The son of Caleb by his wife Azubah ([1 Chronicles 2:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 5:14; 1CH 2:18; 1CH 3:5; 1CH 14:4
