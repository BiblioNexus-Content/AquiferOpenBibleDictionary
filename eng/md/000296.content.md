## Ammah (id: 3557)

AMMAH
=====

A hill north of Jerusalem in the area of Gibeon. A battle was fought there between David’s troops under Joab and Ishbosheth’s troops under Abner. That battle began a long war between followers of Saul and followers of David ([2 Samuel 2:24–32](https://ref.ly/2Sam2:24-2Sam2:32); [3:1](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 3:1; 2SA 2:24–2SA 2:32
