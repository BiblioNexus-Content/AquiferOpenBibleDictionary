## Ashbel, Ashbelite (id: 3793)

ASHBEL, ASHBELITE
=================

Benjamin’s son who moved to Egypt with his grandfather Jacob ([Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21); [1 Chronicles 8:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:1)). The Ashbelites, his descendants, were included in Moses’s census in the wilderness ([Numbers 26:38](https://ref.ly/Num26:38)). Ashbel is also called Jediael ([1 Chronicles 7:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:38; 1CH 7:6; 1CH 8:1
