## Harodite (id: 5676)

HARODITE
========

The title given to Shammah and Elika, two of King David's mighty men ([2 Samuel 23:25](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:25)). This name indicates they came from a place called Harod.

*See* Harod \#2.
