## Skull, Place of the (id: 8539)

SKULL, Place of the
===================

The place in Jerusalem where Jesus was crucified ([Matthew 27:33](https://ref.ly/Matt27:33); [Mark 15:22](https://ref.ly/Mark15:22); [John 19:17](https://ref.ly/John19:17)). Golgotha is the Greek spelling of the Aramaic word for "skull." *See* Golgotha.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 27:33; MRK 15:22; JHN 19:17
