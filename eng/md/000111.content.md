## Addar (Place) (id: 3371)

ADDAR (Place)
=============

A town on Judah’s southwest border, northwest of Kadesh\-barnea ([Joshua 15:3](https://ref.ly/Josh15:3)). The towns Hezron and Addar were called Hazar\-addar ([Numbers 34:4](https://ref.ly/Num34:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:4; JOS 15:3
