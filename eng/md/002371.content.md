## Hammon (id: 5623)

HAMMON
======

1\. One of the cities of Asher mentioned in [Joshua 19:28](https://ref.ly/Josh19:28). It was somewhere south of Tyre on the western border of Asher.

2\. Alternate name for Hammath in [1 Chronicles 6:76](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:76). *See* Hammath (Place).
