## Nergal-Sharezer (id: 7364)

NERGAL\-SHAREZER
================

Babylonian prince who held the title “Rabmag.” Nergal\-sharezer participated with Nebuchadnezzar and the Chaldean army in conquering Jerusalem after a three\-year siege from 588 to 586 BC ([Jer 39:3](https://ref.ly/Jer39:3)) and later entrusted Jeremiah to Gedaliah’s care (v [13](https://ref.ly/Jer39:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 39:3; JER 39:13
