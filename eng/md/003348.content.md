## Kiriath-huzoth (id: 6604)

KIRIATH\-HUZOTH
===============

A town in Moab. Balak and Balaam went there before going to Bamoth\-baal ([Numbers 22:39](https://ref.ly/Num22:39)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 22:39
