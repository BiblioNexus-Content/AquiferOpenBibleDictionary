## Aradus (id: 3692)

ARADUS
======

A Greek name for the Phoenician city of Arvad. Aradus was one of the cities receiving a “letter of recommendation” for the Jews written by the Roman consul (government official) Lucius ([1 Maccabees 15:16–23](https://ref.ly/1Macc15:16-1Macc15:23)).

*See also* Arvad, Arvadite.
