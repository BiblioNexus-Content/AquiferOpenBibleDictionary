## El-Bethel (id: 4971)

EL\-BETHEL
==========

Name Jacob gave to the place at Luz (Bethel) where he built an altar after he returned from Haran with his family ([Gn 35:7](https://ref.ly/Gen35:7)). *See* Bethel (Place), Bethelite.
