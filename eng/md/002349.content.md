## Halak, Mount (id: 5605)

HALAK, Mount
============

Mountain listed as marking the southern boundary of Joshua’s conquests ([Jos 11:17](https://ref.ly/Josh11:17); [12:7](https://ref.ly/Josh12:7)). It is located in the western Arabah and is probably identical with Jebel Halaq on the northwest side of the Wadi Marra.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 11:17; JOS 12:7
