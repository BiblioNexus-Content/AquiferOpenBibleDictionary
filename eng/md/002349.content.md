## Halah (id: 5603)

HALAH
=====

Place in Assyria where the inhabitants of Samaria were taken after its fall in 722 BC ([2 Kgs 17:6](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:6); [18:11](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:11); cf. [1 Chr 5:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 17:6; 2KI 18:11; 1CH 5:26
