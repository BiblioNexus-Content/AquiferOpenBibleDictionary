## Ebron (id: 4933)

EBRON\*
=======

Town belonging to Asher’s tribe, in [Joshua 19:28](https://ref.ly/Josh19:28), according to some Hebrew manuscripts. Other manuscripts read Abdon. It was a Levitical city located about 15 miles (24\.1 kilometers) south of Tyre and inland from Aczib on the Mediterranean coast.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:28
