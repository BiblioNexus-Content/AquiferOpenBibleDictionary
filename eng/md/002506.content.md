## Hebron (Person) (id: 5761)

HEBRON (Person)
===============

1. The third of Kohath’s four sons. Hebron was a descendant of Levi ([Exodus 6:18](https://ref.ly/Exod6:18); [Numbers 3:19](https://ref.ly/Num3:19); [1 Chronicles 6:2, 18](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:2,1Chr6:18); [23:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:12)). Hebron’s sons were Jeriah, Amariah, Jahaziel, and Jekameam ([1 Chronicles 23:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:19)). Hebron’s descendants were called the Hebronites. They are mentioned in a census taken in the plains of Moab ([Numbers 26:58](https://ref.ly/Num26:58)). The Hebronites are mentioned in connection with the transfer of the ark to Jerusalem in David’s time ([1 Chronicles 15:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:9); [26:23, 30–31](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:23,1Chr26:30-1Chr26:31)).
2. Mareshah’s son and Korah’s father ([1 Chronicles 2:42–43](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:42-1Chr2:43)).
