## Shema (Place) (id: 8347)

SHEMA (Place)
=============

One of the 29 cities located near the border of Edom in the southern extremity of the land inherited by Judah, mentioned between the cities of Amam and Moladah ([Jos 15:26](https://ref.ly/Josh15:26)). In the parallel list at [Joshua 19:2](https://ref.ly/Josh19:2), Sheba (the LXX has “Shema”) was one of 13 cities assigned to Simeon’s tribe within the southern portion of Judah’s inheritance. *See* Sheba \#1.
