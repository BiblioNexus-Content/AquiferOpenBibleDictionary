## Dionysius (id: 4851)

DIONYSIUS
=========

An important and well\-respected citizen of Athens who became a believer after hearing Paul preach. Dionysius was a member of the Areopagus (an Areopagite), which was the supreme court of Athens ([Acts 17:34](https://ref.ly/Acts17:34)). Dionysius was one of the few people in Athens who believed Paul's message about Jesus.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 17:34
