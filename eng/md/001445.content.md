## Crispus (id: 4695)

CRISPUS
=======

A leader of the Jewish synagogue in Corinth. Crispus and everyone in his household became followers of Jesus during the apostle Paul's 18\-month missionary visit to the city ([Acts 18:8, 11](https://ref.ly/Acts18:8,Acts18:11)). Paul mentioned Crispus as one of the few people he personally baptized in Corinth ([1 Corinthians 1:14](https://ref.ly/1Cor1:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 18:8; ACT 18:11; 1CO 1:14
