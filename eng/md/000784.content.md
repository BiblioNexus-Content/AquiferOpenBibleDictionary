## Becher, Becherite (id: 4055)

BECHER, Becherite
=================

1. Benjamin’s second son, who moved to Egypt with his grandfather Jacob ([Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21); [1 Chronicles 7:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:6)).
2. Ephraim’s second son. The family of "Becherites" came from his lineage ([Numbers 26:35](https://ref.ly/Num26:35)). He is also called Bered ([1 Chronicles 7:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:35; 1CH 7:6; 1CH 7:20
