## Nephtoah, Waters of (id: 7360)

NEPHTOAH, Waters of
===================

Geographical landmark situated between Mt Ephron to the west and the valley of Hinnom to the east, defining part of the boundary separating the tribes of Judah and Benjamin ([Jos 15:9](https://ref.ly/Josh15:9); [18:15](https://ref.ly/Josh18:15)). Its site is generally identified with Ain Lifta, three miles (4\.8 kilometers) northwest of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:9; JOS 18:15
