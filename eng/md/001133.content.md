## Calcol (id: 4387)

CALCOL
======

One of Mahol’s three sons and a member of Judah’s tribe ([1 Kgs 4:31](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:31), kjv “Chalcol”; [1 Chr 2:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:6)). He and his brothers were noted for their wisdom and musical abilities.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:31; 1CH 2:6
