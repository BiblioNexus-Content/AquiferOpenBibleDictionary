## Sheshach (id: 8375)

SHESHACH\*
==========

Term that is probably a cryptic name for “Babel” (Babylon) found in [Jeremiah 25:26](https://ref.ly/Jer25:26) and [51:41](https://ref.ly/Jer51:41) (nlt mg).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 25:26; JER 51:41
