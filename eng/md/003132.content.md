## Joanna (id: 6389)

JOANNA
======

1. The King James Version form of Joanan in [Luke 3:27](https://ref.ly/Luke3:27).

    *See* Joanan.

2. The wife of Chuza, a steward of Herod the governor of Judea. She was among those healed of evil spirits and sickness by Jesus. She was also one of the women who supported him ([Luke 8:2](https://ref.ly/Luke8:2-Luke8:3)[–](https://ref.ly/Luke8:2-Luke8:3)[3](https://ref.ly/Luke8:2-Luke8:3)). She probably witnessed Jesus's death on the cross and prepared spices for the body. Later, she found Jesus’s tomb empty ([Luke 23:55–24:10](https://ref.ly/Luke23:55-Luke24:10)).
