## Mozah (id: 7239)

MOZAH
=====

Town in the territory assigned to Benjamin’s tribe ([Jos 18:26](https://ref.ly/Josh18:26)); tentatively identified with the village of Qalunyah. It has been suggested that the name is preserved in the modern Khirbet beit Mizza, a small village about four miles (6\.4 kilometers) northwest of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 18:26
