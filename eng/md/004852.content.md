## Salu (id: 8118)

SALU
====

A man from the tribe of Simeon and the father of Zimri. His son Zimri was the head of his father's household. Zimri was killed by Phinehas ([Numbers 25:14](https://ref.ly/Num25:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 25:14
