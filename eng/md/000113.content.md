## Addi (id: 3373)

ADDI
====

1. The ancestor of some who obeyed Ezra’s advice to divorce their non\-Jewish wives after the exile in Babylon ([1 Esdras 9:31](https://ref.ly/1Esd9:31)). In [Ezra 10:30,](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:30) Pahath\-moab is listed instead of Addi.
2. An ancestor of Jesus, mentioned in Luke’s record of Jesus's ancestry ([Luke 3:28](https://ref.ly/Luke3:28)).

    *See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:30; LUK 3:28; 1ES 9:31
* **Associated Articles:** Genealogy of Jesus Christ (ID: `5412@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
