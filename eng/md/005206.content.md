## Shuthelah (id: 8471)

SHUTHELAH
=========

1. A son of Ephraim, the brother of Beker and Tahan, and the father of Eran and Bered. He founded the Shuthelahite family and was an ancestor of Joshua the son of Nun ([Numbers 26:35](https://ref.ly/Num26:35); [1 Chronicles 7:20, 27](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:20,1Chr7:27)).
2. A son of Zabad from the tribe of Ephraim ([1 Chronicles 7:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:35; 1CH 7:20; 1CH 7:21; 1CH 7:27
