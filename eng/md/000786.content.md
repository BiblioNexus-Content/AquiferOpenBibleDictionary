## Bectileth (id: 4057)

BECTILETH
=========

A plain mentioned only in the Apocrypha (ancient writings that are included in some versions of the Bible but are not considered Scripture by all Christian traditions). Nebuchadnezzar’s general, Holofernes, camped there on his western conquest ([Judith 2:21](https://ref.ly/Jdt2:21-Jdt2:23)[–](https://ref.ly/Jdt2:21-Jdt2:23)[23](https://ref.ly/Jdt2:21-Jdt2:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 2:21–JDT 2:23
