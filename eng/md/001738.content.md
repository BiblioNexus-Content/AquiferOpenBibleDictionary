## Elhanan (id: 4990)

ELHANAN
=======

1. A Hebrew soldier who became famous for killing a Philistine giant. In one account, he is called the son of Jaare\-oregim from Bethlehem. [2 Samuel 21:19](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:19) says he killed Goliath the Gittite. [1 Chronicles 20:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr20:5) calls him the son of Jair and says he killed Lahmi, the brother of Goliath.
2. A son of Dodo and a warrior among the mighty men of King David ([2 Samuel 23:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:24); [1 Chronicles 11:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 21:19; 2SA 23:24; 1CH 11:26; 1CH 20:5
