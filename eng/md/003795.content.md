## Mephaath (id: 7054)

MEPHAATH
========

One of the cities of Reuben’s tribe on the plain near Heshbon, which was allotted to the Merari family of Levites ([Jos 13:18](https://ref.ly/Josh13:18); [21:37](https://ref.ly/Josh21:37); [1 Chr 6:79](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:79)). Later, it was numbered among Moabite towns during Jeremiah’s ministry ([Jer 48:21](https://ref.ly/Jer48:21)). It has been identified with modern Jawah, six miles (9\.7 kilometers) south of Ammon.
