## Tel-Assar (id: 8769)

TEL\-ASSAR
==========

Principal city of the people of Eden who were conquered by Sennacherib of Assyria ([2 Kgs 19:11–12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs19:11-2Kgs19:12); [Is 37:11–12](https://ref.ly/Isa37:11-Isa37:12)). The conquest is mentioned in Rabshekah’s taunt that the Lord would similarly be unable to protect Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 19:11–2KI 19:12; ISA 37:11–ISA 37:12
