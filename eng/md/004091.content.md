## Nekeb (id: 7351)

NEKEB\*
=======

KJV rendering of a town defining the boundary of the territory allotted to Naphtali’s tribe for an inheritance, positioned between Za\-anannim and Jabneel ([Jos 19:33](https://ref.ly/Josh19:33)). *See* Adami, Adami\-nekeb.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:33
