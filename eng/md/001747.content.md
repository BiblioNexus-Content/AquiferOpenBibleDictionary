## Eliam (id: 4997)

ELIAM
=====

1. Another name for Ammiel, the father of Bathsheba, the wife of Uriah the Hittite ([2 Samuel 11:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:3)).

    *See* Ammiel \#3.

2. Another name for Ahijah the Pelonite in [2 Samuel 23:34](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:34).
3. *See* Ahijah \#7.
