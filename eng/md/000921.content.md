## Beth-Nimrah (id: 4192)

BETH\-NIMRAH
============

A Moabite city given to and rebuilt by the tribe of Gad at the division of the promised land ([Numbers 32:3](https://ref.ly/Num32:3); [Joshua 13:27](https://ref.ly/Josh13:27)). Beth\-nimrah has been identified with modern Tell el\-Bleibil, 12\.9 kilometers (eight miles) east of the Jordan River.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 32:3; JOS 13:27
