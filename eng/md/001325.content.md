## City of Destruction (id: 4821)

CITY OF DESTRUCTION
===================

A phrase in [Isaiah 19:18](https://ref.ly/Isa19:18) in the King James Version of the Bible. Most Bible scholars believe this refers to Heliopolis, an ancient Egyptian city.

*See* Heliopolis.
