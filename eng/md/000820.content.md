## Bell (id: 4090)

BELL
====

A small noisemaker. Bells were sometimes attached between decorative pomegranates around the bottom of the high priestly robe ([Exodus 28:33](https://ref.ly/Exod28:33-Exod28:34)[–](https://ref.ly/Exod28:33-Exod28:34)[34](https://ref.ly/Exod28:33-Exod28:34); [39:25](https://ref.ly/Exod39:25-Exod39:26)[–](https://ref.ly/Exod39:25-Exod39:26)[26](https://ref.ly/Exod39:25-Exod39:26)).
