## Amana (id: 3539)

AMANA
=====

A mountain ridge, probably in the Anti\-Lebanon range. [Song of Solomon 4:8](https://ref.ly/Song4:8) mentions it along with Mount Senir and Mount Hermon. It is perhaps the source of the Abana (or Amana) River ([2 Kings 5:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs5:12)). *See* Abana.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 5:12; SNG 4:8
* **Associated Articles:** Abana (ID: `3266@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
