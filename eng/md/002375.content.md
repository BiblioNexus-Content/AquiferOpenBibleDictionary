## Hamon-Gog (id: 5628)

HAMON\-GOG\*
============

Valley in the Transjordan where the dead of the armies of Gog (Gog’s “hordes”) will be buried ([Ez 39:11, 15](https://ref.ly/Ezek39:11,Ezek39:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 39:11; EZK 39:15
