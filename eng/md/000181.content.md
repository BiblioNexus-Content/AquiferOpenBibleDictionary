## Ahiam (id: 3440)

AHIAM
=====

Sharar’s son and a warrior among David’s mighty men who were known as “the thirty” ([2 Samuel 23:33](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:33)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:33
