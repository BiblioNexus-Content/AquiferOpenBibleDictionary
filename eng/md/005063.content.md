## Shedeur (id: 8326)

SHEDEUR
=======

The father of Elizur. Elizur was an important leader from the tribe of Reuben. Moses asked Elizur to help count all the men who could serve in the army ([Numbers 1:5](https://ref.ly/Num1:5); [2:10](https://ref.ly/Num2:10); [10:18](https://ref.ly/Num10:18)). Elizur also represented his tribe during the special ceremony to dedicate the altar ([7:30–35](https://ref.ly/Num7:30-Num7:35)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:5; NUM 2:10; NUM 10:18; NUM 7:30–NUM 7:35
