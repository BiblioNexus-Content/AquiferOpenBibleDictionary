## Soco (Person) (id: 8554)

SOCO (Person)
=============

Son of Heber, listed in the genealogy of Caleb ([1 Chr 4:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:18)). Since the Calebites were located in the southern hill country of Judah, Soco may be identified with the city in [Joshua 15:48](https://ref.ly/Josh15:48). *See* Soco (Place), Socoh \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:48; 1CH 4:18
