## Racal (id: 7900)

RACAL
=====

One of the towns in southern Judah in which David distributed the spoils taken from the defeated Amalekites ([1 Sm 30:29](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:29)). The city is named Carmel in the Septuagint.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 30:29
