## Kamon (id: 6517)

KAMON
=====

City in Gilead where Jair the judge was buried ([Jgs 10:5](https://ref.ly/Judg10:5)). While the place has not been identified with certainty, modern Kameim, a small village southeast of the Sea of Galilee, probably reflects the original name, if not the exact location.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 10:5
