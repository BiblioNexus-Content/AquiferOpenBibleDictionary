## Jesus (id: 6356)

JESUS
=====

1. A name meaning “savior” or “Jehovah \[Yahweh] is salvation” given to the Messiah.

    *See* Jesus Christ.

2. The King James Version translation of Joshua, son of Nun ([Acts 7:45](https://ref.ly/Acts7:45) and [Hebrews 4:8](https://ref.ly/Heb4:8)).

    *See* Joshua (Person) \#1.

3. A Jewish Christian, also called, “Justus.” He sent his greetings to the Christians at Colossae in the letter of the apostle Paul ([Colossians 4:11](https://ref.ly/Col4:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 7:45; COL 4:11; HEB 4:8
* **Associated Articles:** Jesus Christ (ID: `6354@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Joshua (Person) (ID: `6459@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
