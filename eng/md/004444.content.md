## Phenice (id: 7703)

PHENICE
=======

1. The King James Version form of Phoenicia. Phoenicia was a region on the eastern Mediterranean coast north of Palestine ([Acts 11:19](https://ref.ly/Acts11:19) and [15:3](https://ref.ly/Acts15:3)). This region was known for its trading cities and seafaring people.

    *See* Phoenicia, Phoenicians.

2. The King James Version form of Phoenix. Phoenix was a harbor along the southern coastline of Crete ([Acts 27:12](https://ref.ly/Acts27:12)).

    *See* Phoenix.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 11:19; ACT 15:3; ACT 27:12
* **Associated Articles:** Phoenicia, Phoenicians (ID: `7721@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Phoenix (ID: `7722@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
