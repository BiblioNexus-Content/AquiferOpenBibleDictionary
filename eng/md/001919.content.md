## Ethnarch (id: 5176)

ETHNARCH
========

The title given to a person who rules because of the permission given by another country. Ethnarch was a position below a king but above a tetrarch or governor. Three ethnarchs ruled in Palestine: 

1. Simon, during the Maccabean period ([1 Maccabees 14:47](https://ref.ly/1Macc14:47))
2. Archelaus in the time of Jesus ([Matthew 2:22](https://ref.ly/Matt2:22))
3. Another in Damascus during the life of the apostle Paul ([2 Corinthians 11:32](https://ref.ly/2Cor11:32))

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 2:22; 2CO 11:32; 1MA 14:47
