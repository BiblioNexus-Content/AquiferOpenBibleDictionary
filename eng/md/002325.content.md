## Hadrach (id: 5579)

HADRACH\*
=========

Settlement in northwest Lebanon mentioned only in association with Tyre, Sidon, Hamath, and Damascus ([Zec 9:1](https://ref.ly/Zech9:1), see nlt mg). The last two cities were listed in Assyrian records with Hatarivia, with which Hadrach is now identified.

* **Associated Passages:** ZEC 9:1
