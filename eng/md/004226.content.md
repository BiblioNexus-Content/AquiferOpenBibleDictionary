## Omar (id: 7485)

OMAR
====

Second son of Eliphaz, grandson of Esau and the great\-grandson of Abraham ([Gn 36:11, 15](https://ref.ly/Gen36:11,Gen36:15); [1 Chr 1:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:36)); an Edomite clan chief.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:11; GEN 36:15; 1CH 1:36
