## Beth-Arabah (id: 4154)

BETH\-ARABAH
============

One of six cities in the wilderness southeast of Jericho on the border between the tribal land of Judah and Benjamin ([Joshua 15:6, 61](https://ref.ly/Josh15:6,Josh15:61); [18:22](https://ref.ly/Josh18:22)). The modern Ain Gharbeh in the Wadi el\-Quelt may be the site of Beth\-arabah.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:6; JOS 15:61; JOS 18:22
