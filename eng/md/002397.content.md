## Hanoch (id: 5652)

HANOCH
======

1. Midian’s third son, and grandson of Abraham by Keturah ([Genesis 25:4](https://ref.ly/Gen25:4); [1 Chronicles 1:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:33)).
2. Reuben’s first son ([Genesis 46:9](https://ref.ly/Gen46:9); [Exodus 6:14](https://ref.ly/Exod6:14); [1 Chronicles 5:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:3)) and ancestor of the Hanochites ([Numbers 26:5](https://ref.ly/Num26:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:4; GEN 46:9; EXO 6:14; NUM 26:5; 1CH 1:33; 1CH 5:3
