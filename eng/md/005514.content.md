## Tema (Person) (id: 8778)

TEMA (Person)
=============

Ninth son of Ishmael who became chief of a powerful nomadic tribe in the north Arabian wilderness ([Gn 25:15](https://ref.ly/Gen25:15); [1 Chr 1:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:30); [Jer 25:23](https://ref.ly/Jer25:23)). The descendants of Tema were primarily caravan traders who controlled access to important routes across the desert ([Jb 6:19](https://ref.ly/Job6:19)). Tema was also associated with the territory and a town. *See* Tema (Place).
