## Regemmelech (id: 7977)

REGEMMELECH
===========

One of the delegation sent to inquire whether fasting to commemorate the temple destruction should continue ([Zec 7:2](https://ref.ly/Zech7:2)). The name may refer to a person or could be a title meaning “friend of the king.”

* **Associated Passages:** ZEC 7:2
