## Ithrite (id: 6114)

ITHRITE
=======

A family or clan that lived at Kiriath\-jearim ([1 Chronicles 2:53](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:53)). Two of King David's mighty warriors, Ira and Gareb, were Ithrites ([2 Samuel 23:38](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:38); [1 Chronicles 11:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:40); in the New Living Translation, they are described as being "from Jattir"). The name Ithrite may come from the place name Jattir or from a person named Jether.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:38; 1CH 2:53; 1CH 11:40
