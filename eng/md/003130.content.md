## Joah (id: 6387)

JOAH
====

1\. Asaph’s son and a court official under King Hezekiah ([2 Kgs 18:18, 26](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:18,2Kgs18:26); [Is 36:3, 11, 22](https://ref.ly/Isa36:3,Isa36:11,Isa36:22)). He was one of the officers sent by Hezekiah to deal with the Assyrians during the siege of Jerusalem.

2\. Zimmah’s son from Levi’s tribe ([1 Chr 6:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:21)).

3\. Levite, Obed\-edom’s son and a gatekeeper of the sanctuary in David’s time ([1 Chr 26:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:4)).

4\. Joahaz’s son and a recorder under King Josiah; he was one of the deputies overseeing the temple repairs ([2 Chr 34:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 18:18; 2KI 18:26; 1CH 6:21; 1CH 26:4; 2CH 34:8; ISA 36:3; ISA 36:11; ISA 36:22
