## Joahaz (id: 6386)

JOAHAZ
======

1\. Variant spelling or contraction of Jehoahaz, Jehu’s son, in [2 Kings 14:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:1). *See* Jehoahaz \#1.

2\. Joah’s father. Joah was King Josiah’s recorder ([2 Chr 34:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 14:1; 2CH 34:8
