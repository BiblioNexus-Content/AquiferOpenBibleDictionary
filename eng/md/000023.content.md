## Abel-mizraim (id: 3280)

ABEL\-MIZRAIM
=============

An alternate name for Atad, where Jacob’s sons stopped during his funeral on the way to Hebron. Abel\-mizraim was a place in Canaan ([Genesis 50:11](https://ref.ly/Gen50:11)). 

*See* Atad.
