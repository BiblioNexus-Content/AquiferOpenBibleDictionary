## Admatha (id: 3385)

ADMATHA
=======

One of seven counselors of King Ahasuerus ([Esther 1:14](https://ref.ly/Esth1:14)). The king’s counselors suggested that he banish Queen Vashti for saying no to his invitation to appear at a drunken party.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:14
