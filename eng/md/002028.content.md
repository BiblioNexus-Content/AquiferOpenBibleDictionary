## Fly (id: 5284)

FLY
===

Two\-winged insect. In Scripture, several species are in view, including the common housefly ([Eccl 10:1](https://ref.ly/Eccl10:1)) and the horsefly ([Is 7:18](https://ref.ly/Isa7:18)). *See* Animals.

* **Associated Passages:** ECC 10:1; ISA 7:18
