## Isshiah (id: 6098)

ISSHIAH
=======

1\. Izrahiah’s son from Issachar’s tribe ([1 Chr 7:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:3)).

2\. Warrior from Benjamin’s tribe who joined David in his struggle against King Saul. Isshiah was one of David’s ambidextrous archers and slingers ([1 Chr 12:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:6)).

3\. Uzziel’s son from Levi’s tribe ([1 Chr 23:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:20); [24:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:25)).

4\. Rehabiah’s son from Levi’s tribe and a descendant of Moses ([1 Chr 24:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:3; 1CH 12:6; 1CH 23:20; 1CH 24:21; 1CH 24:25
