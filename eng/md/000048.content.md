## Abinadab (id: 3306)

ABINADAB
========

1. Someone who lived in Kiriath\-jearim who housed the ark of God after it was brought back from the Philistines ([1 Samuel 6:21–7:2](https://ref.ly/1Sam6:21-1Sam7:2)).
2. Jesse’s second son, and brother of David ([1 Samuel 16:8](https://ref.ly/1Sam16:8); [17:13](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:13); [1 Chronicles 2:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:13)). He served in Saul’s army for part of the Philistine war.
3. Another form of Ben\-abinadab. He was one of King Solomon’s administrative officers in [1 Kings 4:11](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:11). *See* Ben\-abinadab.
4. One of Saul’s sons ([1 Chronicles 8:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:33); [10:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:2)).
