## Zeri (id: 9199)

ZERI
====

One of the sons of Jeduthun who prophesied with the lyre in thanksgiving to the Lord ([1 Chr 25:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:3)). He may be the same person as Izri (v [11](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:3; 1CH 25:11
