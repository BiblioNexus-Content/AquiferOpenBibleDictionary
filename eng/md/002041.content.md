## Former Rain (id: 5295)

FORMER RAIN\*
=============

KJV term for important rainfall that begins the agricultural year in Palestine, usually in October ([Dt 11:14](https://ref.ly/Deut11:14); [Jer 5:24](https://ref.ly/Jer5:24); [Jas 5:7](https://ref.ly/Jas5:7)). *See* Palestine.
