## Amnon (id: 3567)

AMNON
=====

1. David’s oldest son by his wife Ahinoam. He was born in Hebron ([2 Samuel 3:2](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:2); [1 Chronicles 3:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:1)). Amnon deceived and violated Tamar, his beautiful half\-sister. He was killed in revenge by Tamar’s brother Absalom ([2 Samuel 13:1–33](https://ref.ly/2Sam13:1-2Sam13:33)).
2. The first son of Shimon from Judah’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 4:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 3:2; 1CH 3:1; 1CH 4:20; 2SA 13:1–2SA 13:33
