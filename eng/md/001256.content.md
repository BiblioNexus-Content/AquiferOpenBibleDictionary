## Cherub (Place) (id: 4511)

CHERUB (Place)
==============

One of five Babylonian cities. Israelites who came from there could not prove their ancestry, they returned there after the exile ([Ezra 2:59](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:59); [Nehemiah 7:61](https://ref.ly/Neh7:61)).
