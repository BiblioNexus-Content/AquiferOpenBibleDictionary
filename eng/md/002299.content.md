## Gurbaal (id: 5553)

GURBAAL\*
=========

Town in the Negev occupied by Arabs, possibly in the neighborhood of Edom, which Uzziah of Judah conquered ([2 Chr 26:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr26:7), nlt mg).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 26:7
