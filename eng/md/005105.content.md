## Shephi, Shepho (id: 8369)

SHEPHI\*, SHEPHO
================

One of Shobal’s five sons and a descendant of Seir the Horite. Shepho is listed in the genealogy of Abraham through Esau’s contact with the nation ([Gn 36:23](https://ref.ly/Gen36:23)); his name is alternately spelled Shephi ([1 Chr 1:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:40); see nlt mg).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:23; 1CH 1:40
