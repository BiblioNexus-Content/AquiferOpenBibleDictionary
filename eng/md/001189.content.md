## Castor and Pollux (id: 4445)

CASTOR AND POLLUX
=================

According to Greek and Roman mythology, these were twin sons of Zeus. The apostle Paul sailed from Malta to Puteoli on a ship whose sign or figurehead was the "Twin Brothers" ([Acts 28:11](https://ref.ly/Acts28:11)). These twin brothers were also known as the Dioscuri (which means "sons of Zeus").

*See* Dioscuri.
