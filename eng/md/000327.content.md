## Anamim, Anamites (id: 3589)

ANAMIM, ANAMITES
================

An unidentified group of people, possibly related to the Egyptians. They are mentioned in the biblical records of the earliest nations ([Genesis 10:13](https://ref.ly/Gen10:13); [1 Chronicles 1:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:13; 1CH 1:11
