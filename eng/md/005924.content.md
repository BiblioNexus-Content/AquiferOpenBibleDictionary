## Zedad (id: 9169)

ZEDAD
=====

One of the geographical landmarks of Israel’s northern boundary, mentioned between Hamath and Ziphron ([Nm 34:8](https://ref.ly/Num34:8); [Ez 47:15](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:8; EZK 47:15
