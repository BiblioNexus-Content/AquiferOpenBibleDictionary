## Bethulia (id: 4210)

BETHULIA
========

A town described in the book of Judith as being near Dothan on a hill over the plain of Esdraelon ([Judith 4:6](https://ref.ly/Jdt4:6); [6:11](https://ref.ly/Jdt6:11)). Nebuchadnezzar’s general, Holofernes, tried to control Bethulia by controlling its water sources ([Judith 7:6](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:6-Jdt7:7)[–](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:6-Jdt7:7)[7](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:6-Jdt7:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 4:6; JDT 6:11; JDT 7:6–JDT 7:7
