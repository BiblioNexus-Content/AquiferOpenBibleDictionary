## Annan (id: 3617)

ANNAN
=====

The father of five sons who divorced their foreign wives as Ezra had instructed ([1 Esdas 9:32](https://ref.ly/1Esd9:32)). He is also called Harim ([Ezra 10:31](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:31; 1ES 9:32
