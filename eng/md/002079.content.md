## Gabri, Gabrias (id: 5334)

GABRI, GABRIAS\*
================

Brother or father of Gabael. The genitive case of the original does not make the exact relationship certain; it simply specifies that there is a relationship ([Tb 1:14](https://ref.ly/Tob1:14); [4:20](https://ref.ly/Tob4:20)).

*See also* Gabael \#2.
