## Haruz (id: 5688)

HARUZ
=====

Haruz was the maternal grandfather of Amon, who was a king of Judah ([2 Kings 21:19](https://ref.ly/2Kgs21:19)). This means Haruz was the father of Meshullemeth, who was Amon's mother.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 21:19
