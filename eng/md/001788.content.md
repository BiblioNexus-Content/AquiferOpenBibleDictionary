## Elnathan (id: 5040)

ELNATHAN
========

1\. Grandfather of King Jehoiachin. His daughter, Jehoiachin’s mother, was Nehushta ([2 Kgs 24:8](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:8)).

2, 3, 4\. Three Jewish leaders whom Ezra sent to Iddo at Casiphia to obtain Levites and temple servants for the caravan of Jews returning to Palestine from Babylonia ([Ezr 8:16](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:16)).

5\. Acbor’s son, who was ordered by King Jehoiakim to bring back Uriah from Egypt to be executed for prophesying against the king ([Jer 26:22–23](https://ref.ly/Jer26:22-Jer26:23)). Elnathan was present with other princes when Baruch read the Lord’s words of warning written at Jeremiah’s dictation on a scroll ([36:12](https://ref.ly/Jer36:12)); he tried unsuccessfully to prevent Jehoiakim from burning the scroll (v [25](https://ref.ly/Jer36:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 24:8; EZR 8:16; JER 36:12; JER 36:25; JER 26:22–JER 26:23
