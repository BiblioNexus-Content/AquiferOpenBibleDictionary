## Bel (id: 4095)

BEL
===

The title of the Babylonian god Marduk. The title was used disrespectfully by Isaiah ([Isaiah 46:1](https://ref.ly/Isa46:1)). Jeremiah speaks about Bel in [Jeremiah 50:2](https://ref.ly/Jer50:2) and [51:44](https://ref.ly/Jer51:44), and Bel is the idol in the non\-biblical story Bel and the Dragon. 

*See* Marduk.
