## Migdal-Gad (id: 7125)

MIGDAL\-GAD
===========

Village in Judah located in the Shephelah district of Lachish ([Jos 15:37](https://ref.ly/Josh15:37)). It is perhaps identifiable with Khirbet el\-Mejdeleh, southeast of Tell el\-Nuweir.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:37
