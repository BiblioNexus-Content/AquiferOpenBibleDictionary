## Maaziah (id: 6821)

MAAZIAH
=======

1\. Levite who served in the temple during David’s reign ([1 Chr 24:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:18)).

2\. Levite who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant ([Neh 10:8](https://ref.ly/Neh10:8)); sometimes identified with Maadiah, a postexilic priest ([Neh 12:5](https://ref.ly/Neh12:5)).

*See also* Maadiah.
