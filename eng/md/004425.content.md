## Peullethai, Peulthai (id: 7685)

PEULLETHAI, PEUlthaI\*
======================

Obed\-edom’s son, who was a Levite gatekeeper in the sanctuary during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 26:5
