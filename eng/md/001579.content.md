## Diblah (id: 4832)

DIBLAH\*
========

ASV form of Riblah, the name of the place from which King Nebuchadnezzar directed operations against Jerusalem in 588–586 BC ([Ez 6:14](https://ref.ly/Ezek6:14); cf. [Jer 52:9–27](https://ref.ly/Jer52:9-Jer52:27)). *See* Riblah.
