## Birsha (id: 4251)

BIRSHA
======

Ruler of Gomorrah in the days of Abraham and Lot. Birsha was one of five Canaanite city\-kings who unsuccessfully rebelled against King Chedorlaomer of Elam and his three allies ([Gn 14:2](https://ref.ly/Gen14:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:2
