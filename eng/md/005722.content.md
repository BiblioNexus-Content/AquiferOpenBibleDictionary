## Ur (Person) (id: 8979)

UR (Person)
===========

The father of Eliphal, one of the mighty men of King David ([1 Chronicles 11:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:35)). Ur is probably the same person as Ahasbai in the parallel passage ([2 Samuel 23:34](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:34)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:34; 1CH 11:35
