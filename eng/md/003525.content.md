## Lo-Ruhamah (id: 6781)

LO\-RUHAMAH
===========

Symbolic name, meaning “Not pitied” ([Hos 1:6–8](https://ref.ly/Hos1:6-Hos1:8)), given by the prophet Hosea to his daughter, indicating God’s rejection of Israel. *See* Ruhamah.

* **Associated Passages:** HOS 1:6–HOS 1:8
* **Associated Articles:** Ruhamah (ID: `8070@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
