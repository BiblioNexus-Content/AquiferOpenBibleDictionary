## Bunni (id: 4362)

BUNNI
=====

1\. Levite who sang praise to God after Ezra’s public reading of the law ([Neh 9:4](https://ref.ly/Neh9:4)).

2\. Political leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:15](https://ref.ly/Neh10:15)).

3\. Hashabiah’s father ([Neh 11:15](https://ref.ly/Neh11:15)), a Levite descended from Merari ([1 Chr 9:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:14)). Possibly the same as \#1 above.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:14; NEH 9:4; NEH 10:15; NEH 11:15
