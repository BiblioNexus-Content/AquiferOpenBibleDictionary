## Buzite (id: 4369)

BUZITE
======

Resident of Buz. Elihu, one of Job’s protagonists, is described as being the son of Barachel the Buzite ([Jb 32:2, 6](https://ref.ly/Job32:2,Job32:6)). *See* Buz (Place).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 32:2; JOB 32:6
