## Zererah (id: 9195)

ZERERAH
=======

City mentioned in connection with Gideon’s defeat of the Midianites ([Jgs 7:22](https://ref.ly/Judg7:22)); perhaps also identifiable with Zarethan.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 7:22
