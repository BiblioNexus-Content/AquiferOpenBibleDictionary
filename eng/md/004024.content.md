## Nahaliel (id: 7281)

NAHALIEL
========

A temporary camping place for the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings. It was east of the Dead Sea in the region of Moab between Mattanah and Bamoth ([Numbers 21:19](https://ref.ly/Num21:19)).

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
