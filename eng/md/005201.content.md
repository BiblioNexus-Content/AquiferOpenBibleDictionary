## Shupham, Shuphamite (id: 8465)

SHUPHAM, SHUPHAMITE
===================

Shupham was a son of Benjamin and the ancestor of the Shuphamite family. Shupham is the King James Version spelling of Shephupham in [Numbers 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39).
