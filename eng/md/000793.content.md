## Beelzebul (id: 4063)

BEELZEBUL
=========

A name meaning “lord of the flies” or “lord of the manure pile,” referring to Satan. It was used against Jesus by his enemies ([Matthew 10:25](https://ref.ly/Matt10:25); [12:24](https://ref.ly/Matt12:24); [Luke 11:15](https://ref.ly/Luke11:15)). 

*See* Baal\-zebub.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 10:25; MAT 12:24; LUK 11:15
