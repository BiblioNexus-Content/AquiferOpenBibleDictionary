## Amashai, Amashsai (id: 3544)

AMASHAI, AMASHSAI
=================

Azarel’s son. He was one of the leading priests who returned to Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile ([Nehemiah 11:13](https://ref.ly/Neh11:13)). Amashsai may possibly be identical with Maasai ([1 Chronicles 9:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:12; NEH 11:13
