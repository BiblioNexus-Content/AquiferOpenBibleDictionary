## Epher (id: 5103)

EPHER
=====

1\. Son of Midian and grandson of Abraham through his concubine Keturah, whose tribe was sent to the east. Some were supportive of Abraham’s descendants and others became enemies ([Gn 25:4](https://ref.ly/Gen25:4); [1 Chr 1:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:33)).

2\. Son of Ezrah from Judah’s tribe ([1 Chr 4:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:17)).

3\. Head of a household and a great warrior in the half\-tribe of Manasseh. He lived between Bashan and Mt Hermon ([1 Chr 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:4; 1CH 1:33; 1CH 4:17; 1CH 5:24
