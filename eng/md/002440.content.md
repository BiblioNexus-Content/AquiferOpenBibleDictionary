## Hashbaddanah (id: 5695)

HASHBADDANAH
============

Man, possibly of Levite origin, who stood on Ezra’s left when Ezra read the law to the people ([Neh 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 8:4
