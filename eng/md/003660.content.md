## Manaen (id: 6913)

MANAEN
======

One of the prophets and teachers in the church at Antioch ([Acts 13:1](https://ref.ly/Acts13:1)). The Bible identifies him as a close companion of Herod the tetrarch. (A tetrarch was a ruler who governed one of four parts of a region.) The name Manaen is a Greek form of the Hebrew name Menahem.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 13:1
