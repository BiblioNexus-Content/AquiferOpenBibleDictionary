## Mahalath (Person) (id: 6866)

MAHALATH (Person)
=================

1\. Daughter of Ishmael, Nebaioth’s sister, Esau’s third wife, and Reuel’s mother ([Gn 28:9](https://ref.ly/Gen28:9)); alternately called Basemath (kjv “Bashemath”) in [Genesis 36:3–17](https://ref.ly/Gen36:3-Gen36:17).

2\. Jerimoth’s daughter and King Rehoboam’s first wife ([2 Chr 11:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 28:9; 2CH 11:18; GEN 36:3–GEN 36:17
