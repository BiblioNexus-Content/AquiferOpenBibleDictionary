## Ishi (id: 6070)

ISHI
====

1\. Appaim’s son, the father of Sheshan and a descendant of Judah through Jerahmeel’s line ([1 Chr 2:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:31)).

2\. Man from Judah’s tribe whose descendants were Zoheth and Ben\-zoheth ([1 Chr 4:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:20)).

3\. Simeonite whose four sons led 500 men to Mt Seir, where they destroyed the remnant Amalekites and settled their own people ([1 Chr 4:42](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:42)).

4\. One of the leaders of the half\-tribe of Manasseh east of the Jordan ([1 Chr 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:24)).

5\. Name of God, meaning “my husband,” by which Israel will one day address him ([Hos 2:16](https://ref.ly/Hos2:16)).
