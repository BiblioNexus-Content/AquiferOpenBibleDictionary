## Anathoth (Person) (id: 3598)

ANATHOTH (Person)
=================

1. Becher’s son from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 7:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:8)).
2. A political leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile to Babylon ([Nehemiah 10:19](https://ref.ly/Neh10:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:8; NEH 10:19
