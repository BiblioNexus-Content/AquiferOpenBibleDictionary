## Ammishaddai (id: 3563)

AMMISHADDAI
===========

Ahiezer’s father. Ahiezer was the leader of Dan’s tribe when the Israelites were wandering in the Sinai wilderness after their escape from Egypt ([Numbers 1:12](https://ref.ly/Num1:12); [2:25](https://ref.ly/Num2:25); [10:25](https://ref.ly/Num10:25)). As the leader he presented his tribe’s offering at the dedication of the tabernacle ([Numbers 7:66, 71](https://ref.ly/Num7:66,Num7:71)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:12; NUM 2:25; NUM 7:66; NUM 7:71; NUM 10:25
