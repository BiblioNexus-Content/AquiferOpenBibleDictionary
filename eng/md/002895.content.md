## Jabesh (Person) (id: 6148)

JABESH (Person)
===============

The father of Shaullum. Shallum killed Zechariah, who was the king of Israel ([2 Kings 15:10–14](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:10-2Kgs15:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 15:10–2KI 15:14
