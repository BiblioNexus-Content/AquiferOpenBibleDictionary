## Eastern Sea (id: 4918)

EASTERN SEA
===========

Another name for the Salt Sea, or Dead Sea. The name comes from the sea’s location on the eastern boundary of the land of Israel ([Ezekiel 47:18](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:18); [Joel 2:20](https://ref.ly/Joel2:20); [Zechariah 14:8](https://ref.ly/Zech14:8)). 

*See* Dead Sea.
