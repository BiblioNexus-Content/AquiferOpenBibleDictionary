## Matri, Matrites (id: 6977)

MATRI\*, MATRITES
=================

Family of Benjamin’s tribe. Saul, the first king of Israel, came from this family ([1 Sm 10:21](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 10:21
