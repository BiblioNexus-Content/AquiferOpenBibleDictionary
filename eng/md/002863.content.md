## Ittai (id: 6116)

ITTAI
=====

1. A Philistine man from the city of Gath. He and 600 other men from Gath stayed loyal to King David when David had to flee from his son Absalom. Absalom was trying to take over the kingdom ([2 Samuel 15:18–22](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:18-2Sam15:22)). Ittai commanded a third of the army of David in the battle against the forces of Absalom ([18:2, 5](https://ref.ly/2Sam18:2,2Sam18:5)).
2. A warrior from the tribe of Benjamin who was one of the mighty men of King David ([2 Samuel 23:29](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:29); spelled "Ithai" in [1 Chronicles 11:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 18:2; 2SA 18:5; 2SA 23:29; 1CH 11:31; 2SA 15:18–2SA 15:22
