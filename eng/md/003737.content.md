## Meal Offering (id: 6997)

MEAL OFFERING\*
===============

An offering of grain or fine flour. It has also been translated “grain offering” (nlt, niv), “cereal offering” (rsv) and “meat offering” (kjv). *See* Offerings and Sacrifices.
