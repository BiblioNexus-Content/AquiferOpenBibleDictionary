## Uthai (id: 8981)

UTHAI
=====

1. One who returned to Israel after the exile in Babylon. He is listed as the son of Ammihud from the Perez branch of the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 9:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:4)).
2. One of Bigvai’s sons who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra ([Ezra 8:14](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:4; EZR 8:14
