## Lilith (id: 6752)

LILITH\*
========

Hebrew for the night creature referred to in [Isaiah 34:14](https://ref.ly/Isa34:14). According to Hebrew mythology, Lilith was Adam’s first wife, who was replaced by Eve; subsequently, Lilith became a female demon.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 34:14
