## Prison, Court of the (id: 7821)

PRISON, Court of the
====================

The King James Version translation of “Court of the Guard.” It was an open court where they kept the prophet Jeremiah prisoner ([Jeremiah 32:2](https://ref.ly/Jer32:2)).
