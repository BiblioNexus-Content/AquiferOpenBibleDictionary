## Shaveh Kiriathaim (id: 8302)

SHAVEH KIRIATHAIM\*
===================

Plain east of the Dead Sea near the city of Kiriathaim and occupied by the people of Emim. The Emimites in Shaveh Kiriathaim are listed with a number of other tribes and nations that King Kedorlaomer and his allies defeated ([Gn 14:5](https://ref.ly/Gen14:5); nlt, “Kiriathaim”). This plain was later inherited by the sons of Reuben.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:5
