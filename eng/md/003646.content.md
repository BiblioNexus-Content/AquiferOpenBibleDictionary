## Mallothi (id: 6905)

MALLOTHI
========

One of Heman’s 17 children ([1 Chr 25:4–5](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4-1Chr25:5)), who became leader of the 19th of 24 divisions of singers for service in the sanctuary during David’s reign (v [26](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:26; 1CH 25:4–1CH 25:5
