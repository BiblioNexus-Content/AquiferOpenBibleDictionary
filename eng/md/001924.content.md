## Eumenes (id: 5181)

EUMENES
=======

The designation of a king of Pergamum, Eumenes II (197–158 BC) mentioned in [1 Maccabees 8:8](https://ref.ly/1Macc8:8) in connection with an alliance that Judas Maccabeus made with the Romans during their war with Antiochus the Great.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 8:8
