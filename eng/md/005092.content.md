## Sheminith (id: 8354)

SHEMINITH\*
===========

Obscure Hebrew term, meaning “the eighth,” in [1 Chronicles 15:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:21) and the superscriptions of [Psalms 6](https://ref.ly/Ps6:1-Ps6:10) and [12](https://ref.ly/Ps12:1-Ps12:8) (see rsv and nlt mg), whose function as a musical cue or instrument is uncertain. *See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 15:21; PSA 6:1–PSA 6:10; PSA 12:1–PSA 12:8
* **Associated Articles:** Music (ID: `7248@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
