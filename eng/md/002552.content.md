## Hepher (Person) (id: 5806)

HEPHER (Person)
===============

1. A man from the tribe of Manasseh and founder of the Hepherite clan ([Numbers 26:32](https://ref.ly/Num26:32)).
2. A son of Ashhur from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 4:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:6)).
3. One of the "mighty men" of King David ([1 Chronicles 11:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:36)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:32; 1CH 4:6; 1CH 11:36
