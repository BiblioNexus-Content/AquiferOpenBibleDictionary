## Nemuel (id: 7354)

NEMUEL
======

2\. One of Simeon’s sons ([Nm 26:12](https://ref.ly/Num26:12); [1 Chr 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24)), also called Jemuel ([Gn 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10)). *See* Jemuel.
