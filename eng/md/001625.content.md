## Dodavah, Dodavahu (id: 4876)

DODAVAH\*, DODAVAHU
===================

Inhabitant of Mareshah and father of Eliezer the prophet. Eliezer spoke against King Jehoshaphat of Judah because of his alliance with King Ahaziah of Israel ([2 Chr 20:37](https://ref.ly/2Chr20:37), kjv “Dodavah”).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 20:37
