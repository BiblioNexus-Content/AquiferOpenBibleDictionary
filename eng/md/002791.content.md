## Iphtah-El (id: 6042)

IPHTAH\-EL
==========

Valley on Asher and Zebulun’s border ([Jos 19:14, 27](https://ref.ly/Josh19:14,Josh19:27)), possibly the modern Sahl el\-Battof.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:14; JOS 19:27
