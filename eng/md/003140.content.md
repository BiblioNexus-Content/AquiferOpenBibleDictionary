## Joed (id: 6398)

JOED
====

Descendant of Benjamin living in Jerusalem during the days of Nehemiah ([Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)). His name, meaning “Yahweh is witness,” does not appear in a parallel list in [1 Chronicles 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:7).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:7; NEH 11:7
