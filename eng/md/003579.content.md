## Machnadebai (id: 6837)

MACHNADEBAI
===========

A son of Bani (also called Binnui). Machnadebai followed Ezra's instruction to divorce his foreign wife during the time after the exile to Babylon ([Ezra 10:40](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:40)). 

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:40
