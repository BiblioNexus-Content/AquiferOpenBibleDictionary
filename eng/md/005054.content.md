## Shebat (id: 8317)

SHEBAT
======

Shebat is one of the months in the Jewish calendar. On our modern calendar, it usually occurs during parts of January and February ([Zechariah 1:7](https://ref.ly/Zech1:7)).

*See* Calendars, Ancient and Modern.
