## Henadad (id: 5799)

HENADAD
=======

Head of a Levite family that participated in the rebuilding of the temple ([Ezr 3:9](https://ref.ly/Ezra3:9)). Members of this family also helped to build the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 3:18, 24](https://ref.ly/Neh3:18,Neh3:24)), and signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God together with Nehemiah ([10:9](https://ref.ly/Neh10:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 3:9; NEH 3:18; NEH 3:24; NEH 10:9
