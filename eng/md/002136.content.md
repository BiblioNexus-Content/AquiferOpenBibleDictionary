## Geber (id: 5392)

GEBER
=====

1\. Alternate name for Ben\-geber, one of Solomon’s commissariat officers, in [1 Kings 4:13](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:13). *See* Ben\-geber.

2\. Uri’s son, who was responsible for providing food for Solomon’s household. His territory was probably south of Ramoth\-gilead ([1 Kgs 4:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:19)). Perhaps \#1 and \#2 were related.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:13; 1KI 4:19
