## Saph (id: 8137)

SAPH
====

A descendant of the giants, who was killed by Sibbecai the Hushathite (one of David's warriors). Sibbecai killed Saph at Gad in a battle between Israel and Philistia ([2 Samuel 21:18](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:18)). Another name for Saph was Sippai ([1 Chronicles 20:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr20:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 21:18; 1CH 20:4
