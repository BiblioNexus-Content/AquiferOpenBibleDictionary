## Ummah (id: 8958)

UMMAH
=====

One of the villages given to the tribe of Asher after the conquest of Canaan ([Joshua 19:30](https://ref.ly/Josh19:30)). The location of Ummah and nearby villages (Aphek and Rehob) are not known.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:30
