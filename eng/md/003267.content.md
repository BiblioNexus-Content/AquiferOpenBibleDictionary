## Kartan (id: 6525)

KARTAN
======

Levitical town assigned to the Gershonites from the tribe of Naphtali ([Jos 21:32](https://ref.ly/Josh21:32)). It is called Kiriathaim in [1 Chronicles 6:76](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:76). *See* Kiriathaim \#2; Levitical Cities.
