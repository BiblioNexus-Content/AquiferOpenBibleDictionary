## Kartah (id: 6524)

KARTAH
======

Levitical city in Zebulun’s territory. The list of cities assigned to the Merarite clan of Levites in [Joshua 21:34](https://ref.ly/Josh21:34) mentions Kartah, but the parallel passage in [1 Chronicles 6:77](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:77) (in the Hebrew) does not.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 21:34; 1CH 6:77
