## Hoham (id: 5876)

HOHAM
=====

Amorite king of Hebron, confederate with four other kings in reprisals against Gibeon for making peace with Joshua ([Jos 10:3](https://ref.ly/Josh10:3)). They were defeated and put to death at the cave of Makkedah (vv [16–27](https://ref.ly/Josh10:16-Josh10:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 10:3; JOS 10:16–JOS 10:27
