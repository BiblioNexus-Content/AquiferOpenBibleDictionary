## Mecherathite (id: 7004)

MECHERATHITE
============

A title given to Hepher in [1 Chronicles 11:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:36). In the parallel passage in [2 Samuel 23:34](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:34), Hepher is called “the son of the Maachathite” (in the King James Version) or “from Maacah” (in the New Living Translation). 

*See* Maacah, Maachah (Place); Maacathite, Maachathi, Maachathite.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:34; 1CH 11:36
* **Associated Articles:** Maacah, Maachah (Place) (ID: `6809@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Maacathite, Maachathi, Maachathite (ID: `6810@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
