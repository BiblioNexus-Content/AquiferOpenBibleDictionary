## Meshillemoth (id: 7084)

MESHILLEMOTH
============

1\. Father of Berekiah, a chief of Ephraim ([2 Chr 28:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:12)).

2\. Ancestor of the postexilic priest Amashsai ([Neh 11:13](https://ref.ly/Neh11:13)); alternately spelled Meshillemith in [1 Chronicles 9:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:12).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:12; 2CH 28:12; NEH 11:13
