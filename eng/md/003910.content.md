## Mite (id: 7169)

MITE
====

A small bronze or copper coin. It is worth a fraction of a cent. [Mark 12:42](https://ref.ly/Mark12:42) in the King James Version uses this term. *See* Coins; Money.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 12:42
* **Associated Articles:** Coins (ID: `4611@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Money (ID: `7194@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
