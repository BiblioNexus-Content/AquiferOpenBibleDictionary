## Baean (id: 3965)

BAEAN
=====

A tribe that was destroyed by Judas Maccabeus because it regularly ambushed Jewish travelers ([1 Maccabees 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:4-1Macc5:5)[–](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:4-1Macc5:5)[5](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:4-1Macc5:5)). Nothing else is known about this tribe. It was probably located east of the Jordan River.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 5:4–1MA 5:5
