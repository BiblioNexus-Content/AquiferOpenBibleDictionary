## Unni (id: 8965)

UNNI
====

1. One of the musicians that the chief of the Levites chose to sing and play the harp as part of the temple service during David’s reign ([1 Chronicles 15:18–20](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:18-1Chr15:20)).
2. The King James Version's spelling of Unno in [Nehemiah 12:9](https://ref.ly/Neh12:9). *See* Unno.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:9; 1CH 15:18–1CH 15:20
