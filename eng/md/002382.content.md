## Hanan (id: 5640)

HANAN
=====

1\. Shashak’s son and one of the chief men of Benjamin ([1 Chr 8:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:23)).

2\. Azel’s son from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 8:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:38); [9:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:44)).

3\. Warrior among David’s mighty men, who were known as “the thirty” ([1 Chr 11:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:43)).

4\. Ancestor of a group of temple assistants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Ezr 2:46](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:46); [Neh 7:49](https://ref.ly/Neh7:49)).

5\. Levitical assistant who explained to the people passages from the law read by Ezra ([Neh 8:7](https://ref.ly/Neh8:7)).

6\. Levite who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:10](https://ref.ly/Neh10:10)).

7, 8\. Two political leaders who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:22, 26](https://ref.ly/Neh10:22,Neh10:26)).

9\. One of the Levites whom Nehemiah appointed as treasurer over the storehouses ([Neh 13:13](https://ref.ly/Neh13:13)).

10\. Igdaliah’s son and head of a prophetic guild occupying the room in the temple where Jeremiah offered the Rechabites wine to drink ([Jer 35:4](https://ref.ly/Jer35:4)).
