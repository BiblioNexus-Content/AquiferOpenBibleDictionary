## Bethuel (Person) (id: 4209)

BETHUEL (Person)
================

The youngest son of Abraham’s brother Nahor and his wife, Milcah. Bethuel was Abraham’s nephew and the father of Rebekah ([Genesis 22:23](https://ref.ly/Gen22:23); [24:15, 24](https://ref.ly/Gen24:15,Gen24:24)). He was called an Aramean of Paddan\-aram ([Genesis 25:20](https://ref.ly/Gen25:20); [28:5](https://ref.ly/Gen28:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 22:23; GEN 24:15; GEN 24:24; GEN 25:20; GEN 28:5
