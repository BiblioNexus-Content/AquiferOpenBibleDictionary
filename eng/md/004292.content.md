## Pagiel (id: 7550)

PAGIEL
======

Pagiel was a son of Ocran from the tribe of Asher. Moses chose Paigel to help count the Israelites while they were in the wilderness. Paigel also served as the leader of his tribe during that time ([Numbers 1:13](https://ref.ly/Num1:13); [2:27](https://ref.ly/Num2:27); [7:72, 77](https://ref.ly/Num7:72,Num7:77); [10:26](https://ref.ly/Num10:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:13; NUM 2:27; NUM 7:72; NUM 7:77; NUM 10:26
