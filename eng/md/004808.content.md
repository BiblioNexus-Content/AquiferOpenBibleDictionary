## Rumah (id: 8073)

RUMAH
=====

Home of Pedaiah, the father of Zebidah, Jehoiakim’s mother ([2 Kgs 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:36)). Some identify it with Arumah, near Shechem (cf. [Jgs 9:41](https://ref.ly/Judg9:41)) or with Khirbet el\-Rumah in Galilee.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 9:41; 2KI 23:36
