## Penknife (id: 7644)

PENKNIFE\*
==========

Iron tool used to sharpen reed pens, cut papyrus, and carve letters in stone. It is mentioned by name only in [Jeremiah 36:23](https://ref.ly/Jer36:23) (rsv), but it is referred to elsewhere as a pen or tool of iron ([Jb 19:24](https://ref.ly/Job19:24); [Jer 17:1](https://ref.ly/Jer17:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 19:24; JER 17:1; JER 36:23
