## Helon (id: 5791)

HELON
=====

The father of Eliab. He was the leader of the tribe of Zebulun when Moses counted all the Israelite people (called a census) at God's command ([Numbers 1:9](https://ref.ly/Num1:9); [2:7](https://ref.ly/Num2:7); [7:24, 29](https://ref.ly/Num7:24,Num7:29); [10:16](https://ref.ly/Num10:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:9; NUM 2:7; NUM 7:24; NUM 7:29; NUM 10:16
