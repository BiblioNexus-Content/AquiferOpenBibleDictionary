## Cushan-Rishathaim (id: 4715)

CUSHAN\-RISHATHAIM
==================

King of Mesopotamia whom Israel served for eight years. The Lord raised up Othniel, Kenaz’s son, to deliver Israel out of his hand; later, Cushan\-rishathaim was defeated by Othniel in war ([Jgs 3:8–10](https://ref.ly/Judg3:8-Judg3:10)). His exact identity is uncertain.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 3:8–JDG 3:10
