## Seth (id: 8247)

SETH
====

The third son of Adam and Eve. He replaced Abel, whom Cain murdered ([Genesis 4:25](https://ref.ly/Gen4:25)). Seth appears as the firstborn son of Adam in the family lists of [Genesis 5:3](https://ref.ly/Gen5:3-Gen5:8)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen5:3-Gen5:8)[8](https://ref.ly/Gen5:3-Gen5:8), [1 Chronicles 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:1) (“Sheth” in the King James Version), and [Luke 3:38](https://ref.ly/Luke3:38). It was through Seth’s line that Jesus was born. Seth was the father of Enosh and lived 912 years.

*See also* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.
