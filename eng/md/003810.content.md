## Meribah-Kadesh, Meribath-Kadesh (id: 7068)

MERIBAH\-KADESH, MERIBATH\-KADESH\*
===================================

Alternate names for Kadesh\-barnea ([Dt 32:51](https://ref.ly/Deut32:51); [Ez 47:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:19); [48:28](https://ref.ly/Ezek48:28)), a place for lengthy encampment by the Israelites during the wilderness wanderings. *See* Kadesh, Kadesh\-barnea.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 32:51; EZK 47:19; EZK 48:28
