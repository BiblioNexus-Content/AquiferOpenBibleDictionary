## Ahisamach (id: 3456)

AHISAMACH
=========

The father of the craftsman Oholiab, from the tribe of Dan. Oholiab helped build the tabernacle and everything inside of it ([Exodus 31:6](https://ref.ly/Exod31:6); [35:34](https://ref.ly/Exod35:34); [38:23](https://ref.ly/Exod38:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 31:6; EXO 35:34; EXO 38:23
