## Atroth-Shophan (id: 3869)

ATROTH\-SHOPHAN
===============

A city in the Transjordan in the tribal land of Gad ([Numbers 32:35](https://ref.ly/Num32:35)). Some translations mistakenly list Atroth\-Shophan as two cities.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 32:35
