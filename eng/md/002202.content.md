## Gideoni (id: 5457)

GIDEONI
=======

Gideoni was the father of Abidan. Abidan was the leader of the tribe of Benjamin when the Israelites were traveling through the Sinai wilderness after they escaped from Egyp ([Numbers 1:11](https://ref.ly/Num1:11); [2:22](https://ref.ly/Num2:22); [10:24](https://ref.ly/Num10:24)). As a tribal leader, Gideoni presented the tribe's offering during the dedication ceremony of the tabernacle ([7:60–65](https://ref.ly/Num7:60-Num7:65)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:11; NUM 2:22; NUM 10:24; NUM 7:60–NUM 7:65
