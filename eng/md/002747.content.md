## Ikkesh (id: 5996)

IKKESH
======

A man from Tekoa whose son Ira was one of the mighty men of King David ([2 Samuel 23:26](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:26); [1 Chronicles 11:28](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:28)). He was head of a division of 24,000 men during the sixth month of the year ([1 Chronicles 27:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:26; 1CH 11:28; 1CH 27:9
