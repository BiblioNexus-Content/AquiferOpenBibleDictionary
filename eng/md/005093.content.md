## Shemiramoth (id: 8355)

SHEMIRAMOTH
===========

1\. One of the Levites whom David commanded to play the harp when the ark of God was brought from the house of Obed\-edom to Jerusalem ([1 Chr 15:18–22](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:18-1Chr15:22)), and who retained a permanent position under Asaph as one of the ministers of the ark ([16:4–5](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:4-1Chr16:5)).

2\. Levite commissioned by Jehoshaphat to teach the law “through all the cities of Judah” ([2 Chr 17:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 17:8; 1CH 16:4–1CH 16:5; 1CH 15:18–1CH 15:22
