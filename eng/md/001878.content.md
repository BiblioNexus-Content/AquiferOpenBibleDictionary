## Er (id: 5136)

ER
==

1. The eldest son of Judah and Bathshua, a Canaanite woman ([Genesis 38:3](https://ref.ly/Gen38:3)). The Lord killed him before he and his wife, Tamar, could have any children ([Genesis 38:7](https://ref.ly/Gen38:7); [46:12](https://ref.ly/Gen46:12); [1 Chronicles 2:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:3)).
2. The grandson of Judah and father of Lecah ([1 Chronicles 4:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:21)). He was the nephew of \#1 above.
3. Joshua’s son and an ancestor of Joseph, the husband of Mary ([Luke 3:28](https://ref.ly/Luke3:28)). *See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.
