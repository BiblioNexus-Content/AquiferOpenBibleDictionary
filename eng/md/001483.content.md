## Dabbesheth (id: 4733)

DABBESHETH
==========

Designation for a camel’s hump ([Is 30:6](https://ref.ly/Isa30:6)). The name also refers to a town (“camel’s hump hill”) situated on the western border of the land allotted to Zebulun’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 19:11](https://ref.ly/Josh19:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:11; ISA 30:6
