## Deputy (id: 4812)

DEPUTY
======

1. An official of high rank whose authority is normally given by a king ([1 Kings 22:47](https://ref.ly/1Kgs22:47); [Jeremiah 51:28](https://ref.ly/Jer51:28)).

    *See* Governor.

2. The King James Version translation for “proconsul.” A proconsul was an officer appointed over provinces by the Roman senate ([Acts 13:7–12](https://ref.ly/Acts13:7-Acts13:12); [18:12](https://ref.ly/Acts18:12); [19:38](https://ref.ly/Acts19:38)).

    *See also* Proconsul.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 22:47; JER 51:28; ACT 18:12; ACT 19:38; ACT 13:7–ACT 13:12
* **Associated Articles:** Governor (ID: `5520@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Proconsul (ID: `7827@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
