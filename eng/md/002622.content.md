## Hodaviah (id: 5870)

HODAVIAH
========

1\. Postexilic descendant of David ([1 Chr 3:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:24)).

2\. Chieftain of Manasseh’s half\-tribe east of the Jordan ([1 Chr 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:24)).

3\. Hassenuah’s son and the father of Meshullam from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:7)).

4\. Progenitor of a family of Levites who returned with the exiles from Babylon ([Ezr 2:40](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:40)); alternately called Judah in [Ezra 3:9](https://ref.ly/Ezra3:9) and Hodevah in [Nehemiah 7:43](https://ref.ly/Neh7:43).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:24; 1CH 5:24; 1CH 9:7; EZR 2:40; EZR 3:9; NEH 7:43
