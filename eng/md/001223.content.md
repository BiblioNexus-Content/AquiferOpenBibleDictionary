## Chant (id: 4477)

CHANT
=====

A chant is a type of singing or speaking in a repeated, rhythmic way that often sounds like one continuous tone ([Psalm 8:1](https://ref.ly/Ps8:1); [Ezekiel 32:16](https://ref.ly/Ezek32:16)).
