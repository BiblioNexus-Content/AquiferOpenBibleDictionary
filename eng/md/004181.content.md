## Nympha (id: 7440)

NYMPHA
======

Christian woman living in Laodicea (or perhaps Colossae), in whose house believers gathered for worship. Paul sent greetings to her and the church ([Col 4:15](https://ref.ly/Col4:15), kjv uses the masculine form “Nymphas”).

* **Associated Passages:** COL 4:15
