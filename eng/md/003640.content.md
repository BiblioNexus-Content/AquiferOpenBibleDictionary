## Malchus (id: 6899)

MALCHUS
=======

The name of a slave of the high priest in [John 18:10](https://ref.ly/John18:10). At the time of Jesus’s arrest, Peter cut Malchus with a sword and removed his ear. In [Matthew 26:51](https://ref.ly/Matt26:51), [Mark 14:47](https://ref.ly/Mark14:47), and [Luke 22:50–51](https://ref.ly/Luke22:50-Luke22:51), no name is given for this person. According to Luke, Jesus immediately healed the wound.

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 26:51; MRK 14:47; JHN 18:10; LUK 22:50–LUK 22:51
