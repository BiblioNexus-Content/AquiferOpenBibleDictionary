## Malchishua, Malchi-shua (id: 6898)

MALCHISHUA, MALCHI\-SHUA
========================

Malchishua was the third son of King Saul ([1 Samuel 14:49](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:49); [1 Chronicles 8:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:33); [9:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:39)). He was killed by the Philistines at the battle of Gilboa ([1 Samuel 31:2](https://ref.ly/1Sam31:2); [1 Chronicles 10:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr10:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 14:49; 1SA 31:2; 1CH 8:33; 1CH 9:39; 1CH 10:2
