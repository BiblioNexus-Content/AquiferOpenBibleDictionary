## Tahpenes (id: 8717)

TAHPENES
========

Egyptian queen who lived during the reigns of David (1000–961 BC) and Solomon (970–930 BC). Pharaoh gave her sister to Hadad the Edomite in marriage. Tahpenes’s sister bore to Hadad a son named Genubath ([1 Kgs 11:19–20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:19-1Kgs11:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 11:19–1KI 11:20
