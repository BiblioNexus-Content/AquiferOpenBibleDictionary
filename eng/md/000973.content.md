## Bilhah (Place) (id: 4243)

BILHAH (Place)
==============

Town in the territory allotted to Simeon’s tribe ([1 Chr 4:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:29)), probably identical with Baalah ([Jos 15:29](https://ref.ly/Josh15:29)) and Balah ([19:3](https://ref.ly/Josh19:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:29; JOS 19:3; 1CH 4:29
