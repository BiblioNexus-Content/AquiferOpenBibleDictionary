## Hazerim (id: 5737)

HAZERIM\*
=========

KJV transliteration of the corresponding Hebrew word in [Deuteronomy 2:23](https://ref.ly/Deut2:23). Instead of the proper name for a city, it may be a generic term for “villages,” a rendering favored by the nlt.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 2:23
