## Jeriah (id: 6319)

JERIAH
======

Levite of the family of Kohath and head of Hebron’s house ([1 Chr 23:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:19); [24:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:23)). David organized Jeriah and other Levites to manage the religious and civil affairs of the kingdom ([26:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 23:19; 1CH 24:23; 1CH 26:31
