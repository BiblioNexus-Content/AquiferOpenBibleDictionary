## Elkosh (id: 5035)

ELKOSH
======

Home or birthplace of the prophet Nahum ([Na 1:1](https://ref.ly/Nah1:1)). Three sites have been suggested: (1\) Hilkeesei, a village in Galilee, perhaps corresponding to modern el\-Kauzeh; (2\) Capernaum near the Sea of Galilee, where Jesus frequently taught; and (3\) Bein Jebrin in southern Judea.

* **Associated Passages:** NAM 1:1
