## Belah, Belaite (id: 4083)

BELAH, BELAITE
==============

The King James Version spelling of Bela. He was the oldest son of the patriarch Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:1)). His descendants were the Belaites ([Numbers 26:38–40](https://ref.ly/Num26:38-Num26:40)).
