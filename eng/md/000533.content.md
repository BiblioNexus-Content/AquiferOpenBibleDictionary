## Asherah, Asherim, Asheroth (id: 3797)

ASHERAH, ASHERIM, ASHEROTH
==========================

The singular and plural form of the name of a Canaanite goddess who was connected to the Canaanite god Baal ([Judges 3:7](https://ref.ly/Judg3:7); [1 Kings 18:19](https://ref.ly/1Kgs18:19)). The words also refer to the wooden poles erected to worship this goddess.

*See* Canaanite Deities and Religion; Grove.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 3:7; 1KI 18:19
