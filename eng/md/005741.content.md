## Uzzia (id: 8991)

UZZIA
=====

One of David’s mighty men ([1 Chronicles 11:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:44)). He was described as an Ashterathite, which probably means that he was from Ashtaroth, a town on the east side of the Jordan.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 11:44
