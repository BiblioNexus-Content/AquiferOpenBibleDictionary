## Rephaiah (id: 8001)

REPHAIAH
========

1\. Jeshaiah’s son and a descendant of Solomon ([1 Chr 3:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:21)).

2\. Ishi’s son and a captain from Simeon’s tribe who led 500 Israelites to destroy the Amalekites at Mt Seir ([1 Chr 4:42–43](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:42-1Chr4:43)).

3\. Tola’s son and a warrior from Issachar’s tribe in the days of David ([1 Chr 7:1–2](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:1-1Chr7:2)).

4\. Son of Binea and father of Eleasah, a descendant of Saul ([1 Chr 9:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:43)); also called Raphah in [1 Chronicles 8:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:37) (kjv “Rapha”).

5\. Hur’s son, who worked on the Jerusalem wall during the days of Nehemiah ([Neh 3:9](https://ref.ly/Neh3:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:21; 1CH 8:37; 1CH 9:43; NEH 3:9; 1CH 4:42–1CH 4:43; 1CH 7:1–1CH 7:2
