## Zerahiah (id: 9188)

ZERAHIAH
========

1\. Uzzi’s son and ancestor of Ezra from the priestly line of Eleazar ([1 Chr 6:6, 51](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:6,1Chr6:51); [Ezr 7:4](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:4)).

2\. Father of Eliehoenai, who was head of a family who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra ([Ezr 8:4](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:6; 1CH 6:51; EZR 7:4; EZR 8:4
