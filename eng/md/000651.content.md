## Azrikam (id: 3921)

AZRIKAM
=======

1. One of three sons of Neariah. A descendant of David through Zerubbabel ([1 Chronicles 3:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:23)).
2. One of six sons of Azel. A descendant of Saul ([1 Chronicles 8:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:38); [9:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:44)).
3. An ancestor of Shemaiah, a Levite who returned to Jerusalem after the exile to Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:14); [Nehemiah 11:15](https://ref.ly/Neh11:15)).
4. A palace officer under King Ahaz of Judah. He was killed by Zichri ([2 Chronicles 28:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:7)). He might be the same as \#2 above.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:23; 1CH 8:38; 1CH 9:14; 1CH 9:44; 2CH 28:7; NEH 11:15
