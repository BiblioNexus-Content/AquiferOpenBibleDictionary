## Gaash (id: 5327)

GAASH
=====

1. A mountain about 32\.2 kilometers (20 miles) southwest of Shechem. The Israelites buried Joshua at Timnath\-serah (Timnath\-heres). This was in the hill country of Ephraim, near Mount Gaash ([Joshua 24:30](https://ref.ly/Josh24:30); [Judges 2:9](https://ref.ly/Judg2:9)).
2. A stream near the mountain of Gaash. It was the home of Hiddai (or Hurai). Hiddai was one of the mighty men of King David ([2 Samuel 23:30](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:30); [1 Chronicles 11:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:32)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 24:30; JDG 2:9; 2SA 23:30; 1CH 11:32
