## Ephraim, Mount (id: 5115)

EPHRAIM\*, Mount
================

KJV expression referring to the hill country in central Palestine where Ephraim’s tribe was located ([Jos 20:7](https://ref.ly/Josh20:7); [Jgs 19:1, 16, 18](https://ref.ly/Judg19:1,Judg19:16,Judg19:18); [1 Sm 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:1)). The area was one of the most productive in Palestine.
