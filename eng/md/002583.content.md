## Hezir (id: 5836)

HEZIR
=====

1\. Levite and head of the 17th of 24 divisions of priests for sanctuary service formed during David’s reign ([1 Chr 24:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:15)).

2\. Israelite leader who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant during the postexilic era ([Neh 10:20](https://ref.ly/Neh10:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:15; NEH 10:20
