## Hasshub (id: 5707)

HASSHUB
=======

1\. Merari clan leader of Levi’s tribe. Hasshub was the father of Shemaiah, a settler in Jerusalem after the return from captivity ([1 Chr 9:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:14); [Neh 11:15](https://ref.ly/Neh11:15)).

2\. Pahath\-moab’s son, who repaired a section of the Jerusalem wall and the Tower of the Ovens during the time of Nehemiah ([Neh 3:11](https://ref.ly/Neh3:11)).

3\. Another Hasshub who repaired the Jerusalem wall opposite his house ([Neh 3:23](https://ref.ly/Neh3:23)).

4\. Leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:23](https://ref.ly/Neh10:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:14; NEH 3:11; NEH 3:23; NEH 10:23; NEH 11:15
