## Maharai (id: 6870)

MAHARAI
=======

One of the mighty men of King David. He was a Zerahite from Netophah in the hill country of Judah. David chose Maharai to be a commander in charge of 24,000 soldiers during the tenth month of each year ([2 Samuel 23:28](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:28); [1 Chronicles 11:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:30); [27:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:28; 1CH 11:30; 1CH 27:13
