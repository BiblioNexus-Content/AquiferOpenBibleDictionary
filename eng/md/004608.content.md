## Puvah (id: 7872)

PUVAH\*
=======

Issachar’s son, who went with Jacob and his household to Egypt, where they sought refuge from the severe famine in Palestine ([Gn 46:13](https://ref.ly/Gen46:13); nlt “Puah”). Puvah founded the Punite family ([Nm 26:23](https://ref.ly/Num26:23)) and is alternately called Puah in [1 Chronicles 7:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:1).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:13; NUM 26:23; 1CH 7:1
