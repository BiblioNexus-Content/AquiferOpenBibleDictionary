## Allon-Bachuth, Allon-Bacuth (id: 3514)

ALLON\-BACHUTH, ALLON\-BACUTH
=============================

An oak tree near Bethel under which Deborah was buried ([Genesis 35:8](https://ref.ly/Gen35:8)). Deborah is Rebekah’s elderly nurse. The tree was named the “Oak of Weeping.”

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 35:8
