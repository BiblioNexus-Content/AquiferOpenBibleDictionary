## Silla (id: 8491)

SILLA
=====

Geographical landmark defining the whereabouts of “the house of Millo,” the place of King Joash’s murder ([2 Kgs 12:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:20)). Its exact location is unknown, though it was probably in the vicinity of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:20
