## Ishmaiah (id: 6073)

ISHMAIAH
========

1\. Warrior from Benjamin’s tribe who joined David at Ziklag in his struggle against King Saul. Ishmaiah was one of David’s ambidextrous archers and slingers ([1 Chr 12:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:4)).

2\. Obadiah’s son, a chief officer in Zebulun’s tribe in David’s time ([1 Chr 27:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:4; 1CH 27:19
