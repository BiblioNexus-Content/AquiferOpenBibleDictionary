## Enan (id: 5075)

ENAN
====

Ahira’s father. Ahira was appointed by Moses as the commander of the tribe of Naphtali during the first census of Israel in the desert of Sinai ([Nm 1:15](https://ref.ly/Num1:15); [2:29](https://ref.ly/Num2:29); [7:78, 83](https://ref.ly/Num7:78,Num7:83); [10:27](https://ref.ly/Num10:27)). The name is apparently preserved in the name Hazar\-enan (\-enon), a town somewhere between Damascus and Hauran ([Nm 34:9](https://ref.ly/Num34:9); [Ez 47:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:17); [48:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek48:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:15; NUM 2:29; NUM 7:78; NUM 7:83; NUM 10:27; NUM 34:9; EZK 47:17; EZK 48:1
