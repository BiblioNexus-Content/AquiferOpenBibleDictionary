## Nergal (id: 7365)

NERGAL
======

Heathen deity worshiped by the men of Cuth after the fall of Israel in 722 BC ([2 Kgs 17:30](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:30)). Nergal, lord of the netherworld and associated with the sun god, was the city god of the northern Babylonian city of Cuthah (cf. v [24](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:24)). *See* Assyria, Assyrians.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 17:24; 2KI 17:30
