## Neriah (id: 7366)

NERIAH
======

Father of Baruch the scribe ([Jer 32:12, 16](https://ref.ly/Jer32:12,Jer32:16); [36:4, 8](https://ref.ly/Jer36:4,Jer36:8)) and Seraiah the quartermaster ([51:59](https://ref.ly/Jer51:59)), both of whom served Jeremiah the prophet.

* **Associated Passages:** JER 32:12; JER 32:16; JER 36:4; JER 36:8; JER 51:59
