## Ahuzzath (id: 3471)

AHUZZATH
========

A royal advisor to Abimelech of Gerar. Ahuzzath went with Abimelech to Beersheba to make an agreement with Isaac ([Genesis 26:26](https://ref.ly/Gen26:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 26:26
