## Dodo (id: 4877)

DODO
====

1. The grandfather of Tola. Tola was a minor judge who judged Israel from his native city, Shamir ([Judges 10:1](https://ref.ly/Judg10:1)).
2. The father of Eleazar. Eleazar was "one of the three mighty men" of David ([2 Samuel 23:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:9); [1 Chronicles 11:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:12)). Dodo may be identifiable with Dodai the Ahohite in [1 Chronicles 27:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:4). *See* Dodai.
3. The father of Elhanan. Elhanan was one of the mighty men of David known as “the Thirty” ([2 Samuel 23:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:24); [1 Chronicles 11:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:26)). Dodo lived at Bethlehem.
