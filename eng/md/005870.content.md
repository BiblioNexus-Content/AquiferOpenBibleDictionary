## Zabdi (id: 9116)

ZABDI
=====

1\. Zerah’s descendant from Judah’s tribe ([Jos 7:1](https://ref.ly/Josh7:1)). Achan was a Zerahite of the family of Zabdi ([Jos 7:17–18](https://ref.ly/Josh7:17-Josh7:18)); alternately called Zimri (nlt mg, [1 Chr 2:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:6)).

2\. Shimei’s son and a descendant of Ehud from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 8:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:19)).

3\. David’s officer over the produce of the vineyards for the wine cellars ([1 Chr 27:27](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:27)). He is called a Shiphmite, which likely means that he was a native of Shepham.

4\. One of the temple musicians of the order of Asaph ([Neh 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17)); alternately called Zicri ([1 Chr 9:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 7:1; 1CH 2:6; 1CH 8:19; 1CH 9:15; 1CH 27:27; NEH 11:17; JOS 7:17–JOS 7:18
