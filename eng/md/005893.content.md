## Zaphon (id: 9138)

ZAPHON
======

Town located east of the Jordan River ([Jos 13:27](https://ref.ly/Josh13:27)) and included as part of the inheritance of Gad’s tribe (v [24](https://ref.ly/Josh13:24)). Egyptian records (13th century BC) refer to a town known as *Dapuna,* while an Amarna text spells the name *Sapuna.*

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 13:24; JOS 13:27
