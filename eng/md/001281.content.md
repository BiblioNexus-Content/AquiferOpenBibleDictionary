## Chislon (id: 4533)

CHISLON
=======

The father of Elidad, a leader of the tribe of Benjamin during the wilderness wanderings of Israel. Elidad was one of those chosen by Moses to divide the land of Canaan among the tribes ([Numbers 34:21](https://ref.ly/Num34:21)).
