## Sethur (id: 8246)

SETHUR
======

An Asherite and a son of Michael. He was one of the 12 spies sent by Moses to explore the land of Canaan ([Numbers 13:13](https://ref.ly/Num13:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:13
