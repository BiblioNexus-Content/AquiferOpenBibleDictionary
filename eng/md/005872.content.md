## Zabud (id: 9117)

ZABUD
=====

A priest in the court of King Solomon and the “adviser to the king” ([1 Kings 4:5](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:5)). The phrase “adviser to the king” may be an official title. Hushai the Archite had a similar title (“friend”) in the court of King David ([2 Samuel 15:37](https://ref.ly/2Sam15:37); [16:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam16:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 15:37; 2SA 16:16; 1KI 4:5
