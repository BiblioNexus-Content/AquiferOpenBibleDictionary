## Gederoth (id: 5400)

GEDEROTH
========

Town (modern Qatra) in the Shephelah (lowland hills) assigned to Judah’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 15:41](https://ref.ly/Josh15:41)) and later captured by the Philistines from King Ahaz ([2 Chr 28:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:41; 2CH 28:18
