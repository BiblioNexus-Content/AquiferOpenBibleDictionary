## Gederah, Gederathites (id: 5398)

GEDERAH, GEDERATHITES\*
=======================

Town and its inhabitants situated in the Shephelah (lowland hills) of the territory allotted to Judah’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 15:36](https://ref.ly/Josh15:36)). It was a place where potters lived ([1 Chr 4:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:23)). A man from Gederah, Jozabad the Gederathite, is mentioned in [1 Chronicles 12:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:4).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:36; 1CH 4:23; 1CH 12:4
