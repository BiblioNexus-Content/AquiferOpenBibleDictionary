## Jadon (id: 6169)

JADON
=====

Workman on the Jerusalem wall after the return from exile. Jadon worked on the section near the Old Gate of the city with men from Gibeon and Mizpah. He was a Meronothite ([Neh 3:7](https://ref.ly/Neh3:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:7
