## Azarias (id: 3903)

AZARIAS
=======

The name, or flase name, that the angel Raphael used when he joined Tobit’s son Tobias on Tobias’s journey to Media ([Tobit 5:4, 13](https://ref.ly/Tob5:4,Tob5:13); [12:15](https://ref.ly/Tob12:15)). Tobit is a story found in some versions of the Bible, but not all Christians consider it part of their Scriptures.

* **Associated Passages:** TOB 5:4; TOB 5:13; TOB 12:15
