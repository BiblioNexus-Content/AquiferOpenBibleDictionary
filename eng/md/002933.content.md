## Jahzerah (id: 6185)

JAHZERAH
========

Ancestor of a priest who returned to Judah after the Babylonian exile ([1 Chr 9:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:12)). He is called Ahzai in [Nehemiah 11:13](https://ref.ly/Neh11:13). Little else is known about him except that he was a great\-grandson of a priest named Immer who lived in Jerusalem before the exile.

*See also* Ahzai.
