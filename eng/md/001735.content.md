## Eleutherus (id: 4988)

ELEUTHERUS
==========

A small river mentioned in [1 Maccabees 11:7](https://ref.ly/1Macc11:7) and [12:30](https://ref.ly/1Macc12:30) and almost certainly to be identified with the Nahr\-el\-Kebir, which begins in the northeast base of Lebanon and flows into the Mediterranean about 18 miles (29 kilometers) north of Tripoli. It marked the limits of Jonathan’s expeditions.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 11:7; 1MA 12:30
