## Lily-Work (id: 6753)

LILY\-WORK\*
============

Design of a lily or lotus used on ancient pillars, inspired by the large water lily found along the Nile. It appeared at the vestibule of Solomon’s temple ([1 Kgs 7:19–22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs7:19-1Kgs7:22)), around the brim of the basin (v [26](https://ref.ly/1Kgs7:26)), and in numerous artistic creations of the Assyrians, Persians, and other Near Eastern peoples.

*See also* Bronze Sea; Laver.
