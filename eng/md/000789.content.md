## Bedan (id: 4059)

BEDAN
=====

1. One of Israel’s saviors, along with Gideon, Jephthah, and Samuel, during the time of the judges ([1 Samuel 12:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam12:11)). The name Bedan may be either a shortened form of Abdon ([Judges 12:13](https://ref.ly/Judg12:13)) or a scribal error for Barak ([Judges 4:6](https://ref.ly/Judg4:6)).
2. Ulam’s son, a descendant of Manasseh ([1 Chronicles 7:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 4:6; JDG 12:13; 1SA 12:11; 1CH 7:17
