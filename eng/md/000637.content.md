## Azbuk (id: 3907)

AZBUK
=====

The father of Nehemiah. He was ruler of half the Beth\-zur district ([Nehemiah 3:16](https://ref.ly/Neh3:16)). Azbuk’s son helped the more famous Nehemiah, the governor ([Nehemiah 10:1](https://ref.ly/Neh10:1)), in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:16; NEH 10:1
