## Bath (id: 4033)

BATH
====

A unit of liquid measure in the Old Testament ([Ezekiel 45:10](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:10-Ezek45:11)[–](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:10-Ezek45:11)[11](https://ref.ly/Ezek45:10-Ezek45:11)). It is equal to about six gallons or 23 liters. 

*See* Weights and Measures.
