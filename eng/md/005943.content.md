## Zer (id: 9204)

ZER
===

One of the fortified cities of Naphtali’s tribe ([Jos 19:35](https://ref.ly/Josh19:35)). From the surrounding names in the list, it may be inferred that it was located on the southwest side of the Sea of Galilee.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:35
