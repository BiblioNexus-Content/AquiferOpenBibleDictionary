## Amasai (id: 3542)

AMASAI
======

1. Elkanah’s son and Mahath’s father ([1 Chronicles 6:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:25); [6:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:35)). He is listed in the list of ancestors of Heman the singer.
2. A leader of 30 warriors who joined David at Ziklag after deserting King Saul ([1 Chronicles 12:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:18)).
3. A trumpeter priest in the procession when David brought the ark of God to Jerusalem ([1 Chronicles 15:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:24)).
4. The father of another Mahath. This Mahath was Hezekiah’s contemporary and a participant in his revival ([2 Chronicles 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:25; 1CH 6:35; 1CH 12:18; 1CH 15:24; 2CH 29:12
