## Nophah (id: 7425)

NOPHAH
======

A place marking the boundaries between Israel and the Moabites and Amorites ([Numbers 21:30](https://ref.ly/Num21:30)). Some scholars think Nophah is the same as the Nobah of [Judges 8:11](https://ref.ly/Judg8:11).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 21:30; JDG 8:11
