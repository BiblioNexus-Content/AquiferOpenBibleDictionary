## Hundred, Tower of the (id: 5943)

HUNDRED, Tower of the
=====================

Tower at the northernmost part of the Jerusalem wall (near where the wall crosses over the Tyropeon Valley). It stood west of the Sheep Gate near the Tower of Hananel ([Neh 3:1](https://ref.ly/Neh3:1); [12:39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:39); kjv “tower of Meah”). *See also* Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:1; NEH 12:39
