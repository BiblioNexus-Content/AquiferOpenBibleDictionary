## Ammiel (id: 3559)

AMMIEL
======

1. Gemalli’s son. He was one of 12 men sent by Moses as a spy to the land of Canaan. Ammiel represented Dan’s tribe ([Numbers 13:12](https://ref.ly/Num13:12)). He later died because of a plague ([Numbers 14:37](https://ref.ly/Num14:37)).
2. The father of Machir of Lo\-debar. Mephibosheth, Jonathan’s son, was hidden from David in Machir’s house ([2 Samuel 9:4–5](https://ref.ly/2Sam9:4-2Sam9:5)). Machir later helped supply David in his war with Absalom ([2 Samuel 17:27–29](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:27-2Sam17:29)).
3. The father of David’s wife, Bath\-shua or Bathsheba. ([1 Chronicles 3:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:5)). Ammiel is also called Eliam ([2 Samuel 11:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam11:3)).
4. The sixth son of Obed\-edom. He along with his family served as gatekeeper in the temple during David’s reign ([1 Chronicles 26:5, 15](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:5,1Chr26:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:12; NUM 14:37; 2SA 11:3; 1CH 3:5; 1CH 26:5; 1CH 26:15; 2SA 9:4–2SA 9:5; 2SA 17:27–2SA 17:29
