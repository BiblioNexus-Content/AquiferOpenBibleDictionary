## Tryphosa (id: 8937)

TRYPHOSA
========

A Christian woman of Rome. Along with Tryphena, Paul called her one of the “women who have worked hard in the Lord” ([Romans 16:12](https://ref.ly/Rom16:12)). These two women may have been sisters. Or, they may have both served as leaders in the church (called deaconesses).

*See also* Deacon, Deaconess.

* **Associated Passages:** ROM 16:12
