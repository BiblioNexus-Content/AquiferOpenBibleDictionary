## Galal (id: 5348)

GALAL
=====

1\. Levite and Mica’s son, who returned from exile in Babylon ([1 Chr 9:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:15)).

2\. Levite and forefather of Obadiah (Abda). Obadiah returned from exile in Babylon ([1 Chr 9:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:16); [Neh 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:15; 1CH 9:16; NEH 11:17
