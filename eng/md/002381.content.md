## Hamutal (id: 5635)

HAMUTAL
=======

Daughter of Jeremiah of Libnah, one of King Josiah’s wives, and the mother of two kings: Jehoahaz and Zedekiah ([2 Kgs 23:31](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:31); [24:18](https://ref.ly/2Kgs24:18); [Jer 52:1](https://ref.ly/Jer52:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 23:31; 2KI 24:18; JER 52:1
