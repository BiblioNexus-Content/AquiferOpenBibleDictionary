## Zophar (id: 9259)

ZOPHAR
======

One of the “counselors” of Job who is listed as a Naamathite ([Jb 2:11](https://ref.ly/Job2:11); [11:1](https://ref.ly/Job11:1); [20:1](https://ref.ly/Job20:1)). He offers the most direct accusations against Job but later offers sacrifice for Job as commanded by the Lord ([42:9](https://ref.ly/Job42:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 2:11; JOB 11:1; JOB 20:1; JOB 42:9
