## Asriel, Asrielite (id: 3831)

ASRIEL, ASRIELITE
=================

Asriel was the son of Manasseh ([1 Chronicles 7:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:14)). His descendants, the Asrielites, were included in Moses’s census in the wilderness ([Numbers 26:31](https://ref.ly/Num26:31)). They were later given a portion of the land from the tribe of Manasseh ([Joshua 17:2](https://ref.ly/Josh17:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:31; JOS 17:2; 1CH 7:14
