## Corners of the Earth (id: 4656)

CORNERS OF THE EARTH
====================

Figurative term denoting the borders and extremities of the earth ([Jb 37:3](https://ref.ly/Job37:3); [Is 11:12](https://ref.ly/Isa11:12); [Jer 25:32](https://ref.ly/Jer25:32); [31:8](https://ref.ly/Jer31:8); [Ez 7:2](https://ref.ly/Ezek7:2); [Rv 7:1](https://ref.ly/Rev7:1); [20:8](https://ref.ly/Rev20:8)).
