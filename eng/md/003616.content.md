## Maher-Shalal-Hash-Baz (id: 6874)

MAHER\-SHALAL\-HASH\-BAZ
========================

Name of Isaiah’s son, meaning “swift to plunder and quick to spoil” (nlt mg), which prophetically described the imminent destruction to befall Damascus and Samaria by the hand of the Assyrians ([Is 8:1, 3](https://ref.ly/Isa8:1,Isa8:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 8:1; ISA 8:3
