## Mahlah (id: 6875)

MAHLAH
======

1. A Manassite. She was one of the five daughters of Zelophehad. Mahlah and her sisters appealed to Moses and asked him to work out an arrangement that would allow them to keep their inheritance. This was because they had no brothers ([Numbers 26:33](https://ref.ly/Num26:33); [27:1](https://ref.ly/Num27:1); [36:11](https://ref.ly/Num36:11); [Joshua 17:3](https://ref.ly/Josh17:3)).
2. A son of Hammoleketh from the tribe of Manasseh ([1 Chronicles 7:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:33; NUM 27:1; NUM 36:11; JOS 17:3; 1CH 7:18
