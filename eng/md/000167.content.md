## Agur (id: 3427)

AGUR
====

Jakeh’s son. Although he was not an Israelite, he wrote or collected the sayings in [Proverbs 30](https://ref.ly/Prov30:1-Prov30:33). Agur was from Massa ([Proverbs 30:1](https://ref.ly/Prov30:1)), an area of northern Arabia that was settled by a son of Ishmael ([Genesis 25:14](https://ref.ly/Gen25:14); [1 Chronicles 1:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:30)). 

*See* Proverbs, Book of.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:14; 1CH 1:30; PRO 30:1; PRO 30:1–PRO 30:33
