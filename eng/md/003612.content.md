## Mahath (id: 6871)

MAHATH
======

1\. Levite, son of Amasai, and ancestor of Heman the temple singer in David’s time ([1 Chr 6:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:35)).

2\. Levite who assisted in the cleansing of the temple during Hezekiah’s time ([2 Chr 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)). He was appointed an overseer of the contributions, the tithes, and the things dedicated to God ([31:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:35; 2CH 29:12; 2CH 31:13
