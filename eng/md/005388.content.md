## Sukkiim, Sukkiims, Sukkites (id: 8648)

SUKKIIM\*, SUKKIIMS\*, SUKKITES
===============================

A tribe who joined forces with King Shishak of Egypt and entered Palestine to wage war against King Rehoboam of Judah (930–913 BC). The Sukkites are mentioned with the Libyans and the Ethiopians ([2 Chr 12:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr12:3)). They were probably a Libyan people.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 12:3
