## Anem (id: 3607)

ANEM
====

A town in Issachar’s territory given to the priestly family of Gershom ([1 Chronicles 6:73](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:73)). It was also called En\-gannim ([Joshua 21:29](https://ref.ly/Josh21:29)). It was probably located southeast of Mount Tabor.

*See also* Levitical Cities; En\-gannim \#2.
