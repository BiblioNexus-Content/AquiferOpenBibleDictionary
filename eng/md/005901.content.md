## Zartanah, Zarthan (id: 9146)

ZARTANAH, ZARTHAN
=================

Zartanah and Zarthan are different spellings found in the King James Version of the Bible. Zartanah appears in [1 Kings 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:12), and Zarthan appears in [7:46](https://ref.ly/1Kgs7:46). Both names refer to the place called Zarethan.

*See* Zarethan.
