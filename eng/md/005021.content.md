## Shammua (id: 8284)

SHAMMUA
=======

1. A man from the tribe of Reuben. He was the son of Zaccur and one of the 12 spies that Moses sent to explore the land of Canaan ([Numbers 13:4](https://ref.ly/Num13:4)).
2. Another name for Shimea, who was one of the sons of King David ([2 Samuel 5:14](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:14) and [1 Chronicles 14:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:4)).

    *See* Shimea \#2.

3. Another name for Shemaiah, who was the son of Galal ([Nehemiah 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17)).

    *See* Shemaiah \#6.

4. A leader of a family who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel. This was after the people of Israel were taken to Babylon and later allowed to return home ([Nehemiah 12:18](https://ref.ly/Neh12:18)).
