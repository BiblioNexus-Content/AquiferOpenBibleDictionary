## Jehizkiah (id: 6270)

JEHIZKIAH
=========

Shallum’s son and a chief of Ephraim during the reign of Ahaz in Judah. He opposed the enslavement of the men of Judah by victorious Israel ([2 Chr 28:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 28:12
