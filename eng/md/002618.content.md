## Hobaiah (id: 5868)

HOBAIAH
=======

Head of a priestly family who returned to Palestine with Zerubbabel after the exile. He was unable to prove his priestly genealogy and so was not allowed to do priestly service ([Ezr 2:61](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:61); [Neh 7:63](https://ref.ly/Neh7:63)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:61; NEH 7:63
