## Lasha (id: 6686)

LASHA
=====

A place\-name, otherwise unknown. An ancient description uses this name to refer to the southern boundary of the territory occupied by the Canaanites ([Genesis 10:19](https://ref.ly/Gen10:19)). This passage associates Lasha with other cities near the southern end of the Dead Sea.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:19
