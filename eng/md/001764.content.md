## Elioenai (id: 5015)

ELIOENAI
========

1\. Postexilic descendant of Solomon and the father of Hodaviah and Eliashib ([1 Chr 3:23–24](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:23-1Chr3:24)).

2\. Simeonite chieftain ([1 Chr 4:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:36)).

3\. Head of a Benjamite family ([1 Chr 7:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:8)).

4\. KJV spelling of the Levite Eliehoenai in [1 Chronicles 26:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:3). *See* Eliehoenai \#1.

5\. Man of the priestly family of Pashhur who divorced his foreign wife in Ezra’s day ([Ezr 10:22](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:22)).

6\. Zattu’s son, who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife during the postexilic era ([Ezr 10:27](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:27)).

7\. Postexilic priest who assisted in the dedication of the rebuilt Jerusalem wall ([Neh 12:41](https://ref.ly/Neh12:41)).
