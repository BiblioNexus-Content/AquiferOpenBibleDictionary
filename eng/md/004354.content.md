## Patroclus (id: 7613)

PATROCLUS
=========

Father of Nicanor ([2 Macc 8:9](https://ref.ly/2Macc8:9)), the Syrian general whose 20,000 soldiers were routed by a numerically inferior band led by Judas Maccabeus. Patroclus is mentioned only in connection with his embarrassed and dishonored son.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 8:9
