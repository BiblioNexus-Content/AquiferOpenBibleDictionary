## Alemeth (Person) (id: 3497)

ALEMETH (Person)
================

1. Becher’s son from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 7:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:8)).
2. Son of either Jehoaddah ([1 Chronicles 8:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:36)) or Jarah ([1 Chronicles 9:42](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:42)) and a descendant of King Saul.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:8; 1CH 8:36; 1CH 9:42
