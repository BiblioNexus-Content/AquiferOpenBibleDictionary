## Manual of Discipline (id: 6930)

MANUAL OF DISCIPLINE
====================

A book of community rules of conduct belonging to the Essenes who lived at Qumran. The Essenes were a special group of Jews who lived apart from others and followed strict rules. *See* Dead Sea Scrolls; Essenes.
