## Eden (id: 4939)

EDEN
====

1\. Place where Adam and Eve lived until they sinned against God and were banished ([Gn 2:8, 15](https://ref.ly/Gen2:8,Gen2:15); [3:23–24](https://ref.ly/Gen3:23-Gen3:24)). *See* Garden of Eden.

2\. Alternate form of Beth\-eden in [Ezekiel 27:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:23). *See* Beth\-eden.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 2:8; GEN 2:15; EZK 27:23; GEN 3:23–GEN 3:24
