## Eder (Person) (id: 4940)

EDER (Person)
=============

1\. Member of Benjamin’s tribe and the son of Beriah, a leader in the town of Aijalon ([1 Chr 8:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:15)).

2\. Levite of Merari’s clan and the son of Mushi ([1 Chr 23:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:23); [24:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:30)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 8:15; 1CH 23:23; 1CH 24:30
