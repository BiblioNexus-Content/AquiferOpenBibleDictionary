## Balbaim (id: 3981)

BALBAIM
=======

A town to the south of Dothan. It is mentioned in the book of Judith ([Judith 7:3](https://ref.ly/Jdt7:3)). It might be the same as Belmain ([Judith 4:4](https://ref.ly/Jdt4:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDT 4:4; JDT 7:3
