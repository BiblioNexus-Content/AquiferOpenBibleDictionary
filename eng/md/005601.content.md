## Timon (id: 8852)

TIMON
=====

One of the seven men chosen by the early church in Jerusalem to serve the widows and manage the daily distribution of food ([Acts 6:5](https://ref.ly/Acts6:5)). These seven men were selected because they were full of the Holy Spirit and wisdom. The other six men were Stephen, Philip, Prochorus, Nicanor, Parmenas, and Nicolas.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 6:5
