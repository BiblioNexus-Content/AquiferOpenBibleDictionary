## Shinab (id: 8422)

SHINAB
======

King of Admah, who joined an alliance with four neighboring rulers against King Kedorlaomer. Kedorlaomer defeated this confederation of kings in the valley of Siddim—the southern region of the Dead Sea ([Gn 14:2](https://ref.ly/Gen14:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:2
