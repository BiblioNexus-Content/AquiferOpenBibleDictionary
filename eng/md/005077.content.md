## Shelomith (id: 8339)

SHELOMITH
=========

1. A daughter and the mother of a man from the tribe of Dan who blasphemed (spoke against) the name of the Lord. Then the Israelites stoned him to death ([Leviticus 24:11–16](https://ref.ly/Lev24:11-Lev24:16)).
2. A sister of Meshullam and Hananiah. All of them were descendants of King David ([1 Chronicles 3:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:19)).
3. The King James Version spelling of Shelomoth, son of Shimei in [1 Chronicles 23:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:9).

    *See* Shelomoth \#1.

4. Another spelling of Shelomoth, son Izhar in [1 Chronicles 23:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:18).

    *See* Shelomoth \#2.

5. The King James Version spelling of Shelomoth, son of Zicri in [1 Chronicles 26:25–28](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:25-1Chr26:28).

    *See* Shelomoth \#3.

6. A son of Rehoboam and Maachah ([2 Chronicles 11:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:20)).
7. One of the companions of Ezra ([Ezra 8:10](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:10)).
