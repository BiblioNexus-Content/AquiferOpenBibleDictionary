## Sheth (id: 8381)

SHETH
=====

1. A reference to the sons of Moab, who caused trouble and war for the Israelites ([Numbers 24:17](https://ref.ly/Num24:17)).
2. The King James version spelling of Seth, the third son Adam ([1 Chronicles 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:1)).

    *See* Seth.
