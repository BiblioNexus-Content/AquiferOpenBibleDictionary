## Sakkuth (id: 8098)

SAKKUTH
=======

Sakkuth is the name of a Babylonian god similar to Saturn, worshiped in Mesopotamia ([Amos 5:26](https://ref.ly/Amos5:26)). Some think the name may come from the Hebrew word *sukkah*, meaning “shrine” or “tabernacle,” where an idol could be placed.

*See* Kaiwan.
