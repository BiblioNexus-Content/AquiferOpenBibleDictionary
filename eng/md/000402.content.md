## Apollonia (id: 3664)

APOLLONIA
=========

A town located on the Egnatian Way in eastern Macedonia. Paul passed through Apollonia on his second missionary journey ([Acts 17:1\)](https://ref.ly/Acts17:1). He was traveling west from Philippi to Thessalonica, a trip of about 90 miles (145 kilometers). Apollonia is usually identified with modern Pollina.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 17:1
