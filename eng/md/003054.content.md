## Jerah (id: 6310)

JERAH
=====

Son of Joktan and nephew of Peleg, during whose lifetime the earth was divided, probably a reference to the dispersion following Babel. Jerah is likely also the name of an Arabian tribe or district ([Gn 10:25–26](https://ref.ly/Gen10:25-Gen10:26); [1 Chr 1:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:20; GEN 10:25–GEN 10:26
