## Neiel (id: 7349)

NEIEL
=====

Border town in Asher’s territory ([Jos 19:27](https://ref.ly/Josh19:27)). Its site is perhaps identifiable with modern Khirbet Ya’nin, on the east end of the plain of Acco.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:27
