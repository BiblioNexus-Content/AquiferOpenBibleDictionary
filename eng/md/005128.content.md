## Shikkeron (id: 8392)

SHIKKERON
=========

Town near the Mediterranean Sea on the northern border of the land allotted to Judah’s tribe for an inheritance, mentioned between Ekron and Mt Baalah ([Jos 15:11](https://ref.ly/Josh15:11)). Its locality is uncertain, though perhaps identifiable with Tell el\-Ful.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:11
