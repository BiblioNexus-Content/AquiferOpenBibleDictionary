## Artificer (id: 3769)

ARTIFICER
=========

An artificer is a skilled craftsman or someone who makes things with their hands. The King James Version uses "artificer" in [Genesis 4:22](https://ref.ly/Gen4:22); [1 Chronicles 29:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr29:5); [2 Chronicles 34:11](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:11); and [Isaiah 3:3](https://ref.ly/Isa3:3).
