## Shobi (id: 8443)

SHOBI
=====

An Ammonite prince, a son of King Nahash. Shobi, together with Makir of Lo\-debar and Barzillai of Rogelim, generously provided King David with food and supplies at Mahanaim ([2 Samuel 17:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 17:27
