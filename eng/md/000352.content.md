## Anim (id: 3615)

ANIM
====

One of 44 cities of the hill country that Joshua gave to Judah’s tribe ([Joshua 15:50](https://ref.ly/Josh15:50)). Anim is probably modern Khirbet Ghowein et\-Tahta.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:50
