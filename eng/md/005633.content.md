## Tohu (id: 8884)

TOHU
====

One of Samuel's ancestors ([1 Samuel 1:1](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:1)). He belonged to a family group called the Kohathites, who were members of the Levite tribe. The Levites were people who served in God's temple.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 1:1
