## Apelles (id: 3650)

APELLES
=======

A Roman Christian who received special greetings from the apostle Paul. Paul complemented Apelles as one who is “approved in Christ” ([Romans 16:10](https://ref.ly/Rom16:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** ROM 16:10
