## Jozadak (id: 6471)

JOZADAK\*
=========

Seraiah’s son and one of the exiles transported by Nebuchadnezzar to Babylonia ([1 Chr 6:14–15](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:14-1Chr6:15)). He was the father of Jeshua (also called Joshua), the high priest in postexilic Jerusalem during the days of Zerubbabel ([Ezr 3:2, 8](https://ref.ly/Ezra3:2,Ezra3:8); [5:2](https://ref.ly/Ezra5:2); [10:18](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:18); [Neh 12:26](https://ref.ly/Neh12:26); [Hg 1:1–14](https://ref.ly/Hag1:1-Hag1:14); [2:2–4](https://ref.ly/Hag2:2-Hag2:4); [Zec 6:11](https://ref.ly/Zech6:11)). Jozadak is alternately called Jehozadak.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 3:2; EZR 3:8; EZR 5:2; EZR 10:18; NEH 12:26; ZEC 6:11; 1CH 6:14–1CH 6:15; HAG 1:1–HAG 1:14; HAG 2:2–HAG 2:4
