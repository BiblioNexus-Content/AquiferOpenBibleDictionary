## Sherebiah (id: 8373)

SHEREBIAH
=========

1\. Levite, a descendant of Mahli. Sherebiah, described as a man of understanding, was sent as a priest for the temple at Jerusalem following the exile ([Ezr 8:18](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:18); [Neh 12:8](https://ref.ly/Neh12:8)). During the return journey, he was one of 12 chief priests appointed to guard the silver, gold, and vessels presented for temple use ([Ezr 8:24](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:24)).

2\. One who helped the people understand the law read by Ezra ([Neh 8:7](https://ref.ly/Neh8:7)), and among the Levites who stood on the stairs leading the praise service ([9:4–5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:4-Neh9:5)).

3\. One of the leaders of the Levites who led the songs of praise and thanksgiving ([Neh 12:24](https://ref.ly/Neh12:24)).

It is possible that the above references refer to the same person.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 8:18; EZR 8:24; NEH 8:7; NEH 12:8; NEH 12:24; NEH 9:4–NEH 9:5
