## Sela (id: 8215)

SELA
====

1\. Unidentified site on the border of the Amorites ([Jgs 1:36](https://ref.ly/Judg1:36)).

2\. Edomite stronghold ([2 Kgs 14:7](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:7)). On this site the Nabatean city of Petra was built. *See* Petra.

3\. Unidentified place mentioned in Isaiah’s prophecy against Moab ([Is 16:1](https://ref.ly/Isa16:1)).
