## Minnith (id: 7148)

MINNITH
=======

One of the 10 cities conquered by Jephthah in his defeat of the Ammonites ([Jgs 11:33](https://ref.ly/Judg11:33)). The city was a center for the wheat trade ([Ez 27:17](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 11:33; EZK 27:17
