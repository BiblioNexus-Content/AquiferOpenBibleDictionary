## Elah (id: 4963)

ELAH
====

1\. Esau’s descendant and a chief of Edom ([Gn 36:41](https://ref.ly/Gen36:41); [1 Chr 1:52](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:52)).

2\. KJV rendering of Ela, Shimei’s father, in [1 Kings 4:18](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:18). *See* Ela.

3\. Baasha’s son and fourth king of Israel. Elah reigned for only two years (886–885 BC). While in a drunken stupor, he was murdered by one of his generals ([1 Kgs 16:8–14](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:8-1Kgs16:14)).

4\. Father of Hoshea, the last king of the northern kingdom of Israel ([2 Kgs 15:30](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:30); [17:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:1); [18:1, 9](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:1,2Kgs18:9)).

5\. Caleb’s second son and father of Kenaz ([1 Chr 4:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:15)).

6\. Uzzi’s son, descendant of Benjamin ([1 Chr 9:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:8)). Elah was among the first to resettle in Jerusalem after the Babylonian exile. He is not mentioned in the parallel list of [Nehemiah 11](https://ref.ly/Neh11:1-Neh11:36).
