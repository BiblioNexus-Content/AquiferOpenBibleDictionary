## Merab (id: 7056)

MERAB
=====

Eldest of Saul’s two daughters ([1 Sm 14:49](https://ref.ly/1Sam14:49)), who was promised as a wife to David ([18:17–18](https://ref.ly/1Sam18:17-1Sam18:18)). Saul unexplainedly did not keep the agreement, instead giving him Michal.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 14:49; 1SA 18:17–1SA 18:18
