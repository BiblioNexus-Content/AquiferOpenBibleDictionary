## Ben-Hail (id: 4106)

BEN\-HAIL
=========

One of five officials sent out by King Jehoshaphat of the southern kingdom of Judah to teach the people the law of the Lord ([2 Chronicles 17:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 17:7
