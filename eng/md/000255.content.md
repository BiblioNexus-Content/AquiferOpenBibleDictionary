## Allon (Place) (id: 3516)

ALLON (Place)
=============

A landmark oak tree in Naphtali’s territory. Some translations like the King James Version considered it to be the name of a town in [Joshua 19:33](https://ref.ly/Josh19:33). The Berean Standard Bible better reads “the great tree of Zaanannim.” *See* Zaanannim.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:33
* **Associated Articles:** Zaanannim (ID: `9109@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
