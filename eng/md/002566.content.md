## Hermonite (id: 5819)

HERMONITE\*
===========

KJV mistranslation for Hermon (Mount) in [Psalm 42:6](https://ref.ly/Ps42:6). Mt Hermon, a sacred site since antiquity, lies on the northernmost boundary of Joshua’s conquest ([Jos 12:5](https://ref.ly/Josh12:5); [13:11](https://ref.ly/Josh13:11)). *See* Hermon, Mount.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 12:5; JOS 13:11; PSA 42:6
* **Associated Articles:** Hermon, Mount (ID: `5820@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
