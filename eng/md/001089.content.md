## Brood (id: 4346)

BROOD
=====

A term often used in the Bible for young birds, especially trained birds, it is also used for snakes or vipers as a metaphor for “sinners” ([Numbers 32:14](https://ref.ly/Num32:14); [Matthew 3:7](https://ref.ly/Matt3:7); [12:34](https://ref.ly/Matt12:34); [Luke 3:7](https://ref.ly/Luke3:7)).

*See* Birds (Fowl, Domestic; Partridge).
