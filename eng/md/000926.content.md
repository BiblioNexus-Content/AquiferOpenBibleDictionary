## Bethphage (id: 4197)

BETHPHAGE
=========

A village on the Mount of Olives near Jerusalem. At Bethphage, two disciples obtained the donkey colt on which Jesus rode into Jerusalem ([Matthew 21:1–6](https://ref.ly/Matt21:1-Matt21:6); [Mark 11:1–6](https://ref.ly/Mark11:1-Mark11:6); [Luke 19:29–35](https://ref.ly/Luke19:29-Luke19:35)).

* **Associated Passages:** MAT 21:1–MAT 21:6; MRK 11:1–MRK 11:6; LUK 19:29–LUK 19:35
