## Hazar-Enan, Hazar-Enon (id: 5727)

HAZAR\-ENAN, HAZAR\-ENON\*
==========================

Place describing the northeast corner of Israel’s border ([Nm 34:9–10](https://ref.ly/Num34:9-Num34:10)); alternately spelled Hazar\-enon in [Ezekiel 47:17–18](https://ref.ly/Ezek47:17-Ezek47:18) and [48:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek48:1) (rsv). It is identified with modern Hadr at the base of Mt Hermon.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 48:1; NUM 34:9–NUM 34:10; EZK 47:17–EZK 47:18
