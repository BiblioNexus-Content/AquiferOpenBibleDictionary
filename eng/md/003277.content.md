## Keilah (Person) (id: 6534)

KEILAH (Person)
===============

Caleb’s descendant from Judah’s tribe, called the Garmite in [1 Chronicles 4:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:19). Some identify this reference with the city in Judah instead of a person.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:19
