## Anaharath (id: 3585)

ANAHARATH
=========

A town in the valley of Jezreel. It was given to Issachar’s tribe when the land was divided by Joshua ([Joshua 19:19](https://ref.ly/Josh19:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:19
