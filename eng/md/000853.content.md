## Beracah (id: 4124)

BERACAH
=======

A warrior from the tribe of Benjamin who joined David at Ziklag in his struggle against King Saul. Beracah was one of the archers and slingers in David’s army who used both his left and right hands ([1 Chronicles 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:3
