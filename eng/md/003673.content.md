## Maoch (id: 6932)

MAOCH\*
=======

Father of Achish, the Philistine king of Gath. David sought refuge with this king in order to escape Saul’s plots to kill him ([1 Sm 27:2](https://ref.ly/1Sam27:2)). *See* Maacah, Maachah (Person) \#3.
