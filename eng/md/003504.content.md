## Litter (id: 6761)

LITTER
======

A large couch used for carrying officials ([Song of Solomon 3:7–10](https://ref.ly/Song3:7-Song3:10); [Isaiah 66:20](https://ref.ly/Isa66:20)). It is also translated as “carriage” or “palanquin.” 

*See* Travel.
