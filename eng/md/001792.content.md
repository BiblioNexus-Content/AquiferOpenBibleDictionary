## Elon (Place) (id: 5047)

ELON (Place)
============

Village near Timnah, the home of Samson’s first wife. Elon was included in the inheritance allotted to Dan’s tribe in Joshua’s distribution of the Promised Land ([Jos 19:43](https://ref.ly/Josh19:43); cf. [14:1–2](https://ref.ly/Josh14:1-Josh14:2)). It is generally identified with Khirbet Wadi ’Alinm near ’Ain Shems.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:43; JOS 14:1–JOS 14:2
