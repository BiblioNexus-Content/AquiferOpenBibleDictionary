## Arumah (id: 3772)

ARUMAH
======

The city where Gideon’s son Abimelech lived after he was driven out of Shechem by those who lived there ([Judges 9:1, 22–25, 31, 41](https://ref.ly/Judg9:1,Judg9:22-Judg9:25,Judg9:31,Judg9:41)). It may have been called Rumah in [2 Kings 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:36).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 9:1; JDG 9:31; JDG 9:41; 2KI 23:36; JDG 9:22–JDG 9:25
