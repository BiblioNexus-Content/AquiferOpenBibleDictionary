## Jehozabad (id: 6290)

JEHOZABAD
=========

1\. Shomer’s son, who was a servant of King Joash and later, with another assailant, murdered the king at Millo ([2 Kgs 12:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:21)). In a parallel passage, Jehozabad is called the son of Shimrith the Moabitess ([2 Chr 24:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:26)). King Amaziah, Joash’s son, eventually executed Jehozabad for the murder ([25:3](https://ref.ly/2Chr25:3)).

2\. Obed\-edom’s second son and a member of a Levitical Korahite family appointed by King David to be gatekeepers in the temple ([1 Chr 26:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:4)).

3\. Benjamite military commander who served under King Jehoshaphat of Judah and commanded 180,000 men in his army ([2 Chr 17:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:21; 1CH 26:4; 2CH 17:18; 2CH 24:26; 2CH 25:3
