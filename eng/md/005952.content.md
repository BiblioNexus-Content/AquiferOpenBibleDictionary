## Zeresh (id: 9196)

ZERESH
======

The wife of Haman the Agagite. She told him to build the gallows (a tall wooden structure) for hanging Mordecai ([Esther 5:10, 14](https://ref.ly/Esth5:10,Esth5:14)). 

* **Associated Passages:** EST 5:10; EST 5:14
