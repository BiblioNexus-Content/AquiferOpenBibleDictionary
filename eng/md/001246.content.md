## Chemarim, Chemarims (id: 4500)

CHEMARIM\*, CHEMARIMS\*
=======================

Hebrew term often translated “idolatrous priests” ([2 Kgs 23:5](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:5); [Hos 10:5](https://ref.ly/Hos10:5); [Zep 1:4](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4)). In the kjv this word appears in [Zephaniah 1:4](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:4) as a proper name, spelled either Chemarim or Chemarims. The exact meaning of the word is uncertain.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 23:5; HOS 10:5; ZEP 1:4
