## Remaliah (id: 7991)

REMALIAH
========

The father of King Pekah of Israel who ruled from 737 to 732 BC. Pekah took power by secretly killing the previous king ([2 Kings 15:25–37](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:25-2Kgs15:37)). Later, he attacked Jerusalem with his army ([Isaiah 7:1–9](https://ref.ly/Isa7:1-Isa7:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 7:1–ISA 7:9; 2KI 15:25–2KI 15:37
