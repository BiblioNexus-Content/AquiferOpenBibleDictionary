## Ozni, Oznite (id: 7544)

OZNI, OZNITE
============

Ozni was another name for a person called Ezbon. The Oznites were the family group that came from Ozni ([Numbers 26:16](https://ref.ly/Num26:16)). 

*See* Ezbon \#1.
