## Paarai (id: 7545)

PAARAI
======

One of themighty men of King David. Paarai was from Arba, in Judah ([2 Samuel 23:35](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:35)). He may be the same person as Naarai the son of Ezbai ([1 Chronicles 11:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:37)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:35; 1CH 11:37
