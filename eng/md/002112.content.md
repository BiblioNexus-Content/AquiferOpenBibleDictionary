## Gareb (Place) (id: 5368)

GAREB (Place)
=============

Hill near Jerusalem mentioned in [Jeremiah 31:39](https://ref.ly/Jer31:39) as a future boundary of the city, perhaps on the south or west side.

* **Associated Passages:** JER 31:39
