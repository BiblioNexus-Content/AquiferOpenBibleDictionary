## Mortar, the (id: 7209)

MORTAR\*, The
=============

Name given by Zephaniah to a hollow place or depression resembling a mortar in Jerusalem. The “mortar” (Hebrew, Maktesh) was a place of business whose merchants were soon to grieve for their loss of trade ([Zep 1:11](https://ref.ly/Zeph1:11)). Its location is variously identified with the Phoenician quarter, the Kidron Valley, or the Tyropoeon Valley.

*See also* Jerusalem.
