## Ar (id: 3775)

AR
==

The capital city of Moab located on the northern border ([Deuteronomy 2:18, 29](https://ref.ly/Deut2:18,Deut2:29)). It was near the Arnon River ([Numbers 21:28](https://ref.ly/Num21:28)). Ar was sometimes used figuratively to refer to all of Moab ([Deuteronomy 2:9](https://ref.ly/Deut2:9)). The prophet Isaiah predicted the destruction of the Moabite cities Ar and Kir ([Isaiah 15:1](https://ref.ly/Isa15:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 21:28; DEU 2:9; DEU 2:18; DEU 2:29; ISA 15:1
