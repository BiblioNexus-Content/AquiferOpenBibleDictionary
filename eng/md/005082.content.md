## Shema (Person) (id: 8346)

SHEMA (Person)
==============

1\. Judahite, Hebron’s son and a descendant of Caleb ([1 Chr 2:43–44](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:43-1Chr2:44)).

2\. Reubenite and Joel’s son ([1 Chr 5:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:8)). He is perhaps identifiable with Shemaiah or Shimei in [1 Chronicles 5:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:4).

3\. Benjaminite and head of a family in Aijalon, who helped defeat the inhabitants of Gath ([1 Chr 8:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:13)).

4\. Levite who explained to the people passages from the law read by Ezra ([Neh 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 5:4; 1CH 5:8; 1CH 8:13; NEH 8:4; 1CH 2:43–1CH 2:44
