## Dophkah (id: 4885)

DOPHKAH
=======

A name of an area near the wilderness of Sin. Dophkah is where the Israelites camped on their way to Mount Sinai ([Numbers 33:12–13](https://ref.ly/Num33:12-Num33:13)). Dophkah may be the same place as Serabit el\-Khadem. Serabit el\-Khadem was an Egyptian turquoise mining center. 

*See* Wilderness Wanderings.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:12–NUM 33:13
