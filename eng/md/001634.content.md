## Doorkeeper (id: 4884)

DOORKEEPER
==========

A person who guards and controls access to a building by watching its entrance. In the Bible, doorkeepers (or gatekeepers) had the important job of protecting holy places like the temple in Jerusalem. They controlled who could enter and made sure the building stayed secure.

*See* Gatekeepers.
