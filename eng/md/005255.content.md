## Sin, Man of (id: 8518)

SIN\*, Man of
=============

KJV rendering of an inferior textual variant in the Textus Receptus in [2 Thessalonians 2:3](https://ref.ly/2Thess2:3). The correct reading is “man of lawlessness,” an expression used by Paul of the Antichrist. *See* Antichrist.

* **Associated Passages:** 2TH 2:3
* **Associated Articles:** Antichrist (ID: `3630@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
