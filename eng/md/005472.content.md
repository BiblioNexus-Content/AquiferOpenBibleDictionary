## Tannaim (id: 8733)

TANNAIM
=======

Teachers of the Jewish oral law mentioned in the Mishnah. This was during the time beginning with the students of Shammai and Hillel in AD 10\. It ended with the pupils of Judah HaNasi I in AD 220\. 

*See* Mishnah; Talmud.
