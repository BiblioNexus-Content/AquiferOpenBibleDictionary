## Ahira (id: 3455)

AHIRA
=====

Enan’s son. He was the leader of the tribe of Naphtali when the Israelites were wandering in the wilderness after their escape from Egypt. As a leader, he presented his tribe’s offering when the tabernacle was set apart for holy use ([Numbers 1:15](https://ref.ly/Num1:15); [2:29](https://ref.ly/Num2:29); [7:78, 83](https://ref.ly/Num7:78,Num7:83); [10:27](https://ref.ly/Num10:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:15; NUM 2:29; NUM 7:78; NUM 7:83; NUM 10:27
