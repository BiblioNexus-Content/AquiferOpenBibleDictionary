## Maon (Person), Maonite (id: 6933)

MAON (Person), MAONITE
======================

Son of Shammai, and Bethzur’s father ([1 Chr 2:45](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:45)). He was either the founding father of the people of Bethzur and/or the founder of the city. His descendants are perhaps the Maonites of [Judges 10:12](https://ref.ly/Judg10:12).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 10:12; 1CH 2:45
