## Tibhath (id: 8834)

TIBHATH
=======

A city that belonged to King Hadadezer ([1 Chronicles 18:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:8)). The same place is called Betah in the Hebrew text of [2 Samuel 8:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:8).

*See* Tebah (Place).
