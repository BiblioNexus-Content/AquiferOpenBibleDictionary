## Bath-Rabbim, Gate of (id: 4030)

BATH\-RABBIM, Gate of
=====================

A gate in the city of Heshbon that was near several pools of clear water. [Song of Solomon 7:4](https://ref.ly/Song7:4) describes a young woman’s eyes as being like those pools.

* **Associated Passages:** SNG 7:4
