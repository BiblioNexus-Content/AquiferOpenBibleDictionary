## Tabor, Oak of (id: 8702)

TABOR, Oak of
=============

Place near Bethel and perhaps in Benjamin’s tribal territory where Saul was to meet three men ([1 Sm 10:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam10:3)). This encounter was the second of four signs given to Saul to confirm his appointment as king.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 10:3
