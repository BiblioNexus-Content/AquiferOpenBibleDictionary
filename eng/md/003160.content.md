## Joiakim (id: 6418)

JOIAKIM
=======

Levite high priest in a family of high priests. Jeshua’s son and the father of Eliashib the high priest, a contemporary of Nehemiah ([Neh 12:10–12, 26](https://ref.ly/Neh12:10-Neh12:12,Neh12:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:26; NEH 12:10–NEH 12:12
