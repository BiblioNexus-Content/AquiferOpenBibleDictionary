## Zaphenath-Paneah (id: 9137)

ZAPHENATH\-PANEAH
=================

Name given to Joseph by Pharaoh when Joseph assumed his governmental responsibilities in Egypt ([Gn 41:45](https://ref.ly/Gen41:45)). The name most likely means “says the god, he will live.” *See* Joseph \#1.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 41:45
