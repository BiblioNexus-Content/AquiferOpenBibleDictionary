## Conaniah (id: 4623)

CONANIAH
========

1\. Levite and chief officer who supervised tithes, contributions, and the dedicated things given to the temple during the reign of Hezekiah ([2 Chr 31:12–13](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:12-2Chr31:13)).

2\. One of the chief Levites during the time of King Josiah ([2 Chr 35:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr35:9)); perhaps identifiable with Jeconiah in [1 Esdras 1:9](https://ref.ly/1Esd1:9).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 35:9; 1ES 1:9; 2CH 31:12–2CH 31:13
