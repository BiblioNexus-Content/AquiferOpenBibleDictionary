## Ziza (id: 9248)

ZIZA
====

1\. Chief of Simeon’s tribe descending from Shemaiah ([1 Chr 4:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:37)).

2\. Son of Rehoboam and Maacah ([2 Chr 11:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:20)).

3\. Second of Shimei’s sons and a clan chief within the Gershonite branch of Levi’s tribe ([1 Chr 23:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:11)); perhaps the same as Zina in [1 Chronicles 23:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:10).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:37; 1CH 23:10; 1CH 23:11; 2CH 11:20
