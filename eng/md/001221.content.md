## Chamois (id: 4474)

CHAMOIS\*
=========

Small, goatlike antelope that lives primarily in the high mountains of Europe. In [Deuteronomy 14:5](https://ref.ly/Deut14:5) “chamois” is an inaccurate translation of the Hebrew word that is better rendered “mountain sheep.” *See* Animals (Sheep).

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 14:5
