## Mahalaleel, Mahalalel (id: 6863)

MAHALALEEL, MAHALALEL
=====================

1. Kenan’s son and the father of Jared in Seth’s line ([Genesis 5:12](https://ref.ly/Gen5:12-Gen5:17)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen5:12-Gen5:17)[17](https://ref.ly/Gen5:12-Gen5:17); [1 Chronicles 1:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:2)). He is also mentioned in [Luke 3:37](https://ref.ly/Luke3:37).

    *See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

2. A son of Perez and member of the tribe of Judah who lived after the Jewish people returned from exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 11:4](https://ref.ly/Neh11:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 1:2; NEH 11:4; LUK 3:37; GEN 5:12–GEN 5:17
* **Associated Articles:** Genealogy of Jesus Christ (ID: `5412@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
