## Mahalath (Music) (id: 6865)

MAHALATH\* (Music)
==================

Musical cue, perhaps meaning “sadness,” listed in the title of [Psalm 53](https://ref.ly/Ps53:1-Ps53:6) (see rsv), which would designate the way in which and/or the melody to which the psalm should be sung. *See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** PSA 53:1–PSA 53:6
* **Associated Articles:** Music (ID: `7248@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
