## Siloam Tunnel (id: 8496)

SILOAM TUNNEL
=============

A tunnel built by Hezekiah at the time of the Assyrian invasion. It was used to bring water into Jerusalem. It is also known as Hezekiah’s tunnel ([2 Kings 20:20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs20:20); [2 Chronicles 32:2](https://ref.ly/2Chr32:2-2Chr32:4)–[4](https://ref.ly/2Chr32:2-2Chr32:4)).

*See* Siloam, Pool of.
