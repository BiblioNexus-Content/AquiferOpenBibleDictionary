## Shephupham (id: 8370)

SHEPHUPHAM\*
============

Benjamin’s fourth son (called “Muppim” in [Gen 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)) and the father of the Shuphamite family ([Nm 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39); see nlt mg). In the corresponding genealogy of Benjamin ([1 Chr 7:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12)) he is called Shuppim, appearing as Benjamin’s great\-grandson. *See* Shuppim \#1.

*See also* Shephuphan.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:39; 1CH 7:12
