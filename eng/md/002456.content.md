## Hatach, Hathach (id: 5710)

HATHACH, HATACH
===============

A eunuch the Persian king Ahasuerus chose to wait on Esther. Hathach brought Esther messages from Mordecai. In this way, Esther learned of Haman’s plot against the Jews ([Esther 4:5–10](https://ref.ly/Esth4:5-Esth4:10)). Hathach is sometimes spelled Hatach.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 4:5–EST 4:10
