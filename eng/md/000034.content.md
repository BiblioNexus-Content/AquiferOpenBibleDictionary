## Abidan (id: 3291)

ABIDAN
======

Gideoni’s son and the leader of the tribe of Benjamin when the Israelites were wandering in the Sinai wilderness after their escape from Egypt ([Numbers 1:11](https://ref.ly/Num1:11); [2:22](https://ref.ly/Num2:22)). As the leader, he presented his tribe’s offering at the dedication of the tabernacle ([Numbers 7:60](https://ref.ly/Num7:60-Num7:65)–[65](https://ref.ly/Num7:60-Num7:65)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:11; NUM 2:22; NUM 7:60–NUM 7:65
