## Eglon (Person) (id: 4951)

EGLON (Person)
==============

Moabite king who captured Jericho and held it for 18 years, exacting a tribute from Israel. Ehud, an Israelite judge pretending to bring tribute, killed Eglon ([Jgs 3:12–30](https://ref.ly/Judg3:12-Judg3:30)). *See* Moab, Moabites.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 3:12–JDG 3:30
* **Associated Articles:** Moab, Moabites (ID: `7184@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
