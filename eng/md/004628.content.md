## Raamiah (id: 7890)

RAAMIAH\*
=========

One who returned with Zerubbabel to Palestine following the Babylonian captivity ([Neh 7:7](https://ref.ly/Neh7:7), nlt mg). Raamiah is alternately spelled Reelaiah in [Ezra 2:2](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:2). The correct form of the word is uncertain. *See* Reelaiah.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:2; NEH 7:7
* **Associated Articles:** Reelaiah (ID: `7975@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
