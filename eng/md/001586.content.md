## Didrachma (id: 4839)

DIDRACHMA
=========

A Greek silver coin worth two drachmas (or two grams of silver) and equivalent to the Jewish half\-shekel. Every Jew was required to pay this amount as the annual temple tax ([Matthew 17:24](https://ref.ly/Matt17:24)).
