## Ben-Oni (id: 4116)

BEN\-ONI
========

The name Rachel gave to her last son as she died in childbirth ([Genesis 35:18](https://ref.ly/Gen35:18)). His father, Jacob, changed his name from Ben\-oni (“son of my sorrow”) to Benjamin (“son of my right hand”). 

*See* Benjamin (Person) \#1.
