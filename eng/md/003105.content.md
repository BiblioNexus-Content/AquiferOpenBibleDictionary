## Jeuel (id: 6362)

JEUEL
=====

1\. Descendant of Judah residing in postexilic Jerusalem ([1 Chr 9:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:6)).

2\. Levite who took part in Hezekiah’s reforms ([2 Chr 29:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:13)).

3\. Head of a family who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra after the exile ([Ezr 8:13](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:6; 2CH 29:13; EZR 8:13
