## Jetur, Jeturites (id: 6361)

JETUR, JETURITES
================

Son of Ishmael and his descendants ([Genesis 25:15](https://ref.ly/Gen25:15); [1 Chronicles 1:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:31)). 

The Israelite tribes who settled east of the Jordan had to fight them ([1 Chronicles 5:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:19)). They are also called Itureans. The Jeturites continued to live into the New Testament times. The region of Iturea was named after them. It was an area northeast of Galilee ([Luke 3:1](https://ref.ly/Luke3:1)). 

*See* Ituraea, Iturea, Itureans.
