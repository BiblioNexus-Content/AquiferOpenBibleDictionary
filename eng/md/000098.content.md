## Adah (id: 3358)

ADAH
====

1. One of Lamech’s two wives and the mother of his two sons, Jabal and Jubal ([Genesis 4:19](https://ref.ly/Gen4:19-Gen4:21,Gen4:23)–[21, 23](https://ref.ly/Gen4:19-Gen4:21,Gen4:23)).
2. Esau’s first wife, the daughter of Elon the Hittite and mother of Eliphaz ([Genesis 36:2](https://ref.ly/Gen36:2-Gen36:16)–[16](https://ref.ly/Gen36:2-Gen36:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 4:23; GEN 4:19–GEN 4:21; GEN 36:2–GEN 36:16
