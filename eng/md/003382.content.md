## Kola (id: 6641)

KOLA
====

Kola is mentioned in the book of Judith ([Jdt 15:4](https://ref.ly/Jdt15:4)) as a place\-name. It may be identified with Holon ([Jos 15:51](https://ref.ly/Josh15:51)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:51; JDT 15:4
