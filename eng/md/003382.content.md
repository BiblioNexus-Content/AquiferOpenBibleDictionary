## Koine Greek (id: 6639)

KOINE GREEK
===========

A form of the Greek language that was widely used throughout the Near East and Mediterranean regions during the time of the Roman Empire. The word *koine* means "common" in Greek. This was the form of Greek used to write the New Testament and translate the Septuagint (a Greek translation of the Old Testament).
