## Shimeath (id: 8404)

SHIMEATH
========

Ammonitess mother ([2 Chr 24:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:26)) or perhaps father ([2 Kgs 12:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:21)) of one of the royal servants who conspired against and murdered King Jehoash of Judah (835–796 BC).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:21; 2CH 24:26
