## Shelomoth (id: 8341)

SHELOMOTH
=========

1\. Gershonite Levite and one of Shimei’s sons serving in the sanctuary during David’s reign ([1 Chr 23:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:9)).

2\. Levite and priest from the family of Izhar during David’s reign ([1 Chr 23:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:18), “Shelomith”; [24:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:22)).

3\. Zicri’s son, who was in charge of the royal treasuries during David’s reign ([1 Chr 26:25–28](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:25-1Chr26:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 23:9; 1CH 23:18; 1CH 24:22; 1CH 26:25–1CH 26:28
