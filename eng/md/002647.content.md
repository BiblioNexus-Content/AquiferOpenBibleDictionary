## Hophni (id: 5895)

HOPHNI
======

Brother of Phinehas, with whom he served as a priest at Shiloh ([1 Sm 1:3](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:3)). He was an evil man who flouted the sacrificial rituals ([2:12–17](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:12-1Sam2:17)) and behaved immorally (v [22](https://ref.ly/1Sam2:22)). Condemned by God, Hophni died during a Philistine attack on Shiloh and its sanctuary ([4:11](https://ref.ly/1Sam4:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 1:3; 1SA 2:22; 1SA 4:11; 1SA 2:12–1SA 2:17
