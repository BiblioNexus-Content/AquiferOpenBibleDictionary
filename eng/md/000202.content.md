## Ahlab (id: 3462)

AHLAB
=====

A Canaanite city in Asher. The Israelites failed to remove its inhabitants in their conquest of Canaan ([Judges 1:31](https://ref.ly/Judg1:31)). It probably is the same city as Mahalab in [Joshua 19:29](https://ref.ly/Josh19:29). It is the modern\-day Khirbet el\-Mahalib near Tyre. 

*See* Mahalab.
