## Dandelion (id: 718555)

Dandelion
=========

A plant (*Taraxacum officinale*) that is considered one of the bitter herbs. Dandelions are common flowering plants with bright yellow flowers that turn into fluffy seed heads. The leaves have a jagged, tooth\-like shape (the name "dandelion" comes from the French *dent de lion* meaning "lion's tooth").

*See* Bitter Herbs.
