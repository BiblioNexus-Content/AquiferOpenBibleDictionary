## Azael (id: 3894)

AZAEL
=====

Ezora’s son. He obeyed Ezra’s command to divorce his non\-Jewish wife after the exile in Babylon ([1 Esdras 9:34](https://ref.ly/1Esd9:34)). Azael is not included in the list of [Ezra 10:40](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:40-Ezra10:42)[–](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:40-Ezra10:42)[42](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:40-Ezra10:42).

* **Associated Passages:** 1ES 9:34; EZR 10:40–EZR 10:42
