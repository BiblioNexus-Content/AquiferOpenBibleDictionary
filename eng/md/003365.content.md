## Kislev (id: 6623)

KISLEV
======

Kislev is one of the months in the Hebrew calendar. On our modern calendar, it usually occurs during parts of November and December. The name can also be written as Chislev or Chisleu.

*See* Calendars, Ancient and Modern.
