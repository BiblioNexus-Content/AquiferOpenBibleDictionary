## Magdalene, Mary (id: 6850)

MAGDALENE, Mary
===============

Name of one of several Marys who followed Jesus. This Mary was the first to see the risen Christ ([Jn 20:11–18](https://ref.ly/John20:11-John20:18)). *See* Mary \#3.

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 20:11–JHN 20:18
