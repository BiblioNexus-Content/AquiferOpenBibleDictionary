## Shuah (id: 8452)

SHUAH
=====

1\. One of six sons borne to Abraham by Keturah ([Gn 25:2](https://ref.ly/Gen25:2); [1 Chr 1:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:32)). He was perhaps the forefather of the Shuhite Arab tribe that dwelt near the land of Uz ([Jb 2:11](https://ref.ly/Job2:11)).

2\. KJV spelling of Shua, Judah’s father\-in\-law, in [Genesis 38:2, 12](https://ref.ly/Gen38:2,Gen38:12). *See* Shua \#1.

3\. KJV spelling of Shuhah, Kelub’s brother, in [1 Chronicles 4:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:11). *See* Shuhah.
