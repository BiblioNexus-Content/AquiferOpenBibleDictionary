## Moserah, Moseroth (id: 7210)

MOSERAH, MOSEROTH
=================

Temporary camping place of the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings. It was positioned between Hashmonah and Bene\-jaakan ([Nm 33:30–31](https://ref.ly/Num33:30-Num33:31)). Later, Aaron died and was buried there ([Dt 10:6](https://ref.ly/Deut10:6)). Moseroth is the plural form of Moserah.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 10:6; NUM 33:30–NUM 33:31
