## Dathema (id: 4763)

DATHEMA
=======

A fortress in Bashan where Jews took refuge during the Maccabean revolt ([1 Macc 5:9](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:9)). Here they hid from Timothy until Judas the Maccabee rescued them by defeating the enemy (v [29](https://ref.ly/1Macc5:29)). The location of Dathema has not been identified by modern archeologists.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 5:9; 1MA 5:29
