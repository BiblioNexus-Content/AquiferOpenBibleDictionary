## Avvites (id: 3892)

AVVITES
=======

1. An ancient people who lived in villages near Gaza before they were destroyed by a Philistine invasion ([Deuteronomy 2:23](https://ref.ly/Deut2:23); [Joshua 13:3](https://ref.ly/Josh13:3)).
2. The name for the people who lived in the Syrian district of Avva. Shalmaneser of Assyria moved them into Samaria after the conquest in 722 BC ([2 Kings 17:31](https://ref.ly/2Kgs17:31)).

    *See* Avva.
