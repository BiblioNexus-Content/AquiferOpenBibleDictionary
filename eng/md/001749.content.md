## Eliasaph (id: 4998)

ELIASAPH
========

1. A leader of the tribe of Gad chosen by Moses. He was the son of Deuel (Reuel) ([Numbers 1:14](https://ref.ly/Num1:14); [2:14](https://ref.ly/Num2:14); [7:42, 47](https://ref.ly/Num7:42,Num7:47); [10:20](https://ref.ly/Num10:20)).
2. A Gershonite from the tribe of Levi and the son of Lael. He had three responsibilities in the tribe:

    1. to take care of the tabernacle coverings;
        2. to take care of the curtains of the court; and
        3. to take care of the main altar ([Numbers 3:24–25](https://ref.ly/Num3:24-Num3:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:14; NUM 2:14; NUM 7:42; NUM 7:47; NUM 10:20; NUM 3:24–NUM 3:25
