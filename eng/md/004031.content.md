## Nahath (id: 7288)

NAHATH
======

1\. Chief of a clan in Edom and Reuel’s firstborn son ([Gn 36:13, 17](https://ref.ly/Gen36:13,Gen36:17); [1 Chr 1:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:37)).

2\. Levite of the family of Kohath and Elkanah’s grandson ([1 Chr 6:26](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:26)).

3\. Levite who oversaw the temple during King Hezekiah’s reign ([2 Chr 31:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:13; GEN 36:17; 1CH 1:37; 1CH 6:26; 2CH 31:13
