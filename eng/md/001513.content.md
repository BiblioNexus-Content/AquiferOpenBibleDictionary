## Date, Date Palm (id: 4761)

Date
====

The fruit of the date palm tree. The Bible mentions this fruit and tree only a few times in [2 Samuel 6:19](https://ref.ly/2Sam6:19), [1 Chronicles 16:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr16:3), and [Song of Solomon 7:7](https://ref.ly/Song7:7).
