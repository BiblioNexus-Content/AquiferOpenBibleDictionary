## Asphar (id: 3829)

ASPHAR
======

A watering hole in the Tekoa wilderness. It might be modern\-day Bir Selhub south of Engedi. 

Jonathan and Simon Maccabeus camped at Asphar when fleeing from the Syrians under General Bacchides ([1 Maccabees 9:33](https://ref.ly/1Macc9:33)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 9:33
