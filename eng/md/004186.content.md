## Oak of Weeping (id: 7444)

OAK OF WEEPING
==============

Tree near Bethel under which Deborah, Rebekah’s nurse, was buried ([Gn 35:8](https://ref.ly/Gen35:8)), hence called Allon\-bacuth*,* meaning “Oak of Weeping.”

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 35:8
