## Tabeal, Tabeel (id: 8693)

TABEAL\*, TABEEL
================

1\. Ruler in Samaria who, with his associates, wrote a letter to King Artaxerxes I of Persia (464–424 BC) protesting Zerubbabel’s rebuilding of the Jerusalem wall ([Ezr 4:7](https://ref.ly/Ezra4:7)).

2\. Father of the man whom King Pekah of Israel and King Rezin of Syria wanted to put on the throne of Jerusalem after they conquered it and subdued Ahaz, king of Judah (735–715 BC; [Is 7:6](https://ref.ly/Isa7:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 4:7; ISA 7:6
