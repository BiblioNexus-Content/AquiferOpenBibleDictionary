## Imnah (id: 6007)

IMNAH
=====

1\. Asher’s son ([Gn 46:17](https://ref.ly/Gen46:17); [1 Chr 7:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:30)) and founder of the Imnite family ([Nm 26:44](https://ref.ly/Num26:44)).

2\. Levite and Kore’s father. Kore was a temple assistant during King Hezekiah’s reign ([2 Chr 31:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:17; NUM 26:44; 1CH 7:30; 2CH 31:14
