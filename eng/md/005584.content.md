## Tibni (id: 8835)

TIBNI
=====

Ginath’s son, who competed with Omri to be king of Israel after Zimri’s suicide ([1 Kgs 16:21–22](https://ref.ly/1Kgs16:21-1Kgs16:22)). Tibni ruled over half of Israel from 884–880 BC before Omri defeated him in a civil war.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 16:21–1KI 16:22
