## Zeredah (id: 9191)

ZEREDAH
=======

1\. Birthplace (or hometown) of Jeroboam, Israel’s first king during the period of the divided kingdom ([1 Kgs 11:26](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:26)).

2\. City in the Jordan Valley ([2 Chr 4:17](https://ref.ly/2Chr4:17); [1 Kgs 7:46](https://ref.ly/1Kgs7:46) “Zarethan”). *See* Zarethan.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 7:46; 1KI 11:26; 2CH 4:17
