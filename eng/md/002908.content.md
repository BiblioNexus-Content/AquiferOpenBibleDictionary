## Jackal’s Well (id: 6159)

JACKAL’S WELL
=============

Unknown location along the Jerusalem wall between the Valley Gate and the Dung Gate visited by Nehemiah during his night inspection of the wall ([Neh 2:13](https://ref.ly/Neh2:13)). It is also called Dragon’s Well or Serpent’s Well.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 2:13
