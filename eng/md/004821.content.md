## Sacar, Sachar (id: 8086)

SACAR, SACHAR
=============

1. A Hararite and father of Ahiam. Sacar was one of the mighty men of King David ([1 Chronicles 11:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:35)). In another account of this same event, Sachar is called “Sharar the Hararite” ([2 Samuel 23:33](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:33)).
2. A Korahite and one of the eight sons of Obed\-edom. [1 Chronicles 26:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:4) lists Sachar and his brothers among the families of gatekeepers.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:33; 1CH 11:35; 1CH 26:4
