## Nimshi (id: 7408)

NIMSHI
======

Father of Jehoshaphat and grandfather of Jehu, who was king of Israel ([1 Kgs 19:16](https://ref.ly/1Kgs19:16); [2 Kgs 9:2–20](https://ref.ly/2Kgs9:2-2Kgs9:20); [2 Chr 22:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr22:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 19:16; 2CH 22:7; 2KI 9:2–2KI 9:20
