## Not My People (id: 7430)

NOT MY PEOPLE
=============

A symbolic name given by the prophet Hosea to his third son ([Hosea 1:9](https://ref.ly/Hos1:9)). It was a warning of the coming judgment of God upon Israel. 

*See* Ammi.
