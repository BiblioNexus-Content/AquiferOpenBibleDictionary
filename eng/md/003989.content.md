## Mushi, Mushite (id: 7245)

MUSHI, MUSHITE
==============

The son of Merari, the grandson of Levi, and Mahli’s brother ([Exodus 6:19](https://ref.ly/Exod6:19); [Numbers 3:20](https://ref.ly/Num3:20); [1 Chronicles 6:19, 47](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:19,1Chr6:47)). He was the father of Mahli, Eder, and Jeremoth ([1 Chronicles 23:21–23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:21-1Chr23:23); [24:26, 30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:26,1Chr24:30)). He was the founder of the family of Mushites ([Numbers 3:33](https://ref.ly/Num3:33); [26:58](https://ref.ly/Num26:58)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:19; NUM 3:20; NUM 3:33; NUM 26:58; 1CH 6:19; 1CH 6:47; 1CH 24:26; 1CH 24:30; 1CH 23:21–1CH 23:23
