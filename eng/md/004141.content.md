## Night Hag (id: 7400)

NIGHT HAG
=========

The night hag is mentioned in some translations of the Bible as a creature living in desert areas ([Isaiah 34:14](https://ref.ly/Isa34:14)). In modern Bible translations, this creature is usually understood to be a type of owl. Different translations use various names for this bird, including "night creature," "night bird," or "screech owl." 

*See* Scops Owl.
