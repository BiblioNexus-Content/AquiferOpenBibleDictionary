## Ishbi-benob (id: 6065)

ISHBI\-BENOB
============

A giant who almost killed David. During one of his many battles with the Philistines, David grew faint and Ishbi\-benob almost killed him. Abishai killed the giant and saved David ([2 Samuel 21:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam21:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 21:16
