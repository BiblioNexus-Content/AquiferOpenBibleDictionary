## Shiloah (id: 8396)

SHILOAH
=======

The name of a water channel or aqueduct in Jerusalem ([Isaiah 8:6](https://ref.ly/Isa8:6)). It is the same place as the Pool of Siloam mentioned elsewhere in the Bible.

*See* Siloam, Pool of.
