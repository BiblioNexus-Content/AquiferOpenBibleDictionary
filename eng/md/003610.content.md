## Mahaneh-Dan (id: 6869)

MAHANEH\-DAN
============

Place west of Kiriath\-jearim between Zorab and Eshtaol, where the Spirit of the Lord began to stir in Samson ([Jgs 13:25](https://ref.ly/Judg13:25)) and where Dan’s tribe encamped on the way to the hill country of Ephraim ([18:12](https://ref.ly/Judg18:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 13:25; JDG 18:12
