## Amittai (id: 3555)

AMITTAI
=======

The father of the prophet Jonah from Zebulun’s tribe. Amittai came from the small village of Gath\-hepher. Gath\-hepher is northeast of Nazareth ([2 Kings 14:25](https://ref.ly/2Kgs14:25); [Jonah 1:1](https://ref.ly/Jonah1:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 14:25; JON 1:1
