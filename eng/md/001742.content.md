## Eliada (id: 4993)

ELIADA
======

1. One of the sons of King David. He was born in Jerusalem ([2 Samuel 5:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:16); [1 Chronicles 3:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:8)). He is also called Beeliada in [1 Chronicles 14:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:7).
2. The father of Rezon. He was the king of Damascus and an adversary of King Solomon ([1 Kings 11:23](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:23)).
3. A general who served under King Jehoshaphat. Eliada and the 200,000 warriors he commanded were from the tribe of Benjamin ([2 Chronicles 17:17](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 5:16; 1KI 11:23; 1CH 3:8; 1CH 14:7; 2CH 17:17
