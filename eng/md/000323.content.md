## Anah (id: 3586)

ANAH
====

1. The son of Zibeon the Hivite and father of Oholibamah. Oholibamah was one of Esau’s wives ([Genesis 36:2, 18](https://ref.ly/Gen36:2,Gen36:18)).
2. The fourth son of Seir the Horite. Anah was a chief among the Horites who also had a daughter named Oholibamah ([Genesis 36:20, 25](https://ref.ly/Gen36:20,Gen36:25); [1 Chronicles 1:38, 41](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:38,1Chr1:41)).
3. The son of Zibeon who found hot springs in the wasteland ([Genesis 36:24](https://ref.ly/Gen36:24)). This Zibeon was a brother to \#2 above. *See* Zibeon.
