## Amok (id: 3568)

AMOK
====

A priest who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile to Babylon. Amok was the ancestor of Eber, who was a priest under Joiakim ([Nehemiah 12:7, 20](https://ref.ly/Neh12:7,Neh12:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:7; NEH 12:20
