## Bigthan, Bigthana (id: 4235)

BIGTHAN, BIGTHANA
=================

A eunuch who served King Ahasuerus of Persia as a palace guard. He and a fellow guard named Teresh planned an assassination attempt on the king’s life. When their plot was overheard by Queen Esther’s uncle Mordecai, Bigthan and Teresh were executed ([Esther 2:21–23](https://ref.ly/Esth2:21-Esth2:23)). Bigthan is called “Bigthana” in [Esther 6:2](https://ref.ly/Esth6:2).
