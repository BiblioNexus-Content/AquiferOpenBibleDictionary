## Zebidah (id: 9157)

ZEBIDAH
=======

The mother of Jehoiakim, who was a king of Judah. Zebidah was King Josiah's wife and the daughter of Pedaiah ([2 Kings 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:36)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 23:36
