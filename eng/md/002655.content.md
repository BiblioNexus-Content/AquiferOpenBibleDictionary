## Hor-Haggidgad (id: 5902)

HOR\-HAGGIDGAD
==============

Camping place of the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings ([Nm 33:32–33](https://ref.ly/Num33:32-Num33:33)). It may be the Gudgodah of [Deuteronomy 10:7](https://ref.ly/Deut10:7), and has been identified with Wadi Ghadaghed.

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 10:7; NUM 33:32–NUM 33:33
* **Associated Articles:** Wilderness Wanderings (ID: `9063@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
