## Hori (id: 5904)

HORI
====

1\. Lotan’s first son. Lotan was the founder of a Horite subclan in Edom ([Gn 36:22](https://ref.ly/Gen36:22); [1 Chr 1:39](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:39)).

2\. Shaphat’s father and a member of Simeon’s tribe. Shaphat was one of the 12 spies ([Nm 13:5](https://ref.ly/Num13:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:22; NUM 13:5; 1CH 1:39
