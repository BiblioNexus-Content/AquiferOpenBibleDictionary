## Turtledove (id: 8944)

TURTLEDOVE
==========

A turtledove is a type of small wild pigeon. Turtledoves are known for their gentle cooing sound. In the Bible, they were often offered as sacrifices, especially by people who could not afford larger animals ([Leviticus 5:7](https://ref.ly/Lev5:7)).
