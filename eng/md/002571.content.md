## Herodion (id: 5824)

HERODION
========

Christian of Jewish ancestry to whom Paul sent greetings at the conclusion of his Epistle to the Romans ([Rom 16:11](https://ref.ly/Rom16:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** ROM 16:11
