## Basilisk (id: 4026)

BASILISK
========

A word used in two passages of some translations ([Proverbs 23:32](https://ref.ly/Prov23:32); [Isaiah 14:29](https://ref.ly/Isa14:29)). “Basilisk” refers to a kind of lizard. It is a mistranslation. It has been corrected to read “adder” or “viper” in more recent translations.

* **Associated Passages:** PRO 23:32; ISA 14:29
