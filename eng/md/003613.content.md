## Mahavah, Mahavite (id: 6872)

MAHAVAH, MAHAVITE\*
===================

Term used in [1 Chronicles 11:46](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:46) to designate Eliel, one of David’s mighty men. The word was probably added to indicate where he came from so as to distinguish him from the Eliel in verse [47](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:47).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 11:46; 1CH 11:47
