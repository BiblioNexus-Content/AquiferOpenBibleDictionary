## Muster Gate (id: 7250)

MUSTER GATE\*
=============

RSV translation for the Jerusalem gate located opposite the house of the temple servants and the merchants during the days of Nehemiah ([Neh 3:31](https://ref.ly/Neh3:31); kjv “Miphkad Gate”; niv “Inspection Gate”). Its exact location is uncertain. Some suggest that it was a temple gate or a gate in the wall of the old city of David. *See* Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:31
