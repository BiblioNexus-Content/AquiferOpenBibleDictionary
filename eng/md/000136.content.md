## Adoraim (id: 3396)

ADORAIM
=======

A city in the southern kingdom of Judah, which was strengthened by King Rehoboam ([2 Chronicles 11:9](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:9)). The two cities of Adoraim and Mareshah later joined together to make Idumea. In [1 Maccabees 13:20](https://ref.ly/1Macc13:20) it is called Adora. It is modern\-day Dura, south of Hebron.

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 11:9; 1MA 13:20
