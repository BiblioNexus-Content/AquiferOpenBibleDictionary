## Alvah (id: 3532)

ALVAH
=====

Esau’s descendant and a chief of Edom ([Genesis 36:40](https://ref.ly/Gen36:40)). Alvah is alternately called Aliah in [1 Chronicles 1:51](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:51).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:40; 1CH 1:51
