## Aenon (id: 3411)

AENON
=====

A small town near the Jordan River. Scholars believe it may have been located about 30 miles (48 kilometers) north of the Dead Sea. The Bible mentions that John the Baptist baptized people there ([John 3:23](https://ref.ly/John3:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 3:23
