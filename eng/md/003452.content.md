## Lecah (id: 6711)

LECAH
=====

Either a person, descendant of the Judahite Er, or an otherwise unknown place in Judah settled by Er, depending on one’s understanding of “father” ([1 Chr 4:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:21
