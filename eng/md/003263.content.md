## Karka (id: 6521)

KARKA
=====

Unidentified town marking a part of Judah’s southern boundary ([Jos 15:3](https://ref.ly/Josh15:3)). It was located in the southwest section of Palestine between Kadesh\-barnea and Wadi el\-Arish (Besor Brook).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:3
