## Kareah (id: 6520)

KAREAH
======

Father of Jonathan and Johanan ([2 Kgs 25:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs25:23)). After Jerusalem fell to Nebuchadnezzar’s army, his sons joined Gedaliah at Mizpah ([Jer 40:8–43:5](https://ref.ly/Jer40:8-Jer43:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 25:23; JER 40:8–JER 43:5
