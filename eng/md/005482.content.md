## Tarpel, Tarpelites (id: 8743)

TARPEL, TARPELITES\*
====================

KJV rendering of “officials” in [Ezra 4:9](https://ref.ly/Ezra4:9). Its precise meaning is uncertain but possibly is a Persian title or an ethnic name.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 4:9
