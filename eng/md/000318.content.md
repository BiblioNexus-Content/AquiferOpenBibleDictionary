## Amraphel (id: 3580)

AMRAPHEL
========

The king of Shinar or Babylonia. He helped King Chedorlaomer of Elam stop a revolt of five cities in Palestine ([Genesis 14:1–11](https://ref.ly/Gen14:1-Gen14:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:1–GEN 14:11
