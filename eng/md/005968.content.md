## Zibiah (id: 9212)

ZIBIAH
======

The mother of King Jehoash of Judah. She was from the town of Beersheba ([2 Kings 12:1](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:1); [2 Chronicles 24:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:1; 2CH 24:1
