## Pedahel (id: 7623)

PEDAHEL
=======

Pedahel was the son of Ammihud. He came from the tribe of Naphtali. Pedahel was chosen to work together with Joshua and Eleazar (the high priest) to help divide the land of Canaan west of the Jordan River among the tribes of Israel ([Numbers 34:28](https://ref.ly/Num34:28)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:28
