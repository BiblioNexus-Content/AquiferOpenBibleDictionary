## Sallai (id: 8106)

SALLAI
======

1\. One of 928 Benjaminites who lived in the city of Jerusalem during the postexilic period ([Neh 11:8](https://ref.ly/Neh11:8)).

2\. Levitical household in the postexilic period during the days of Joiakim, the high priest ([Neh 12:20](https://ref.ly/Neh12:20); nlt “Sallu”); perhaps the same as Sallu in [Nehemiah 12:7](https://ref.ly/Neh12:7). *See* Sallu \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:8; NEH 12:7; NEH 12:20
