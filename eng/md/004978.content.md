## Serug (id: 8242)

SERUG
=====

Reu’s son from the family line of Shem ([Genesis 11:20](https://ref.ly/Gen11:20-Gen11:23)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen11:20-Gen11:23)[23](https://ref.ly/Gen11:20-Gen11:23)). He is a forefather of Abraham and an ancestor of Jesus Christ ([Luke 3:35](https://ref.ly/Luke3:35); “Saruch” in the King James Version). 

*See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 3:35; GEN 11:20–GEN 11:23
