## Tatian (id: 8750)

TATIAN
======

Tatian was a person who defended Christian beliefs (an apologist) but later taught ideas that went against traditional Christian teachings (a heretic). He was the creator of the *Diatessaron* (a book that combined the four gospels into one story). *See* Bible, Versions of the (Ancient).
