## Sisera (id: 8530)

SISERA
======

1\. Commander of the army of Jabin, king of Canaan. Sisera resided in Harosheth\-haggoyim, from where he attacked northern Israel for 20 years. His army, strengthened by 900 iron chariots, was routed at the swollen river of Kishon near Megiddo under the leadership of Barak and Deborah. Having fled the battlefield, Sisera was killed by the hand of Jael, the wife of Heber the Kenite, in the hill country overlooking the Jordan Valley ([Jgs 4](https://ref.ly/Judg4:1-Judg4:24); [1 Sm 12:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam12:9)). The events of this battle were remembered in the Song of Deborah ([Jgs 5:19–30](https://ref.ly/Judg5:19-Judg5:30)) and [Psalm 83:9](https://ref.ly/Ps83:9).

*See also* Judges, Book of.

2\. Forefather of a family of temple servants who returned with Zerubbabel to Palestine following the Babylonian captivity ([Ezr 2:53](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:53); [Neh 7:55](https://ref.ly/Neh7:55)).
