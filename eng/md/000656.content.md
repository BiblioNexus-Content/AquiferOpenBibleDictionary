## Azzur (id: 3926)

AZZUR
=====

1. A political leader who signed Ezra’s promise of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile to Babylon ([Nehemiah 10:17](https://ref.ly/Neh10:17)).
2. The father of the false prophet Hananiah ([Jeremiah 28:1](https://ref.ly/Jer28:1)).
3. The father of Jaazaniah, one of the important men from Jerusalem whom Ezekiel saw in a vision ([Ezekiel 11:1](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 10:17; JER 28:1; EZK 11:1
