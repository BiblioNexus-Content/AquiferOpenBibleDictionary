## Shalisha, Shalishah (id: 8264)

SHALISHA\*, SHALISHAH
=====================

One of the regions (mentioned between the hill country of Ephraim and the district of Shaalim) through which Saul traveled in search of his father’s lost donkeys ([1 Sm 9:4](https://ref.ly/1Sam9:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 9:4
