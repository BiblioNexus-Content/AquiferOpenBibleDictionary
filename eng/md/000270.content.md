## Al-Taschith (id: 3530)

AL\-TASCHITH
============

A Hebrew phrase in the titles of [Psalms 57](https://ref.ly/Ps57:1-Ps57:11), [58](https://ref.ly/Ps58:1-Ps58:11), [59,](https://ref.ly/Ps59:1-Ps59:17) and [75](https://ref.ly/Ps75:1-Ps75:10). It is translated as “Do Not Destroy!” It might be a familiar ancient melody to which the psalms were sung.

*See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** PSA 57:1–PSA 57:11; PSA 58:1–PSA 58:11; PSA 59:1–PSA 59:17; PSA 75:1–PSA 75:10
