## Mijamin (id: 7128)

MIJAMIN
=======

1\. Priest who ministered during the time of David ([1 Chr 24:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:9)).

2\. Parosh’s son, who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife during the postexilic period ([Ezr 10:25](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:25)).

3\. One of the priests who signed Ezra’s covenant during the postexilic period ([Neh 10:7](https://ref.ly/Neh10:7)).

4\. Priest who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Neh 12:5](https://ref.ly/Neh12:5), nlt “Miniamin”).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 24:9; EZR 10:25; NEH 10:7; NEH 12:5
