## Darkon (id: 4759)

DARKON
======

Ancestor of a group of people who returned to Judah with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Ezr 2:56](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:56); [Neh 7:58](https://ref.ly/Neh7:58)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:56; NEH 7:58
