## Snare (id: 8550)

SNARE
=====

Literally, a trap used for entangling birds or others mammals; figuratively, anything that entangles or impedes another person. The word is often used in the Scriptures in the figurative sense to describe anything that ensnares people in sin (see [Dt 7:25](https://ref.ly/Deut7:25); [Eccl 7:26](https://ref.ly/Eccl7:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 7:25; ECC 7:26
