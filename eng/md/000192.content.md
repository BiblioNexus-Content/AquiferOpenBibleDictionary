## Ahinadab (id: 3451)

AHINADAB
========

Iddo’s son and one of 12 officers in charge of bringing food to King Solomon’s house. Ahinadab’s home was in Mahanaim ([1 Kings 4:14](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:14
