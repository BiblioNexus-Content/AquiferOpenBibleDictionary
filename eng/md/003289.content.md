## Kenaniah (id: 6544)

KENANIAH
========

1\. Levite chief who led processional singing when King David brought the ark of the covenant to the new tabernacle in Jerusalem ([1 Chr 15:1–3, 22, 27](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:1-1Chr15:3,1Chr15:22,1Chr15:27)).

2\. Public administrator during David’s reign. His sons also served as public officials ([1 Chr 26:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 15:22; 1CH 15:27; 1CH 26:29; 1CH 15:1–1CH 15:3
