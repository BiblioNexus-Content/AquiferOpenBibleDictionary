## Shoshannim, Shoshannim-Eduth (id: 8449)

SHOSHANNIM\*, SHOSHANNIM\-EDUTH\*
=================================

Hebrew word and phrase in the superscriptions of [Psalms 45](https://ref.ly/Ps45:1-Ps45:17), [69](https://ref.ly/Ps69:1-Ps69:36) and [80](https://ref.ly/Ps80:1-Ps80:19) (kjv), translated “according to Lilies” (rsv; see also nlt); perhaps a familiar ancient melody to which the psalms were performed. *See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** PSA 80:1–PSA 80:19; PSA 45:1–PSA 45:17; PSA 69:1–PSA 69:36
* **Associated Articles:** Music (ID: `7248@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
