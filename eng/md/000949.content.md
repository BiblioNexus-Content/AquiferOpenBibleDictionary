## Bezaleel, Bezalel (id: 4220)

BEZALEEL, BEZALEL
=================

1. Uri’s son and the master craftsman from the tribe of Judah. God gave him special skills to be in charge of the construction and furnishing of the tabernacle ([Exodus 31:2](https://ref.ly/Exod31:2); [35:30](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30-Exod35:31)[–](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30-Exod35:31)[31](https://ref.ly/Exod35:30-Exod35:31); [36:1](https://ref.ly/Exod36:1-Exod36:2)[–](https://ref.ly/Exod36:1-Exod36:2)[2](https://ref.ly/Exod36:1-Exod36:2); [37:1](https://ref.ly/Exod37:1); [38:22](https://ref.ly/Exod38:22); [1 Chronicles 2:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:20); [2 Chronicles 1:5](https://ref.ly/2Chr1:5)) In the King James Version, his name is Bezaleel.
2. Pahath\-moab’s son, who obeyed Ezra’s command to divorce his non\-Jewish wife after the exile in Babylon ([Ezra 10:30](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:30)). In the King James Version, his name is Bezaleel.
