## Elasah (id: 4966)

ELASAH
======

1\. Priest of Pashhur’s clan who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his pagan wife after the exile ([Ezr 10:22](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:22)).

2\. Shaphan’s son and King Zedekiah’s envoy to King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon. On his trip to Babylon, Elasah also carried a letter of encouragement from the prophet Jeremiah to the Jewish exiles there ([Jer 29:3](https://ref.ly/Jer29:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:22; JER 29:3
