## Sin (Place) (id: 8520)

SIN (Place)
===========

Sin is the Hebrew name for the Egyptian city of Pelusium. This city is mentioned in [Ezekiel 30:15–16](https://ref.ly/Ezek30:15-Ezek30:16).
