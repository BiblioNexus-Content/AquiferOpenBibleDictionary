## Zuar (id: 9264)

ZUAR
====

The father of Nethanel. Nethanel was the leader of the tribe of Issachar when the Israelites began their journey through the wilderness after leaving Egypt ([Numbers 1:8](https://ref.ly/Num1:8); [2:5](https://ref.ly/Num2:5); [7:18, 23](https://ref.ly/Num7:18,Num7:23); [10:15](https://ref.ly/Num10:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:8; NUM 2:5; NUM 7:18; NUM 7:23; NUM 10:15
