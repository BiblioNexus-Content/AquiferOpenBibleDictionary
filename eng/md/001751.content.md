## Elidad (id: 5002)

ELIDAD
======

A man from the tribe of Benjamin. He was the son of Kislon. Elidad was chosen to work under the leadership of Eleazar (the high priest) and Joshua. Their task was to divide the land of Canaan west of the Jordan River among the ten tribes of Israel ([Numbers 34:21](https://ref.ly/Num34:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:21
