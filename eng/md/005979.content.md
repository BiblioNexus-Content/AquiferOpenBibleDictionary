## Zilpah (id: 9224)

ZILPAH
======

Mother of Jacob’s sons Gad and Asher. Laban had given her to his daughter Leah as a handmaid ([Gn 29:24](https://ref.ly/Gen29:24); [46:18](https://ref.ly/Gen46:18)). Later, at Leah’s insistence, she became Jacob’s concubine for the purpose of bearing sons ([30:9](https://ref.ly/Gen30:9); [37:2](https://ref.ly/Gen37:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 29:24; GEN 30:9; GEN 37:2; GEN 46:18
