## Geron (id: 5429)

GERON\*
=======

Athenian senator who compelled the Jews to forsake the laws of their fathers and their God during the time of Antiochus IV Epiphanes ([2 Macc 6:1](https://ref.ly/2Macc6:1), rsv mg).

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 6:1
