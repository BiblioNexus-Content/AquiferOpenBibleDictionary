## Rehob (Person) (id: 7984)

REHOB (Person)
==============

1. The King of Zobah. King David defeated his son, Hadadezer, at the Euphrates River ([2 Samuel 8:3, 12](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:3,2Sam8:12)).
2. One of the Levites who signed his name on Ezra's covenant agreement ([Nehemiah 10:11](https://ref.ly/Neh10:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:3; 2SA 8:12; NEH 10:11
