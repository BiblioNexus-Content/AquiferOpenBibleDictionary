## Sheep Gate (id: 8327)

SHEEP GATE
==========

Jerusalem gate repaired by Eliashib and the priests under Nehemiah’s supervision during the postexilic era ([Neh 3:1, 32](https://ref.ly/Neh3:1,Neh3:32); [12:39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:39)). It was positioned east of the Fish Gate by the Tower of the Hundred, near the pool of Bethesda (Bethsaida) and a short distance from the modern St Stephen’s Gate. In [John 5:2](https://ref.ly/John5:2)the kjv translates it “Sheep Market” and the neb, “Sheep\-Pool.”

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:1; NEH 3:32; NEH 12:39; JHN 5:2
