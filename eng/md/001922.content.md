## Euergetes (id: 5180)

EUERGETES\*
===========

Title of honor belonging to two Ptolemaic rulers. From the Greek word meaning “Benefactor,” the title was ascribed to rulers known for their benevolent actions. *See* Ptolemaic Empire.
