## Miniamin (id: 7146)

MINIAMIN
========

1\. Levite who assisted Kore, the son of Imnah, with the distribution of the “contribution reserved for the Lord” among the priests in the cities of Judah ([2 Chr 31:14–15](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:14-2Chr31:15)).

2\. Head of a priestly house during the postexilic era ([Neh 12:17](https://ref.ly/Neh12:17)). He was also called “Mijamin” ([12:5](https://ref.ly/Neh12:5)).

3\. Participant in the dedication of the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 12:41](https://ref.ly/Neh12:41)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:5; NEH 12:17; NEH 12:41; 2CH 31:14–2CH 31:15
