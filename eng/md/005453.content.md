## Tahath (Place) (id: 8714)

TAHATH (Place)
==============

A temporary camping place for the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings. It was located between Makheloth and Terah ([Numbers 33:26–27](https://ref.ly/Num33:26-Num33:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:26–NUM 33:27
