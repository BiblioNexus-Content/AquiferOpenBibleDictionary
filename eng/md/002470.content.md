## Hazaiah (id: 5725)

HAZAIAH
=======

Maaseiah’s descendant from Judah’s tribe, who was one of the leaders in Jerusalem after the exile ([Neh 11:5](https://ref.ly/Neh11:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:5
