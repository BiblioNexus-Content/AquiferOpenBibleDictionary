## Shoa (id: 8437)

SHOA
====

Assyrian people listed with the Babylonians, Chaldeans, and other Assyrian tribes, who were used by the Lord to punish Judah ([Ez 23:23](https://ref.ly/Ezek23:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 23:23
