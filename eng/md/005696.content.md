## Twin Brothers (id: 8946)

TWIN BROTHERS
=============

The twin sons of Zeus in Greek mythology, also known as Castor and Pollux or the Dioscuri. Paul sailed from Malta to Rome on a ship that had these twin brothers as its figurehead ([Acts 28:11](https://ref.ly/Acts28:11)).
