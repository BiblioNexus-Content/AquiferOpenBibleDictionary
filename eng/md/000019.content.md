## Abel-beth-maacah (Maachah) (id: 3276)

ABEL\-BETH\-MAACAH (MAACHAH)
============================

Another name for Abel, a fortified city in upper Galilee in [1 Kings 15:20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs15:20) and [2 Kings 15:29](https://ref.ly/2Kgs15:29). *See* Abel (Place).
