## Shuppim (id: 8466)

SHUPPIM
=======

1\. Son of Ir and a great\-grandson of Benjamin ([1 Chr 7:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12)). Shuppim is perhaps a shortened form of Shephupham (Shupham), mentioned in [Numbers 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39) as the son of Benjamin. It could also be an alternate spelling for Muppim ([Gen 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)). *See* Shephupham.

2\. Levite gatekeeper, who, with Hosah, watched the gate of Shallecheth on the western side of Jerusalem ([1 Chr 26:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:39; 1CH 7:12; 1CH 26:16
* **Associated Articles:** Shephupham (ID: `8370@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
