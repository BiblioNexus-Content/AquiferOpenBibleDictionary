## Mahalath Leannoth (id: 6864)

MAHALATH LEANNOTH\*
===================

Hebrew phrase in the title of [Psalm 88](https://ref.ly/Ps88:1-Ps88:18), translated “The Suffering of Affliction”; perhaps a familiar ancient melody to which the psalm was performed. *See* Music.
