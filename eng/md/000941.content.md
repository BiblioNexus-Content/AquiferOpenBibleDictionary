## Beth-Zatha (id: 4212)

BETH\-ZATHA
===========

A variant name for the pool in Jerusalem. The name is generally believed to mean “house of olives,” occurring only in [John 5:2](https://ref.ly/John5:2). In many translations, this variant is written in the margin for Bethesda. 

*ee* Bethesda.

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 5:2
