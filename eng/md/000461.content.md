## Areli, Arelite (id: 3726)

ARELI, ARELITE
==============

One of Gad’s seven sons ([Genesis 46:16](https://ref.ly/Gen46:16)). After the plague of Baal\-peor, Moses took a census to prepare for war with the Midianites ([Numbers 25:6–18](https://ref.ly/Num25:6-Num25:18); [26:17](https://ref.ly/Num26:17)). Areli’s descendants, called the Arelites, were counted in the census.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:16; NUM 26:17; NUM 25:6–NUM 25:18
