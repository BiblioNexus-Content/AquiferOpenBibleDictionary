## Tanhumeth (id: 8732)

TANHUMETH
=========

Seraiah’s father from the town of Netophah in Judah. Seraiah was the captain of an army of Netophathite men, who served under Gedaliah during the Babylonian suzerainty ([2 Kgs 25:23](https://ref.ly/2Kgs25:23); [Jer 40:8](https://ref.ly/Jer40:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 25:23; JER 40:8
