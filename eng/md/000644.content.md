## Azmaveth (Person) (id: 3914)

AZMAVETH (Person)
=================

1. A warrior among David’s mighty men, known as “the thirty.” His hometown was Bahurim ([2 Samuel 23:31](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:31); [1 Chronicles 11:33](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:33)).
2. Jehoaddah’s son. He was a descendant of King Saul through Jonathan ([1 Chronicles 8:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:36); compare [1 Chronicles 9:42](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:42), when he is called “Jadah’s son”).
3. The father of Jeziel and Pelet from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 12:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:3)). He may be the same as \#1 above.
4. Adiel’s son. King David put him in charge of the palace money ([1 Chronicles 27:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:31; 1CH 8:36; 1CH 9:42; 1CH 11:33; 1CH 12:3; 1CH 27:25
