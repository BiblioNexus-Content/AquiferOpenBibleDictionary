## Zimmah (id: 9226)

ZIMMAH
======

Gershonite Levite and ancestor of Joah ([1 Chr 6:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:20)); possibly the same Joah who assisted Hezekiah ([2 Chr 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:20; 2CH 29:12
