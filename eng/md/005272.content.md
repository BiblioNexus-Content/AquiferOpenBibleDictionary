## Sivan (id: 8536)

SIVAN
=====

Sivan is the name of a month in the Jewish calendar. The word likely came from the Babylonian language ([Esther 8:9](https://ref.ly/Esth8:9)). This month usually occurs during parts of May and June in the modern calendar.

*See* Calendars, Ancient and Modern.
