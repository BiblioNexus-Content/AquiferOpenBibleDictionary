## Bakbukiah (id: 3971)

BAKBUKIAH
=========

1. Shammua’s son. He was a Levite who helped Mattaniah at the thanksgiving services in the temple ([Nehemiah 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17)).
2. A Levite who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 12:9](https://ref.ly/Neh12:9)).
3. One of the gatekeepers who was in charge of storage rooms at the temple gates ([Nehemiah 12:25](https://ref.ly/Neh12:25)).

It is not clear whether these references are to one, two, or three persons.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 11:17; NEH 12:9; NEH 12:25
