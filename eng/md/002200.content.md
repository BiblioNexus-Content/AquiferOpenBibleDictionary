## Giddel (id: 5456)

GIDDEL
======

1\. Ancestor of a group of temple assistants who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Ezr 2:47](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:47); [Neh 7:49](https://ref.ly/Neh7:49)).

2\. Ancestor of a group of King Solomon’s servants who returned with Zerubbabel after the Babylonian exile ([Ezr 2:56](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:56); [Neh 7:58](https://ref.ly/Neh7:58)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:47; EZR 2:56; NEH 7:49; NEH 7:58
