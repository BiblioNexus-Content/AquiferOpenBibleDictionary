## Giddalti (id: 5455)

GIDDALTI
========

A son of Heman who served as a temple singer. King David appointed Giddalti to serve under the direction of his father ([1 Chronicles 25:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4)). Giddalti was in charge of the 22nd of the 24 divisions of temple service ([1 Chronicles 25:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:29
