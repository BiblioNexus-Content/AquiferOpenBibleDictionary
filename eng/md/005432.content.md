## Tabbath (id: 8692)

TABBATH
=======

Town on the outskirts of Abel\-meholah on the east side of the Jordan River in the hill country of Gilead, to which Gideon and his small army chased the fleeing Midianites ([Jgs 7:22](https://ref.ly/Judg7:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 7:22
