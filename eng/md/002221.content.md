## Gishpa, Gispa (id: 5478)

GISHPA, GISPA\*
===============

Overseer of the temple servants in Nehemiah’s time ([Neh 11:21](https://ref.ly/Neh11:21), kjv “Gispa”); perhaps alternately called Hasupha in [Ezra 2:43](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:43) and [Nehemiah 7:46](https://ref.ly/Neh7:46). *See* Hasupha.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:43; NEH 7:46; NEH 11:21
* **Associated Articles:** Hasupha (ID: `5709@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
