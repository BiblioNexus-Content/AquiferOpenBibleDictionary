## Anaiah (id: 3587)

ANAIAH
======

1. A priest and assistant of Ezra who explained to the people passages from the law read by Ezra ([Nehemiah 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).
2. A political leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile to Babylon ([Nehemiah 10:22](https://ref.ly/Neh10:22)). He may be the same person as \#1 above.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 8:4; NEH 10:22
