## Beth-Eked (id: 4168)

BETH\-EKED
==========

The place between Jezreel and Samaria where Jehu killed Ahaziah’s family at a pit or cistern ([2 Kings 10:12, 14](https://ref.ly/2Kgs10:12,2Kgs10:14), King James Version translates it “shearing house”).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 10:12; 2KI 10:14
