## Ephrath (Place) (id: 5122)

EPHRATH (Place)
===============

1\. Town in the Judean hill country later named Bethlehem. It was on the road to Ephrath that Rachel died while giving birth to Benjamin ([Gn 35:16–19](https://ref.ly/Gen35:16-Gen35:19)). This town was the home of Naomi’s family, who identified themselves as Ephrathites ([Ru 1:2](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:2)). Ephrath was the dwelling place of Ruth and Boaz ([Ru 4:11](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:11)), the childhood home of David ([1 Sm 17:12](https://ref.ly/1Sam17:12)), and the announced birthplace of the Messiah ([Mi 5:2](https://ref.ly/Mic5:2)).

*See also* Bethlehem \#1.

2\. District in which the city of Kiriath\-jearim was situated and where the ark of the covenant was kept ([Ps 132:6](https://ref.ly/Ps132:6)).
