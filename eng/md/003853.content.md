## Michaiah (id: 7111)

MICHAIAH\*
==========

1\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, Acbor’s father, in [2 Kings 22:12](https://ref.ly/2Kgs22:12). *See* Micaiah \#2.

2\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, the mother of Judah’s king Abijah, in [2 Chronicles 13:2](https://ref.ly/2Chr13:2). *See* Maacah, Maachah (Person) \#4.

3\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, one of King Jehoshaphat’s officials, in [2 Chronicles 17:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:7). *See* Micaiah \#4.

4\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, an alternate name for Mica, Zicri’s son, in [Nehemiah 12:35](https://ref.ly/Neh12:35). *See* Mica \#2.

5\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, a postexilic priest, in [Nehemiah 12:41](https://ref.ly/Neh12:41). *See* Micaiah \#6.

6\. KJV spelling of Micaiah, Gemariah’s son, in [Jeremiah 36:11–13](https://ref.ly/Jer36:11-Jer36:13). *See* Micaiah \#7.
