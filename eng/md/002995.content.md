## Jeberechiah, Jeberekiah (id: 6249)

JEBERECHIAH\*, JEBEREKIAH
=========================

Father of Zechariah the scribe. Zechariah, with Uriah the priest, witnessed Isaiah’s prophecy of the Assyrian conquest of Israel ([Is 8:2](https://ref.ly/Isa8:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 8:2
