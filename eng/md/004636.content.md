## Rabsaris (id: 7898)

RABSARIS
========

The title of a high\-ranking Assyrian and Babylonian court official, usually a eunuch and sometimes supervisor of the royal harem. A rabsaris was:

* part of the Assyrian delegation ([2 Kings 18:17](https://ref.ly/2Kgs18:17)),
* a judge at the gate ([Jeremiah 39:3](https://ref.ly/Jer39:3)),
* the official who released Jeremiah from prison (verse [13](https://ref.ly/Jer39:13)), and
* one of Nebuchadnezzar’s officials ([Daniel 1:3, 7](https://ref.ly/Dan1:3,Dan1:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 18:17; JER 39:3; JER 39:13; DAN 1:3; DAN 1:7
