## Jerimoth (id: 6324)

JERIMOTH
========

1\. One of Bela’s five sons and a leader in the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chr 7:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:7)).

2\. Alternate spelling of Jeremoth, Beker’s son, in [1 Chronicles 7:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:8). *See* Jeremoth \#1.

3\. Benjamite and one of the ambidextrous warriors who came to David’s support at Ziklag ([1 Chr 12:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:5)).

4\. Alternate spelling of Jeremoth, Mushi’s son, in [1 Chronicles 23:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:23) and [24:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:30). *See* Jeremoth \#3.

5\. Alternate spelling of Jeremoth, Heman’s son, in [1 Chronicles 25:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4) and [25:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:22). *See* Jeremoth \#4.

6\. Alternate spelling of Jeremoth, Azriel’s son, in [1 Chronicles 27:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:19). *See* Jeremoth \#5.

7\. David’s son and Mahalath’s father. Mahalath was married to King Rehoboam of Judah ([2 Chr 11:18](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:18)).

8\. One of the Levites who assisted with the administration of the temple contributions during King Hezekiah’s reign ([2 Chr 31:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr31:13)).
