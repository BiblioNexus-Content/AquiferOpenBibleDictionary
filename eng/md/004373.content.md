## Pelatiah (id: 7631)

PELATIAH
========

1\. Hananiah’s son, in a list of King Solomon’s descendants ([1 Chr 3:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:21)).

2\. Military leader among the Simeonites who helped destroy an Amalekite remnant at Mt Seir during Hezekiah’s reign ([1 Chr 4:42](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:42)).

3\. Political leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:22](https://ref.ly/Neh10:22)).

4\. Benaiah’s son and one of the two princes seen by Ezekiel in a vision of judgment, identified by the Spirit of the Lord as one who devises wickedness and gives wicked counsel in the city ([Ez 11:1–2, 13](https://ref.ly/Ezek11:1-Ezek11:2,Ezek11:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:21; 1CH 4:42; NEH 10:22; EZK 11:13; EZK 11:1–EZK 11:2
