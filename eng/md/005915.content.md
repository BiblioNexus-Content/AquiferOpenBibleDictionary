## Zeboim (id: 9160)

ZEBOIM
======

1\. KJV spelling of Zeboiim in [Genesis 10:19](https://ref.ly/Gen10:19), [Deuteronomy 29:23](https://ref.ly/Deut29:23), and [Hosea 11:8](https://ref.ly/Hos11:8). *See* Zeboiim.

2\. Valley where one of the raiding parties of the Philistines turned toward the border of the wilderness ([1 Sm 13:18](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:18)). It may be identified with Shuk ed\-Dubba.

3\. One of the villages outside of Jerusalem where the Benjaminites settled after the exile ([Neh 11:34](https://ref.ly/Neh11:34)).
