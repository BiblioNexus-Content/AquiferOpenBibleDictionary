## Hannathon (id: 5649)

HANNATHON
=========

Northern border town of Zebulun ([Jos 19:14](https://ref.ly/Josh19:14)), mentioned in the Amarna tablets (c. 1370 BC) and in the annals of Tiglath\-pileser III (745–727 BC). Not yet precisely located, it has been identified with Kefr ‘Anau near Rimmon and with Tell el\-Bedeiwiyah, north of Nazareth.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:14
