## Elymais (id: 5059)

ELYMAIS
=======

Formerly thought to be a city in Persia, greatly renowned for riches, silver, and gold ([1 Macc 6:1](https://ref.ly/1Macc6:1)), but now generally regarded as a district of Persia. Josephus (*Antiquities* 12\.9\.1\), following 1 Maccabees, alludes to it as a city, but it is otherwise unknown.

* **Associated Passages:** 1MA 6:1
