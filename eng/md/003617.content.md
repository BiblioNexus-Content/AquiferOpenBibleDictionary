## Mahli (id: 6877)

MAHLI
=====

1. Merari’s son and Levi’s grandson ([Exodus 6:19](https://ref.ly/Exod6:19); [Numbers 3:20](https://ref.ly/Num3:20); [1 Chronicles 6:19, 29](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:19,1Chr6:29); [23:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:21); [24:26–28](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:26-1Chr24:28); [Ezra 8:18](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:18)). The founder of the Mahlite family ([Numbers 3:33](https://ref.ly/Num3:33); [26:58](https://ref.ly/Num26:58)). The Mahlites, along with the other families of Merari, were chosen to carry the frames of the tabernacle and the pillars of the court ([Numbers 4:29–33](https://ref.ly/Num4:29-Num4:33)).
2. Mushi’s son and the nephew of \#1 above ([1 Chronicles 6:47](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:47); [23:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:23); [24:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:30)).
