## Zetham (id: 9205)

ZETHAM
======

One of Ladan’s descendants, a Gershonite, in charge of the temple treasuries ([1 Chr 23:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:8); [26:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 23:8; 1CH 26:22
