## Reu (id: 8018)

REU
===

Peleg’s son, the father of Serug. He was a descendant of Shem ([Genesis 11:18](https://ref.ly/Gen11:18-Gen11:21)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen11:18-Gen11:21)[21](https://ref.ly/Gen11:18-Gen11:21); [1 Chronicles 1:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:25)). He is listed as one of Jesus's ancestors in [Luke 3:35](https://ref.ly/Luke3:35). 

*See* Ancestry of Jesus Christ.
