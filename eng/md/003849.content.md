## Micha (id: 7113)

MICHA
=====

1. The King James Version spelling of Mica. This Mica was a son of Mephibosheth ([2 Samuel 9:12](https://ref.ly/2Sam9:12)).

    *See* Mica \#1.

2. The King James Version spelling of Micah. This Micah was a son of Uzziel ([1 Chronicles 23:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:20)).

    *See* Micah (Person) \#5.

3. The King James Version spelling of Mica. This Mica was a Levite ([Nehemiah 10:11](https://ref.ly/Neh10:11)).

    *See* Mica \#3.

4. The King James Version spelling of Mica. This Mica was the father of Mattaniah ([Nehemiah 11:17, 22](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17,Neh11:22)).

    *See* Mica \#2.
