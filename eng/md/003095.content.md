## Jesui, Jesuite (id: 6351)

JESUI, JESUITE
==============

Jesui and Jesuite are names used in the King James Version of the Bible. These names refer to Ishvi and Ishvite, who were one of Asher's descendants and his family ([Numbers 26:44](https://ref.ly/Num26:44)). 

*See* Ishvi \#1.
