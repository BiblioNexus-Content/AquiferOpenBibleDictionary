## Peruda (id: 7676)

PERUDA
======

Head of a family of Solomon’s servants ([Ezr 2:55](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:55)); alternately called Perida in [Nehemiah 7:57](https://ref.ly/Neh7:57). His descendants formed part of the remnant of Israel that returned to Jerusalem after the exile ([1 Esd 5:33](https://ref.ly/1Esd5:33)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:55; NEH 7:57; 1ES 5:33
