## Zerahite (id: 9189)

ZERAHITE
========

1. Any descendant of Zerah, son of Simeon ([Numbers 26:13](https://ref.ly/Num26:13)). *See* Zerah \#3.
2. Any descendant of Zerah, son of the Judah by Tamar ([Numbers 26:20](https://ref.ly/Num26:20)). *See* Zerah \#2.

*See also* Izrahite.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:13; NUM 26:20
