## Apocalypse (id: 3661)

APOCALYPSE
==========

A term which means a “revelation” or a “disclosure.” An apocalypse is a type of writing that shows or tells about hidden things. The books of Daniel and Revelation are the two apocalypses in the Bible. *See* Apocalyptic; Daniel, Book of; Revelation, Book of.
