## Hukkok (id: 5936)

HUKKOK
======

Town near Naphtali and Zebulun’s boundary, listed next to Aznoth\-tabor ([Jos 19:34](https://ref.ly/Josh19:34)). It has been identified with Yaquaq, northwest of Gennesaret.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:34
