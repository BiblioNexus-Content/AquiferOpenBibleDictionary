## Debir (Person) (id: 4784)

DEBIR (Person)
==============

One of the kings of Eglon who became an ally of Adoni\-zedek, the king of Jerusalem. Debir was executed by Joshua ([Jos 10:22–27](https://ref.ly/Josh10:22-Josh10:27)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 10:22–JOS 10:27
