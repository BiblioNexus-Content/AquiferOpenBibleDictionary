## Heber (id: 5757)

HEBER
=====

1. A descendant of Jacob through Asher and Beriah ([Genesis 46:17](https://ref.ly/Gen46:17)) and father of the family of Heberites ([Numbers 26:45](https://ref.ly/Num26:45); [1 Chronicles 7:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:31-1Chr7:32)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:31-1Chr7:32)[32](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:31-1Chr7:32)).
2. Husband of Jael, the woman who sneakily killed Sisera, known as Heber the Kenite ([Judges 4:11](https://ref.ly/Judg4:11-Judg4:21)[–](https://ref.ly/Judg4:11-Judg4:21)[21](https://ref.ly/Judg4:11-Judg4:21); [5:24](https://ref.ly/Judg5:24)).
3. Judahite, Mered’s son and the father of Soco ([1 Chronicles 4:18](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:18)).
4. Elpaal’s son from Judah’s tribe ([1 Chronicles 8:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:17)).
5. The King James Version spelling for Eber in [1 Chronicles 5:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:13); [8:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:22); and [Luke 3:35](https://ref.ly/Luke3:35). *See* Eber \#1, \#2, \#4.
