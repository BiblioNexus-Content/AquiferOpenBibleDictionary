## Jehoiarib (id: 6279)

JEHOIARIB
=========

1\. Alternate form of Joiarib, a priestly family in Jerusalem, in [1 Chronicles 9:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:10). *See* Joiarib \#1.

2\. Priest in the time of King David, assigned to head the first of 24 divisions of priests for annual temple duty ([1 Chr 24:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:7)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:10; 1CH 24:7
