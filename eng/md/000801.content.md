## Beeroth Bene-Jaakan (id: 4069)

BEEROTH BENE\-JAAKAN
====================

The site where the Israelites camped during their wilderness journey from Egypt. It was near Moserah or Moseroth ([Deuteronomy 10:6](https://ref.ly/Deut10:6)). It is also called Bene\-jaakan in [Numbers 33:31](https://ref.ly/Num33:31-Num33:32)[–](https://ref.ly/Num33:31-Num33:32)[32](https://ref.ly/Num33:31-Num33:32). Beeroth Bene\-jaakan means “well of Jaakan’s sons.” 

*See* Wilderness Wanderings.

* **Associated Passages:** DEU 10:6; NUM 33:31–NUM 33:32
