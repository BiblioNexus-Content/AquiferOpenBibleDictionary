## Jarib (id: 6225)

JARIB
=====

1\. Alternate name for Jachin, Simeon’s son, in [1 Chronicles 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24). *See* Jachin \#1.

2\. Man who assisted Ezra in securing temple servants before the return to Palestine from exile ([Ezr 8:16](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:16)).

3\. From Jeshua’s family, a priest who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his pagan wife after the exile ([Ezr 10:18](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:24; EZR 8:16; EZR 10:18
