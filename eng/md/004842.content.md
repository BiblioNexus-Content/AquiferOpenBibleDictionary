## Sallu (id: 8107)

SALLU
=====

1\. Son of Meshullam and a Benjaminite, who resided in the city of Jerusalem during the postexilic period ([1 Chr 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:7); [Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)).

2\. Levitical priest who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel following the Babylonian captivity ([Neh 12:7](https://ref.ly/Neh12:7)). The Sallai mentioned in some translations of [Nehemiah 12:20](https://ref.ly/Neh12:20) is thought to be a variant spelling of Sallu.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:7; NEH 11:7; NEH 12:7; NEH 12:20
