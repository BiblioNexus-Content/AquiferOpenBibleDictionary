## Jabneel (id: 6152)

JABNEEL
=======

1\. Alternate name for the town Jabneh in Judah’s tribe ([Jos 15:11](https://ref.ly/Josh15:11)). *See* Jabneh.

2\. Town of Galilee near Tiberias, on Naphtali’s southern border ([Jos 19:33](https://ref.ly/Josh19:33)). It was located south of modern Jabneel.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:11; JOS 19:33
* **Associated Articles:** Jabneh (ID: `6153@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
