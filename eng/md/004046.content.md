## Naphtali, Mount of (id: 7304)

NAPHTALI\*, Mount of
====================

Hill country comprising the majority of Naphtali’s territory, in which the town of Kedesh was set apart as a city of refuge ([Jos 20:7](https://ref.ly/Josh20:7)).
