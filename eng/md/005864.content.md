## Zaanan (id: 9110)

ZAANAN
======

Village mentioned in Micah’s lament over Jerusalem ([Mi 1:11](https://ref.ly/Mic1:11)). Zaanan is probably a play on the Hebrew word yatsa’*,* “to come forth.” The village was in the Shephelah; it is probably the same place as Zenan.

*See also* Zenan.
