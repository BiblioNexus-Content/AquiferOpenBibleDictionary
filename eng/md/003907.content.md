## Mispar, Mispereth (id: 7167)

MISPAR, MISPERETH\*
===================

One of the men who returned with Zerubbabel to Palestine following the Babylonian captivity ([Ezr 2:2](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:2)); alternately called Mispereth in [Nehemiah 7:7](https://ref.ly/Neh7:7).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:2; NEH 7:7
