## Craftsmen, Valley of (id: 4681)

CRAFTSMEN, Valley of
====================

Place named for a community of craftsmen who lived in a valley on the southern border of the plain of Sharon ([1 Chr 4:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:14); [Neh 11:35](https://ref.ly/Neh11:35)). *See* Ge\-harashim.
