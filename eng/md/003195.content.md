## Joshaphat (id: 6453)

JOSHAPHAT
=========

1\. Mithnite (nlt “from Mithna”) and one of David’s mighty men ([1 Chr 11:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:43)).

2\. One of the seven priests assigned to blow a trumpet before the ark of God in the procession led by David when the ark was brought to Jerusalem ([1 Chr 15:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:24)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 11:43; 1CH 15:24
