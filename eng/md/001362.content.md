## Col-Hozeh (id: 4612)

COL\-HOZEH
==========

The father of Shallum, who was ruler of the district of Mizpah ([Neh 3:15](https://ref.ly/Neh3:15)). Col\-hozeh, the son of Hazaiah in [Nehemiah 11:5](https://ref.ly/Neh11:5), may be another person.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 3:15; NEH 11:5
