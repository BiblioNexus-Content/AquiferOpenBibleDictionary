## Abagtha (id: 3265)

ABAGTHA
=======

One of the seven eunuchs (officers in the king's court) commanded by King Ahasuerus to bring Queen Vashti to his drunken party ([Esther 1:10](https://ref.ly/Esth1:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:10
