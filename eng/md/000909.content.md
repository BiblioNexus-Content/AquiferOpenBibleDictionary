## Beth-Haram, Beth-Haran (id: 4180)

BETH\-HARAM, BETH\-HARAN
========================

A town given to the tribe of Gad at the division of Canaan ([Joshua 13:27](https://ref.ly/Josh13:27)). It was defended and used to house sheep herds ([Numbers 32:36](https://ref.ly/Num32:36)). 

It is probably the same as Beth\-aramphtha, a city mentioned by the Jewish historian Josephus.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 32:36; JOS 13:27
