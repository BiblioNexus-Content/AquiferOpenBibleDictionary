## Ulam (id: 8955)

ULAM
====

1. A clan in the tribe of Manasseh ([1 Chronicles 7:16–17](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:16-1Chr7:17)).
2. Eshek’s firstborn son and a mighty warrior in the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:39–40](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:39-1Chr8:40)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:16–1CH 7:17; 1CH 8:39–1CH 8:40
