## Jashobeam (id: 6231)

JASHOBEAM
=========

1. The son of Zabdiel who was the leader of David's "Three" strongest warriors ([1 Chronicles 11:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:11)). He was also appointed as the chief of a division of 24,000 soldiers who served during the first month of the year ([1 Chronicles 27:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:2)). He is the same person as Josheb\-basshebeth, the Tahkemonite ([2 Samuel 23:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:8)). Jashobeam became famous by killing 300 men ([1 Chronicles 11:11\)](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:11). [2 Samuel 23:8](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:8) numbers it at 800\.
2. A warrior who could fight with both his right and left hands who joined David's army at Ziklag ([1 Chronicles 12:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:8; 1CH 11:11; 1CH 12:6; 1CH 27:2
