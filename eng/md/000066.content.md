## Abubus (id: 3325)

ABUBUS
======

Ptolemy’s father. Ptolemy was a military governor in Jericho. He killed his father\-in\-law, Simon Maccabeus, and two of Simon’s sons in 134 BC ([1 Maccabees 16:11](https://ref.ly/1Macc16:11-1Macc16:17)–[17](https://ref.ly/1Macc16:11-1Macc16:17)). 

*See* Maccabean Period.
