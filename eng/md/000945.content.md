## Betonim (id: 4216)

BETONIM
=======

A city in the territory of the tribe of Gad ([Joshua 13:26](https://ref.ly/Josh13:26)). It has been identified with modern Khirbet Bat\-neh, 25\.7 kilometers (16 miles) northeast of Jericho.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 13:26
