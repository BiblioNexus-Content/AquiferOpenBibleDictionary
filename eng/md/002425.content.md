## Harosheth-Haggoyim (id: 5680)

HAROSHETH\-HAGGOYIM
===================

Town in Canaan that was the home of Sisera. This Canaanite general led his forces from Harosheth against Deborah and Barak ([Jgs 4:2–13](https://ref.ly/Judg4:2-Judg4:13), kjv “Harosheth of the Gentiles”). After his soldiers panicked, they fled back to Harosheth where they were defeated (v [16](https://ref.ly/Judg4:16)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 4:16; JDG 4:2–JDG 4:13
