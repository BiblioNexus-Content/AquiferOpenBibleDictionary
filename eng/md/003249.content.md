## Juttah (id: 6507)

JUTTAH
======

One of the cities of refuge assigned to Aaron’s descendants ([Jos 21:16](https://ref.ly/Josh21:16)). It was in the hill country of Judah’s territory and in the district of Maon ([15:55](https://ref.ly/Josh15:55)). It has been identified with modern Yatta, about five and a half miles (8\.8 kilometers) southwest of Hebron.

*See also* Cities of Refuge.
