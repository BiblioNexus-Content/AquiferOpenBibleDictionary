## Bathshua (id: 4032)

BATHSHUA
========

1. The Canaanite wife of Judah. She bore him three sons: Er, Onan, and Shelah ([Genesis 38:2](https://ref.ly/Gen38:2-Gen38:5)[–](https://ref.ly/Gen38:2-Gen38:5)[5](https://ref.ly/Gen38:2-Gen38:5); [1 Chronicles 2:3](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:3)).
2. An alternate spelling of Bathsheba in [1 Chronicles 3:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:5).
3. *See* Bathsheba.
