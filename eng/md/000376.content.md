## Antiochis (id: 3635)

ANTIOCHIS
=========

A concubine of the Syrian king Antiochus Epiphanes ([2 Maccabees 4:30](https://ref.ly/2Macc4:30)). The cities of Tarsus and Mallus were given to Antiochis as a gift. Their residents revolted in protest.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 4:30
