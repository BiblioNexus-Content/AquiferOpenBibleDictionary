## Orpah (id: 7520)

ORPAH
=====

Woman of Moab who married Chilion ([Ru 1:1–14](https://ref.ly/Ruth1:1-Ruth1:14)), son of Elimelech and Naomi. After her husband and sons died, Naomi decided to return to Judah. Both Orpah and Ruth resolved to go with Naomi, but at Naomi’s urging Orpah remained in her homeland.

*See also* Ruth, Book of.
