## Besor, Brook of (id: 4147)

BESOR, Brook of
===============

A brook, or stream, that David crossed to follow the Amalekites to the south after they had raided Ziklag, his home base. They were exhausted, so 200 of David’s men remained at the brook while the other 400 caught and defeated the enemy ([1 Samuel 30:9](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:9-1Sam30:21)[–](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:9-1Sam30:21)[21](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:9-1Sam30:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 30:9–1SA 30:21
