## Shagee (id: 8260)

SHAGEE
======

A person from Harar and the father of Jonathan ([2 Samuel 23:33](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:33); [1 Chronicles 11:34](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:34)). His son Jonathan was one of King David's mighty men.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:33; 1CH 11:34
