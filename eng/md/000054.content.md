## Abishua (id: 3312)

ABISHUA
=======

1. Aaron’s great\-grandson, son of Phinehas and ancestor of Ezra ([1 Chronicles 6:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:4-1Chr6:5,1Chr6:50)–[5, 50](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:4-1Chr6:5,1Chr6:50); [Ezra 7:5](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:5)). Abishua’s name also appears in the record of Ezra's ancestry in 1 Esdras ([1 Esdras 8:2](https://ref.ly/1Esd8:2); [2 Esdras 1:2](https://ref.ly/2Esd1:2)).
2. Bela’s son, and grandson of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:50; 1CH 8:4; EZR 7:5; 1ES 8:2; 2ES 1:2; 1CH 6:4–1CH 6:5
