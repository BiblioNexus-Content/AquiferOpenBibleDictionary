## Elizaphan (id: 5030)

ELIZAPHAN
=========

1. A Levite man from the Kohath family and the son of Uzziel ([Numbers 3:29–30](https://ref.ly/Num3:29-Num3:30)). Elizaphan helped remove the bodies of Nadab and Abihu from the camp ([Leviticus 10:4](https://ref.ly/Lev10:4)). Elizaphan’s descendants were responsible for caring for the ark, the table, the lampstand, and vessels of the sanctuary (compare [1 Chronicles 15:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:8); [2 Chronicles 29:13](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:13)). His name is also spelled Elzaphan ([Exodus 6:22](https://ref.ly/Exod6:22); [Leviticus 10:4](https://ref.ly/Lev10:4)).
2. Parnach’s son and a leader from Zebulun’s tribe. He helped Eleazar and Joshua divide the Canaanite territory west of the Jordan among the nine\-and\-a\-half tribes ([Numbers 34:25](https://ref.ly/Num34:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:22; LEV 10:4; NUM 34:25; 1CH 15:8; 2CH 29:13; NUM 3:29–NUM 3:30
