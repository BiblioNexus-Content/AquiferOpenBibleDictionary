## Elizur (id: 5031)

ELIZUR
======

The son of Shedeur and leader of the tribe of Reuben when the Israelites began their journey through the wilderness after leaving Egypt ([Numbers 1:5](https://ref.ly/Num1:5); [2:10](https://ref.ly/Num2:10); [7:30–35](https://ref.ly/Num7:30-Num7:35); [10:18](https://ref.ly/Num10:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 1:5; NUM 2:10; NUM 10:18; NUM 7:30–NUM 7:35
