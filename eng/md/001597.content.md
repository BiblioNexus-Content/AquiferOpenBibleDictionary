## Dinaites (id: 4849)

DINAITES\*
==========

Postexilic group involved in a protest to Artaxerxes about the rebuilding of the Jerusalem temple ([Ezr 4:9](https://ref.ly/Ezra4:9), kjv). The name is evidently an Aramaic title for “judge” (rsv); such judges are mentioned in fifth\-century BC Aramaic administrative papyri.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 4:9
