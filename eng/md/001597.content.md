## Dinhabah (id: 4850)

DINHABAH
========

Capital city of Edom before the time of Israel’s monarchy, whose king Bela is mentioned in the Bible ([Gn 36:32](https://ref.ly/Gen36:32); [1 Chr 1:43](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:43)). Its site is unknown.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:32; 1CH 1:43
