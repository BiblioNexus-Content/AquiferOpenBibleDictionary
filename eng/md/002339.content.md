## Haggith (id: 5592)

HAGGITH
=======

One of the wives of King David. She was the mother of Adonijah ([2 Samuel 3:4](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:4); [1 Kings 1:5, 11](https://ref.ly/1Kgs1:5,1Kgs1:11); [2:13](https://ref.ly/1Kgs2:13); [1 Chronicles 3:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:2)). Haggith gave birth to Adonijah in the city of Hebron. This happened while David had his capital city there. In the list of David's wives and sons in 2 Samuel, Haggith and her son Adonijah are mentioned fourth.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 3:4; 1KI 1:5; 1KI 1:11; 1KI 2:13; 1CH 3:2
