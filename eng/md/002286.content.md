## Grief (id: 5540)

GRIEF
=====

The emotional suffering caused by loss, misfortune, or disaster. To grieve means to cause or feel sorrow or distress. 

What Are Examples of Grief in the Bible?
----------------------------------------

The concept of grief appears in the Bible frequently:

* Isaac and Rebekah experienced grief when their son Esau married a Hittite woman ([Genesis 26:35](https://ref.ly/Gen26:35))
* God mourned the pain Israel caused themselves by their disobedience ([Judges 10:16](https://ref.ly/Judg10:16))
* Hannah was sad because she had no son, so much so that she looked drunk while praying ([1 Samuel 1:16](https://ref.ly/1Sam1:16))
* Samuel prayed all night because he was grieved by King Saul's disobedience ([1 Samuel 15:11\)](https://ref.ly/1Sam15:11)
* Job was very sad over his personal loss ([Job 2:13](https://ref.ly/Job2:13); compare [6:2](https://ref.ly/Job6:2); [16:6](https://ref.ly/Job16:6))
* The psalmists expressed distress and sadness ([Psalms 6:7](https://ref.ly/Ps6:7); [31:9](https://ref.ly/Ps31:9-Ps31:10)–[10](https://ref.ly/Ps31:9-Ps31:10); [69:26](https://ref.ly/Ps69:26); [73:21](https://ref.ly/Ps73:21); [95:10](https://ref.ly/Ps95:10); [112:10](https://ref.ly/Ps112:10))
* The book of Lamentations is devoted to the expression of grief
* The prophets speak of a judgment that is caused because Israel grieves God
* Jesus experienced sorrow and distress, including crying over the death of a friend ([Mark 3:5](https://ref.ly/Mark3:5); [John 11:33](https://ref.ly/John11:33); [35](https://ref.ly/John11:35))
* The Jews grieved when the apostles taught about Christ ([Acts 4:2](https://ref.ly/Acts4:2))
* The apostle Paul taught believers not to grieve one another ([Romans 14:15](https://ref.ly/Rom14:15))
* The apostle Paul did not want to cause any sorrow himself ([2 Corinthians 2:1](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:1-2Cor2:5)–[5](https://ref.ly/2Cor2:1-2Cor2:5))
* The believer is not to grieve the Holy Spirit ([Ephesians 4:30](https://ref.ly/Eph4:30))
* A believer may experience grief and suffering in a foreign world ([1 Peter 2:19](https://ref.ly/1Pet2:19))
* Grief was expressed at a time of death through screams, wails, and laments ([Jeremiah 9:17](https://ref.ly/Jer9:17-Jer9:18)–[18](https://ref.ly/Jer9:17-Jer9:18); [Amos 5:16](https://ref.ly/Amos5:16); [Mark 5:38](https://ref.ly/Mark5:38))
