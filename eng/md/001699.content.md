## Eglon (Place) (id: 4952)

EGLON (Place)
=============

Town situated seven miles (11\.3 kilometers) southwest of Lachish, assigned to Judah’s tribe for an inheritance ([Jos 15:39](https://ref.ly/Josh15:39)). It is generally identified with the modern Tell el\-Hesi.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:39
