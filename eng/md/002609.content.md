## Hirah (id: 5859)

HIRAH
=====

Adullamite and friend of Judah to whose house Judah went after he and his brothers sold Joseph ([Gn 38:1](https://ref.ly/Gen38:1)). He accompanied Judah to the sheepshearing after Judah’s wife died (v [12](https://ref.ly/Gen38:12)), and he served as the messenger to carry a kid from Judah to Tamar (v [20](https://ref.ly/Gen38:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 38:1; GEN 38:12; GEN 38:20
