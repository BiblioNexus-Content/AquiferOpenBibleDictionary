## Mamre (Person) (id: 6911)

MAMRE (Person)
==============

Owner of a parcel of land called “the plain of Mamre.” He was an Amorite and is recorded as having two brothers: Aner and Eshcol ([Gn 14:13](https://ref.ly/Gen14:13)). These became confederates of Abraham when he fought to save his nephew Lot.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:13
