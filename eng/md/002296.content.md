## Gum (id: 5549)

GUM\*
=====

General name for tragacanth, used in trade and obtained from the sap of shrubs of the *Astragalus* genus ([Gn 43:11](https://ref.ly/Gen43:11)). These shrubs grew widely in the Near East. Gum from the *Astragalus tragacantha* is still used commercially. *See* Plants (Aloe; Balm; Myrrh).
