## Ezer (id: 5219)

EZER
====

1\. Chieftain of a Horite tribe ([Gn 36:21](https://ref.ly/Gen36:21); [1 Chr 1:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:38)).

2\. Descendant and probably the son of Ephraim. He was killed while making a raid on the cattle of the Philistines ([1 Chr 7:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:21)).

3\. Man of Judah, descended from Hur ([1 Chr 4:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:4)).

4\. Gadite who joined David at Ziklag ([1 Chr 12:9](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:9)).

5\. Jeshua’s son, who ruled Mizpah and repaired the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 3:19](https://ref.ly/Neh3:19)).

6\. Priest who took part in the ceremony at the dedication of the Jerusalem wall ([Neh 12:42](https://ref.ly/Neh12:42)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:21; 1CH 1:38; 1CH 4:4; 1CH 7:21; 1CH 12:9; NEH 3:19; NEH 12:42
