## Ezem (id: 5218)

EZEM
====

City allotted to Judah’s tribe ([Jos 15:29](https://ref.ly/Josh15:29)), then later to Simeon’s tribe, for an inheritance ([Jos 19:3](https://ref.ly/Josh19:3); [1 Chr 4:29](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:29)). [Joshua 15](https://ref.ly/Josh15:1-Josh15:63) locates the city in the extreme southern part of Canaan.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:29; JOS 19:3; 1CH 4:29; JOS 15:1–JOS 15:63
