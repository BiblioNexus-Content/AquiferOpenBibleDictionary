## Resen (id: 8008)

RESEN
=====

City built by Nimrod between Nineveh and Calah ([Gn 10:12](https://ref.ly/Gen10:12)). It was part of the complex known as “the great city” and could have been a suburb of Nineveh. Some interpreters suggest it was a waterwork between Nineveh and Calah.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 10:12
