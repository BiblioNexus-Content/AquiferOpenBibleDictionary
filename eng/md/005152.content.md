## Shimrith (id: 8415)

SHIMRITH\*
==========

Moabitess mother of Jehozabad, a royal servant, who along with Zabad conspired against and murdered King Joash of Judah ([2 Chr 24:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:26), nlt mg). She is alternately called Shomer in [2 Kings 12:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:21); Shimrith is the feminine form of Shomer. *See* Shomer \#1.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:21; 2CH 24:26
