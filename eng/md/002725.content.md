## Ibleam (id: 5975)

IBLEAM
======

City in Manasseh’s territory ([Jos 17:11](https://ref.ly/Josh17:11); [Jgs 1:27](https://ref.ly/Judg1:27); [2 Kgs 9:27](https://ref.ly/2Kgs9:27)), perhaps identifiable with Bileam, a Levitical city west of the Jordan River between Samaria and Jezreel ([1 Chr 6:70](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:70)). *See* Levitical Cities.
