## Aram of Damascus (id: 3699)

ARAM OF DAMASCUS
================

One of the several city\-kingdoms in the land of Aram (also known as Syria). This kingdom's main city was Damascus. Aram of Damascus was conquered by King David ([1 Chronicles 18:3–6](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:3-1Chr18:6)). *See* Damascus.
