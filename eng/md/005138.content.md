## Shimeah (id: 8401)

SHIMEAH
=======

1. Another name for Shammah, brother of King David ([2 Samuel 13:3, 32](https://ref.ly/2Sam13:3,2Sam13:32)).

    *See* Shammah \#2.

2. A son of Mikloth and the grandson of Jeiel from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:32)). In [1 Chronicles 9:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:38), Shimeah is called Shimeam.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 13:3; 2SA 13:32; 1CH 8:32; 1CH 9:38
