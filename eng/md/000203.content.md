## Ahlai (id: 3463)

AHLAI
=====

1. Sheshan’s daughter, a member of the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 2:31, 34](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:31,1Chr2:34)). In [1 Chronicles 2:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:31), Ahlai is sometimes called a son.
2. Zabad’s father or ancestor. Zabad was one of David’s mighty men who were known as “the thirty” ([1 Chronicles 11:41](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:41)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:31; 1CH 2:34; 1CH 11:41
