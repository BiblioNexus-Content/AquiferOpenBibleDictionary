## Phaltiel (id: 7688)

PHALTIEL
========

An alternate spelling used in the King James Version for the name Paltiel. This refers to the son of Laish mentioned in [2 Samuel 3:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam3:15). Phaltiel is another name for Palti. 

*See* Palti \#2.
