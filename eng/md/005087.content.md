## Shemariah (id: 8348)

SHEMARIAH
=========

1\. Warrior from the tribe of Benjamin who joined David at Ziklag in his struggle against King Saul. Shemariah was one of David’s ambidextrous archers and slingers ([1 Chr 12:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:5)).

2\. One of Rehoboam’s sons ([2 Chr 11:19](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:19); kjv “Shamariah”).

3\. Harim’s son, who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his foreign wife after the exile ([Ezr 10:32](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:32)).

4\. Binnui’s son, who obeyed Ezra’s exhortation to divorce his foreign wife ([Ezr 10:41](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:41)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:5; 2CH 11:19; EZR 10:32; EZR 10:41
