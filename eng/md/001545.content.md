## Dedication, Feast of (id: 4795)

DEDICATION\*, Feast of
======================

Designation by the apostle John for the Feast of Lights, or Hanukkah ([Jn 10:22](https://ref.ly/John10:22)). The feast lasts eight days and begins on the 25th day of Kislev (November to December). *See* Feasts and Festivals of Israel.

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 10:22
