## Purple (id: 7864)

PURPLE
======

A highly prized dye extracted from sea snails. Purple was used to dye fabrics for the tabernacle. It was also used for the clothes of the wealthy ([Exodus 25:4](https://ref.ly/Exod25:4); [Judges 8:26](https://ref.ly/Judg8:26)).
