## Casiphia (id: 4440)

CASIPHIA
========

Place to which Ezra sent for Levites when he realized that his company of returnees from the exile lacked persons qualified for temple service ([Ezr 8:17](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:17)). Casiphia was perhaps Ctesiphon on the Tigris River near modern Baghdad.

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 8:17
