## Kehelathah (id: 6533)

KEHELATHAH
==========

One of the places where the Israelites camped during their journey from Egypt to Mount Sinai. It was located somewhere between Rissah and Mount Shepher ([Numbers 33:22–23](https://ref.ly/Num33:22-Num33:23)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:22–NUM 33:23
