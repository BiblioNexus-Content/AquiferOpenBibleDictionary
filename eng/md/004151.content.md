## Nisan (id: 7410)

NISAN
=====

Nisan is one of the months in the Jewish calendar. The name comes from the ancient language of Babylon ([Nehemiah 2:1](https://ref.ly/Neh2:1); [Esther 3:7](https://ref.ly/Esth3:7)). According to our modern calendar, Nisan usually occurs during parts of March and April.

*See* Calendars, Ancient and Modern.
