## Dodai (id: 4874)

DODAI
=====

A descendant of Ahohi. Dodai was a commander of one of the 12 divisions of Israelite soldiers during the reign of King David. There were 24,000 men in each division ([1 Chronicles 27:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:4)). Dodai might also refer to Dodo, the father of Eleazar in [2 Samuel 23:9](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:9) and [1 Chronicles 11:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:12). 

*See* Dodo \#2.
