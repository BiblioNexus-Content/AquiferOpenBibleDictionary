## Elon-Bethhanan (id: 5044)

ELON\-BETHHANAN
===============

Administrative district in Dan during the reign of Solomon ([1 Kgs 4:9](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:9)). It is perhaps identifiable with the Danite town of Elon.

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 4:9
