## Lice (id: 6743)

LICE
====

The King James Version translation for some sort of small insects, probably gnats. These insects were the third plague in Egypt ([Exodus 8:16–18](https://ref.ly/Exod8:16-Exod8:18)). 

*See* Animals (Gnat).
