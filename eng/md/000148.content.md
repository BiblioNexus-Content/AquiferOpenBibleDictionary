## Advocate (id: 3408)

ADVOCATE
========

A translation for the Greek term *parakletos*. It describes the Holy Spirit in the Gospel of John, as well as Jesus in [1 John 2:1](https://ref.ly/1John2:1). 

*See* Spirit of God; Paraclete.

* **Associated Passages:** 1JN 2:1
* **Associated Articles:** Paraclete (ID: `7575@TyndaleBibleDictionary`); Spirit of God (ID: `8601@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
