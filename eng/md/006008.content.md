## Zohar (id: 9254)

ZOHAR
=====

1. The father of Ephron the Hittite. Abraham bought the cave of Machpelah from Ephron ([Genesis 23:7–9](https://ref.ly/Gen23:7-Gen23:9); [25:9](https://ref.ly/Gen25:9)).
2. An alternate spelling of Zerah, Simeon’s son, in [Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10) and [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15).

    *See* Zerah \#3.

3. An alternate spelling of Izhar in [1 Chronicles 4:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:7).
