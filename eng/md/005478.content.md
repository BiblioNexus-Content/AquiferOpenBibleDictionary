## Taralah (id: 8739)

TARALAH
=======

One of 26 cities in the land assigned to Benjamin’s tribe for an inheritance, listed between Irpeel and Zelah ([Jos 18:27](https://ref.ly/Josh18:27)). Taralah was possibly situated to the northwest of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 18:27
