## Tebaliah (id: 8759)

TEBALIAH
========

Tebaliah was the son of Hosah. Tebaliah was a Merarite Levite and a temple gatekeeper. He served after the Israelites returned from exile in Babylon ([1 Chronicles 26:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 26:11
