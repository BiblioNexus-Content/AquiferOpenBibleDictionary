## En-Tappuah (id: 5095)

EN\-TAPPUAH\*
=============

Canaanite town located on the border between Ephraim’s and Manasseh’s territory ([Jos 17:7](https://ref.ly/Josh17:7)). It is usually identified with the modern Sheikh Abu\-Zarad, about eight miles (12\.9 kilometers) south of Shechem. *See* Tappuah (Place) \#2.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 17:7
