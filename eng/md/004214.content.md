## Old Gate (id: 7474)

OLD GATE
========

Jerusalem gate repaired by Joiada and Meshullam under Nehemiah’s supervision ([Neh 3:6](https://ref.ly/Neh3:6)), and subsequently mentioned in the northerly route traveled by one of the companies of celebrants during the Jerusalem wall’s dedication ([12:39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:39)). The Old Gate was located in the city’s northern wall between the Fish Gate and the Gate of Ephraim ([12:38–39](https://ref.ly/Neh12:38-Neh12:39)).

*See also* Jerusalem.
