## Attai (id: 3870)

ATTAI
=====

1. The son of Sheshan’s daughter and of Jarha, Sheshan’s Egyptian slave. Attai was from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chronicles 2:35](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:35-1Chr2:36)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:35-1Chr2:36)[36](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:35-1Chr2:36)).
2. A warrior from the tribe of Gad who joined David at Ziklag in his struggle against King Saul ([1 Chronicles 12:11](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:11)).
3. The son of King Rehoboam of Judah by Maachah. He was Solomon’s grandson ([2 Chronicles 11:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr11:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:11; 2CH 11:20; 1CH 2:35–1CH 2:36
