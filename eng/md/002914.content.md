## Jaddai (id: 6166)

JADDAI
======

Nebo’s descendant, who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife during the postexilic era ([Ezr 10:43](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:43)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 10:43
