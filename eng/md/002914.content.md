## Jaddua (id: 6167)

JADDUA
======

1\. Leader who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant during the postexilic era ([Neh 10:21](https://ref.ly/Neh10:21)).

2\. Eliashib’s descendant and a contemporary of Nehemiah ([Neh 12:11, 22](https://ref.ly/Neh12:11,Neh12:22)). Jaddua’s father, Jonathan (v [11](https://ref.ly/Neh12:11)), is mentioned in the Elephantine papyri as Johanan (see also v [22](https://ref.ly/Neh12:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 10:21; NEH 12:11; NEH 12:22
