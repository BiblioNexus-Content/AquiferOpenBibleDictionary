## Ellasar (id: 5036)

ELLASAR
=======

Place in Babylonia; a few scholars have suggested that Arioch was its king. He joined a coalition that included Kedorlaomer, king of Elam, and together they raided the Jordan Valley during the time of Abraham ([Gn 14:1, 9](https://ref.ly/Gen14:1,Gen14:9)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:1; GEN 14:9
