## Nahshon (id: 7292)

NAHSHON
=======

Amminadab’s son and brother of Elisheba and Salmon’s father ([Exodus 6:23](https://ref.ly/Exod6:23); [1 Chronicles 2:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:10-1Chr2:11)[–](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:10-1Chr2:11)[11](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:10-1Chr2:11)). 

Nahshon, the prince of Judah’s tribe at the start of Israel’s wandering in the wilderness wanderings ([Numbers 1:7](https://ref.ly/Num1:7); [2:3](https://ref.ly/Num2:3); [10:14](https://ref.ly/Num10:14)), represented his tribe at the dedication of the altar ([Numbers 7:12](https://ref.ly/Num7:12)).

In [Ruth 4:20,](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:20) he is listed as David’s ancestor and a descendant of Judah through the line of Perez. In Matthew and Luke’s family lists, he is listed as an ancestor of Jesus Christ ([Matthew 1:4](https://ref.ly/Matt1:4); [Luke 3:32](https://ref.ly/Luke3:32)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:23; NUM 1:7; NUM 2:3; NUM 7:12; NUM 10:14; RUT 4:20; MAT 1:4; LUK 3:32; 1CH 2:10–1CH 2:11
