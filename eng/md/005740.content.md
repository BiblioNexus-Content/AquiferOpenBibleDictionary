## Uzzi (id: 8993)

UZZI
====

1. A descendant of Eliezer, a direct ancestor of the high priests. Though, he never served as a high priest ([1 Chronicles 6:5–6, 51](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:5-1Chr6:6,1Chr6:51)). He is listed as the son of Bukki and the father of Zerahiah. He was an ancestor of Zadok and later Ezra ([Ezra 7:4](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:4)).
2. A clan chief and mighty warrior of the tribe of Issachar. He was one of the six sons of Tola and the father of Izrahiah, who was clan chief after him ([1 Chronicles 7:2–3](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:2-1Chr7:3)).
3. A clan chief and mighty warrior of the tribe of Benjamin, listed as one of the sons of Bela ([1 Chronicles 7:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:7)).
4. The head of one of the Benjaminite clans that returned from Babylon, listed as the son of Micri and the father of Elah ([1 Chronicles 9:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:8)).
5. One of the leaders of the Levites in Jerusalem, listed as the son of Bani from the clan of Asaph ([Nehemiah 11:22](https://ref.ly/Neh11:22)).
6. The head of the priestly house of Jedaiah during the days of Joiakim the high priest ([Nehemiah 12:19](https://ref.ly/Neh12:19)).
7. One of the priests (or Levites) who helped dedicate the rebuilt temple ([Nehemiah 12:42](https://ref.ly/Neh12:42)). He may be the same as \#5 or \#6 above.
