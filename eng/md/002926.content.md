## Jahdiel (id: 6178)

JAHDIEL
=======

One of the family heads of Manasseh’s tribe dwelling east of the Jordan following the allotment of the land ([1 Chr 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:24)). He was noted as one of the mighty warriors in his tribe.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 5:24
