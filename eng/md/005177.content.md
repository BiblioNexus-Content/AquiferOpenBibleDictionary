## Shobal (id: 8441)

SHOBAL
======

1\. One of the seven sons of Seir the Horite in Edom ([Gn 36:20](https://ref.ly/Gen36:20); [1 Chr 1:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:38)). Shobal became the father of five sons ([Gn 36:23](https://ref.ly/Gen36:23); [1 Chr 1:40](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:40)) and a chief among the Horites ([Gn 36:29](https://ref.ly/Gen36:29)).

2\. Hur’s son, the father of Haroeh, and the founder of the families of Kiriath\-jearim ([1 Chr 2:50–52](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:50-1Chr2:52)).

3\. One of Judah’s five sons and the father of Reiah ([1 Chr 4:1–2](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:1-1Chr4:2)); perhaps the same as \#2 above.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:20; GEN 36:23; GEN 36:29; 1CH 1:38; 1CH 1:40; 1CH 2:50–1CH 2:52; 1CH 4:1–1CH 4:2
