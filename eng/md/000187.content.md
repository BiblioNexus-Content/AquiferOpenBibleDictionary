## Ahilud (id: 3446)

AHILUD
======

The father of the court historian Jehoshaphat. Jehoshaphat served under both David and Solomon ([2 Samuel 8:16](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:16); [20:24](https://ref.ly/2Sam20:24); [1 Kings 4:3](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:3); [1 Chronicles 18:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr18:15)). Ahilud was probably also the father of Baana, one of Solomon’s tax officials ([1 Kings 4:12](https://ref.ly/1Kgs4:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:16; 2SA 20:24; 1KI 4:3; 1KI 4:12; 1CH 18:15
