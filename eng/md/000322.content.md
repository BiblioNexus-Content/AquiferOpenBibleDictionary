## Anael (id: 3584)

ANAEL
=====

The brother of Tobit ([Tobit 1:21–22](https://ref.ly/Tob1:21-Tob1:22)). Sennacherib, the King of Assyria, died in 681 BC. After this, Anael’s son Ahikar was appointed by the Assyrian king Esarhaddon to a high court position in Nineveh.

* **Associated Passages:** TOB 1:21–TOB 1:22
