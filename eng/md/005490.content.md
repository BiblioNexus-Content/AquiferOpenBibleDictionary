## Tattenai (id: 8751)

TATTENAI
========

Persian governor of a province west of the Euphrates River who opposed the rebuilding of the Jerusalem temple and walls under Zerubbabel during the postexilic period ([Ezr 5:3, 6](https://ref.ly/Ezra5:3,Ezra5:6); [6:6, 13](https://ref.ly/Ezra6:6,Ezra6:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 5:3; EZR 5:6; EZR 6:6; EZR 6:13
