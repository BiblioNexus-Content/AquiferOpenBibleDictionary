## Garmite (id: 5370)

GARMITE
=======

Designation for Keilah in [1 Chronicles 4:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:19). The word, which means “bony,” seems to denote strength (the same Hebrew word is used in [Jb 40:18](https://ref.ly/Job40:18) and [Prv 25:15](https://ref.ly/Prov25:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:19; JOB 40:18; PRO 25:15
