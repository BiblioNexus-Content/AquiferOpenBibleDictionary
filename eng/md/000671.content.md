## Baalis (id: 3938)

BAALIS
======

An Ammonite king who arranged for the murder of Gedaliah, the governor of the people left behind after Nebuchadnezzar’s capture of Jerusalem and deportation of its inhabitants ([Jeremiah 40:14](https://ref.ly/Jer40:14)). Gedaliah was warned by a guerrilla leader, Johanan, but he refused to take listen and was killed ([Jeremiah 41:1](https://ref.ly/Jer41:1-Jer41:3)[–](https://ref.ly/Jer41:1-Jer41:3)[3](https://ref.ly/Jer41:1-Jer41:3)).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 40:14; JER 41:1–JER 41:3
