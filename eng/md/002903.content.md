## Jachin (id: 6157)

JACHIN
======

1. The son of Simeon and leader of the Jachinites, who moved to Egypt with his grandfather Jacob ([Genesis 46:10](https://ref.ly/Gen46:10); [Exodus 6:15](https://ref.ly/Exod6:15); [Numbers 26:12](https://ref.ly/Num26:12)). He is called Jarib in [1 Chronicles 4:24](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:24).
2. A priest who lived in Jerusalem after the exile to Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:10](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:10); [Nehemiah 11:10](https://ref.ly/Neh11:10)). The name Jachin may possibly refer to a family of priests that Jachin led.
3. A descendant of Aaron who led the twenty\-first division of priests who served in the temple during King David's time ([1 Chronicles 24:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:10; EXO 6:15; NUM 26:12; 1CH 4:24; 1CH 9:10; 1CH 24:17; NEH 11:10
