## Goah (id: 5493)

GOAH
====

Location mentioned in connection with the hill of Gareb, to which the restored city of Jerusalem will extend. Goah is situated south of Gareb ([Jer 31:39](https://ref.ly/Jer31:39), kjv “Goath”).

* **Associated Passages:** JER 31:39
