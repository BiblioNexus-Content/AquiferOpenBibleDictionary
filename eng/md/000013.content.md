## Abdiel (id: 3271)

ABDIEL
======

Guni’s son and the father of Ahi ([1 Chronicles 5:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:15)). Ahi was a clan leader in Gad’s tribe during the reigns of King Jotham of Judah and King Jeroboam II of Israel ([1 Chronicles 5:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:15-1Chr5:17)–[17](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:15-1Chr5:17)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 5:15; 1CH 5:15–1CH 5:17
