## Achsa, Achsah (id: 3344)

ACHSA, ACHSAH
=============

Caleb’s daughter ([1 Chronicles 2:49](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:49)). Othniel, Caleb’s nephew, accepted his uncle’s request to capture Kiriath\-sepher in order to marry Achsah. She persuaded Othniel to ask her father, Caleb, for a field. She also asked Caleb for two springs of water, which was necessary for life in the desert ([Joshua 15:16](https://ref.ly/Josh15:16-Josh15:19)–[19](https://ref.ly/Josh15:16-Josh15:19); [Judges 1:12](https://ref.ly/Judg1:12-Judg1:15)–[15](https://ref.ly/Judg1:12-Judg1:15)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:49; JOS 15:16–JOS 15:19; JDG 1:12–JDG 1:15
