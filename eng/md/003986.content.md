## Muppim (id: 7243)

MUPPIM
======

One of the ten sons of Benjamin ([Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)). He is elsewhere called Shephupham ([Numbers 26:39](https://ref.ly/Num26:39)) and Shuppim ([1 Chronicles 7:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12)). He may be the same person as Shephuphan ([1 Chronicles 8:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:5)). 

*See* Shephupham.
