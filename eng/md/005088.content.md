## Shemeber (id: 8350)

SHEMEBER
========

King of Zeboiim, who joined a confederacy with four other kings in rebellion against Kedorlaomer and his allies. Abraham rescued Lot from captivity after Shemeber, along with Sodom and Gomorrah, was defeated ([Gn 14:2](https://ref.ly/Gen14:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 14:2
