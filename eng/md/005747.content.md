## Valley of Decision (id: 4791)

VALLEY OF DECISION
==================

A place mentioned in [Joel 3:14](https://ref.ly/Joel3:14), where the Lord will judge the non\-Jewish nations who gathered against Judah. It is the same as the valley of Jehoshaphat (see [Joel 3:2](https://ref.ly/Joel3:2)). 

*See* Jehoshaphat, Valley of.

* **Associated Passages:** JOL 3:2; JOL 3:14
