## Trespass (id: 8921)

TRESPASS
========

Doing something wrong against God’s law or against another person. In the Bible, trespass is often another word for sin ([Matthew 6:14–15](https://ref.ly/Matt6:14-Matt6:15)).

*See* Sin.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 18:24; MAT 6:14–MAT 6:15
