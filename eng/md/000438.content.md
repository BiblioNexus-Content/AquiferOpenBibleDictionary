## Aram-Zobah (id: 3702)

ARAM\-ZOBAH
===========

A Syrian territory ruled in David’s time by King Hadadezer. David defeated him in [2 Samuel 8:3](https://ref.ly/2Sam8:3). This form of the name occurs in the title of [Psalm 60](https://ref.ly/Ps60:1-Ps60:12). *See* Zobah.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 8:3; PSA 60:1–PSA 60:12
* **Associated Articles:** Zobah (ID: `9251@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
