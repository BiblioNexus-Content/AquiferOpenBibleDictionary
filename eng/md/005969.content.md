## Zichri, Zicri (id: 9214)

ZICHRI, ZICRI
=============

1. A Kohathite Levite and a descendant of Izhar ([Exodus 6:21](https://ref.ly/Exod6:21)).
2. One of Shimei’s sons from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:19)).
3. One of Shashak’s sons from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:23)).
4. One of Jeroham’s sons from the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 8:27](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:27)).
5. An ancestor of Mattaniah. Mattaniah returned with Zerubbabel to Israel following the exile in Babylon ([1 Chronicles 9:15](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:15)). Zicri is probably Zabdi from [Nehemiah 11:17](https://ref.ly/Neh11:17).
6. A descendant of Eliezer, the son of Moses. His son, Shelomoth, was in charge of the treasuries of the dedicated gifts ([1 Chronicles 26:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr26:25)).
7. The father of Eliezer, the chief officer of the Reubenites during David’s reign ([1 Chronicles 27:16](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:16)).
8. The father of Amasiah, a volunteer in charge of 200,000 men during Jehoshaphat’s reign ([2 Chronicles 17:16](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:16)).
9. The father of Elishaphat, a member of the conspiracy against Athaliah led by Jehoiada ([2 Chronicles 23:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:1)).
10. A mighty man from Ephraim who participated in Pekah’s domination of Judah. Zicri killed Ahaz’s son Maaseiah, Azrikam the commander of the palace, and Elkanah the king’s deputy ([2 Chronicles 28:7](https://ref.ly/2Chr28:7)).
11. The father of Joel, overseer of the Benjaminites who returned to Jerusalem following the exile in Babylon ([Nehemiah 11:9](https://ref.ly/Neh11:9)).
12. A Levite who served as a priest and the head of the clan of Abijah during the days of Joiakim the high priest ([Nehemiah 12:17](https://ref.ly/Neh12:17)).
