## Tob-Adonijah (id: 8876)

TOB\-ADONIJAH
=============

One of the Levites under Jehoshaphat who went out into the cities of Judah to teach the law ([2 Chr 17:8](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:8)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 17:8
