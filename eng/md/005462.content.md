## Talmon (id: 8723)

TALMON
======

Head of a Levite family who served as temple gatekeepers ([1 Chr 9:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:17)). His descendants returned from the exile with Zerubbabel and served as gatekeepers in the rebuilt temple ([Ezr 2:42](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:42); [Neh 7:45](https://ref.ly/Neh7:45); [11:19](https://ref.ly/Neh11:19); [12:25](https://ref.ly/Neh12:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:17; EZR 2:42; NEH 7:45; NEH 11:19; NEH 12:25
