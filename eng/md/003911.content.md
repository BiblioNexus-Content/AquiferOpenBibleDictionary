## Mithcah, Mithkah (id: 7170)

MITHCAH, MITHKAH
================

One of the temporary camping places of the Israelites during their wilderness wanderings. [Numbers 33:28–29](https://ref.ly/Num33:28-Num33:29) mentions it as between Terah and Hashmonah.

*See also* Wilderness Wanderings.
