## Artemis (id: 3768)

ARTEMIS
=======

The Greek goddess of the moon, wild animals, and hunting. The cult of Artemis at Ephesus, where she is known as Diana by the Romans ([Acts 19:23–41](https://ref.ly/Acts19:23-Acts19:41)), regarded her especially as a fertility goddess.
