## Raguel (id: 7908)

RAGUEL
======

1. The King James Version spelling of Reuel. Reuel was another name for Jethro, the father\-in\-law of Moses ([Numbers 10:29](https://ref.ly/Num10:29).).

    *See* Jethro.

2. A member of the tribe of Naphtali living in Ecbatana. According to [Tobit 3:7](https://ref.ly/Tob3:7), Raguel was the father of Sarah, soon to become the wife of Tobias. This was due to the intervention of the angel Raphael.

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 10:29; TOB 3:7
* **Associated Articles:** Jethro (ID: `6360@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
