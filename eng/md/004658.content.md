## Ram (Person) (id: 7936)

RAM (Person)
============

1. An ancestor of King David ([Ruth 4:19](https://ref.ly/Ruth4:19); [1 Chronicles 2:9–10](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:9-1Chr2:10)), listed in Matthew’s family list of Christ ([Matthew 1:3–4](https://ref.ly/Matt1:3-Matt1:4); called Arni in [Luke 3:33](https://ref.ly/Luke3:33)).

    *See* Genealogy of Jesus Christ.

2. Jerahmeel’s eldest son ([1 Chronicles 2:25–27](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:25-1Chr2:27)), and perhaps the nephew of \#1 above.
3. Head of the family of Elihu, one of Job’s friends ([Job 32:2](https://ref.ly/Job32:2)).
