## Amzi (id: 3582)

AMZI
====

1. A person from the Merarite family group of the Levites and an ancestor of Ethan the musician ([1 Chronicles 6:46](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:46)).
2. An ancestor of Adaiah and a priest of Malchijah’s division ([Nehemiah 11:12](https://ref.ly/Neh11:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:46; NEH 11:12
