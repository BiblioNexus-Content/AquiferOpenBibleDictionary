## Asenath (id: 3788)

ASENATH
=======

Joseph’s Egyptian wife who became the mother of Manasseh and Ephraim. Asenath was the daughter of the priest Potiphera ([Genesis 41:45, 50](https://ref.ly/Gen41:45,Gen41:50-Gen41:52)–[52](https://ref.ly/Gen41:45,Gen41:50-Gen41:52); [46:20](https://ref.ly/Gen46:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 41:45; GEN 46:20; GEN 41:50–GEN 41:52
