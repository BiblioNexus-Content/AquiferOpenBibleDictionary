## Flea (id: 5275)

FLEA
====

Small, wingless insect with strong legs for jumping. The flea is mentioned only in [1 Samuel 24:14](https://ref.ly/1Sam24:14) and [26:20](https://ref.ly/1Sam26:20), where David refers to himself as a flea. *See* Animals.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 24:14; 1SA 26:20
* **Associated Articles:** Animals (ID: `3614@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
