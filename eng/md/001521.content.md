## David, Tower of (id: 4768)

DAVID, Tower of
===============

1. A fortress built by David, with a thousand shields hung on it. It is mentioned in [Song of Songs 4:4](https://ref.ly/Song4:4) but nowhere else.
2. David’s Tower in Jerusalem, near the Jaffa Gate. It was built in medieval times.

    *See* Jerusalem.
