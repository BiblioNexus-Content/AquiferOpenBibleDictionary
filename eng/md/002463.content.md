## Hattush (id: 5716)

HATTUSH
=======

1\. Shemaiah’s son and a descendant of David ([1 Chr 3:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:22)). Hattush returned from the Babylonian exile with Ezra ([Ezr 8:3](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:3)).

2\. Son of Hashabneiah, who helped Nehemiah rebuild the walls of Jerusalem ([Neh 3:10](https://ref.ly/Neh3:10)).

3\. Priest who returned from Babylon with Zerubbabel ([Neh 12:2](https://ref.ly/Neh12:2)). One of his descendants signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God ([Neh 10:4](https://ref.ly/Neh10:4)). His name is omitted from [Nehemiah 12:14](https://ref.ly/Neh12:14) through scribal error.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:22; EZR 8:3; NEH 3:10; NEH 10:4; NEH 12:2; NEH 12:14
