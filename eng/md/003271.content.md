## Kedemah (id: 6529)

KEDEMAH
=======

A son of Ishmael mentioned in [Genesis 25:15](https://ref.ly/Gen25:15). Later, his descendants were known by his name ([1 Chronicles 1:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:15; 1CH 1:31
