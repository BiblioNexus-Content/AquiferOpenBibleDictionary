## Jether (id: 6357)

JETHER
======

1. The firstborn son of Gideon. Because of his youth, Jether was afraid to execute the Midianite kings Zebah and Zalmunna ([Judges 8:20](https://ref.ly/Judg8:20)).
2. An Ishmaelite and the father of the commander Amasa ([1 Kings 2:5, 32](https://ref.ly/1Kgs2:5,1Kgs2:32); [1 Chronicles 2:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:17)). Another name for him is "Ithra" ([2 Samuel 17:25](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:25)). *See* Ithra.
3. The firstborn son of Jada, the brother of Jonathan. Jether was a descendant of Judah through the family line of Hezron. He fathered no children ([1 Chronicles 2:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:32)).
4. A Judahite and the firstborn of the four sons of Ezrah ([1 Chronicles 4:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:17)).
5. An Asherite, the father of three sons ([1 Chronicles 7:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:38)). He was likely the same person as, Ithran, son of Zophah ([1 Chronicles 7:37](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:37)).
