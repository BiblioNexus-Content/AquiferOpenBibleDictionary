## Jattir (id: 6240)

JATTIR
======

Town in the hill country of Judah given to the Levites ([Jos 15:48](https://ref.ly/Josh15:48); [21:14](https://ref.ly/Josh21:14); [1 Chr 6:57](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:57)). David sent spoils from his victory over the Amalekites to Jattir ([1 Sm 30:27](https://ref.ly/1Sam30:27)). It is identified with modern Khirbet ‘Attir, 13 miles (21 kilometers) southeast of Hebron.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:48; JOS 21:14; 1SA 30:27; 1CH 6:57
