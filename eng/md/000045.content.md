## Abilene (id: 3303)

ABILENE
=======

A region on the east side of the Anti\-Lebanon Mountains in Syria. The district is named after the capital city of Abila, located about 18 miles (29 kilometers) from Damascus. At the time of John the Baptist, Abilene was governed by Lysanias ([Luke 3:1](https://ref.ly/Luke3:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 3:1
