## Ahiram, Ahiramite (id: 3454)

AHIRAM, AHIRAMITE
=================

Benjamin's third son. He was the ancestor of the Ahiramite clan ([Numbers 26:38](https://ref.ly/Num26:38); [1 Chronicles 8:1](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:1), here called "Aharah"). Two shortened forms of "Ahiram" in lists of ancestors may be Ehi ([Genesis 46:21](https://ref.ly/Gen46:21)) and Aher ([1 Chronicles 7:12](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:12)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 46:21; NUM 26:38; 1CH 7:12; 1CH 8:1
