## Aram-Geshur (id: 3696)

ARAM\-GESHUR
============

A small kingdom between Mount Hermon and Bashan. Basha is the region east and northeast of the Sea of Galilee. It borders Argob which is located east of the Sea of Galilee. *See* Geshur, Geshurites.
