## Shemuel (id: 8356)

SHEMUEL
=======

1. A son of Ammihud and the representative of the tribe of Simeon. This was in the division of the land of Canaan among the 10 tribes of Israel west of the Jordan ([Numbers 34:20](https://ref.ly/Num34:20)).
2. The King James Version translation of Samuel, son of Elkanah ([1 Chronicles 6:33–34](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:33-1Chr6:34)).

    *See* Samuel (Person).

3. A son of Tola and leader in the tribe of Issachar ([1 Chronicles 7:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:20; 1CH 7:2; 1CH 6:33–1CH 6:34
