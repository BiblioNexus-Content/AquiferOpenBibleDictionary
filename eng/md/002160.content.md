## Gennaeus, Genneus (id: 5416)

GENNAEUS\*, GENNEUS
===================

Apollonius’s father ([2 Macc 12:2](https://ref.ly/2Macc12:2)). Since Gennaeus means “noble” or “highborn,” it may be an epithet rather than a name.

* **Associated Passages:** 2MA 12:2
