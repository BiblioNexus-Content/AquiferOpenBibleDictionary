## Followers of the Way (id: 5285)

FOLLOWERS OF THE WAY
====================

A name used for Christians in the book of Acts ([Acts 9:2](https://ref.ly/Acts9:2); [19:9, 23](https://ref.ly/Acts19:9,Acts19:23); [22:4](https://ref.ly/Acts22:4); [24:22](https://ref.ly/Acts24:22)). In its early years, Christianity was called "the Way."

*See* Way, The.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 9:2; ACT 19:9; ACT 19:23; ACT 22:4; ACT 24:22
* **Associated Articles:** The Way (ID: `9042@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
