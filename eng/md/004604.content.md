## Put (Person) (id: 7870)

PUT (Person)
============

Third of Ham’s four sons, who most likely settled in northern Africa and is perhaps the forefather of the peoples of Egypt and Libya ([Gn 10:6](https://ref.ly/Gen10:6); [1 Chr 1:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:8)).
