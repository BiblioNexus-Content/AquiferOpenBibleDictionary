## Shomer (id: 8446)

SHOMER
======

1\. Father ([2 Kgs 12:21](https://ref.ly/2Kgs12:21)), or perhaps the Moabite mother ([2 Chr 24:26](https://ref.ly/2Chr24:26)), of Jehozabad, a royal servant, who, with Jozacar, conspired against and murdered Joash, king of Judah. Shimrith is the feminine form of Shomer. *See* Shimrith.

2\. Alternate name for Shemer, Heber’s son, in [1 Chronicles 7:34](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:34). *See* Shemer \#3.

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 12:21; 1CH 7:34; 2CH 24:26
