## Embroiderer, Embroidery (id: 5066)

EMBROIDERER, EMBROIDERY
=======================

The art of making decorative designs on clothing. This was an art practiced both in the home and as a profession. Embroidery was done on a loom or with a needle. The tabernacle and temple had delicate, embroidered fabrics ([Exodus 26:1, 31](https://ref.ly/Exod26:1,Exod26:31)). The clothing of priests were beautifully embroidered ([Exodus 28:6, 8](https://ref.ly/Exod28:6,Exod28:8); [39:2](https://ref.ly/Exod39:2)). Embroidery was practiced in: 

* Canaan ([Judges 5:30](https://ref.ly/Judg5:30))
* Egypt ([Ezekiel 27:7](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:7))
* Syria ([Ezekiel 27:16](https://ref.ly/Exod27:16))
* Babylonia ([Joshua 7:21](https://ref.ly/Josh7:21))
* Assyria
* Persia ([Esther 1:6](https://ref.ly/Esth1:6))
