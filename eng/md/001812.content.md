## Emek-Keziz (id: 5067)

EMEK\-KEZIZ
===========

City allotted to Benjamin’s tribe for an inheritance; it was mentioned between Beth\-hoglah and Beth\-arabah ([Jos 18:21](https://ref.ly/Josh18:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 18:21
