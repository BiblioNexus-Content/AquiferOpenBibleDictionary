## Sithri (id: 8534)

SITHRI
======

A Kohathite Levite and Uzziel’s third son. Sithri was the cousin of Aaron and Moses ([Exodus 6:22](https://ref.ly/Exod6:22)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:22
