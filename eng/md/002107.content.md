## Gammadims (id: 5361)

GAMMADIMS
=========

The word "Gammadims" is found in the King James Version of the Bible in [Ezekiel 27:11](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:11). The Berean Standard Bible translates this as "men of Gamad." This refers to people from a place called Gamad.

*See* Gammad.
