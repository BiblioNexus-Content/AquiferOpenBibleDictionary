## Myra (id: 7252)

MYRA
====

A port city on the southern coast of Asia Minor in the province of Lycia, identified with the modern Demre in Turkey. According to [Acts 27:5–6](https://ref.ly/Acts27:5-Acts27:6), Paul and his military escort stopped here for a short time. They changed ships on their journey to Rome, where Paul was going to trial before Caesar.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 27:5–ACT 27:6
