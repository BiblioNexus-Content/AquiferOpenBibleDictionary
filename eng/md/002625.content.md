## Hodiah, Hodijah (id: 5873)

HODIAH, HODIJAH\*
=================

1\. A man of Judah mentioned in [1 Chronicles 4:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:19).

2, 3, 4\. Three of the men who signed the covenant of Ezra ([Neh 10:10, 13, 18](https://ref.ly/Neh10:10,Neh10:13,Neh10:18)) bear this name; two of them are perhaps among those who interpreted the covenant to the people at Ezra’s public reading of the law ([8:7](https://ref.ly/Neh8:7)) and stood upon the stairs of the Levites during the service of covenant renewal ([9:5](https://ref.ly/Neh9:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:19; NEH 8:7; NEH 9:5; NEH 10:10; NEH 10:13; NEH 10:18
