## The Great Sea (id: 5535)

THE GREAT SEA
=============

The Great Sea is another name for the Mediterranean Sea. The ancient Near Eastern peoples called it the Great Sea because of its great size in comparison to the other seas they knew ([Numbers 34:6](https://ref.ly/Num34:6); [Joshua 1:4](https://ref.ly/Josh1:4)).
