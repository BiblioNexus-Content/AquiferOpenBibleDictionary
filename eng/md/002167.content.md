## Genubath (id: 5422)

GENUBATH
========

Son of Hadad, the Edomite prince who, as a young lad, was taken to Egypt to escape Joab’s slaughter. There Hadad married a sister of Queen Tahpenes. She bore Genubath, who was raised by the queen as a son of Pharaoh ([1 Kgs 11:20](https://ref.ly/1Kgs11:20)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1KI 11:20
