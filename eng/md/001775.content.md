## Elishua (id: 5027)

ELISHUA
=======

A son of King David. He was one of the 13 children David fathered during the time he ruled as king in Jerusalem ([2 Samuel 5:15](https://ref.ly/2Sam5:15); [1 Chronicles 14:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:5)). In a similar list found in [1 Chronicles 3:6,](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:6) most Hebrew manuscripts show the name "Elishama" in the place where Elishua's name should be.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 5:15; 1CH 3:6; 1CH 14:5
