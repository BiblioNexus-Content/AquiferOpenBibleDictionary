## Elisheba (id: 5026)

ELISHEBA
========

The wife of Aaron ([Exodus 6:23](https://ref.ly/Exod6:23)), who gave birth to Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. Her father was Amminadab and her brother was Nahshon, the leader of Judah ([Numbers 1:7](https://ref.ly/Num1:7); [2:3](https://ref.ly/Num2:3)). Elisheba was also from Judah. After Aaron died ([Numbers 20:28](https://ref.ly/Num20:28)), Moses made Eleazar, Elisheba’s third son, with the office of chief priest.

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:23; NUM 1:7; NUM 2:3; NUM 20:28
