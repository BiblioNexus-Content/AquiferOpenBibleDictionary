## Cheese (id: 4493)

CHEESE
======

Milk product produced by curdling milk, draining the whey, pressing the curds into cakes, and drying them. One of the earliest biblical references to cheese is in [Job 10:10](https://ref.ly/Job10:10): “Didst thou not pour me out like milk and curdle me like cheese?” (rsv). *See* Food and Food Preparation.
