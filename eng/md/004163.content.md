## Nogah (id: 7421)

NOGAH
=====

One of 13 sons of David born in Jerusalem after David established his kingdom ([1 Chr 3:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:7); [14:6](https://ref.ly/1Chr14:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 3:7; 1CH 14:6
