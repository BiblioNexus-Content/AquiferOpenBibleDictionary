## Beth-Tappuah (id: 4206)

BETH\-TAPPUAH
=============

A town in the hill country of the tribal land of Judah ([Joshua 15:53](https://ref.ly/Josh15:53)). It was named the “place of fruit trees” because of its high ridge location and many fruitful orchards. Beth\-tappuah is the modern Taffuh, which is about 6\.4 kilometers (four miles) northwest of Hebron.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:53
