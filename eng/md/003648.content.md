## Malluch (id: 6908)

MALLUCH
=======

1\. Merarite Levite and ancestor of Ethan the singer in Solomon’s temple ([1 Chr 6:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:44)).

2\. Bani’s son, whom Ezra required to divorce his foreign wife ([Ezr 10:29](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:29)).

3\. Harim’s son, whom Ezra required to divorce his foreign wife ([Ezr 10:32](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:32)).

4\. Priest who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant ([Neh 10:4](https://ref.ly/Neh10:4)).

5\. Another priest who set his seal on Ezra’s covenant ([Neh 10:27](https://ref.ly/Neh10:27)).

6\. Priest who returned from the exile with Zerubbabel ([Neh 12:2](https://ref.ly/Neh12:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:44; EZR 10:29; EZR 10:32; NEH 10:4; NEH 10:27; NEH 12:2
