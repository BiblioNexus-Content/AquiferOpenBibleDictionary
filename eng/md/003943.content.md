## Moreh, Oak of (id: 7203)

MOREH, Oak of
=============

Abraham’s first recorded stopping place upon entering Palestine after leaving Mesopotamia. Here he built an altar to God ([Gn 12:6](https://ref.ly/Gen12:6)). Later, Moses mentioned this place as a geographical landmark to identify the whereabouts of Mt Gerizim and Mt Ebal ([Dt 11:30](https://ref.ly/Deut11:30)). The kjv improperly reads “plain” of Moreh. The oak of Moreh was located near Shechem.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 12:6; DEU 11:30
