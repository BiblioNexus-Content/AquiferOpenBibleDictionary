## Diviners’ Oak (id: 4868)

DIVINERS’ OAK
=============

Place apparently near Shechem ([Jgs 9:37](https://ref.ly/Judg9:37)); mistakenly called “the plain of Meonenim” in the kjv. The Diviners’ Oak may have been associated with those who practiced divination, hence the name.

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 9:37
