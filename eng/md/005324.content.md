## Sorghum (id: 8584)

SORGHUM
=======

A type of grass that grows and dies each year. It can grow to over 1\.8 meters (six feet) tall. This plant may have been what is called "hyssop." A sponge soaked in vinegar was attached to a tall plant stalk during Jesus’s crucifixion to offer him a drink ([Matthew 27:48](https://ref.ly/Matt27:48); [Mark 15:36](https://ref.ly/Mark15:36); [John 19:29](https://ref.ly/John19:29)). *See* Plants (Hyssop).
