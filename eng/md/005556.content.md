## The Paltite (id: 7565)

THE PALTITE
===========

A name for Hele, one of the mighty men of King David. He may have been a descendant of Palti ([2 Samuel 23:26](https://ref.ly/2Sam23:26), New Living Translation marginal reading). He may have lived in Beth\-pelet. In [1 Chronicles 11:27](https://ref.ly/1Chr11:27) he is called a “Pelonite.” 

*See* Beth\-Pelet.

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 23:26; 1CH 11:27
