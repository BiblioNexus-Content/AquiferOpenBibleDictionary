## Keren-Happuch (id: 6555)

KEREN\-HAPPUCH
==============

Job’s third daughter and the sister of Jemimah and Keziah. She was listed as a member of Job’s family at the time of his restoration ([Jb 42:14](https://ref.ly/Job42:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOB 42:14
