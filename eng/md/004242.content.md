## Onion (id: 7498)

ONION
=====

The onions mentioned in [Numbers 11:5](https://ref.ly/Num11:5) are certainly *Allium cepa*, the Egyptian onion. This onion has a compact bulb covered with a coat and formed of layers. These layers consist of broad, fleshy bases of leaves that closely overlap each other. The leaves are slender and hollow. The entire plant has a strong, distinctive taste and smell.

Closely related to the onion is garlicand leek.

*See* Food and Food Preparation; Garlic; Leek.
