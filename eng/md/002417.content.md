## Hariph (id: 5672)

HARIPH
======

Ancestor of a family who returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Neh 7:24](https://ref.ly/Neh7:24)). The name Jorah appears in the parallel list of [Ezra 2:18](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:18). A representative of this family signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others ([Neh 10:19](https://ref.ly/Neh10:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:18; NEH 7:24; NEH 10:19
