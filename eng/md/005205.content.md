## Shushan Eduth (id: 8468)

SHUSHAN EDUTH\*
===============

Hebrew phrase in the superscription of [Psalm 60](https://ref.ly/Ps60:1-Ps60:12), translated “To \[the tune] of ‘The Lily of the Covenant’ ” (niv), perhaps a familiar ancient melody to which the psalm was performed. *See* Music.

* **Associated Passages:** PSA 60:1–PSA 60:12
