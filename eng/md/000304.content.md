## Ammizabad (id: 3564)

AMMIZABAD
=========

Benaiah’s son. Both Benaiah and Ammizabad were high\-ranking officers in King David’s army ([1 Chronicles 27:5–6](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:5-1Chr27:6)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 27:5–1CH 27:6
