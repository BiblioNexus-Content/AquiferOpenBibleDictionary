## Rufus (id: 8069)

RUFUS
=====

1. One of the sons of Simon of Cyrene ([Mark 15:21](https://ref.ly/Mark15:21)).
2. A Christian greeted by Paul in the letter to the Romans. Paul added a special endearing comment about his mother ([Rom 16:13](https://ref.ly/Rom16:13)). This Roman Rufus may be the same one referenced as the son of Simone of Cyrene in [Mark 15:21](https://ref.ly/Mark15:21).

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 15:21; ROM 16:13
