## Alemeth (Place) (id: 3498)

ALEMETH (Place)
===============

A city given to the Levites (priests) in the territory of the tribe of Benjamin ([1 Chronicles 6:60](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:60); it may be written “Allemeth”). It is also called Almon in [Joshua 21:18](https://ref.ly/Josh21:18). It is located at modern\-day Khirbet ‘Almit, about five miles (8 kilometers) northeast of Jerusalem.
