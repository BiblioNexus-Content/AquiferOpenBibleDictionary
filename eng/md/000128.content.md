## Adnah (id: 3387)

ADNAH
=====

1. A captain from the tribe of Manasseh who left Saul to join David’s army at Ziklag ([1 Chronicles 12:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr12:20)).
2. A general under King Jehoshaphat of Judah ([2 Chronicles 17:14](https://ref.ly/2Chr17:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 12:20; 2CH 17:14
