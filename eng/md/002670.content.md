## Hoshaiah (id: 5919)

HOSHAIAH
========

1\. Prince of Judah who led a contingent of princes in procession at the dedication of the walls of Jerusalem after they were rebuilt ([Neh 12:32](https://ref.ly/Neh12:32)).

2\. Father of Azariah ([Jer 42:1](https://ref.ly/Jer42:1); [43:2](https://ref.ly/Jer43:2)). Azariah was a leader of the people of Judah after the fall of Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** NEH 12:32; JER 42:1; JER 43:2
