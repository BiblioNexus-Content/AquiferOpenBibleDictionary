## Hassenuah (id: 5706)

HASSENUAH
=========

Ancestor of a Benjamite family that returned to Judah with Zerubbabel after the exile ([1 Chr 9:7](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:7); [Neh 11:9](https://ref.ly/Neh11:9), kjv “Senuah”); perhaps alternately called Senaah ([Ezr 2:35](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:35); [Neh 7:38](https://ref.ly/Neh7:38)), and Hassenaah ([Neh 3:3](https://ref.ly/Neh3:3)). *See* Senaah.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 9:7; EZR 2:35; NEH 3:3; NEH 7:38; NEH 11:9
* **Associated Articles:** Senaah (ID: `8221@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
