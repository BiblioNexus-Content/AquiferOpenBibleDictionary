## Jeremoth (id: 6317)

JEREMOTH
========

1\. One of Beker’s nine sons and a leader in Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 7:8](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:8)). His name is rendered Jerimoth in some versions.

2\. Benjamite, the son of Beriah and head of his family living in Jerusalem ([1 Chr 8:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:14)).

3\. Levite of the family at Merari and one of Mushi’s three sons registered during David’s reign ([1 Chr 23:23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:23)). His name is alternately spelled Jerimoth here and in [1 Chronicles 24:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:30).

4\. Heman’s son and the leader of the 15th of 24 divisions of musicians trained for service in the house of the Lord ([1 Chr 25:22](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:22), nlt mg). Here and also in [1 Chronicles 25:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr25:4) his name is spelled Jerimoth.

5\. Azriel’s son and the chief official of Naphtali’s tribe during David’s reign ([1 Chr 27:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:19)). His name is spelled Jerimoth in some texts.

6\. One of Elam’s descendants who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife during the postexilic period ([Ezr 10:26](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:26)).

7\. One of Zattu’s descendants who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife ([Ezr 10:27](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:27)).

8\. One of Bani’s descendants who was encouraged by Ezra to divorce his foreign wife ([Ezr 10:29](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:29)). He is named “Ramoth” in the kjv.

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 7:8; 1CH 8:14; 1CH 23:23; 1CH 24:30; 1CH 25:4; 1CH 25:22; 1CH 27:19; EZR 10:26; EZR 10:27; EZR 10:29
