## Tel-Melah (id: 8774)

TEL\-MELAH
==========

One of the Babylonian towns situated in the vicinity of the Kebar River near the city of Nippur from which exiles, who were unable to establish their Israelite descent, returned to Palestine with Zerubbabel following the Babylonian captivity ([Ezr 2:59](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:59); [Neh 7:61](https://ref.ly/Neh7:61)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:59; NEH 7:61
