## Magdala (id: 6849)

MAGDALA
=======

A King James Version name used in [Matthew 15:39](https://ref.ly/Matt15:39). Recent versions and translations use the name Magadan. Sources dating from New Testament times locate the town of Magdala a short distance north of the city of Tiberias on the western shore of the Sea of Galilee.
