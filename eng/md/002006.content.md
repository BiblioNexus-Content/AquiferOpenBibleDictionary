## Firkin (id: 5262)

FIRKIN\*
========

Measure of about 10 gallons (37\.9 liters). In [John 2:6](https://ref.ly/John2:6), firkin is the kjv translation for the name of a Greek liquid measure. *See* Weights and Measures.

* **Associated Passages:** JHN 2:6
* **Associated Articles:** Weights and Measures (ID: `9051@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
