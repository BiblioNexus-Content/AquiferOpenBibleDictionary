## Selah (id: 8214)

SELAH\*
=======

Musical notation, perhaps designating a pause in performance, occurring over 70 times in psalm texts and in [Habakkuk 3:3, 9, 13](https://ref.ly/Hab3:3,Hab3:9,Hab3:13). *See* Music; Psalms, Book of.

* **Associated Passages:** HAB 3:3; HAB 3:9; HAB 3:13
