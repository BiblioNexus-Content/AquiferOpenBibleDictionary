## Hedgehog (id: 5763)

HEDGEHOG\*
==========

Small, insect\-eating mammal with a coat of short spines and similar to the porcupine ([Is 14:23](https://ref.ly/Isa14:23); [Zep 2:14](https://ref.ly/Zeph2:14)). *See* Animals (Porcupine).
