## Maarath (id: 6816)

MAARATH
=======

One of Judah’s cities of inheritance located in the hill country ([Jos 15:59](https://ref.ly/Josh15:59)), perhaps modern Biet Ummar, seven miles (11\.3 kilometers) north of Hebron. It may be the same as Maroth, mentioned in [Micah 1:12](https://ref.ly/Mic1:12).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:59; MIC 1:12
