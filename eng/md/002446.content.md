## Hashum (id: 5700)

HASHUM
======

1\. Ancestor of a family who returned from Babylon with Zerubbabel after the exile ([Ezr 2:19](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:19); [10:33](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:33); [Neh 7:22](https://ref.ly/Neh7:22)).

2\. Israelite who stood to Ezra’s left at the reading of the law ([Neh 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).

3\. Leader who signed Ezra’s covenant of faithfulness to God with Nehemiah and others after the exile ([Neh 10:18](https://ref.ly/Neh10:18)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 2:19; EZR 10:33; NEH 7:22; NEH 8:4; NEH 10:18
