## Chicory (id: 716004)

Chicory
=======

A plant (*Cichorium intybus*) that grows wild in many parts of Israel and the surrounding regions. It has bright blue flowers and leaves that can be eaten as a vegetable. Some scholars suggest it may have been one of the bitter herbs used in ancient times, though this is uncertain. 

*See* Bitter Herbs.
