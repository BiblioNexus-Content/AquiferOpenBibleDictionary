## Bear (Astronomy) (id: 4047)

BEAR (Astronomy)
================

The constellation of stars also called Ursa Major or the Big Dipper. It is mentioned in [Job 9:9](https://ref.ly/Job9:9) and [38:32](https://ref.ly/Job38:32).
