## Beeri (id: 4067)

BEERI
=====

1. Judith’s father who was a Hittite (sometimes spelled Hethite). Judith was one of the wives of Esau ([Genesis 26:34](https://ref.ly/Gen26:34)).
2. The father of Hosea the prophet ([Hosea 1:1](https://ref.ly/Hos1:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 26:34; HOS 1:1
