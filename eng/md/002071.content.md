## Gaal (id: 5326)

GAAL
====

Ebed’s son, who persuaded the men of Shechem to revolt against Abimelech, the judge of Israel. The revolt, however, was quickly crushed and Shechem was destroyed ([Jgs 9:26–41](https://ref.ly/Judg9:26-Judg9:41)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 9:26–JDG 9:41
