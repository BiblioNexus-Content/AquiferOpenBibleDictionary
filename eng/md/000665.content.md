## Baal-Gad (id: 3932)

BAAL\-GAD
=========

A site in the valley of Lebanon at the bottom of Mount Hermon in the north of Joshua’s conquest of Canaan ([Joshua 11:17](https://ref.ly/Josh11:17); [12:7](https://ref.ly/Josh12:7); [13:5](https://ref.ly/Josh13:5)). 

*See* Hermon, Mount.
