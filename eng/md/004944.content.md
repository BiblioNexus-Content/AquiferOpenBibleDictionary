## Secundus (id: 8206)

SECUNDUS
========

A Christian from Thessalonica. He was a traveling companion of Aristarchus. Secundus accompanied Paul on his third missionary journey through Macedonia and Greece. Then he waited for Paul at Troas in Asia Minor ([Acts 20:4–6](https://ref.ly/Acts20:4)). It is not known whether Secundus remained at Troas or went with Paul on his final trip to Jerusalem.

* **Associated Passages:** ACT 20:4
