## Horse Gate (id: 5911)

HORSE GATE
==========

Gate near the palace in Jerusalem ([Jer 31:40](https://ref.ly/Jer31:40)), in the southeast part of the city wall. Here Queen Athaliah was put to death ([2 Kgs 11:16](https://ref.ly/2Kgs11:16); [2 Chr 23:15](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:15)). The gate was restored under Nehemiah ([Neh 3:28](https://ref.ly/Neh3:28)).
