## Elishaphat (id: 5024)

ELISHAPHAT
==========

Military commander in Judah who supported Jehoiada the priest in overthrowing Queen Athaliah and making the young Joash king ([2 Chr 23:1](https://ref.ly/2Chr23:1)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2CH 23:1
