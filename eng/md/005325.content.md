## Sosipater (id: 8585)

SOSIPATER
=========

Jewish Christian who joined Paul, Timothy, Lucius, and Jason in sending greetings to the church at Rome ([Rom 16:21](https://ref.ly/Rom16:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** ROM 16:21
