## Dimonah (id: 4846)

DIMONAH
=======

Town mentioned in [Joshua 15:22](https://ref.ly/Josh15:22) as being located in the Judean Negev, close to Edomite territory. It was one of 29 towns in the general area of Beersheba; some scholars have identified it with the Dibon mentioned in [Nehemiah 11:25](https://ref.ly/Neh11:25).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:22; NEH 11:25
