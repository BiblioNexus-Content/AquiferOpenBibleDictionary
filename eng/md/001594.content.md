## Dimon (id: 4847)

DIMON\*
=======

KJV translation of a Moabite city in [Isaiah 15:9](https://ref.ly/Isa15:9), alternately named Dibon in the large Dead Sea Isaiah Scroll. The site of Dimon is identified with Khirbet Dimneh, nearly three miles (5 kilometers) northwest of Rabbah. *See* Dibon \#1.

* **Associated Passages:** ISA 15:9
* **Associated Articles:** Dibon (ID: `4836@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
