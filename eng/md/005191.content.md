## Shual (Place) (id: 8454)

SHUAL (Place)
=============

Region that included the town of Ophrah and was perhaps situated in the territory of Benjamin and Ephraim ([1 Sm 13:17](https://ref.ly/1Sam13:17)). Shual was located to the north of Micmash.

* **Associated Passages:** 1SA 13:17
