## Mareshah (Person) (id: 6944)

MARESHAH (Person)
=================

1\. Caleb’s firstborn son and the father of Hebron ([1 Chr 2:42](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:42); rsv, based on the Greek text). He is alternately called Mesha in the Hebrew text (rsv mg). *See* Mesha (Person) \#2.

2\. Perhaps a Judahite son of Laadah ([1 Chr 4:21](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:21)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:42; 1CH 4:21
