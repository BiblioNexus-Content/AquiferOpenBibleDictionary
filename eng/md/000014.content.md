## Abdon (Person) (id: 3273)

ABDON (Person)
==============

1. Hillel’s son who judged Israel for eight years ([Judges 12:13](https://ref.ly/Judg12:13-Judg12:15)–[15](https://ref.ly/Judg12:13-Judg12:15)). He was a very wealthy man. He owned 70 donkeys.
2. Shashak’s son from the tribe of Benjamin. He lived in Jerusalem ([1 Chronicles 8:23, 28](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:23,1Chr8:28)).
3. Jeiel’s oldest son from the tribe of Benjamin. He lived in Gibeon. This Abdon is mentioned in Saul’s list of ancestors ([1 Chronicles 8:30](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:30); [9:36](https://ref.ly/1Chr9:36)).
4. Micah’s son ([2 Chronicles 34:20](https://ref.ly/2Chr34:20)). He is also called Acbor, son of Micaiah. *See* Acbor \#2.
