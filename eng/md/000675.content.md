## Baalsamus (id: 3945)

BAALSAMUS
=========

The Levite assistant who explained the passages of the law which were read by Ezra to the people ([1 Esdras 9:43](https://ref.ly/1Esd9:43)). In the similar passage in Nehemiah, his name is Maaseiah.

*See* Maaseiah \#11.
