## Sidon (Person) (id: 8481)

SIDON (Person)
==============

Canaan’s firstborn son; Canaan was the son of Ham and grandson of Noah ([Gn 10:15, 19](https://ref.ly/Gen10:15,Gen10:19); [1 Chr 1:13](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:13)). Sidon founded a city (bearing his name) that set the northern boundary of the land of Canaan and later played a dominant role in Palestinian history.

*See also* Sidon (Place), Sidonian.
