## Addus (id: 3375)

ADDUS
=====

One of Solomon’s servants. His descendants returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel from exile ([1 Esdras 5:24](https://ref.ly/1Esd5:24)). His name is not mentioned in Ezra or Nehemiah.

* **Associated Passages:** 1ES 5:24
