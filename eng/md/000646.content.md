## Azmon (id: 3916)

AZMON
=====

A city on the south of Judah between Kadesh\-barnea and the “brook of Egypt” ([Numbers 34:4](https://ref.ly/Num34:4-Num34:5)[–](https://ref.ly/Num34:4-Num34:5)[5](https://ref.ly/Num34:4-Num34:5); [Joshua 15:4](https://ref.ly/Josh15:4)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:4; NUM 34:4–NUM 34:5
