## Abdi (id: 3272)

ABDI
====

1\. A member of the Merari clan of Levites. Abdi's grandson Ethan was a musician during King David’s time ([1 Chronicles 6:44](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:44); [15:17](https://ref.ly/1Chr15:17)).

2\. A Levite whose son Kish served during King Hezekiah’s time ([2 Chronicles 29:12](https://ref.ly/2Chr29:12)). This Abdi has sometimes been confused with Abdi \#1\.

3\. A member of the Elam clan in Ezra’s time. He married a non\-Israelite woman after the exile in Babylon ([Ezra 10:26](https://ref.ly/Ezra10:26)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 6:44; 1CH 15:17; 2CH 29:12; EZR 10:26
