## Shua (id: 8455)

SHUA
====

1\. Canaanite whose daughter Judah married. She bore Judah three sons: Er, Onan, and Shelah ([Gn 38:2–5, 12](https://ref.ly/Gen38:2-Gen38:5,Gen38:12)). *See* Bathshua \#1.

2\. Asherite, Heber’s daughter and the sister of Japhlet, Shomer, and Hotham ([1 Chr 7:32](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:32)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 38:12; 1CH 7:32; GEN 38:2–GEN 38:5
