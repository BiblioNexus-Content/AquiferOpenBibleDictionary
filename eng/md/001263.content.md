## Chesulloth (id: 4516)

CHESULLOTH
==========

A town in Issachar ([Joshua 19:18](https://ref.ly/Josh19:18)). It is also called Kisloth\-tabor in [Joshua 19:12](https://ref.ly/Josh19:12). Chesulloth is probably the modern village of Iksal, about 4\.8 kilometers (three miles) southeast of Nazareth.
