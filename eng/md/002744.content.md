## Iim (id: 5993)

IIM
===

1. The King James Version spelling of Iyim. Iim is a shortened form of Iye\-abarim. ([Numbers 33:45](https://ref.ly/Num33:45)).

    *See* Iye\-abarim, Iyim.

2. A town located near Edom. It was in the southern part of the land that was assigned to the tribe of Judah ([Joshua 15:29](https://ref.ly/Josh15:29)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 33:45; JOS 15:29
* **Associated Articles:** Iye-abarim, Iyim (ID: `6121@TyndaleBibleDictionary`)
