## Pedaiah (id: 7625)

PEDAIAH
=======

1\. Maternal grandfather of Judah’s King Jehoiakim. Pedaiah was from Rumah ([2 Kgs 23:36](https://ref.ly/2Kgs23:36)).

2\. Jeconiah’s third son ([1 Chr 3:18–19](https://ref.ly/1Chr3:18-1Chr3:19)).

3\. Joel’s father from the half\-tribe of Manasseh ([1 Chr 27:20](https://ref.ly/1Chr27:20)).

4\. Parosh’s son, who worked with the temple servants in repairing the Jerusalem wall opposite the Water Gate ([Neh 3:25](https://ref.ly/Neh3:25)).

5\. One who stood beside Ezra during the public reading of the Law ([Neh 8:4](https://ref.ly/Neh8:4)).

6\. Kolaiah’s son and Joed’s father ([Neh 11:7](https://ref.ly/Neh11:7)). He was a member of Benjamin’s tribe and lived in Jerusalem after the return from exile.

7\. Levite appointed by Nehemiah as treasurer of the storehouse to distribute grain, wine, and oil to the priests who served in the temple ([Neh 13:13](https://ref.ly/Neh13:13)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2KI 23:36; 1CH 27:20; NEH 3:25; NEH 8:4; NEH 11:7; NEH 13:13; 1CH 3:18–1CH 3:19
