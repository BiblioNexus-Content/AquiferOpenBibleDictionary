## Penuel (Person) (id: 7648)

PENUEL (Person)
===============

1\. Descendant (possibly son) of Hur and father (in the sense of progenitor) of Gedor from Judah’s tribe ([1 Chr 4:4](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:4)).

2\. Shashak’s son from Benjamin’s tribe ([1 Chr 8:25](https://ref.ly/1Chr8:25)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 4:4; 1CH 8:25
