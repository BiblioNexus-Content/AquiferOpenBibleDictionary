## Phanuel (id: 7690)

PHANUEL
=======

The father of Anna. the prophetess. Anna was a prophetess who thanked God and spoke about Jesus when he was brought to the temple as a baby ([Luke 2:36](https://ref.ly/Luke2:36)).

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 2:36
