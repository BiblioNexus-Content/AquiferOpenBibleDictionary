## Uzal (Place) (id: 8984)

UZAL (Place)
============

Place mentioned with Dan and Javan in an obscure passage ([Ez 27:19](https://ref.ly/Ezek27:19)); it is identifiable with modern Sana (ancient Awzal), the capital of Yemen.

* **Associated Passages:** EZK 27:19
