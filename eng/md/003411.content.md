## Lakkum, Lakum (id: 6669)

LAKKUM, LAKUM\*
===============

Fortified border town within the territory of Naphtali ([Jos 19:33](https://ref.ly/Josh19:33)). Its site is identifiable with Khirbet el\-Mansurah, about three miles (4\.8 kilometers) southwest of Khirbet Kerak, at the head of Wadi Fejjas.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:33
