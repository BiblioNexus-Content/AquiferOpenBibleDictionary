## Reaiah (id: 7956)

REAIAH
======

1\. Shobal’s son and the father of Jahath from the tribe of Judah ([1 Chr 4:2](https://ref.ly/1Chr4:2)), perhaps identifiable with Haroeh ([2:52](https://ref.ly/1Chr2:52)).

2\. Reubenite, Micah’s son and the father of Baal ([1 Chr 5:5](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:5)).

3\. Head or founder of a family of temple servants who returned with Zerubbabel from captivity in Babylon ([Ezr 2:47](https://ref.ly/Ezra2:47); [Neh 7:50](https://ref.ly/Neh7:50)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 2:52; 1CH 4:2; 1CH 5:5; EZR 2:47; NEH 7:50
