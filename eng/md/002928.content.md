## Jahdo (id: 6179)

JAHDO
=====

Gadite, son of Buz and a forefather of a number of valiant men who were registered during the reigns of King Jeroboam of Israel (793–753 BC) and King Jotham of Judah (750–735 BC; [1 Chr 5:14](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:14)).

* **Associated Passages:** 1CH 5:14
