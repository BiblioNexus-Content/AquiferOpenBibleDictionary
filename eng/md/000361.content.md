## Annunus (id: 3623)

ANNUNUS
=======

One of the men the Lord supplied in response to Ezra’s request for priests to serve in Jerusalem ([1 Esdras 8:48](https://ref.ly/1Esd8:48)). Annunus was probably one of Jeshaiah’s brothers ([Ezra 8:19](https://ref.ly/Ezra8:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** EZR 8:19; 1ES 8:48
