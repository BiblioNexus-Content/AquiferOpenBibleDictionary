## Purah (id: 7860)

PURAH
=====

Gideon’s servant, who accompanied his master on a secret night visit to the Midianite camp, where they were encouraged by the Lord ([Jgs 7:10–11](https://ref.ly/Judg7:10-Judg7:11)).

* **Associated Passages:** JDG 7:10–JDG 7:11
