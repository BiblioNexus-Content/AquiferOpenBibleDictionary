## Convocation, Holy (id: 4642)

CONVOCATION\*, Holy
===================

Solemn assemblies celebrated in Israel at appointed feasts in order that the people and the temple might be sanctified; the days were specially devoted to rest and sacrifice to God. *See* Feasts and Festivals of Israel; Atonement, Day of; Sabbath.
