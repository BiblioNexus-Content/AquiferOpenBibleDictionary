## Shamir (Place) (id: 8278)

SHAMIR (Place)
==============

1\. One of the 11 towns in the hill country allotted to Judah. First in the list of towns, Shamir is followed by Jattir ([Jos 15:48](https://ref.ly/Josh15:48)). Its location is perhaps at el\-Bireh, about 12 miles (19\.3 kilometers) southwest of Hebron.

2\. Town where Tola the judge lived and was later buried. Shamir was in the land of Ephraim ([Jgs 10:1–2](https://ref.ly/Judg10:1-Judg10:2)).

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 15:48; JDG 10:1–JDG 10:2
