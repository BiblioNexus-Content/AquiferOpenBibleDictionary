## Laish (Place) (id: 6667)

LAISH (Place)
=============

1\. Early name for the city of Dan ([Jgs 18:7, 14, 27–29](https://ref.ly/Judg18:7,Judg18:14,Judg18:27-Judg18:29)). *See* Dan (Place) \#1.

2\. KJV spelling of Laishah, a Benjamite town, in [Isaiah 10:30](https://ref.ly/Isa10:30). *See* Laishah.
