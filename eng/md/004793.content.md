## Rogelim (id: 8057)

ROGELIM
=======

A town in the highlands east of the Jordan River. Rogelim was the home of Barzillai the Gileadite. Barzillai served King David at Mahanaim when David was seeking safety from his son Absalom who had rebelled against him ([2 Samuel 17:27](https://ref.ly/2Sam17:27); [19:31](https://ref.ly/2Sam19:31)).

* **Associated Passages:** 2SA 17:27; 2SA 19:31
