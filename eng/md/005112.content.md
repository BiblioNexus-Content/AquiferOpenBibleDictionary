## Sheshai (id: 8376)

SHESHAI
=======

Sheshai was a descendant of Anak. Sheshai lived in the city of Hebron when the 12 Israelite spies explored the land of Canaan ([Numbers 13:22](https://ref.ly/Num13:22)). Later, the Israelites defeated Sheshai and took over his land ([Joshua 15:14](https://ref.ly/Josh15:14); [Judges 1:10](https://ref.ly/Judg1:10)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 13:22; JOS 15:14; JDG 1:10
