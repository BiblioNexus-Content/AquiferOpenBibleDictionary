## Korazin (id: 6645)

KORAZIN
=======

Another spelling of Chorazin, a Palestinian city on which Jesus pronounced woe ([Matthew 11:21](https://ref.ly/Matt11:21-Matt11:24)[–](https://ref.ly/Matt11:21-Matt11:24)[24](https://ref.ly/Matt11:21-Matt11:24); [Luke 10:13](https://ref.ly/Luke10:13-Luke10:14)[–](https://ref.ly/Luke10:13-Luke10:14)[14](https://ref.ly/Luke10:13-Luke10:14)).
