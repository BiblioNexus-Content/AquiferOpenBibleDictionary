## Dishon (id: 4862)

DISHON
======

1\. Seir’s fifth son and a Horite leader in Edom ([Gn 36:21](https://ref.ly/Gen36:21); [1 Chr 1:38](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:38)), whose people were eventually displaced by the Edomites.

2\. Grandson of Seir and son of Anah, a Horite leader. This Dishon was also the brother of Oholibamah, Esau’s wife ([Gn 36:25](https://ref.ly/Gen36:25); [1 Chr 1:41](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:41)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 36:21; GEN 36:25; 1CH 1:38; 1CH 1:41
