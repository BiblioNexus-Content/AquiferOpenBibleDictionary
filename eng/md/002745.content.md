## Ije-abarim (id: 5994)

IJE\-ABARIM
===========

The King James Version spelling of Iye\-abarim. This was one of the places Israel stopped at during their journey through the wilderness ([Numbers 21:11](https://ref.ly/Num21:11) and [33:44](https://ref.ly/Num33:44)).
