## Naphish (id: 7302)

NAPHISH
=======

Eleventh of Ishmael’s 12 sons ([Gn 25:15](https://ref.ly/Gen25:15); [1 Chr 1:31](https://ref.ly/1Chr1:31)) and the founder of a tribe that later went to war against the tribes of Israel living east of the Jordan ([1 Chr 5:19](https://ref.ly/1Chr5:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 25:15; 1CH 1:31; 1CH 5:19
