## Yiron (id: 9105)

YIRON
=====

One of the fortified cities of Naphtali’s tribe ([Jos 19:38](https://ref.ly/Josh19:38)). Some have identified Yiron with the present village of Jarun, southeast of Bint Jebeil.

* **Associated Passages:** JOS 19:38
