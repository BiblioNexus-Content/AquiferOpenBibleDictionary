## Musi, musita (id: 717187)

MUSI, MUSITA
============

O filho de Merari, neto de Levi e irmão de Mali ([Êx 6\.19](https://ref.ly/Exod6:19); [Nm 3\.20](https://ref.ly/Num3:20); [1Cr 6\.19,47](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:19,1Chr6:47)). Ele foi o pai de Mali, Éder e Jeremote ([1 Cr 23\.21–23](https://ref.ly/1Chr23:21-1Chr23:23); [24\.26,30](https://ref.ly/1Chr24:26,1Chr24:30)). Ele foi o fundador da família dos musitas ([Nm 3\.33](https://ref.ly/Num3:33); [26\.58](https://ref.ly/Num26:58)).

* **Associated Passages:** EXO 6:19; NUM 3:20; NUM 3:33; NUM 26:58; 1CH 6:19; 1CH 6:47; 1CH 24:26; 1CH 24:30; 1CH 23:21–1CH 23:23
