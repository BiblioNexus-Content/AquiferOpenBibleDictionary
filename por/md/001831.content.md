## Semida (id: 759996)

SEMIDA
======

Semida era o pai do grupo familiar chamado os semidaítas ([Nm 26\.32](https://ref.ly/Num26:32)). Este clã fazia parte da tribo de Manassés ([Js 17\.2](https://ref.ly/Josh17:2); [1Cr 7\.19](https://ref.ly/1Chr7:19)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 26:32; JOS 17:2; 1CH 7:19
