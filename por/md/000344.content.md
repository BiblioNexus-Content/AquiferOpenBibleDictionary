## Buqui (id: 759683)

BUQUI
=====

1. Um líder da tribo de Dã que ajudou Josué a dividir a terra de Canaã entre os israelitas ([Nm 34\.22](https://ref.ly/Num34:22)).
2. Um ancestral de Esdras ([1Cr 6\.5,51](https://ref.ly/1Chr6:5,1Chr6:51); [Ed 7\.1,4–5](https://ref.ly/Ezra7:1,Ezra7:4-Ezra7:5)).

* **Associated Passages:** NUM 34:22; 1CH 6:5; 1CH 6:51; EZR 7:1; EZR 7:4–EZR 7:5
